Veggie Noodle Side Dish

1 small julienned sweet red pepper
3/4 c. green beans, carrots
1/4 red onion
1 T. canola oil
3 oz ramen noodles
1 yellow squash
1 med. zucchini
1/4 c. chicken broth
1 T. soy sauce
1 t. fajita seasoning mix

In wok, stir fry the pepper, green beans, carrots and onion for 4 minutes.  
Meanwhile cook noodles discarding seasoning packet.  
Add the remaining ingredients to the veggie mix.  
Cook until veggies are crisp tender.  
Drain noodles and add to veggies and mix.
