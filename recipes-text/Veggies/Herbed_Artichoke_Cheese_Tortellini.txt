Herbed Artichoke Cheese Tortellini

1 can italian diced tomatoes
1 can artichoke hearts
9 oz. refrigerated tortellini
1 c. onion
1/4 c. parsley
2-4 t. dried basil
1 t. garlic
1/4 t. oregano
1/8 t. crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 c. olive oil
2oz can sliced olives, drained
1/4 t. salt
1/8 c. grated Parmesan

Drain tomatoes, reserving 2/3 c. juice.
Drain artichokes, reserving 3/4 c. liquid; chop and set aside.
Cook tortellini.
In large skillet sate the onions, parsley, basil, garlic, oregano and pepper flakes in oil for 4-5 minutes or until onions are tender.
Add the reserved tomatoes, tomato juice and artichoke liquid.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 10-12 minutes or until slightly thickened.
Drain tortellini; add to tomato mix.
Stir in olives, salt and reserved artichokes; heat through.
Sprinkle with parmesan.
