Eggplant Casserole 2

1 large eggplant
1/4 c. butter
1 onion
1 stalk celery
1/4 c. water
1/4 c. bell pepper
1/4 c. grated cheese
2 tomatoes
1 c. bread crumbs, toasted

salt and pepper to taste
beef or sausage, browned
Split eggplant in half, remove inside.
Place butter in pan, add onion and eggplant.
Start cooking, then add water, celery and bell pepper.
Let cook until veggies are tender.
Then add tomatoes and cheese.
Stir in bread crumbs and place in empty eggplant shells.
Garnish with chopped tomatoes and bake until done.
