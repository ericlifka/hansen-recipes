Cranberry Whip

1 bag cranberries
1 c. sugar, divided
12-14 oz. crushed pineapple, drained
1 1/2 c. mini marshmallows
chopped nuts
1/2 pt. whipping cream

Drain and grind a bag of cranberries.
Stir in 1/2 c. sugar, crushed pineapple, mini marshmallows, chopped nuts.
Let sit in the fridge over night.
Next day:  Whip cream with 1/2 c. sugar.
Fold into the cranberry mix.
