Crab-Shrimp Bake

2 cans crab
1 bag shrimp so you have a total of 1 lb. can be both or crab or shrimp
1 can cr. of shrimp soup
2/3 c. milk
1/3 c. sour cream
2 c. egg noodles, uncooked
1/2 c. cheddar

Drain meat and blend soup, milk and sour cream.
Stir in noodles and top with cheese.
Put in a 10 x 6 pan.
Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
