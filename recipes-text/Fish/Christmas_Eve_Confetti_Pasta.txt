Christmas Eve Confetti Pasta

16 oz. linguine
1 c. red pepper
1 c. green pepper
1/3 c. onion
3 garlic cloves, peeled and thinly sliced
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. dried oregano
1/8 t. red pepper flakes
1/8 t. pepper
1/4 c. olive oil
2 lbs. cooked shrimp
1/2 c. Parmesan cheese
