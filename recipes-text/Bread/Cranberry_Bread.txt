Cranberry Bread

2 c. flour
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. baking soda
1 1/2 t. baking powder
3/4 c. sugar       
1 egg
2 T. shortening
1/2 c. orange juice     
2T. hot water
1/2 c. nuts
1 c. cranberry

Cut cranberries and nuts.  
Sift the dry ingredients together.  
Add liquid and combine until dry ingredients are moistened.  
Fold in nuts, cranberries.  
Bake 1 hour and 10 minutes at 350.
