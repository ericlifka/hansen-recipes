Pumpkin Bread

4 eggs
2/3 c. butter   
3 c. sugar     
15 oz pumpkin      
3 c. flour
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. baking powder
2 t. baking soda
1 t. cinnamon    
1/2 t. cloves
1/2 t. nutmeg         
1 c. chopped nuts (optional)

Combine eggs, butter and sugar, blending well.  
Add the rest of ingredients.  
Pour into 2 greased 8 1/2 x 5 1/2 loaf pans and bake at 350 for 1 hour.  
Turn onto racks and cool.
