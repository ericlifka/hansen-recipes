Spiced Squash Muffins

2 c. flour
1/3 c. brown sugar
2 t. baking powder
1 t. cinnamon
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. ginger
1/4 t. nutmeg
2 eggs
3/4 c. squash
3/4 c. light corn syrup
1/4 c. butter, melted 
1/4 c. veggie oil
1 t. vanilla
Topping:  
4 T. butter 
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 t. cinnamon

Combine all dry ingredients and then wet muffin mix stuff.  
Fill paper lined muffin cups 3/4 full.   
Combine topping mix until crumbly.  
Sprinkle on muffins.  
Bake 400 for 15-20 minutes.    
Cool 5 minutes before putting on wire rack.  
Serve warm.
