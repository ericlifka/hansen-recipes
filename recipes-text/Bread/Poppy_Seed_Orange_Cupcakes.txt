Poppy Seed Orange Cupcakes

Cupcake Portion:
1 1/2 c. flour
1 c. sugar      
1/2 c. sour cream
1/3 c. butter, softened
1 egg        
1 T. poppy seeds
2 T. orange peel zest, and orange juice   
1/2 t. baking soda and salt
Icing Portion:
1/2 c. butter
3 oz. cream cheese
1/4 c. powdered sugar
1 T. orange zest

Cupcake Portion:
In large bowl combine all ingredients; beat on low speed until ingredients are moistened.  
Spoon into muffin pans.  
Bake at 400 for 18-22 minutes or until lightly browned.
Icing Portion:  
Beat together all icing ingredients until creamy, then add on top of the cupcakes.
