Hawaiian Cheese Bread

1 loaf Hawaiian sweet bread (1 lb.)
8 oz. swiss cheese
3 slices red onion, chopped
1/2 c. melted butter
1 T.  minced garlic
1 t. salt

Cut bread diagonally into 1” slices to within 1” of bottom.  
Repeat cuts in opposite direction.  
Cut swiss cheese into 1/4 in. slices; cut slices into small pieces;  
Insert into bread.  
Combine the onion, butter, garlic and salt; spoon over bread.  
Wrap loaf in foil.  
Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes or until cheese is melted.  
Serve warm.
