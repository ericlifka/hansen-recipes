Sandra Lee’s Chicken Tenders

1/2 c. flour
1/2 packet southwest steak marinade mix      
pepper    
3 c. corn flakes
Mix egg and 1/2 c. buttermilk
1 lb. tenders
Paula Deen’s honey mustard:  
3/4 c. mayo
3 T. honey
2 T. mustard 
1 T. lemon juice or juice from 1/2 lemon
horseradish to taste   
2 T. orange juice (adjust amount for desired dressing consistency)

Combine flour, marinade and pepper.
Soak chicken in buttermilk, toss into flour, shake off and dip in milk again, then crumbs.
Fry until done (my deep fryer is 4 min. 30 sec.).
