Ham & Cheese Sandwich

1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. mayo
3 T. Creole mustard
1 T. onion powder
2 t. poppy seeds
Buns 
ham
cheese

Combine butter, mayo, creole, onion powder, and poppy seeds, spread on buns.
Top with ham and cheese.
Wrap in foil.
Can freeze.
Bake frozen at 350 for 1 hour, or bake unfrozen at 350 for 20-30 minutes.
