Goulash

1 1/4 lb. hamburger
onion
celery
1 can tomato soup     
S&P
can corn
tomato juice
stewed tomato
2 1/2 c. egg noodles, cooked

Brown hamburger and drain.
Add onion and celery.
Turn off heat.
Add noodles, corn, add soup, stewed tomatoes with liquid and add juice or tomato sauce to moisten.
Heat in oven for 45-60 minutes at 350.
