Salisbury Steak with Gemelli

1 egg
1/2 c. soft bread crumbs
1 t. Italian seasoning
1/2 t. pepper and minced garlic
1 lb. hamburger
1 T. olive oil
1 c. sliced fresh mushrooms
2 T. flour
1 c. chicken broth
1 T. ketchup
1 t. Worcestershire sauce
hot gemelli or spiral pasta

In a large bowl combine the egg, bread crumbs, Italian seasoning, pepper and garlic.
Crumble beef over mix and mix well.
Shape into 4 patties.
In a large skillet, cook patties in oil over medium high heat for 5-7 minutes on each side or until meat is no longer pink.
Remove and keep warm.
Drain, reserving 2 T. drippings.
Saute mushrooms in drippings until tender.
Stir in flour until blended.
Gradually stir in broth, ketchup, and Worcestershire sauce.
Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
Return patties to the skillet.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 3-4 minutes or until heated through.
Serve with gemelli.
