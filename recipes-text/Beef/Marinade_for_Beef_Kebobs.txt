Marinade for Beef Kebobs

1 c. red wine vinegar and veggie oil
1/4 c. vinegar and ketchup
1 clove garlic
1/4 c. sugar
2 t. Worcestershire
1 t. salt
1 T. rosemary or basil

Combine all ingredients and marinade at least 6 hours or overnight in refrigerator.
Use 3 lbs.. sirloin or top round.
Grill 20-25 minutes.
