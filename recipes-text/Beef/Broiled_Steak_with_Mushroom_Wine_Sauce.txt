Broiled Steak with Mushroom Wine Sauce

2 boneless rib steaks
2 T. veggie oil
1 c. mushrooms, sliced
2 T. onion
4 T. red wine
1/2 c. sour cream
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper

Heat oil.
Add onions and mushrooms and saut√© until golden.
Add wine.
Cook for minute.
Add sour cream and cook until heated.
Remove from heat, keep warm.
Broil steak.
Pour sauce over steak.
