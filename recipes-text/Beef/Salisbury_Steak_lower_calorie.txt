Salisbury Steak (lower calorie)

1/2 c. fat free milk
14 fat free saltines, crushed
2 T. dried minced onion
2 t. dried parsley flakes
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 jar fat free beef gravy
2 T. ketchup
2 t. Worcestershire sauce
1 4/ t. pepper

In a large bowl combine the milk, saltines, onion and parsley.
Crumble beef over mix and Mix well.
Shape into 4 patties.
Place in an 8 in square baking dish coated with PAM.
In a small bowl combine the gravy, ketchup, Worcestershire and pepper; pour over patties.
Bake uncovered at 350 for 50-55 minutes or until meat is no longer pink. (266 calories)
