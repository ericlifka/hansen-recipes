Open-Face Mexican Toasted Sandwich

1 lb. hamburger
1 onion
1 garlic
1/4 c. gr. pepper
2 T. olive oil, divided
1/2 t. salt, pepper
1/4 t. chili powder
4 oz. can drained chopped green chilies
1 c. drained tomatoes
1 lb. Velveeta
1/4 c. half and half
toast
