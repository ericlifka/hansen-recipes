South of the Border Casserole

7 oz. elbow macaroni
1 lb. turkey
1 onion
1 c. celery
1 garlic
16 oz. can whole tomatoes, cut up and undrained   
1/2 c. ripe olive
4 oz. green chilies
2 t. beef flavored instant bouillon
1/2 t. seasoned salt   
1/4 t. cumin
dash cayenne pepper
6 oz. muenster cheese
6 oz. cheddar cheese

Prepare macaroni and drain.
In skillet combine turkey, onion, celery and garlic.
Cook until turkey is browned.
Stir in rest of ingredients except the cheeses.
Simmer 10 minutes.
Combine macaroni, meat mix and cheeses.
Put in casserole dish.
Cover.
Bake at 350 about 20 minutes.
