Chicken with Cranberries

3 lb. chicken
1 c. peanut oil
3/4 c. orange juice
1 c. flour
onion
16 oz. cranberries
1/4 t. cinnamon and ginger      
salt, pepper, cayenne pepper

Mix flour and salt and peppers.
Dredge chicken and panfry in oil.
Mix rest of ingredients and boil.
Pour over chicken and cover.
Cook slowly 35-40 minutes.Chicken with Peanut Butter (Frugal Gourmet) 
Put chicken, onion, dill, bay leaf in water and cook 45 min.
Add 1/2 c. peanut butter or more to hot stock.
Add 3 T. cornstarch dissolved in 1/2 c. water till thick.
Put chicken&sauce on rice
