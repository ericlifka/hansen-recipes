Thai Chicken Fettuccine

1 c. salsa
1/4 c. creamy peanut butter
2 T. Orange juice
2 T. honey
1 t. soy sauce
8 oz. fettucine
3/4 lb. chicken breast, cut in strips
1 T. v. oil
1 medium red pepper, julienned
1/4 c. minced fresh cilantro

Cook fettucine and drain.
Microwave first 5 ingredients for 1 minute.
Stir and set aside.
In pan cook chicken in oil over medium heat 3-5 minutes or until browned.
Add red pepper; cook and stir until crisp tender.
Top fettuccine with chicken mix and sauce, top with cilantro.
