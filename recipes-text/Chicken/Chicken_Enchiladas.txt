Chicken Enchiladas

2 c. shredded chicken
8 oz. cream cheese
1 can olives, chopped 
gr. chilies
cheese
onion 
1-2 cans green sauce
tortillas

Mix chicken with cream cheese, olives, gr. chilies, and onion.
Put in tortillas, top with a little bit of cheese.
Roll up.
Put some green sauce on bottom of pan, top with tortillas.
Pour remaining sauce over.
Bake 25 minutes at 350.
Top with some cheese and olives, bake about 5 more minutes.
