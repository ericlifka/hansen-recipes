Light and Easy Lemon Broccoli Chicken

1 T. veggie oil
1 lb. boneless breasts
1 can cr. of broccoli soup
1/4 c. milk
2 t. lemon juice
1/8 t. pepper
4 thin lemon slices

In skillet in hot oil cook chicken 10 minutes or until browned on both sides.
Spoon off fat.
Combine soup and milk; stir in lemon juice and pepper.
Pour over chicken;  top each chicken piece with lemon slice.
Reduce heat to low.
Cover simmer 5 min. or until chicken is fork tender, stirring occasionally.
