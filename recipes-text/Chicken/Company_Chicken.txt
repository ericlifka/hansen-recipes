Company Chicken

5 boneless breasts        
5 slices Swiss, muenster, or provolone        
1 can cr. chicken soup
1/4 c. white wine, sherry or water
1 c. herb dressing
1/4 to 1/2 c. butter 

Place chicken in baking dish.
Top each with a slice of cheese.
Combine soup and wine.
Pour over chicken.
Top with dressing mix and dot with butter.
Bake at 250 for 2 hours or 350 for 1 hour.
Serve over rice.
