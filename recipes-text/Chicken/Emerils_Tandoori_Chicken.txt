Emerils Tandoori Chicken

8 pieces chicken, skinless
2 T. veggie oil      
1/2 c. onion
2 T. each garlic, ginger
1 t. jalapeno pepper, chopped
1 T. paprika    
1 1/2 t. salt     
1 t. cumin, turmeric
1 t. coriander    
1 t. garam masala     
1/2 t. cayenne     
1/2 c. plain yogurt    
1 T. lemon juice

With a fork, prick holes in chicken.
Cut 3 diagonal slices.
Place chicken in ziploc bag.
In blender combine oil, onion, garlic, ginger, pepper and process to a paste.
Add paprika, salt, cumin, turmeric, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne, process until blended.
Add yogurt and lemon juice, and process to smooth sauce.
Pour over chicken.
Turn to coat evenly.
Refrigerate at least 4 hours, up to 24, turning occasionally.
Preheat grill.
Remove chicken from marinade.
Place on grill and cook 8-10 minutes on the first side.
Turn, baste as need, cook on second side for 8-10 minutes.
Turn and continue cooking until chicken is cooked through, but still tender 25-30 minutes.
You can also bake in a preheated 425 oven for 35 minutes.
