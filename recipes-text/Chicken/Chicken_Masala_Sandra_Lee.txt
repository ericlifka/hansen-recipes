Chicken Masala (Sandra Lee)

4 breasts
1/3 c. flour
2 T. oil
1 onion
1 t. garlic
1 T. garam masala
15 oz. diced tomato, drained
1/2 c. cream of chicken soup
1 c. light coconut milk
salt and pepper
1 c. peas (optional)
rice

Shake chicken in flour.
Heat oil.
Fry breast on both sides.
Remove.
Add onion and garlic--cook until soft.
Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.
Reduce to simmer, put breasts back in pan, and simmer 15 minutes.
Add peas, and simmer 10 more minutes.
