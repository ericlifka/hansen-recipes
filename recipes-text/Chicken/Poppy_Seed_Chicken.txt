Poppy Seed Chicken
6 chicken breast halves
1 can cr. of chicken soup
1 c. cr. of mushroom soup
1/2 c. sour cream
15 Ritz or club crackers, crushed
1/4 stick butter, melted   
2 T. poppy seeds

Coat a 9 x 13 inch pan with Pam.
Place breasts in pan.
Combine soups with sour cream and pour over chicken.
Combine crumbs, butter, and poppy seeds.
Sprinkle over sauce.
Bake for 45-60 minutes at 350.
