Chicken With Orange Sauce

4 chicken breasts       
1/4 c. flour with salt and pepper
1 onion
1 can orange juice concentrated

Slice and fry onion in butter until clear.
Remove from pan and save.
Brown flour dusted chicken in remaining butter.
Place onions on top of chicken and add orange juice concentrate and enough water to cook.
Bake at 300 for 1 1/2 hours.
Serve with rice.
