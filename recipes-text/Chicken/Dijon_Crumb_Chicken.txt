Dijon Crumb Chicken

2 eggs
2 T. Dijon
1 c. seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 c. Parm cheese
1 1/2 t. onion powder and garlic powder
4-4 oz breasts, flatten to 1/4"

In bowl beat eggs and mustard.
In another bowl combine bread crumbs, parm cheese, onion and garlic powder.
Dip chicken in egg mix and then in crumb mix.
In skillet cook chicken in oil over med heat for 15-20 minutes.
