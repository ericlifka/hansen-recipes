1 c. grated Swiss cheese
1/4 c. crisp crumbled bacon
1/4 c. minced onions
1/4 c. mayo
2 T. chives
1 t. worcestershire
Black olives, halved
20-25 cocktail rye or white sandwich bread with crusts cut off

Heat oven to 375.  
Combine all ingredients except olives.  
Mix well.  
Spread about 1 T. cheese mix on each slice.  
Heat for 10-15 minutes.  
Place  olive on top.
