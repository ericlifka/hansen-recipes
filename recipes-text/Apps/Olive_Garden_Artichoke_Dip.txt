Olive Garden Artichoke Dip

1 can artichoke hearts chopped
1/2 c. chopped spinach
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 c. Parmesan
1/2 t. red pepper flakes
Dash salt, pepper, garlic powder

Boil spinach and hearts in a cup of water for 10 min.  
Drain. 
Heat cream cheese and add spinach and hearts.  
Stir.  
Mix in rest of stuff.
