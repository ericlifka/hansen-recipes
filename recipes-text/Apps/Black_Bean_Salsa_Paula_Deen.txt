Black Bean Salsa (Paula Deen)

2 15 oz. cans black beans, rinsed and drained
17 oz. frozen corn, thawed
2 large tomatoes, seeded and diced
1 avocado, peeled and diced
1 onion, diced
1/8-1/4 c. cilantro leaves, chopped
2 T. lime juice
1 t. red wine vinegar
Salt and pepper

Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.  
Cover and chill overnight.  
Taste and add salt, pepper or more lime juice as necessary.  
Serve with chips as appetizer or with grilled chicken as meal.
