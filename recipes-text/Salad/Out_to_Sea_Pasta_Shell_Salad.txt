Out to Sea Pasta Shell Salad

3 c. medium pasta shells
2/3 c. shredded carrots
2/3 c. thawed peas
4 bacon strips, cooked and diced
4 oz. cr. cheese
1/2 c. sour cream
3/4 c. milk
1 envelope ranch dressing

Cook shells and drain.
In bowl combine carrots, peas and bacon.
In small bowl beat cream cheese and sour cream.
Add milk and dressing mix; beat until combined.
Add pasta to veggies and add dressing mix, toss to coat.
Chill until serving.
