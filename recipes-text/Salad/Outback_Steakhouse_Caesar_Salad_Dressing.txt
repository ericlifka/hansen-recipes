Outback Steakhouse Caesar Salad Dressing

1 c. mayo
1/4 c. egg substitute
1/4 c. parmesan cheese
2 T. water
2 T. olive oil
1 1/2 T. lemon juice
1 T. anchovy paste
2 cloves garlic
2 t. sugar
1/2 t. pepper
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. dried parsley flakes, crushed

Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.
Beat with mixer for about 1 minutes.
Cover bowl and chill for several hours for flavors to develop.
