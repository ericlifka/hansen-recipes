Wild Rice With Pork Chops

Pork Chops
1 c. wild rice
1 1/2 c. water
2 cans mushrooms
3 T. chicken bouillon
1 can cream of mushroom soup

In bottom of 9 x 13 pan place 1 c. wild rice.
Add 1 1/2 c. water and 2 cans mushrooms with juice.
Sprinkle 3 T. chicken bouillon over.
Spoon 1 cr. of mushroom over mix.
Brown chops and place on rice.
Cover pan and bake at 350 for 2 hours.
