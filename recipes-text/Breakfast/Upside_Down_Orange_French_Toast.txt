Upside Down Orange French Toast

1/2 c. melted butter
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. chopped pecans
2 T. orange peel
2 t. cinnamon
12 slices french bread
4 oz. cream cheese, cube
8 eggs
1 c. orange juice
1/2 t. butter flavor

Place butter in 9 x 13.  
Combine sugar pecans, orange peel and cinnamon; sprinkle over butter.  
Arrange bread in dish.  
Dot with cream cheese.  
In bowl, whisk eggs, orange juice, and butter flavor; pour over bread.  
Cover and refrigerate overnight.  
Remove from fridge 30 minutes before baking.  
Bake uncovered at 325 for 25-30 minutes.
