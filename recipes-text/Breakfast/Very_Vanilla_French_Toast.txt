Very Vanilla French Toast

1 c. milk
1 3 oz. package cook and serve vanilla pudding
1 egg
1/2 t. cinnamon
8 slices Texas Toast or French bread
2 t. butter

In a bowl whisk the milk, pudding mix, egg and cinnamon for 2 minutes or until well blended.  
Dip toast in pudding mix, coating both sides.  
Melt butter on a griddle; cook french toast for 2-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
