Eggs Scramble

4 c. croutons
1 t. salt
1 t. prepared mustard
2 c. shredded cheese
8 beaten eggs
4 c. milk
1/8 t. pepper
2 c. ham or 1 lb. sausage, cooked

Place croutons and cheese in greased 9 x 13.  
Combine rest of ingredients except meat.  
Pour over croutons and cheese.  
Top with meat.  
Bake at 325 for 1 hour.  
You can halve this recipe for an 8 x 8 pan.
