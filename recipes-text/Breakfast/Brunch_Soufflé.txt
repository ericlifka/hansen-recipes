Brunch Soufflé

8 slices bread, cubed
2/3 lb. velveeta cheese, sliced
8 eggs
4 c. milk
1/8 t. each salt, pepper and dry mustard

Cut crusts from bread and cube.  
Put bread in greased 9 x 13” pan and put on sliced cheese.  
Beat eggs slightly and add seasonings.  
Refrigerate overnight.  
Bake at 325 for 1 hour.
