Apple Windsor

apples
sugar
cinnamon
flour
baking powder
shortening
1 egg

Slice apples in bottom of baking pan. 
Add 1 c. sugar and cinnamon over apples.
Then mix 1 c. sugar, 1 c. flour, 1 t. baking powder, 1/2 c. shortening like a pie crust then add 1 egg.
Crumble over apples and bake in 350 oven for 1 hour.
Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
