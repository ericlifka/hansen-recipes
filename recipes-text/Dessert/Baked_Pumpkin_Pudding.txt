Baked Pumpkin Pudding

1/2 c. butter      
2 c. sugar
5  eggs
1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. evaporated milk
2 c. pumpkin
1 t. cinnamon       
1/2 t. each nutmeg, ginger, mace, cloves
2 t. vanilla

Combine butter and sugar.
Mix well.
Add eggs, mix well.
Stir in flour, pumpkin, spices and vanilla.
Mix until smooth.
Pour into a well greased 2 qt. casserole.
Bake at 350 for 55 minutes or until firm.
Top with pecan halves.
