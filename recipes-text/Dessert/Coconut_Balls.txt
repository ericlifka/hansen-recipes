Coconut Balls

1/2 c. butter
3/4 c. sugar
1/2 lb. chopped dates
1 T. milk
1 t. vanilla
2 c. Rice Krispies
1/2 c. nuts

Mix butter, sugar, chopped dates, milk, and vanilla.
Cook over low heat until mushy.
Stirring constantly or it will burn.
Remove from heat.
Stir in Rice Krispies and nuts.
As soon as cool enough roll into balls and then roll in flaked coconut.
