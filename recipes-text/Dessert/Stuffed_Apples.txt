Stuffed Apples

4 large baking apples
1/2 c. raisins or dates
1/2 c. dry sherry or water
2 T. nuts, brown sugar and cherries    
1/4 t. cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg        
1 T. butter

Core apples, enlarge each opening slightly.
Place each apple on a 12 x 18 piece of foil.
Combine raisins, water, nuts, sugar, cherries and spices.
Divide filling among apples.
Dot with butter.
Bring foil up around apples.
Seal loosely.
Grill over slow coals for 1 hour.
Open foil and serve with ice cream.
