Oatmeal Scotchies

1 c. flour
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt and cinnamon
1 c. butter, soft
3/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. brown sugar
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla
3 c. oatmeal
12 oz. butterscotch morsels

In bowl combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon, set aside.
In bowl combine butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla; beat until creamy.
Gradually add flour mix.
Stir in oats and butterscotch morsels.
Drop by tablespoons onto cookie sheets.
Bake at 375 for 7-8 minutes for chewier cookies or 9-10 minutes for crisper cookies.
