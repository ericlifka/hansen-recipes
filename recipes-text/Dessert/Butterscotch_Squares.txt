Butterscotch Squares

6 T. butter, divided
10 1/2 oz. mini marshmallows
6 c. rice cereal       
1/4 c. milk
12 oz. butterscotch morsels
1 c. honey roasted peanuts

Grease a 9 x 13 pan.
Melt 4 T. butter in pot.
Add marshmallow and cook over low heat until melted and smooth.
Remove from heat and add cereal.
Stir to coat.
Pat into pan, oiling your fingers with oil to prevent sticking.
In another pan melt 2 T. mutter and add butterscotch morsels.
Stir until soft.
Add the milk stirring until smooth.
Mix in nuts and coat well.
Pour over cereal and smooth the top.
