Pecan Tassies 1

2-3 oz. pkg. cream cheese
2 sticks butter
2 c. flour
Filling
:2 eggs
1 1/2 c. brown sugar
2 T. butter
2 t. vanilla       
1/2 c. pecans

Combine cream cheese, butter and flour, refrigerate for 1 hour.
Roll dough into balls.
Form in cup of mini pans up to top making well for filling.
Mix filling ingredients, and chop pecans.
Fill and bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes.
Allow to cool 5 minutes, remove from pans.
