1 c. sugar
3 T. cornstarch
4 c. rhubarb            
1/2 c. water    
1/2 c. cool whip
1 graham cracker crust
1 1/2 c. tiny marshmallows
1 pkg. instant vanilla pudding

Make graham cracker crust.
Combine sugar and cornstarch.
Stir in rhubarb and water.
Cook and stir till thickened.
Reduce heat and cook 2-3 minutes.
Spread on graham cracker crust and cool.
Combine marshmallows and cool whip.
Spoon over rhubarb mix.
Prepare pudding mix according to package.
Spread over all.
Sprinkle with remaining graham cracker crumbs.
Keep in  refrigerator.
