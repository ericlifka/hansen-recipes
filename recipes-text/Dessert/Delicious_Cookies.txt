Delicious Cookies

1 c. white sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. butter
1 c. veggie oil
1 egg
1 t. vanilla      
Mix those ingredients together and add:
3 1/2 c. flour
1 c. flaked coconut
1 c. Rice Krispies
1 c. oatmeal
1/2 t. salt
1 t. cream of tartar
1 t. soda
1 c. chocolate chips

Let stand in refrigerator for 15 minutes.
Make small balls or drop by teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet.
Press down with fork dipped in sugar.
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
