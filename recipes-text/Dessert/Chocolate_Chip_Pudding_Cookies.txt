Chocolate Chip Pudding Cookies

2 1/2 c. flour       
1 t. baking soda       
1 c. butter or 1/2 c. oil and melted butter
3/4 c. light brown sugar
1/4 c. sugar    
1 pkg. (4 serving size) instant vanilla pudding
1 t. vanilla
2 eggs
12 oz. Hershey's milk chocolate chips
1 c. nuts

Combine butter, sugars, pudding mix and vanilla in large bowl; beat until creamy.
Beat in eggs.
Gradually add flour and baking soda, then stir in chips and nuts.
Batter will be sort of stiff.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes.
