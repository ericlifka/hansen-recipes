Rhubarb Pie

1 premade Pie crust
4 c. rhubarb (remove long strands)
1 2/3 c. sugar
1/3 c. flour
dash salt
butter

Mix that together and let stand 15 minutes.
If you  use more than 4 c. be sure to add more sugar and flour.
Prepare pastry (or buy crust)  Line a 9â€ pie plate with pastry.
Fill with rhubarb mix.
Dot with 2 T. butter.
Adjust top crust, cutting slits for escape of steam.
Seal; flute.
Bake at 400 for 50 minutes.
(I used Pillsbury crust from the refrigerator section that you roll out.)
