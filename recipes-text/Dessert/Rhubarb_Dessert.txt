Rhubarb Dessert

Crust:  
1 c. flour
2 T. butter
1 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt
1 beaten egg
2 T. milk  
Filling:  
3 c. rhubarb
1 pkg. red jello
Topping:  
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. flour
1/3 c. butter

Mix as for pie crust.
Pat in bottom and sides of 9â€ square pan.
Place rhubarb in crust (unbaked) sprinkle jello over top.
Mix topping ingredients together and sprinkle over top.
Bake 45 minutes at 350.
