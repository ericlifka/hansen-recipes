Indian Pudding

6 T. unsalted butter
3/4 t. allspice and mace     
1/2 c. + 3 T. dark brown sugar
2-12 oz. cans evaporated milk
1/2  t. salt
2 eggs
1 t. baking soda
1 t. vanilla
1 c. yellow cornmeal

Mix in pan butter, allspice and mace for 2 minutes.
Stir in brown sugar until dissolved.
Add milk and salt.
Cook until milk is ready to boil.
Remove from heat.
Add soda, vanilla and cornmeal until it is well blended.
Add frothy eggs.
Pour in casserole dish.
Bake at 350 for 45-55 minutes.
