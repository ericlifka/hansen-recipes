Carrot Cake

4 eggs
2 c. sugar
1 1/2 c. veggie oil
1 t. salt
2 t. cinnamon
2 t. soda
2 c. flour
1 c. nuts
1 c. coconut       
3 c. grated carrots

Mix all and bake in a 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 40-45 minutes.
Frosting is made with 3-4 oz. cream cheese, 1/2 c. butter, 2 c. powdered sugar (may need more) 1 T. milk, vanilla.
