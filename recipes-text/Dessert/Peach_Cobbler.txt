Peach Cobbler

Filling:
4 c. peaches
juice of 1 lemon
1/2 c. bread crumbs
1/2 c. sugar
2 drops vanilla
Topping:
3 oz. sugar
2 oz. butter
salt
cinnamon
1/2 t. vanilla
5 oz flour 

Mix together filling ingredients and put in pan.
Putting topping ingredients in food processor and blend.
Put over peaches.
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
