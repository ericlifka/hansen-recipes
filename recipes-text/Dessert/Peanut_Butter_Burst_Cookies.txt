Peanut Butter Burst Cookies

2 c. flour
1 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt
1 c. butter, soft
1/2 c. sugar
3/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 t. vanilla
1 egg
12 oz. peanut butter morsels

In bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.
In bowl combine butter, brown sugar, sugar and vanilla; beat at medium until creamy.
Add egg; beat well.
Gradually add flour.
Stir in peanut butter morsels.
Drop by teaspoons onto cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 for 7-9 minutes.
