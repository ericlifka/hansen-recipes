Jello Dirt Cups

2 c. milk
1 pkg. chocolate instant pudding
8 oz. cool whip
16 oz. oreos
8-10 7 oz. plastic cups

Pour milk into large bowl, add pudding mix and beat until well blended.
Let sit 5 minutes.
Stir in cool whip and half of the cookies.
Place 1 T. cookies into the cups.
Fill cups 3/4 full with pudding mix.
Top with remaining cookies.
Refrigerate 1 hour.
Decorate with gummy worms, frogs, candy flowers or nuts.
