Philly Free Mini Cheesecakes

12 vanilla wafers
3-8 oz. pkgs. cream cheese fat free, soft       
3/4 c. sugar
1 t. vanilla
3 eggs
1 c. fruit or reduced calorie pie filling

Place wafers, flat side down on bottom of each of 12 paper-lined muffin cups.
Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla on medium speed until well  blended.
Add eggs; mix just until blended.
Do not over beat after adding eggs.
Pour over wafers, filling each cup.
Bake at 325 for 30 minutes.
Cool.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight.
Top with fruit.
