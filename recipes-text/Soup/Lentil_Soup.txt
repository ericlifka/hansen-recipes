Lentil Soup

1 ham hock 
1 lb. lentils
3 c. mushrooms
1 onion     
1 clove garlic        
salt, pepper
3 carrots
1 t. thyme
1 bay leaf
4 c. chicken broth
8 c. water
3 T. parsley

Combine all ingredients, boil over moderately high heat.  
Reduce heat, simmer partially covered 1 hour.  
Remove bone.  
Add meat from bone and simmer 20 more minutes.
