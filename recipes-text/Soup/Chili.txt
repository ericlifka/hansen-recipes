Chili

2 T. oil 
2 lb. chuck steak (cubed)
1 T. flour. 
1 onion
3/4 c. red wine
1 c. tomato sauce
1 clove garlic
1 carrot
1 green pepper
40 oz. canned beans (different kinds)
2 bay leaves
1/2 t. cumin
1 t. oregano
10 1/2 oz. can minestrone and lentil soup
1/4 t. red pepper

Cube steak into 1/2 to 1 inch pieces.
Toss steak in flour, brown in oil.
Add all ingredients.
Simmer 1 1/2 hr.  
Uncover last 30 minutes if needed to thicken.
