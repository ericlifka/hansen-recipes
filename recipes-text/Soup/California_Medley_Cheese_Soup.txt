California Medley Cheese Soup

1 1/2 qt. water
6 chicken bouillon cubes
1 c. celery
1/2-2 c. onion
2 1/2 c. potato cubes
1 c. diced carrots
16 oz. pkg. frozen california medley vet.
2 cans cr. of chicken soup
1/2 to 1 lb. cubed velveeta cheese

Combine water, bouillon, celery and onion.  
Cooked covered 20 min.  
Add potatoes, carrots and frozen veggies.  
Cook until tender, about 30 min.  
Add chicken soup and cheese.  
Heat until cheese melts.
