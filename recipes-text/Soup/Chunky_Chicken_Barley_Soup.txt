Chunky Chicken Barley Soup

1 c. onion, carrot and celery
2 garlic cloves
2 t. olive oil
2 cans chicken broth
1 3/4 c. water
1/4 t. salt, thyme and pepper
1 c. cooked chicken
1/2 c. quick cooking barley.  

Saut√© onion, carrot, celery, and garlic in oil for 5 minutes.  
Add broth, water and spices.
Bring to a boil
Reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes until veggies are tender.  
Add chicken and barley; cook 8-10 minutes until barley is tender.
