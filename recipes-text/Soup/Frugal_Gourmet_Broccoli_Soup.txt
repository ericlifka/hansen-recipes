Frugal Gourmet Broccoli Soup

2 lb. broccoli
2 T. butter
1/2 c. onion
1/4 c. green pepper
2 T. flour    
1 bay leaf
6 c. chicken stock
6 peppercorns
1 t. thyme
3 egg yolks whipped with 1 c. milk

Chop up broccoli.  
Saut√© in butter with onions and gr. pepper.  
Sprinkle with flour and stir.  
Add to stock with bay leaf, parsley, thyme, and peppercorns.  
Cook about 30 minutes.  
Puree in blender.  Remember to do small batches of hot liquid at a time.  
Return to pan and add milk mix.  
You may want to mix a little of the hot soup into the milk first.
