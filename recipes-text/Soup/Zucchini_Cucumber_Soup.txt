Zucchini Cucumber Soup

4 T. butter
2 c. onion
2 lb. zucchini
2 cucumbers, seeded but with skins on
3 c. chicken broth
1/4 c. fresh dillsalt and pepper         
Garnish: 
1/2 c. cuke, tomato

Melt butter and add onions.  
Cook 10 min.  
Add zucchini and cukes.  
Cook over medium for 10 min.  
Add broth and cook 15 minutes more.  
Remove from heat.  
Cool.   
Process in blender or food processor.  
Serve hot or cold.
