Beef Stroganoff
1 lb. hamburger1 onion1 garlic1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz. sour creamsalt and peppercan mushrooms
Brown hamburger.  I drain off the fat.  Add onion.  Stir in soup and seasonings.  Simmer
about 20 minutes.  I add the mushrooms and sour cream and cook on low for another 15-20 
minutes.  Serve over noodles.  Hamburger can be ground chuck.  I spoon off fat.
