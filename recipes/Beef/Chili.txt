Chili

2 T. oil
2 T. chuck steak (cubed)
1 T. flour 1 onion
3/4 c. red wine
1 c. tomato sauce
1 garlic
1 carrot
1 green pepper  
2 bay leaves
40 oz. canned beans (different kinds)
1/2 t. cumin
1 t. oregano 
1/4 t. crushed red pepper
10 1/2 oz. can minestrone and lentil soup.

Heat oil.
Brown steak in oil with flour.
Add rest of ingredients.
Simmer 1 1/2 hour.
Uncover last 30 minutes if needed to thicken.
