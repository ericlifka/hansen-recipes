Baked Stroganoff Meatballs
1 1/4 lb. hamburger1 onion1/4 c. Parmesan1/3 c. milk1 egg
1 t. salt, Worcestershiredash pepper1 beef bouillon cube
6 oz. long and wild rice4 oz. mushrooms1 T. butter2 1/4 c. boiling water
1/2 c. sour cream2 oz. pimento, drained1 cr. of mushroom soup
Heat oven to 400.  In a bowl combine beef, onion, cheese, milk, egg, salt, Worcestershire 
and pepper.  Mix well.  Shape into 18 meatballs.  Place in a 2 qt. dish.  Bake uncovered for 
15 min.  Drain fat.  Stir in soup and bouillon cube.  Reduce temp to 350 and bake 
uncovered for 45 min.  Place contents of rice and seasoning packets, mushrooms and butter 
in a 1 qt. casserole.  Add boiling water, stir and cover.  Bake in oven 40 minutes or until all 
liquid is absorbed.  Remove meatballs from oven; stir in sour cream.  Return to oven and 
heat 5 minutes.  Stir pimento into rice.  Serve with meatballs.
