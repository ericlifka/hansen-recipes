Italian Spaghetti

1 1/4 lb. hamburger
onion
garlic
2 envelopes sauce mix
2 cans tomato sauce
2 cans tomato paste
3 c. water (mix water/red wine)
1 can mushrooms
1 t. oregano leaves
1 t. basil leaves
spaghetti

Cook beef, drain.
Add sauce mix and remaining ingredients except spaghetti.
Simmer until thickened about 30 minutes.
