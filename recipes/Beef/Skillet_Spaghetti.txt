Skillet Spaghetti

1 lb. hamburger
1 t. garlic
1 can tomato paste
1 t. chili powder
1 t. oregano
8 oz. spaghetti
dash pepper
onion    
8 oz. tomato sauce
1 1/2 c. water
2 c. tomato juice
2 t. salt
1 t. sugar

Combine all ingredients but spaghetti.
Cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.
Add spaghetti and simmer covered another 30 minutes.
