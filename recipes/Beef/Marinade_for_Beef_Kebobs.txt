Marinade for Beef Kebobs
1 c. red wine vinegar and veggie oil1/4 c.  vinegar and ketchup1 clove garlic
1/4 c. sugar2 t. Worcestershire1 t. salt1 T. rosemary or basil
Combine all ingredients and marinade at least 6 hours or overnight in refrigerator.  Use 3 
lbs.. sirloin or top round.   Grill 20-25 minutes.
