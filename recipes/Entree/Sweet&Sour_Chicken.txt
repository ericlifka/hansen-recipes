Sweet&Sour Chicken
chicken8 oz.  each Russian dressing, apricot preserves1 onion soup mix
1/4 c. water       pineapple, green pepper
Put chicken in pan.  Mix Russian dressing, apricot preserves, onions soup mix and water 
together and pour over chicken.  Cover with foil.  Bake covered at 350 for 1 hour.  Uncover 
and bake another 30 minutes with pineapple and green pepper.
