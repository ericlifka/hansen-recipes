Thai Chicken Fettuccine
1 c. salsa1/4 c. creamy peanut butter2 T. Orange juice2 T. honey
1 t. soy sauce8 oz. fettucine3/4 lb. chicken breast, cut in strips1 T. v. oil
1 medium red pepper, julienned1/4 c. minced fresh cilantro
Cook fettucine and drain.  Microwave first 5 ingredients for 1 minute.  Stir and set aside.  In 
pan cook chicken in oil over medium heat 3-5 minutes or until browned.  Add red pepper; 
cook and stir until crisp tender.  Top fettuccine with chicken mix and sauce, top with cilantro.
