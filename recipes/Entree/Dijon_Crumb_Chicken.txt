Dijon Crumb Chicken
2 eggs2 T. Dijon1 c. seasoned bread crumbs1/2 c. Parm cheese
1 1/2 t. onion powder and garlic powder4-4 oz breasts, flatten to 1/4
In bowl beat eggs and mustard.  In another bowl combine bread crumbs, parm cheese, 
onion and garlic powder.  Dip chicken in egg mix and then in crumb mix.  In skillet cook 
chicken in oil over med heat for 15-20 minutes.
