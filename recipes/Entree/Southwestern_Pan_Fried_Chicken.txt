Southwestern Pan Fried Chicken
4 chicken breasts1 c. Cheddar     1 T. garlic      3 egg whites  2 chopped tomato 
2 t. cilantro1 chili pepper4 gr. onion1/2 c. cream
Process breasts in whites in processor.  Add remaining ingredients.  Shape into patties.  
Dip in 2 c. flour, 1 c. milk, and 4 c. bread crumbs.  Brown on both sides.  Cook in oven 7 
minutes at 350.  Top with salsa.
