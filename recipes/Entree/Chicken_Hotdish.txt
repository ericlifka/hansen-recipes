Chicken Hotdish
2 c. cooked chicken cover 9 x 13 pan       16 oz. cooked broccoli       1 pkg. cooked noodles
Spread 2 cans cr. of chicken soup on topMix together 1 1/2 c. mayo, 1 1/2 t. curry,
1/2 t. lemon juice spread over soup        1 c. shredded cheddar cheese(put on last 15 min.)
Bake 350 for 1 hour.  Can be made in advance and can make it smaller.
