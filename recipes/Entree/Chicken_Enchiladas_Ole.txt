Chicken Enchiladas  Ole
2 1/2 c. chopped cooked chicken16 oz. sour cream1 1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 c. chopped onion12 tortillas1 1/2 c. salsa
Combine chicken, 1 c. sour cream, 3/4 c. cheese and onions; mix well.  Warm tortillas.  Top 
each tortilla with 1/4 c. chicken mix; roll up tightly.  Place seam side down in 9 x 13 pan.  Top 
with salsa.  Bake at 375 for 25 minutes.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Heat a few 
minutes more and serve. 
I food process the chicken so it is more like shredded, but can do cubed.
