Salisbury Steak with Gemelli
1 egg1/2 c. soft bread crumbs1 t. Italian seasoning1/2 t. pepper and minced garlic
1 lb. hamburger1 T. olive oil1 c. sliced fresh mushrooms2 T. flour
1 c. chicken broth1 T. ketchup1 t. Worcestershire saucehot gemelli or spiral pasta
In a large bowl combine the egg, bread crumbs, Italian seasoning, pepper and garlic.  
Crumble beef over mix and mix well.  Shape into 4 patties.  In a large skillet, cook patties in 
oil over medium high heat for 5-7 minutes on each side or until meat is no longer pink.  
Remove and keep warm.  Drain, reserving 2 T. drippings.  Saute mushrooms in drippings 
until tender.  Stir in flour until blended.  Gradually stir in broth, ketchup, and Worcestershire 
sauce.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Return patties to the 
skillet.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 3-4 minutes or until heated 
through.  Serve with gemelli.
