Chicken Pasta

2-4 T. butter
2 breast halves, cut up
1 of each green, red pepper
1 garlic
1 onion, diced
1 tomato, chopped    
1 c. chicken stock      
3/4 t. cayenne pepper 
2 t. cajun seasoning 
salt, pepper
1 can mushrooms
8 oz. heavy cream

Cook pasta, drain, set aside
Cook breasts and vegetables in butter
Deglaze with chicken stock
mix in seasonings and cream
reduce heat and bring to a simmer
serve over pasta
