Fettuccine With Pepper Sauce

Pam
1 1/2 c. broccoli florets
1 pepper, (any color)
1 onion
1 T. oil
12 oz. chicken, cut in strips
1 can cream soup  (chicken, celery, or broccoli)
1/2 c. water
1 t. basil
1/2 c. Swiss or cheddar
8 oz. fettuccine, cooked

Spray skillet over medium heat.
Stir fry broccoli, sweet pepper and onion 3-4 minutes; remove from skillet.
Add oil to skillet.
Add poultry, stir fry 3-4 minutes or until no longer pink.
Add soup, water, and basil; mix thoroughly.
Stir in sweet pepper mix.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat.
Add cheese; cook and stir until cheese is almost melted.
Serve over pasta.
