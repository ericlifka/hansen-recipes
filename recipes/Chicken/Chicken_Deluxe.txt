Chicken Deluxe

20 oz. frozen chopped spinach
2 chicken breasts, cooked
1 c. mayo
1 can cr. of chicken soup
1-2 t. curry
1 T. lemon juice
1/2 c. stuffing

Cook spinach until tender and drain.
Spread spinach on bottom of 1 1/2 qt. greased dish.
Layer chicken on top.
Combine mayo, soup, curry and lemon juice.
Pour over chicken and spinach.
Sprinkle stuffing on top.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
