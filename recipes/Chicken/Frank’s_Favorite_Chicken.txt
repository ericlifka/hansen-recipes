1 large onion, sliced
3 lb. chicken cut up
1/2 t. hickory smoked salt
1/8 t. pepper
1/4 c. ketchup
1/4 c. maple syrup
2 T. cider vinegar
1 T. Dijon mustard

Heat oven to 350. Place onion slices in a 9 x 13 dish.
Arrange chicken in a layer, skin side up on top of onion.
Sprinkle with hickory salt and pepper.
In small bowl combine ketchup, maple syrup, vinegar and mustard mixing well.
Pour over chicken.
Bake uncovered for 50 minutes, until chicken is tender.
