Light and Easy Lemon Broccoli Chicken
1 T. veggie oil1 lb. boneless breasts1 can cr. of broccoli soup
1/4 c. milk2 t. lemon juice1/8 t. pepper4 thin lemon slices
In skillet in hot oil cook chicken 10 minutes or until browned on both sides.  Spoon off fat.  
Combine soup and milk; stir in lemon juice and pepper.  Pour over chicken;  top each 
chicken piece with lemon slice.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover simmer 5 min. or until chicken is 
fork tender, stirring occasionally.
