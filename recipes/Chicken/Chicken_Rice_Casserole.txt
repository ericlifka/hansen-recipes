Chicken Rice Casserole

2 T. oil
1 onion
3 c. cooked chicken
2 15 oz. cans green beans, drain and rinse
8 oz. water chestnuts, chopped
4 oz. pimento
1 cr. of celery soup
1/2 c. mayo
1/2 c. sour cream
6 oz. box cooked wild rice
1 c. cheddar cheese

Saute onion in oil
Mix in all other ingredients
Put in greased pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
