Stir Fry Vegetables With Chicken Breast

1 chicken breast, diced
1/2 lb. green beans
2 carrots, cut thinly
1 stalk celery, sliced
1 c. mushrooms
1 c. cauliflower florets
2 c. cabbage, cut in 2x2” piecessalt and pepper
2 T. cornstarch with 2 T. water 
2 T. fish sauce
6 T. oil
1/2 onion, sliced
marinade:
1 t. sherry
1 t. cornstarch
salt & pepper

Mix marinade ingredients and marinade chicken.
Sauté chicken breast in 3 T. oil till tender.
Set aside.
Heat 3 T. oil.
Sauté garlic and onions.
Add fish sauce.
Stir in vegetables according to their tenderness.
Heat and stir for about 4-5 minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Put in cornstarch mix last and stir another 1-2 minutes.
