Shrimp and Tortellini
1 lb. shrimp              9 oz pkg. tortellini with cheese filling1/3 c. butter
1 shallot, minced2 T. fresh basil or 2 t. dried basil1/2 c. parmesan
Peel and devein shrimp; set aside.  Cook tortellini according to package directions, drain and 
set aside.  Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat; add shrimp, shallot and basil.  
Cook about 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Add pasta and cheese, toss gently.
