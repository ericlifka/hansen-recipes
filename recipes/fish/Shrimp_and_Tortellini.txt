Shrimp and Tortellini

1 lb. shrimp              
9 oz pkg. tortellini with cheese filling
1/3 c. butter
1 shallot, minced
2 T. fresh basil or 2 t. dried basil
1/2 c. parmesan

Peel and devein shrimp; set aside.
Cook tortellini according to package directions, drain and set aside.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat; add shrimp, shallot and basil.
Cook about 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Add pasta and cheese, toss gently.
