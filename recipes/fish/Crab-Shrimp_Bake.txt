Crab-Shrimp Bake
2 cans crab1 bag shrimp so you have a total of 1 lb.  can be both or crab or shrimp
1 can cr. of shrimp soup2/3 c. milk1/3 c. sour cream2 c. egg noodles, uncooked
1/2 c. cheddar
Drain meat and blend soup, milk and sour cream.  Stir in noodles and top with cheese.  Put 
in a 10 x 6 pan.  Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
