Italian Baked Fish
tomatoseasoned bread crumbssalt and pepper8 flounder or trout fillets
1/2 c. vermouth or white wine4 T. butter1 t. lemon juiceParmesan
Heat oven to 500.  In buttered 9 x 13 pan thinly slice tomato to cover bottom.  Sprinkle 
with bread crumbs, salt and pepper.  Arrange fish over mixture.  Combine wine, butter and 
lemon juice in pan.  Heat to boil and cook 3 minutes.  Pour over fish.  Sprinkle with cheese 
and paprika and lemon juice over all.  Bake for 10-20 minutes.
