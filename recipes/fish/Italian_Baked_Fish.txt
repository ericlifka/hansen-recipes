Italian Baked Fish

tomato
seasoned bread crumbs
salt and pepper
8 flounder or trout fillets
1/2 c. vermouth or white wine
4 T. butter
1 t. lemon juice
Parmesan

Heat oven to 500.
In buttered 9 x 13 pan thinly slice tomato to cover bottom.
Sprinkle with bread crumbs, salt and pepper.
Arrange fish over mixture.
Combine wine, butter and lemon juice in pan.
Heat to boil and cook 3 minutes.
Pour over fish.
Sprinkle with cheese and paprika and lemon juice over all.
Bake for 10-20 minutes.
