Shrimp Linguine with Parmesan Cream Sauce

6 oz uncooked linguine
1/3 c. chopped onion and sliced fresh mushrooms
2 garlic
1/4 c. butter
2 T. o. oil
1/4 c. whip cream
2 T. Parmesan
1/4 t. crushed red pepper
15 uncooked med. shrimp
1/8 t. s & p

Cook linguine.
In skillet saut√© onion, mushrooms and garlic in butter and oil for 2-3 minutes or until veggies are tender.
Stir in cream and cheese; sprinkle with pepper flakes.
Drain linguine.
Add shrimp and linguine, cook over m-low heat for 5-7 min. until shrimp are done.
