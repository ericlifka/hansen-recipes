Soy Sesame Marinade for Salmon

3/4 c. soy sauce and rice wine vinegar
3 T. brown sugar
1 T. sesame oil
2 green onions
1 1/2 t. crushed red pepper flakes
salmon
Other marinades:  
Pineapple soy: 12 oz. pineapple juice concentrate 3 T. soy sauce1 t. ginger
Orange Pepper: 12 oz. orange juice concentrate1 1/2 t. red pepper flakes

Marinade salmon for 3 hours.
Grill or broil it depends on the thickness as to how long it takes.
It generally takes about 15 minutes.
You can also bake it at 400 for 30 minutes.
