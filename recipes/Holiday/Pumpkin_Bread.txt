Pumpkin Bread

4 eggs
1/2 t. baking powder
2/3 c. soft butter
2 t. baking soda
3 c. sugar
1 t. cinnamon
15 oz. pumpkin
1/2 t. cloves
3 c. flour
1/2 t. nutmeg
1/2 t. salt
1 c. chopped nuts

Combine eggs, butter and sugar, blending well.
Add the rest of the ingredients.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans.
Bake at 350ยบ for 1 hour.
Turn onto racks and cool.
