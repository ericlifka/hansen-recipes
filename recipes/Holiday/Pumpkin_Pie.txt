Pumpkin Pie
1 unbaked pie shell (9”)1/4 t. ground cloves
3/4 c. sugar2 eggs
1/2 t. salt1 3/4 c. pumpkin (15 oz. can)
1 t. ground cinnamon            1 1/2 c. evaporated milk (12 oz. can)
1/2 t. ginger
Combine sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger and cloves in small bowl.  Beat eggs lightly in large 
bowl.  Stir in pumpkin and sugar mix.     Gradually stir in milk.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake for 
15 min. at 425º.  Reduce temp to 350º and bake 40-50 minutes until knife in center comes 
out clean.  You may want to put it on a baking sheet in case it bakes over.  If you use 2 
shallow pies bake 15 min. at 425º, 350º for 20-30 minutes.
