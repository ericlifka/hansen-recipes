Oyster Stew

2 pts. oysters, including liquid
2 T. finely chopped onion and garlic
2-4 T. butter
1/2 c. cream
1 qt. half and half
1 t. salt and pepper
1/4 t. Worcestershire sauce
Paprika and parsley flakes

Slowly cook onion in butter in saucepan.
Do not brown.
Add oysters including liquid and cook slowly until edges curl.
Stir in half and half, cream, worcestershire.
Heat through, but do not boil.
Season with salt and pepper.
Ladle into bowls.
Garnish with paprika and parsley.
