Holiday Wreaths
1/2 c. margarine30 large marshmallows4 c. corn flakes
Spray waxed paper and 2 spoons with Pam.  Over low heat melt margarine and 
marshmallows.  Stir constantly until smooth.  Remove from heat.  Stir in corn flakes and food 
coloring.  Drop onto waxed paper and mold into wreaths.  Decorate with red hot candies.
