Deviled Eggs

eggs
option 1:
salad dressing
salt and pepper
relish
mustard
option 2: (grandma h)
1 T. mustard
1 or more T. sweet relish
1 T. sugar
1 T. vinegar
2 whopping T. miracle whip
salt and pepper

Put eggs in pan.
Boil 2 minutes.
Remove from heat, place cover on pan.
Let stand 17 minutes.
Drain.
slice in half, remove egg yolks.
Mash the egg yolks with the ingeredients from either option 1 or 2.
Spoon mixture back into egg halves.
Refrigerate.
