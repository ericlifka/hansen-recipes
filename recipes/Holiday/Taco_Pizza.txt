Taco Pizza
8 oz. cream cheese1 tomato, chopped
8 oz. sour creamlettuce, chopped
1 pkg. taco seasoningshredded cheese
black olive slicesonion
Mix cream cheese, sour cream and taco seasoning.  Spread onto large plate.  Then layer 
lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and olives.  Serve with taco chips.
Shrimp
Boil water with Old Bay Seasoning.  Add shrimp and simmer about 5 minutes.  Cool and 
peel.  Refrigerate.  Serve with cocktail sauce.
