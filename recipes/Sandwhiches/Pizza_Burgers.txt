Pizza Burgers

1-1 1/2 lb. hamburger
1 small can grated spam
1/2 t. oregano
1 onion
1 can pizza sauce
buns
cheese slices - mozarella or american or grated

Brown hamburger.
Add spam, oregano, onion and pizza sauce.
Simmer for a few minutes until onion is tender.
Split buns and place halves on a cookie sheet.
Top each bun half with meat mix, top with cheese.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
