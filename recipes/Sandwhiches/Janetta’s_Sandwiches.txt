Janetta’s Sandwiches

2 boxes Steak-ums
1 can sliced mushrooms
3-4 green peppers  
1 onion
4 beef bouillon cubes
1 pkg. onion soup mix
salt
pepper
basil
2 garlic 

Brown meat.
Sauté mushrooms, green peppers and onion.
Add to meat, then add other ingredients.
Simmer 1 hour.
Serve on kaiser rolls or sandwich buns.
