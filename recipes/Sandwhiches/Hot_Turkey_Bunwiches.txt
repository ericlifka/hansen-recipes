Hot Turkey Bunwiches
1 1/2 c. chopped turkey2 hard cooked eggs, chopped1/3 c. onion
1/2 c. celery1/2 c. mayo1/8 t. salt and pepper4 t. butter 4 buns
In a bowl combine the first eight ingredients.  Spread butter over sides of each bun.  
Spread about 2/3 c. turkey over each bun bottom.  Replace tops.  Wrap individually in foil  
Place on baking sheet.  Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted.
