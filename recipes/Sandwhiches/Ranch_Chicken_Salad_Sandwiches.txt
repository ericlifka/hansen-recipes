Ranch Chicken Salad Sandwiches
1/4 c. mayo3 T. ranch dressing3 T. sour cream1 T. lemon juice1/8 t. pepper
2 c. cooked chicken breast1/2 c. diced celery2 T. diced sweet red pepper
1 T. green onion6 hamburger bunslettuce and tomato slices
In a small bowl, combine mayo, ranch dressing, sour cream, lemon juice, and pepper.  Stir 
in chicken, celery, red pepper and onion until combined.  Spoon 1/3 c. onto each bun 
bottom; top with lettuce and tomato.  Replace bun tops.
