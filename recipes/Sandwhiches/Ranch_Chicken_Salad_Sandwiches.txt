Ranch Chicken Salad Sandwiches

1/4 c. mayo
3 T. ranch dressing
3 T. sour cream
1 T. lemon juice
1/8 t. pepper
2 c. cooked chicken breast
1/2 c. diced celery
2 T. diced sweet red pepper
1 T. green onion
6 hamburger buns
lettuce
tomato slices

In a small bowl, combine mayo, ranch dressing, sour cream, lemon juice, and pepper.
Stir in chicken, celery, red pepper and onion until combined.
Spoon 1/3 c. onto each bun bottom; top with lettuce and tomato.
Replace bun tops.
