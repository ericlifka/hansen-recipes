Grilled Tomato Cheese Sandwiches
3/4 c grape tomatoes, chopped or roma1 green onion sliced    8 slices Italian bread
4 slices cheddar cheese4 slices swiss cheese4 slices provolone
4 slices monterey jack cheese8 t. butter, softened
Sprinkle tomato and onion over four bread slices.  Layer with cheese slices; top with bread.  
Butter outsides of sandwiches.  Toast on heated griddle for 2-3 minutes on each side until 
bread is golden brown and cheese is melted.
