Sandra Lee’s Chicken Tenders
1/2 c. flour1/2 packet southwest steak marinade mix      pepper    3 c. corn flakes
Mix egg and 1/2 c. buttermilk1 lb. tenders
Combine flour, marinade and pepper.  Soak chicken in buttermilk, toss into flour, shake off 
and dip in milk again, then crumbs.  Fry until done (my deep fryer is 4 min. 30 sec.).
Paula Deen’s honey mustard is good with them:  3/4 c. mayo3 T. honey2 T. mustard
1 T. lemon juice or juice from 1/2 lemonhorseradish to taste   2 T. orange juice 
Combine all except orange juice; stir well.  Thin to pouring consistency for dressing or 
dipping consistency for dips with orange juice.  Cover and chill for 2-3 hours.
