Santa Fe Wraps (Paula Deen)
2 8 oz. pkg. soft cream cheese1 c. sour cream4 1/2 oz. green chilies and olives
1 c. shredded pepper jack2 T. salsa1/2 c. green onion tops, sliced1 c. chopped 
fresh spinach(I use more)2 pkgs. flour tortillas
In a bowl beat cream cheese until creamy.  Then add sour cream, green chilies, olives, 
pepper jack cheese, salsa and onion tops.  Beat at medium speed with a mixer.  Stir in 
spinach.  Spread mix over the surface of each tortilla.  Roll up tightly and cut each tortilla 
crosswise into 6 slices.  Skewer each wrap with a wooden toothpick.  Serve immediately or 
chill.  Serve with salsa for dipping.
