Shredded Beef Sandwiches
3-4 lb. arm cut roast or chuck roast, bonelessonion soup mixsalt, pepper
bouillon cube (I use about 4)buns  
Put that stuff on top of the roast and pour water over the meat.  Cook in slow oven 275 for 
5 or 6 hours.  When meat is tender, pull apart with forks.  You can also do it overnight or in a 
crock pot.
