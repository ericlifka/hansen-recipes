Beer Brats and Kraut
3 bottles beer6 brats1 large jar sauerkraut 6 bunsmustard
Place beer in pot and heat over medium heat.  Place brats and kraut in beer and simmer for 
approximately 10 minutes.  With tongs, remove brats from beer and place 1 in each bun.  
Using a slotted spoon, cover brat in sauerkraut.  Drizzle mustard on top of kraut.
