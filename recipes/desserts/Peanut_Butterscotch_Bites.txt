Peanut Butterscotch Bites
1 c. butterscotch1/2 c. peanut butter3 c. rice krispiesPAM
In 3 qt. saucepan combine butterscotch morsels and peanut butter.  Cook over low heat, 
stirring constantly, until smooth.  Remove from heat.  Stir in rice krispies mixing until well 
coated.  Press mix into 9 x 9 pan coated with PAM.  Chill until firm.
