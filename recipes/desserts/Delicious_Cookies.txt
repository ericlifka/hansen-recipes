Delicious Cookies
1 c. white sugar1 c. brown sugar1 c. butter1 c. veggie oil1 egg
1 t. vanilla      Mix those ingredients together and add:
3 1/2 c. flour1 c. flaked coconut1 c. Rice Krispies1 c. oatmeal
1/2 t. salt1 t. cream of tartar1 t. soda1 c. chocolate chips
Let stand in refrigerator for 15 minutes.   Make small balls or drop by teaspoon on 
ungreased cookie sheet.  Press down with fork dipped in sugar.  Bake at 350 for 15 
minutes.
