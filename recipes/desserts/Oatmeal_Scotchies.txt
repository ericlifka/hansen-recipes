Oatmeal Scotchies
1 c. flour1 t. baking soda1/2 t. salt and cinnamon1 c. butter, soft
3/4 c. sugar3/4 c. brown sugar2 eggs1 t. vanilla3 c. oatmeal
12 oz. butterscotch morsels
In bowl combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon, set aside.  In bowl combine butter, 
sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla; beat until creamy.  Gradually add flour mix.  Stir in 
oats and butterscotch morsels.  Drop by tablespoons onto cookie sheets.  Bake at 375 for 
7-8 minutes for chewier cookies or 9-10 minutes for crisper cookies.
