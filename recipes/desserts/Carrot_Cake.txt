Carrot Cake
4 eggs2 c. sugar1 1/2 c. veggie oil1 t. salt2 t. cinnamon
2 t. soda2 c. flour1 c. nuts1 c. coconut       3 c. grated carrots
Mix all and bake in a 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 40-45 minutes.    Frosting is made with 3-4 oz. 
cream cheese, 1/2 c. butter, 2 c. powdered sugar (may need more) 1 T. milk, vanilla.
