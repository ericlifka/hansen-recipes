Sugar Cookies
1 c. powdered sugar1 c. white sugar1 c. butter1 c. veggie oil          2 eggs      
1 t. vanilla4 c. +4 T. flour1 t. salt, soda and cream of tartar
Cream together butter, oil and sugars until fluffy.  Beat in eggs.  Add rest of ingredients.  
Roll into small balls.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Press down cookies with glass 
dipped in sugar.  Bake at 375 for about 10 minutes or until  golden brown.
