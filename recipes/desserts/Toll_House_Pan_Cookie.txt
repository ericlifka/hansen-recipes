Toll House Pan Cookie
2 1/4 c. flour      3/4 c. brown sugar1 t. baking soda, salt, and vanilla    2 eggs
1 c. butter12 oz. semisweet  chocolate morsels3/4 c. sugar          1 c. nuts
Heat oven to 375.  In bowl mix butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla.  Beat until creamy.   
Beat in eggs.  Add flour, salt, baking soda, mix.  Stir in morsels and nuts.  Spread the 
dough in greased 15 x 10 pan.  Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.  Can also be cookies.
