Rhubarb Dessert
Crust:  1 c. flour2 T. butter1 t. baking powder1/4 t. salt1 beaten egg
2 T. milk  Mix as for pie crust.  Pat in bottom and sides of 9” square pan.
Filling:  3 c. rhubarb1 pkg. red jelloPlace rhubarb in crust (unbaked) sprinkle jello 
over top.  Topping:  1 c. sugar1/2 c. flour1/3 c. butter.  Mix together and sprinkle 
over top.   Bake 45 minutes at 350.
