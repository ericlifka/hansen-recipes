Indian Pudding
Mix in pan:  6 T. unsalted butter3/4 t. allspice and mace     1/2 c. + 3 T. dark brown sugar
2-12 oz. cans evaporated milk1/2  t. salt2 eggs1 t. baking soda
1 t. vanilla1 c. yellow cornmeal
Mix in pan butter, allspice and mace for 2 minutes.  Stir in brown sugar until dissolved.  Add 
milk and salt.  Cook until milk is ready to boil.  Remove from heat.  Add soda, vanilla and 
cornmeal until it is well blended.  Add frothy eggs.  Pour in casserole dish.  Bake at 350 for 
45-55 minutes.
