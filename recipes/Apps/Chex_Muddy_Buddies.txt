Chex Muddy Buddies

9 c. Chex cereal (variety of chex cereals)
1 c. semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 c. peanut butter
1/4 c. butter   
1 t. vanilla    
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar

Pour cereals into large bowl; set aside.  
In a 1 qt. microwave bowl, combine chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter.  
Microwave 1-1 1/2 minutes or until smooth.  
Stir in vanilla.  
Pour chocolate mix over cereals, and stir until all pieces are coated.  
Pour cereal mix into resealable bag with powdered sugar.  
Seal and shake until all pieces are coated.
