Tortilla Roll-ups (with Salsa and Green chilies)

1 dozen flour burrito tortillas
7 oz. can chili salsa 
16 oz. cream cheese (softened)
2 c. grated cheese
4 oz. can diced green chilies   
4-5 gr. onions
1/2 t. garlic salt
