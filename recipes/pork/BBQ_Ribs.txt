B.B.Q. Ribs

1 c. chili sauce
1 T. worcestershire
1/2 c. water
1 T. oil        
1/2 t. salt
2 T. vinegar                 
minced onion
1/4 t. tabasco
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 T. brown sugar
1/4 t. paprika                    
1/2 c. ketchup
Ribs

Mix all but the ribs together.
Put sauce on ribs.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 325.
