B.B.Q. Ribs
1 c. chili sauce1 T. worcestershire1/2 c. water1 T. oil        1/2 t. salt
2 T. vinegar                 minced onion1/4 t. tabasco1/4 c. lemon juice
2 T. brown sugar1/4 t. paprika                    1/2 c. ketchupRibs
Mix all but the ribs together.  Put sauce on ribs.  Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 325.
