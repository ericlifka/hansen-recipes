Pork Tenderloin  (Paula Deen)
1 1/2- 2 lb. pork tenderloin
Marinade:1/4 c. soy sauce2 T. red wine1 T. honey1 T. brown sugar
2 cloves garlic, minced1 t. ginger1/2 t. cinnamon2 green onion tops
Combine marinade and whisk.  Pour over tenderloin and marinade overnight.
Heat oven to 350.  Bake tenderloin 45 minutes or until meat is 145 degrees.  Let meat rest 
10 minutes before cutting into piece.  Drizzle with a small amount of the meat juices from 
cooking.
