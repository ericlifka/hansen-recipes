Outback Steakhouse Caesar Salad Dressing
1 c. mayo1/4 c. egg substitute1/4 c. parmesan cheese2 T. water
2 T. olive oil1 1/2 T. lemon juice1 T. anchovy paste2 cloves garlic2 t. sugar
1/2 t. pepper1/4 t. salt 1/4 t. dried parsley flakes, crushed
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.  Beat with mixer for about 1 minutes.  Cover 
bowl and chill for several hours for flavors to develop.
