Cole Slaw

1 head cabbage
1 c. sugar
3/4 c. oil
1 c. vinegar
1 t. each dry mustard and celery seed
2 t. sugar
1/2 t. salt

Grate a head of cabbage, add sugar and let sit for 1 hour.
Boil  together:  oil, vinegar, mustard, celery, 2 t. sugar, and salt.
Pour dressing over cabbage and toss together.
Refrigerate for 24 hours before serving.
