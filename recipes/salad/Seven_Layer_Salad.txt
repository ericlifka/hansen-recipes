Seven Layer Salad

1 head lettuce, shredded
1 c. celery
1 green pepper
2 gr. onions
1/2 pkg. peas, uncooked
2 t. sugar
1 c. salad dressing 
Options:
cheese
bacon bits
water chestnuts
shrimp

Arrange vegetables in layers in a pretty bowl, topping with salad dressing mixed with sugar.
Cover and chill several hours or overnight.
Just before serving top with cheese and bits.
