Seven Layer Salad
1 head lettuce, shredded1 c. celery1 green pepper2 gr. onions
1/2 pkg. peas, uncooked2 t. sugar1 c. salad dressing 
Options:  cheese, bacon bits, water chestnuts, shrimp
Arrange vegetables in layers in a pretty bowl, topping with salad dressing mixed with 
sugar.  Cover and chill several hours or overnight.  Just before serving top with cheese and 
bits.
