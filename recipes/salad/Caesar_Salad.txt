Caesar Salad
2 cloves garlic1 head romaine lettuce1/2 t. salt and pepper
1 T. anchovy paste1 T. lemon juice1 egg or 2 T. liquid egg stuff
1/3 c. olive oil1/2 c. parmesan cheese1 c. caesar croutons (or onion and garlic)
In bowl mix garlic, salt, pepper, anchovies, lemon juice, egg.  Add olive oil and mix.  Add 
lettuce and toss.  Add cheese and croutons.
