Island Fruit Medley
1/2 c. Catalina2 T. light brown sugar1/4 t. cinnamon1 mango or peach  
1/2 c. pineapple chunks2 kiwi1 c. cantaloupe8 pineapple slices
1/2 c. raspberry or strawberry halves8 cantaloupe slices
Mix catalina, brown sugar and cinnamon.  Toss pineapple chunks, mango, kiwi, cantaloupe 
and raspberry with dressing.   Arrange slices of pineapple and cantaloupe on plate, top 
with marinated fruit mix.
