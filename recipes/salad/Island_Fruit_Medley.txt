Island Fruit Medley

1/2 c. Catalina
2 T. light brown sugar
1/4 t. cinnamon
1 mango or peach  
1/2 c. pineapple chunks
2 kiwi
1 c. cantaloupe
8 pineapple slices
1/2 c. raspberry or strawberry halves
8 cantaloupe slices

Mix catalina, brown sugar and cinnamon.
Toss pineapple chunks, mango, kiwi, cantaloupe and raspberry with dressing.
Arrange slices of pineapple and cantaloupe on plate, top with marinated fruit mix.
