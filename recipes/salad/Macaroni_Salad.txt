Macaroni Salad
Cook 8 oz. or more small macaroni, drain, cool.  Add 1 c. celery, small can sliced black 
olives, small jar pimento, 1/2 c. cut up dills, 1 c.  cut up in small chunks cheddar cheese (or 
colby), onion to taste (1/2 c. or so).  You can add chicken or turkey instead of cheese.
Dressing:  1 c. mayo1 c. sour cream1 t. garlic powder, pepper
1/2 t. cayenne pepper 1 T. mustard1 t. horseradish (can be omitted)
1 t. celery salt.   Mix dressing and put over the rest of the ingredients.   May top with 
parsley.
