Veggie Tortellini Soup
5 c. chicken broth16 oz. Cal. blend veggies8 oz. dried tortellini`15 oz. Italian diced 
tomatoes, undrainedBring broth to a boil, stir in veggies and tortellini, return to a boil.  
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 10-12 minutes or until veggies are tender.   Stir in 
tomatoes.  Cover and cook for 5-6 minutes until heated through.
