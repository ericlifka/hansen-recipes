Veggie Tortellini Soup

5 c. chicken broth
16 oz. Cal. blend veggies
8 oz. dried tortellini
15 oz. Italian diced tomatoes, undrained

Bring broth to a boil, stir in veggies and tortellini, return to a boil.  
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 10-12 minutes or until veggies are tender.   
Stir in tomatoes.  
Cover and cook for 5-6 minutes until heated through.
