Black Bean Soup
1 lb. black turtle beans2 qt. water1/2 lb. thick bacon1 1/2 c. onion, celery, carrot 
1 T. garlic1 bay leaf1 t. thyme 3 T. cumin1/2 T. oregano  salt and pepper
10 c. chicken broth   2 ham hocks  1/4 t. cayenne4 T. lime juice 
Soak beans overnight in water.  Drain.  Saut√© bacon until brown.  Add onion, celery, and 
carrots about 5 min.  Add garlic, bay leaf, thyme, 1 T. cumin, oregano.  Cook 5 min.  Add 
broth and ham hocks.  Boil and cook over med. heat 2 to 2 1/2 hours.  Remove hocks and 
add meat, cayenne, lime juice and cumin.  Serve with dollop of sour cream.
