Cream of Cilantro Soup
1 bunch cilantro32 oz. chicken broth2 T. butter2 T. flour8 oz. cream cheese
8 oz. sour cream1 clove garlic1/4 t. salt, red pepper and cumin
Remove stems and chop cilantro.  Process cilantro and 1 c. broth in blender.   Melt butter, 
whisk in flour.  Slowly add 3 c. broth till smooth.  Boil 1 minute.  Stir in cilantro and rest of 
ingredients.  Simmer 15 minutes.
