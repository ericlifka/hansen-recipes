Cream of Cilantro Soup

1 bunch cilantro
32 oz. chicken broth
2 T. butter
2 T. flour
8 oz. cream cheese
8 oz. sour cream
1 clove garlic
1/4 t. salt, red pepper and cumin

Remove stems and chop cilantro.  
Process cilantro and 1 c. broth in blender.   
Melt butter, whisk in flour.  
Slowly add 3 c. broth till smooth.  
Boil 1 minute.  
Stir in cilantro and rest of ingredients.  
Simmer 15 minutes.
