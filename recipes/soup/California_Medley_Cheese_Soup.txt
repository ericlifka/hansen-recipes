California Medley Cheese Soup
1 1/2 qt. water6 chicken bouillon cubes1 c. celery1/2-2 c. onion
2 1/2 c. potato cubes1 c. diced carrots16 oz. pkg. frozen california medley vet.
2 cans cr. of chicken soup1/2 to 1 lb. cubed velveeta cheese
Combine water, bouillon, celery and onion.  Cooked covered 20 min.  Add potatoes, 
carrots and frozen veggies.  Cook until tender, about 30 min.  Add chicken soup and 
cheese.  Heat until cheese melts.
