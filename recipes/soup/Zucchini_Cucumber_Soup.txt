Zucchini Cucumber Soup
4 T. butter2 c. onion2 lb. zucchini2 cucumbers, seeded but with skins on
3 c. chicken broth1/4 c. fresh dillsalt and pepper         Garnish: 1/2 c. cuke, tomato
Melt butter and add onions.  Cook 10 min.  Add zucchini and cukes.  Cook over medium for 
10 min.  Add broth and cook 15 minutes more.  Remove from heat.  Cool.   Process in 
blender or food processor.  Serve hot or cold.
