Oyster Stew
2 pts. oysters, including liquid2 T. finely chopped onion and garlic2-4 T. butter
1/2 c. cream1 qt. half and half1/4 t. Worcestershire salt, pepper
Slowly cook onion in butter.  Do not brown.  Add oysters, including liquid and cook slowly 
until edges curl.  Stir in milk, cream, Worcestershire.  Heat through but do not boil.  Season 
with salt and pepper.  Garnish with paprika and parsley.
