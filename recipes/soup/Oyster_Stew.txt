Oyster Stew

2 pts. oysters, including liquid
2 T. finely chopped onion and garlic
2-4 T. butter
1/2 c. cream
1 qt. half and half
1/4 t. Worcestershire salt, pepper

Slowly cook onion in butter.  
Do not brown.  
Add oysters, including liquid and cook slowly until edges curl.  
Stir in milk, cream, Worcestershire.  
Heat through but do not boil.  
Season with salt and pepper.  
Garnish with paprika and parsley.
