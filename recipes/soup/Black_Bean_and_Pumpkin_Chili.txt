Black Bean and Pumpkin Chili
1 med onion1 med yellow pepper3 garlic2 T. o. oil3 c. chick broth
2 cans black beans, rinse and drain2 1/2  c. chicken15 oz pumpkin
14 1/2 oz diced tomato, undrained2 t. parsley, chili powder1 1/2 t. oregano, cumin
In skillet saute onion, yellow pepper and garlic in oil.  Put in slow cooker with rest of 
ingredients.  Cook on low 4-5 hours
