Black Bean and Pumpkin Chili

1 med onion
1 med yellow pepper
3 garlic
2 T. o. oil
3 c. chick broth
2 cans black beans, rinse and drain
2 1/2  c. chicken
15 oz pumpkin
14 1/2 oz diced tomato, undrained
2 t. parsley
2 t. chili powder
1 1/2 t. oregano
1 1/2 t. cumin

In skillet saute onion, yellow pepper and garlic in oil.  
Put in slow cooker with rest of ingredients.  
Cook on low 4-5 hours
