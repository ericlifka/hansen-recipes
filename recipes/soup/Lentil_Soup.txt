Lentil Soup
1 ham hock lb. lentils3 c. mushrooms1 onion     1 clove garlic        salt, pepper
3 carrots1 t. thyme1 bay leaf4 c. chicken broth8 c. water3 T. parsley
Combine all ingredients, boil over moderately high heat.  Reduce heat, simmer partially 
covered 1 hour.  Remove bone.  Add meat from bone and simmer 20 more minutes.
