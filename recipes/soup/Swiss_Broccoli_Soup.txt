Swiss Broccoli Soup
1 c. instant dry milk3 T. flour1/2 t. salt, pepper, garlic powder1 can chicken broth
1 1/2 c. shredded Swiss cheese2 1/4 c. water1/4 stick butter
1/4 c. chopped green onion10 oz. chopped broccoli, cooked
Combine dry milk, flour and seasonings in 3 qt. pan.  Stir in water and chicken broth until 
smooth.  Bring to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir 1 min.  Saut√© 
onion in butter until tender.  Stir onion and broccoli into milk.  Remove from heat, add cheese 
until melted.  Serve.
