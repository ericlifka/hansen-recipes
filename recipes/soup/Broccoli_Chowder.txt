Broccoli Chowder

4 c. fresh broccoli florets
2 med. potatoes, diced
1 1/2 c. water    
2 carrots, sliced
1 onion, chopped
1 celery, chopped
4 c. milk, divided
2 t. chicken bouillon granules
1 t. worcestershire sauce
3/4 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1/3 c. flour
1 c. velveeta

In pan combine first 6 ingredients (broc.-celery).  
Bring to a boil.  
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for -10 minutes or until veggie are tender.  
Add 3 c. milk and the bouillon, worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.  
In a small bowl combine flour and remaining milk and mix until smooth; gradually add to soup.  
Bring to a boil. 
Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  
Remove from heat, stir in cheese until melted.  
Serve.
