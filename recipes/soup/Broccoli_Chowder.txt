Broccoli Chowder
4 c. fresh broccoli florets2 med. potatoes, diced1 1/2 c. water    2 carrots, sliced
1 onion, chopped1 celery, chopped4 c. milk, divided2 t. chicken bouillon granules
1 t. worcestershire sauce3/4 t. salt1/2 t. pepper1/3 c. flour1 c. velveeta
In pan combine first 6 ingredients (broc.-celery).  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and 
simmer for -10 minutes or until veggie are tender.  Add 3 c. milk and the bouillon, 
worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.  In a small bowl combine flour and remaining milk 
and mix until smooth; gradually add to soup.  Bring to a boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or 
until thickened.  Remove from heat, stir in cheese until melted.  Serve.
