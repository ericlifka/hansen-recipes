Senate Bean Soup

1 lb. white navy beans
1 meaty ham bone or 1 1/2 lb. ham hocks
1 c. onions
2 garlic cloves
1 c. celery
2/3 c. instant mashed potato flakes
1/4 c. parsley
1 1/2 t. salt
1 tsp each nutmeg, oregano, basil1 bay leaf

In pot, over beans with hot water.  
Bring to a boil.  
Boil 2 minutes.  
Turn off and let stand 1 hr.  
Drain.  
Add 2 qts.. cold water and ham bone or hocks.  
Bring to a boil.  
Simmer 1 1/2 hours.  
Stir in rest of ingredients.   
Simmer 30 min. or until beans are tender.  
Remove bones from meat and discard bone.  
Put meat in soup in small pieces.  
This can be frozen.
