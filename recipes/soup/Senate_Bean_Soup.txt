Senate Bean Soup
1 lb. white navy beans1 meaty ham bone or 1 1/2 lb. ham hocks1 c. onions
2 garlic cloves1 c. celery2/3 c. instant mashed potato flakes1/4 c. parsley
1 1/2 t. salt1 tsp each nutmeg, oregano, basil1 bay leaf
In pot, over beans with hot water.  Bring to a boil.  Boil 2 minutes.  Turn off and let stand 1 
hr.  Drain.  Add 2 qts.. cold water and ham bone or hocks.  Bring to a boil.  Simmer 1 1/2 
hours.  Stir in rest of ingredients.   Simmer 30 min. or until beans are tender.  Remove 
bones from meat and discard bone.  Put meat in soup in small pieces.  This can be frozen.
Vegetable Soup
Boil soup bone for about an hour.  Remove from water and cut off meat.  Add stewed 
tomato, carrots, onion, celery, potato, bay leaf and salt and pepper.  Simmer for about an 
hour.
