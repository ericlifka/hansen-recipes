Chili
2 T. oil 2 lb. chuck steak (cubed)1 T. flour.  Brown those together.
Add:  1 onion3/4 c. red wine1 c. tomato sauce1 clove garlic1 carrot
1 green pepper40 oz. canned beans (different kinds)2 bay leaves
1/2 t. cumin1 t. oregano10 1/2 oz. can minestrone and lentil soup1/4 t. red pepper
Simmer 1 1/2 hr.  Uncover last 30 minutes if needed to thicken.
