Vegetable Soup

4 c. water
1 Soup Bone
1 can stewed tomatoes
2 c. chopped carrots, onion, & celery
1 c. diced potato
2 bay leaf
salt and pepper

Boil soup bone for about an hour.  
Remove from water and cut off meat.  
Add stewed tomato, carrots, onion, celery, potato, bay leaf and salt and pepper.  
Simmer for about an hour.
