Top Secret Applebee’s Onion Soup
3 T. oil6 medium white onions, sliced8 c. beef broth1 c. water
2 1/2 t. salt1/2 t. garlic powder1/4 t. pepper
Add oil to pan over medium high heat.  Add onions and sauté 20 minutes until the onions 
begin  to get soft and become translucent.  Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.  
Reduce heat to a simmer and simmer for 45 minutes.
