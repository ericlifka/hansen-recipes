Shrimp Potato Soup

1/2 stick butter
1 onion
2 carrots
2 T. flour
8 potatoes      
4 c. milk
1/2 c. milk
2 chicken cubes
1 c. half and half
1 t. salt, pepper  
2 c. water 
1 lb shrimp (or corn).  

Saut√© butter, onion, and carrots for 5 minutes.  
Whisk in flour, add potatoes, milk, chicken cubes.  
Cook 15 minutes.      
Add half and half, salt and pepper.   
Cook shrimp in water and add to soup.
