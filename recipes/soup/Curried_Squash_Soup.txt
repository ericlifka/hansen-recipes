Curried Squash Soup

3/4 c. chopped onion
2 T. butter
4 c. peeled, diced squash
4 c. chicken broth
2 t. sugar
2 t. curry powder
3/4 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
3 T. sherry
1/2 c. whipped cream

Melt butter, add onion.  
Saut√© until limp, add squash, broth, sugar, curry powder, salt and pepper.  
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer till veggies are tender.  
Run soup through food processor.  
Stir in sherry and cream.
