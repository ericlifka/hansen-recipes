Broccoli Cheese Soup
6 c. chicken stock1 c. onion1 t. salt, pepper, garlic powder1 stick butter
2 1/2 c. broccoli or 1 16 oz. bag frozen broccoli1 c. flour    2 c. milk cheese to taste
Bring stock, onion and seasonings to a boil.  Add broccoli, reduce heat to simmer and cook 
until broccoli is soft about 8 minutes.  In separate pan melt butter, and add flour.  Whisk for 5 
minutes until smooth over med. heat.  Slowly add milk stirring constantly to avoid lumps.  
Add broccoli mix slowly until soup begins to thicken.  Garnish with cheese.
