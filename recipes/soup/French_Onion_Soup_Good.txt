French Onion Soup (Good)
2 T. butter5 medium red onions (3 lb.) 6 c. chicken broth1 3/4 c. beef broth
1/4 c. red wine      2 sprigs parsley    1 t. thyme  1 bay leaf  1 T. balsamic vinegar
Melt butter.  Add onions.  Cook 3 minutes until onions are reduced.  Stir in rest of stuff.  
Simmer about 20 minutes.   Stir in vinegar and seasonings.
