Asparagus Potato Soup
3 medium potatoes1 3/4 c. chicken broth 1/3 c. onion1 t. salt
1/8 t. nutmeg1 pkg. frozen asparagus (10 oz.)1 1/2 c. cream
4 oz. cream cheese with pimento
Dice potatoes and cook in broth, onion, salt and nutmeg.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and 
simmer covered 5-8 min. until potatoes are tender.  Add asparagus and boil.  Reduce heat, 
simmer 5 min. until tender.   Add cream cheese.  When melted, serve.
