Upside Down Orange French Toast
1/2 c. melted butter1/2 c. sugar1/4 c. chopped pecans2 T. orange peel
2 t. cinnamon12 slices french bread4 oz. cream cheese, cube8 eggs
1 c. orange juice1/2 t. butter flavor
Place butter in 9 x 13.  Combine sugar pecans, orange peel and cinnamon; sprinkle over 
butter.  Arrange bread inn dish.  Dot with cream cheese.  In bowl whisk eggs, oj and butter 
flavor; pour over bread.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Remove from refrig 30 minutes 
before baking.  Bake uncovered at 325 for 25-30 minutes.
