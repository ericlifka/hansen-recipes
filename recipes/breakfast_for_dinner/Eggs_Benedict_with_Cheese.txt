Eggs Benedict with Cheese
4 toasted, buttered English muffin halves4 slices ham or Canadian bacon, cooked
4 broiled tomato slices4 poached eggs
Top muffin halves with ham, tomato, egg and cover with cheese sauce.  
Cheese sauce:  1 c. hot milk1 c. American cheese1 1/2 T. flour2T. butter
1/8 t. paprika1/2 t. salt
Melt butter.  Add flour and seasonings.  Pour milk into mix, slowly stirring constantly.  Add 
cheese.  Cook until thick and creamy.  You can substitute broccoli for tomato.
