Easy Brunch Bake
12 oz. sausage1 green pepper, chopped1/2 small onion3 c. croutons
1 1/4 c. milk3 eggs1/4 t. salt1 1/2 c. mozzarella cheese
Spray 9‚Äù square baking pan or pie plate with Pam.  Crumble sausage into skillet; add 
green pepper and onion.  Stirring frequently, cook over medium heat 10 minutes or until 
sausage is browned.  Drain fat.  Stir in croutons, spoon into baking pan.  In bowl whisk milk, 
eggs and salt until blended.  Pour over crouton mix, stir gently to combine.  Sprinkle with 
cheese.  Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until top is browned and mixture is set.  Cool 10 
minutes.  Serve.
