Using a biscuit cutter, cut a hole in the center of the bread.  Butter both sides of bread and 
place in a cast iron saut√© pan or griddle over medium heat.  Crack the eggs into the holes 
and season with salt and pepper.  Once the egg starts to set, flip over and continue cooking 
the other side until set.  Serve with cheese sauce.  Cheese sauce:  2 T. butter and flour to 
make a roux.  Add 1 1/2 c. warm milk slowly until combined.  Stir in 1/8 t. dry mustard, and 
cayenne pepper, dash tabasco & worcestershire, 2 c. grated cheddar, 1/2 c. Parmesan, 
s&p.  Constantly stir until cheese is smooth, thick and melted.
