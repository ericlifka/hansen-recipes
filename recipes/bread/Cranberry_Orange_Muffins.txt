Cranberry Orange Muffins
1 3/4 c. flour       5 T. sugar2 t. baking powder            1 beaten egg
3/4 c. orange juice    1/3 c. veggie oil1 c. cranberries, chopped
In bowl stir together flour, 2 T. sugar, baking powder, and 1/2 t. salt.  Make a well in center.  
In a small bowl combine egg, orange juice, and oil; add all at once to flour mixture.  Stir until 
moistened.  Toss cranberries with 2 T. sugar, fold into batter.  Fill muffin cups 3/4 full.  
Sprinkle muffins with remaining sugar.  Bake at 400 for 18-20 minutes.
