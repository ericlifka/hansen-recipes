Crescent Rolls
2 pkg. yeast1 c. milk1/2 c. butter1/2 c. sugar        1/2 t. salt
2 eggs1/4 c. warm water   4 c. flour
Mix together, let rise until double.  Divide dough into thirds, roll out into circle.  Cut into 
sixteen pieces (cut like a pie).  Roll up large side to small.  Let rise again, half an hour to one 
hour.  Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.  Dough is very sticky.
