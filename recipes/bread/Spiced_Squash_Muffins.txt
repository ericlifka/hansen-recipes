Spiced Squash Muffins
2 c. flour1/3 c. brown sugar2 t. baking powder1 t. cinnamon1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. ginger, nutmeg2 eggs3/4 c. squash3/4 c. light corn syrup1/4 c. 
butter, melted 1/4 c. veggie oil1 t. vanillaTopping:  4 T. butter 1/2 c. brown 
sugar1 t. cinnamon
Combine all dry ingredients and then wet muffin mix stuff.  Fill paper lined muffin cups 3/4 
full.   Combine topping mix until crumbly.  Sprinkle on muffins.  Bake 400 for 15-20 
minutes.    Cool 5 minutes before putting on wire rack.  Serve warm.
