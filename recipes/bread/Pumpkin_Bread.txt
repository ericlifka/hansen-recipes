Pumpkin Bread
4 eggs2/3 c. butter   3 c. sugar     15 oz pumpkin      3 c. flour
1/2 t. salt, baking powder2 t. baking soda1 t. cinnamon    1/2 t. cloves
1/2 t. nutmeg         1 c. chopped nuts
Combine eggs, butter and sugar, blending well.  Add the rest of ingredients.  Pour into 2 
greased 8 1/2 x 5 1/2 loaf pans and bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Turn onto racks and cool.
