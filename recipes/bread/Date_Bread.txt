Date Bread

1 c. chopped dates (hand chop)
3/4 c. nuts
1 1/2 t. baking soda   
1/2 t. salt
1/4 c. butter
3/4 c. boiling water
2 eggs    
1 t. vanilla     
1 c. sugar    
1 1/2 c. flour

Combine nuts, dates, soda, salt in bowl.   
Add butter and water.  
Let sit 15 minutes.  
Stir.  
Beat eggs slightly.  
Add vanilla.  
Stir in sugar and flour.  
Add to date mix.  
Do not overmix.
Grease pans bake at 350 for 1 hour.  
Makes 2 loaves.
