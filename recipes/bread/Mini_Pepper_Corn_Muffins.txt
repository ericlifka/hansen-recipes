Mini Pepper Corn Muffins
2 T. each red, green peppers, diced1 1/2 t. olive oil1/2 c. plus 2 T. cornmeal
6 T. flour1 1/2 t. sugar1/2 t. baking powder1/4 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt1 egg1/2 c. buttermilk3 T. butter, melted
In a small skillet, saut√© peppers in oil for 5 minutes or until tender; cool slightly.  In a bowl, 
combine the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  In a small bowl, 
whisk the egg, buttermilk, butter and peppers until blended.  Stir into dry ingredients just 
until moistened.  Fill the paper lined mini muffin cups 3/4 full.  Bake at 425 for 6-9 minutes or 
until toothpick comes out clean.
