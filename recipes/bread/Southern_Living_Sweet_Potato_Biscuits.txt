Southern Living Sweet Potato Biscuits
4 c. flour2 T. baking powder     2 t. salt1 c. butter3/4-1 c. buttermilk
1 c. sweet potato, mashed
Combine flour, baking powder and salt.  Cut in butter.  Stir together potato and buttermilk.  
Turn dough onto floured surface.  Roll to 1/2” thick.  Grease cookie sheet.  Use a 3” biscuit 
cutter.  Bake at 425 for 10-15 minutes.
