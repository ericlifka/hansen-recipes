Breakfast Puff
1/2 c. sugar    1/3 c. shortening      1 egg    1 1/2 c. flour        1 1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt1/4 t. nutmeg    1/2 c. milk     1/2 c. sugar    1 t. cinnamon   6T. melted butter
In mixer cream sugar, shortening and egg.  Add flour, baking powder, salt, nutmeg adding 
alternately with milk.  Beat well.  Fill 12 muffin cups 2/3 full.  Bake 350 for 20-25 minutes.  
Combine sugar and cinnamon.  Remove muffins from oven, immediately dip muffins in 
butter, then in cinnamon until coated.  Serve warm.
