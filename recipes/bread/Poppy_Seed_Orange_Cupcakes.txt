Poppy Seed Orange Cupcakes
1 1/2 c. flour1 c. sugar      1/2 c. sour cream1/3 c. butter, softened
1 egg        1 T. poppy seeds2 T. orange peel zest, and orange juice   
1/2 t. baking soda and salt
In large bowl combine all ingredients; beat on low speed until ingredients are moistened.  
Spoon into muffin pans.  Bake at 400 for 18-22 minutes or until lightly browned.  Top with 
mix of 1/2 c. butter, 3 oz. cream cheese, 1/4 c. powdered sugar and 1 T. orange zest beat 
together until creamy.
