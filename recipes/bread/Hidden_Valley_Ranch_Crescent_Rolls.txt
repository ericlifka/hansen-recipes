Hidden Valley Ranch Crescent Rolls
1 8 oz. tube crescent rolls1 pkg. Ranch Milk Recipe Salad Dressing Mix
1 egg yolk1 t. water
Separate dough. Beat yolk with water.  Brush on dough.  Sprinkle with dressing mix.  Roll 
up in crescent shape.  Brush with egg mix.  Bake on greased sheet at 375 for 11-13 
minutes.
