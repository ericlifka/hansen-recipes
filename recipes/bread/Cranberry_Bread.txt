Cranberry Bread
2 c. flour1/2 t. salt, soda1 1/2 t. baking powder3/4 c. sugar       1 egg
2 T. shortening1/2 c. orange juice     2T. hot water1/2 c. nuts1 c. cranberry
Cut cranberries and nuts.  Sift the dry ingredients together.  Add liquid and combine until dry 
ingredients are moistened.  Fold in nuts, cranberries.  Bake 1 hour and 10 minutes at 350.
