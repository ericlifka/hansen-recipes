Herbed Artichoke Cheese Tortellini
1 can italian diced tomatoes   1 can artichoke hearts  9 oz. refrigerated tortellini
1 c. onion1/4 c. parsley2-4 t. dried basil1 t. garlic1/4 t. oregano
1/8 t. crushed red pepper flakes1/4 c. olive oil1 2 1/4 oz. drained sliced olives
1/4 t. salt1/8 c. grated Parmesan
Drain tomatoes, reserving 2/3 c. juice.  Drain artichokes, reserving 3/4 c. liquid; chop and set 
aside.  Cook tortellini.  In large skillet sate the onions, parsley, basil, garlic, oregano and 
pepper flakes in oil for 4-5 minutes or until onions are tender.  Add the reserved tomatoes, 
tomato juice and artichoke liquid.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 10-12 
minutes or until slightly thickened.  Drain tortellini; add to tomato mix.  Stir in olives, salt and 
reserved artichokes; heat through.  Sprinkle with parmesan.
