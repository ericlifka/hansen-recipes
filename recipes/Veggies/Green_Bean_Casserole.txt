Green Bean Casserole

1 can cr. of mushroom
1/2 c. milk
1 t. soy sauce
4 c. green beans
1 1/3 c. french fried onions

Mix soup, milk, soy sauce, green beans and 2/3 c. french fried onions.
Put in 1 1/2 qt. casserole.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
Stir and sprinkle with rest of onions.
Cook another 5 minutes.
