Green Bean Casserole
1 can cr. of mushroom1/2 c. milk1 t. soy sauce4 c. green beans
1 1/3 c. french fried onions
Mix soup, milk, soy sauce, green beans and 2/3 c. french fried onions.  Put in 1 1/2 qt. 
casserole.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  Stir and sprinkle with rest of onions.  Cook another 
5 minutes.
