Eggplant Casserole 1

1 med. eggplant, cut in cubes and parboiled
1/2 c. celery
1 c. green onions
1/2 c. bell pepper
1 can stewed tomatoes, drained
5-6 slices bread, toasted & cubed
sharp cheese, cut into small pieces
1 lb. hot sausage, cooked, drained, optional

Grease 2 qt. dish.
Cover bottom with bread crumbs, then add eggplant, celery, bell pepper, onions, sausage and tomatoes.
Cover and bake at 350 for 45 min.
Add remaining crumbs and cheese.
Return to over 3-4 minutes until cheese melts.
