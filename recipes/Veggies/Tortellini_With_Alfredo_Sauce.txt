Tortellini With Alfredo Sauce

16 oz. tortellini (cheese)
1 1/2 c. heavy cream
1 clove garlic
1/2 t. Italian seasoning
1/2 c. Parmesan
salt and pepper

Cook tortellini according to package directions.  
Drain.  
Transfer tortellini to a saut√© pan over medium-high heat and immediately add cream, garlic, Italian seasoning and 1/4 c. Parmesan cheese.  
Bring to a boil and heat, stirring occasionally, 7-10 minutes until sauce has thickened.  
Season to taste with salt and pepper.  
Top with remaining 1/4 c. Parmesan and serve.
