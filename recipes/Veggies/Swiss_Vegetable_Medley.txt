Swiss Vegetable Medley

16 oz. broccoli, carrot, cauliflower combo, thawed and drained
1/3 c. sour cream
1/4 t. pepper    
4 oz. pimento (opt.)
1 can cr. mushroom soup
4 oz. Swiss cheese        
1 can french fried onion 

Combine veggies, soup 1/2 c. cheese, sour cream, pepper, pimento and 1/2 can onions in 1 qt. casserole.  
Bake covered at 350 for 30 minutes.  Top with remaining cheese and onions.  
Bake uncovered 5 minutes longer.
