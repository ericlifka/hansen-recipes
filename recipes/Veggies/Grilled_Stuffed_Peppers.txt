Grilled Stuffed Peppers
4 medium green peppers1/4 c. veggie oil1 lb. Monterey jack cheese, shredded
2 T. buttertabasco
Wash and dry peppers.  Slice in half lengthwise, remove core and seeds.  Coat outside 
skin with thin coating of oil.  Stuff pepper halves with cheese, filling not quite full.  Add a dab 
of butter, and dash of tabasco to taste.  Place peppers on lightly oiled aluminum foil.  Place 
on hot coals and grill 20-30 minutes.   Can also be done in oven.
