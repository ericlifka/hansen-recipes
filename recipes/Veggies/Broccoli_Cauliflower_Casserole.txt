Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole
20 oz. frozen broccoli10 oz. cauliflower1 can cr. of celery8 oz. cheese whiz
1 can fried onion rings
Cook broccoli and cauliflower separately.  Drain and place in buttered dish.  Combine soup 
and cheese.  Pour over veggies and bake uncovered 30 min. at 325 or 350.  Add onion 
rings for last 10 minutes.
