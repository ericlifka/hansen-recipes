Zucchini Casserole With Noodles

1/2 c. oil
6 medium zucchini
2 garlic
8 oz. fresh mushrooms
14 oz. can tomatoes
1 c. bread crumbs
1 c. sour cream
1 T. almonds
1 t. paprika
1/2 c. Parmesan

Heat 3/8 c. oil in pan.  
Sauté zucchini and remove from pan.  
Put remaining oil in pan with garlic and mushrooms.  
Sauté.  
Add tomato and cook.  A
dd crumbs, sour cream, almonds and paprika.  
Cook 1 minute.  
Re add zucchini and stir.  
Put in casserole dish and put cheese over.  
Bake 30 min. at 350.  
Serve over noodles.
