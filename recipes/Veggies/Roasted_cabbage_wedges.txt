Roasted cabbage wedges
Preheat oven to 400.  Brush a rimmed baking sheet with 1 T. olive oil.  Place one med. 
head of cabbage, cut into 1 inch thick rounds, in a single layer on sheet and brush with 2 T. 
oil.  Season with coarse salt and gr. pepper, and sprinkle with 1 t. caraway or fennel seeds.  
Roast until cabbage is tender and edges are golden 40-45 minutes.
