Eggplant Casserole 2
1 large eggplant1/4 c. butter1 onion1 stalk celery1/4 c. water
1/4 c. bell pepper1/4 c. grated cheese2 tomatoes1 c. bread crumbs, toasted
salt and pepper to tastebeef or sausage, browned
Split eggplant in half, remove inside.  Place butter in pan, add onion and eggplant.  Start 
cooking, then add water, celery and bell pepper.  Let cook until veggies are tender.  Then 
add tomatoes and cheese.  Stir in bread crumbs and place in empty eggplant shells.  
Garnish with chopped tomatoes and bake until done.
