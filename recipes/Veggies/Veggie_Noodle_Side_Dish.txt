Veggie Noodle Side Dish
1 small julienned sweet red pepper3/4 c. green beans, carrots1/4 red onion
1 T. canola oil3 oz ramen noodles1 yellow squash1 med. zucchini
1/4 c. chicken broth1 T. soy sauce1 t. fajita seasoning mix
In wok, stir fry the pepper, green beans, carrots and onion for 4 minutes.  Meanwhile cook 
noodles discarding seasoning packet.  Add the remaining ingredients to the veggie mix.  
Cook until veggies are crisp tender.  Drain noodles and add to veggies and mix.
