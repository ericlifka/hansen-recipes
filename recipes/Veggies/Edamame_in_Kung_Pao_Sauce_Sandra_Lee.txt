Edamame in Kung Pao Sauce (Sandra Lee)
1 lb. frozen, shelled edamame2 T. canola oil1/2 c. diced carrots
1/2 c. chopped onion1/2 c. Kung Pao sauce2 T. low sodium soy sauce
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.  Add edamame and return to a boil.  Cook for 5 
minutes.  Drain and set aside.  In frying pan over medium heat add the oil and cook the 
carrots and onions until tender.  In bowl stir together kung pao sauce, soy sauce and red 
pepper flakes (if desired).  Add in edamame, carrots, and onions.  Toss until vegetables 
are coated.  Serve.
