Beef Casserole Italiano
1 lb. hamburger1 onion16 oz. tomato sauce1 t. parsley flakes, basil
1/2 t. oregano     1/2 t. salt, divided      1/4 t. pepper      20 oz. spinach, thawed,drained
2 c. ricotta cheese8 oz. mozzarella cheese slices
Brown ground beef and onion, drain.  Stir in tomato sauce, parsley, oregano. basil, 1/4 t. 
salt and pepper.  Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Meanwhile, 
combine spinach, ricotta and remaining salt.  Spoon spinach mix around edges of 9 x 13 
pan; pour beef mix in center.  Cut mozzarella slices into 3 strips and arrange in a lattice 
pattern over meat.  Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
