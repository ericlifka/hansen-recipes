South of the Border Casserole
7 oz. elbow macaroni1 lb. turkey1 onion1 c. celery1 garlic
16 oz. can whole tomatoes, cut up and undrained   1/2 c. ripe olive4 oz. green chilies
2 t. beef flavored instant bouillon1/2 t. seasoned salt   1/4 t. cumin
dash cayenne pepper6 oz. muenster cheese6 oz. cheddar cheese
Prepare macaroni and drain.  In skillet combine turkey, onion, celery and garlic.  Cook until 
turkey is browned.  Stir in rest of ingredients except the cheeses.  Simmer 10 minutes.  
Combine macaroni, meat mix and cheeses.  Put in casserole dish.  Cover.  Bake at 350 
about 20 minutes.
