Chicken Zucchini Hotdish
4 oz. uncooked spag. noodles1 can cr. of mushroom        1 zucchini          1/2  c. onion
1/4 c. parmesan1 c. mozzarella1 clove garlic    1/2 c. milk
2 c. cubed cooked chicken
Break noodles in half.  Cook, drain.  Cook onion and garlic.  Blend in soup.  Add zucchini 
and milk.  Cook until zucchini is tender.  Stir in mozzarella.  Add noodles and chicken.  Put in 
casserole; add parmesan on top.  Bake 40 min. at 350.
