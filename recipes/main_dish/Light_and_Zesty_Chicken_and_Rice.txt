Light and Zesty Chicken and Rice
4 breast halves1/3 c. Italian dressing      2/3 c. rice (uncooked)   1 can french fried onion   
16 oz. frozen veggie combo 1 3/4 c. chicken bouillon1/2 t. Italian seasoning
Place breasts in 12 x 8 dish, top with dressings.  Bake uncovered at 400 for 20 min.  Place 
rice, veggies and 1/2 c. onion under chicken.  Combine bouillon and seasoning; pour over 
rice and veggies.  Bake uncovered 25 min.  Top with onions and bake 2-3  minutes.
