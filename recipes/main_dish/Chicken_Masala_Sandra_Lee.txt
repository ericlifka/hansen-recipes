Chicken Masala (Sandra Lee)
4 breasts1/3 c. flour2 T. oil1 onion1 t. garlic1 T. garam masala
15 oz. diced tomato, drained1/2 c. cream of chicken soup1 c. light coconut milk
salt and pepper1 c. peas (optional)rice
Shake chicken in flour.  Heat oil.  Fry breast on both sides.  Remove.  Add onion and garlic--
cook until soft.   Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.  Reduce to simmer, put breasts 
back in pan, and simmer 15 minutes.  Add peas, and simmer 10 more minutes.
