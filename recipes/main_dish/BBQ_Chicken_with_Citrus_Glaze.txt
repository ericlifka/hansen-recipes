BBQ Chicken with Citrus Glaze
1/2 c.  each catsup,  orange juice        1/4 c. each brown sugar, lemon juice
2 t. cornstarch1/2 t. each grated orange peel, dry mustard3 lb. chicken pieces
Combine ingredients except chicken in pan.  Bring to boil.  Cook stirring constantly until 
thickened and translucent.  Pour into jar and cover tightly.  Cool and refrigerate.  Place chicken  
on grill.  Grill 30 minutes.  Brush with glaze and cook until chicken is done, turning and basting 
every 10 minutes until done.
