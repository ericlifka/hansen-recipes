Penne pasta or fettuccine
Melt butter in pan.  Add chicken and cook until no longer pink about 6-10 minutes.  Remove 
from pan.  Cook peppers, onion, garlic in pan about 5 minutes.  Add mushrooms and 
chicken.  Add seasonings--cajun, salt, pepper, cayenne.  Cook a few minutes.  Add 
tomato, stock and cream.  Cook all together on low.  Add cooked pasta, mix, and heat a 
few minutes longer.
