Chicken with Cranberries
3 lb. chicken1 c. peanut oil3/4 c. orange juice1 c. flouronion
16 oz. cranberries1/4 t. cinnamon and ginger      salt, pepper, cayenne pepper
Mix flour and salt and peppers.  Dredge chicken and panfry in oil.  Mix rest of ingredients 
and boil.  Pour over chicken and cover.  Cook slowly 35-40 minutes.
Chicken with Peanut Butter (Frugal Gourmet)
Put chicken, onion, dill, bay leaf in water and cook 45 min.  Add 1/2 c. peanut butter or more 
to hot stock.  Add 3 T. cornstarch dissolved in 1/2 c. water till thick.  Put chicken&sauce on rice
