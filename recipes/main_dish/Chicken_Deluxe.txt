Chicken Deluxe
20 oz. frozen chopped spinach2 chicken breasts, cooked1 c. mayo
1 can cr. of chicken soup1-2 t. curry1 T. lemon juice1/2 c. stuffing
Cook spinach until tender and drain.  Spread spinach on bottom of 1 1/2 qt. greased dish.  
Layer chicken on top.  Combine mayo, soup, curry and lemon juice.  Pour over chicken and 
spinach.  Sprinkle stuffing on top.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
