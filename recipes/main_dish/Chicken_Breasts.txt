Chicken Breasts
6 breasts, skinned, deboned if desired1 can mushroom soup1/2 c. sherry or wine
1 c. sour cream1 small can mushrooms with juice
Lay chicken in baking dish.  Mix other ingredients together and pour over chicken.  Sprinkle 
with paprika.  Bake uncovered 325-350 about 1 1/2  to 2 hours.
