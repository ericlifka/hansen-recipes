Salisbury Steak (lower calorie)
1/2 c. fat free milk14 fat free saltines, crushed2 T. dried minced onion
2 t. dried parsley flakes1 lb. lean ground beef1 jar fat free beef gravy
2 T. ketchup2 t. Worcestershire sauce1 4/ t. pepper
In a large bowl combine the milk, saltines, onion and parsley.  Crumble beef over mix and 
Mix well.  Shape into 4 patties.  Place in an 8 in square baking dish coated with PAM.  In a 
small bowl combine the gravy, ketchup, Worcestershire and pepper; pour over patties.  
Bake uncovered at 350 for 50-55 minutes or until meat is no longer pink. (266 calories)
