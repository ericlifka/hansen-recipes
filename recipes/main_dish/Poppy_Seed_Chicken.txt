Poppy Seed Chicken
6 chicken breast halves1 can cr. of chicken soup1 c. cr. of mushroom soup
1/2 c. sour cream15 Ritz or club crackers, crushed1/4 stick butter, melted   
2 T. poppy seeds
Coat a 9 x 13 inch pan with Pam.  Place breasts in pan.  Combine soups with sour cream 
and pour over chicken.  Combine crumbs, butter, and poppy seeds.  Sprinkle over sauce.  
Bake for 45-60 minutes at 350.
