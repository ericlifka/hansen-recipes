Fettuccine With Pepper Sauce
Pam1 1/2 c. broccoli florets1 pepper, (any color)1 onion1 T. oil
12 oz. chicken, cut in strips1 can cream soup  (chicken, celery, or broccoli)
1/2 c. water1 t. basil1/2 c. Swiss or cheddar8 oz. fettuccine, cooked
Spray skillet over medium heat.  Stir fry broccoli, sweet pepper and onion 3-4 minutes; 
remove from skillet.  Add oil to skillet.  Add poultry, stir fry 3-4 minutes or until no longer 
pink.  Add soup, water, and basil; mix thoroughly.  Stir in sweet pepper mix.  Bring to a 
boil, reduce heat.  Add cheese; cook and stir until cheese is almost melted.  Serve over 
pasta.
