Korean Style Steak
2 T. veggie oil3 T. honey1 small onion1/2 c. reduced sodium soy sauce
2 t. garlic1 t. ginger1/4 t. pepper4 strip steaks (4 oz.)
In small bowl mix together oil and honey.  Add onion to mix.  Add soy sauce, garlic, ginger 
and pepper.  Whisk  well.  Place steaks in a ziplock bag.  Pour marinade over.  Seal bag 
and marinate for at least 2 hours, but up to 8 hours.  Remove meat from marinade.  Place 
steaks on grill until desired doneness is reached (4 minutes/side for medium rare).
