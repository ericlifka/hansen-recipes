Chicken Quesadilla
chicken, cut uptaco seasoning mix        green, red, yellow peppers    cheese
Cook peppers in pan for a few minutes(5-10).  Cook chicken in pan, when chicken is done, 
add taco seasoning mix and water.  I use flavored tortillas.  Spray pan with PAM, put tortilla 
in pan, top with cheese, chicken, peppers, more cheese.  Cook 1-2 minutes on each side.  
Top Secret Recipe version of Taco Bell  sauce to go on tortilla if desired:  1/4 c. mayo
2 t. minced jalapeno slices2 t. juice from jar3/4 t. sugar1/2 t. paprika, cumin     
1/8 t. cayenne pepper, garlic powderdash salt
Mix all sauce ingredients together and cover and chill until ready to use.  Can use less 
jalapeno if want less spicy.
