Chili
2 T. oil2 T. chuck steak (cubed)1 T. flour 1 onion3/4 c. red wine
1 c. tomato sauce1 garlic1 carrot, green pepper  2 bay leaves
40 oz. canned beans (different kinds)1/2 t. cumin1 t. oregano
1/4 t. crushed red pepper10 1/2 oz. can minestrone and lentil soup.
Heat oil.  Brown steak in oil with flour.  Add rest of ingredients.  Simmer 1 1/2 hour.  Uncover 
last 30 minutes if needed to thicken.
