1 large onion, sliced3 lb. chicken cut up1/2 t. hickory smoked salt1/8 t. pepper
1/4 c. ketchup1/4 c. maple syrup2 T. cider vinegar1 T. Dijon mustard
Heat oven to 350. Place onion slices in a 9 x 13 dish.  Arrange chicken in a layer, skin side 
up on top of onion.  Sprinkle with hickory salt and pepper.   In small bowl combine ketchup, 
maple syrup, vinegar and mustard mixing well.  Pour over  chicken.  Bake uncovered for 50 
minutes, until chicken is tender.
