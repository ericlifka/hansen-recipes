Viva Mushrooms
1 lb. mushrooms1 t. salt
1/3 c. softened butter1/8 t. pepper
1 T. parsley flakes1/8 t. nutmeg
2 T. dry onions1 1/2 T. flour
1 T. prepared mustard1 c. heavy cream
Place cleaned mushrooms in buttered casserole dish.  Combine butter, parsley, onion, 
mustard, seasonings and flour.  It will be like a paste.  Dot on top of mushrooms.  Pour 
cream over.  Bake at 350ยบ for 55 minutes.
