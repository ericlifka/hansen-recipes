Eggplant Casserole 1
1 med. eggplant, cut in cubes and parboiled1/2 c. celery1 c. green onions
1/2 c. bell pepper1 can stewed tomatoes, drained5-6 slices bread, toasted,  cubed  
sharp cheese, cut into small pieces1 lb. hot sausage, cooked, drained, optional
Grease 2 qt. dish.  Cover bottom with bread crumbs, then add eggplant, celery, bell 
pepper, onions, sausage and tomatoes.  Cover and bake at 350 for 45 min.  Add 
remaining crumbs and cheese.  Return to over 3-4 minutes until cheese melts.
