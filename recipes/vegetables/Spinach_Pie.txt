Spinach Pie
4 pkg. spinach1 onion1/2 bunch dill8 oz. cr. cheese
4 eggs     2 c. feta1 lb. phyllo 1 stick butter1/4 c. olive oil
Mix spinach, cheese and cook in oil.  For phyllo, melt butter and spread on layers of phyllo.  
Layer half the phyllo and then put spinach on and layer other half of phyllo.  Bake at 350 for 
30 minutes.  (Make sure to read the phyllo label as it dries quickly.)
