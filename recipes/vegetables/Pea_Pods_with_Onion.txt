Pea Pods with Onion
1/2 lb. fresh pea pods2 T. water1/4 c. onion3 T. butter, dividedsalt, pepper
Place pea pods and water in a microwave bowl; cover and cook for 3-5 minutes until crisp 
tender.  Meanwhile in small skillet, cook onion in 2 T. butter over medium heat until crisp 
tender.  Drain peas, add to onion.  Add salt, pepper and remaining butter.  Cook till heated.
