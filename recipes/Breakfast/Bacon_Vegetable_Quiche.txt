Bacon Vegetable Quiche
1 9‚Äù unbaked pastry shell2 c. fresh baby spinach1 c. sliced fresh mushrooms
1 c. chopped broccoli3/4 c. sweet onion2 1/2 t. olive oil3 eggs, beaten
5 oz. evaporated milk1 t. dried rosemary, crushed       1/4 t. salt and pepper
4 oz. shredded cheddar6 crumbled bacon strips1/2 c. crumbled tomato and basil 
feta cheese
Saute in skillet the spinach, mushrooms, broccoli and onion in oil until tender.  In bowl whisk 
the eggs, milk, rosemary, salt and pepper.  Transfer cooled vegetables into egg mix.  Stir 
in cheese and bacon.  Pour into crust and top with feta.  Cover edges loosely with foil.  
Bake at 375 for 30-35 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.  Let stand 
5 minutes before cutting.
