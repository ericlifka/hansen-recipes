Eggs Benedict with Cheese

4 toasted, buttered English muffin halves
4 slices ham or Canadian bacon, cooked
4 broiled tomato slices
4 poached eggs
Cheese sauce:  
1 c. hot milk
1 c. American cheese
1 1/2 T. flour
2 T. butter
1/8 t. paprika
1/2 t. salt

Top muffin halves with ham, tomato, egg and cover with cheese sauce. 
Melt butter.  
Add flour and seasonings.  
Pour milk into mix, slowly stirring constantly.  
Add cheese.  
Cook until thick and creamy.  
You can substitute broccoli for tomato.
