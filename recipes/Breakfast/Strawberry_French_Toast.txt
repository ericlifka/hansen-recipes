Strawberry French Toast
Combine:  5 eggs, 3/4 c. milk, 1 t. vanilla.  Dip 8 slices of french bread in eggs for 30 
seconds each side.  Let stand 10 minutes.  Grease 9 x 13.  Spread 5 c. frozen 
strawberries in pan.  Combine 2/3 c. sugar and 1/2 t. cinnamon.  Sprinkle half over berries.  
Put bread on top.  Sprinkle with sugar.  Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.
