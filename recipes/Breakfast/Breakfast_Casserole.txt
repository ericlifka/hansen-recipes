Breakfast Casserole
3 c. frozen hash browns 1 c. diced ham4 eggs1/4 t. pepper, salt
3/4 c. Monterey jack cheese    1/4 c. green onion12 oz evaporated milk
Grease 2 qt. dish.  Put potatoes on bottom.  Sprinkle with cheese, ham, onion.  Combine 
rest of ingredients and pour over.  Bake uncovered 350 for 45 min. or until set.
