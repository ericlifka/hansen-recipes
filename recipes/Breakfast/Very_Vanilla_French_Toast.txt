Very Vanilla French Toast
1 c. milk1 3 oz. package cook and serve vanilla pudding1 egg
1/2 t. cinnamon8 slices Texas Toast or French bread2 t. butter
In a bowl whisk the milk, pudding mix, egg and cinnamon for 2 minutes or until well blended.  
Dip toast in pudding mix, coating both sides.  Melt butter on a griddle; cook french toast for 
2-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
