French Toast

6 eggs
2 t. cinnamon
2/3 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. butter
1 3/4 c. milk
1 loaf french bread

Mix eggs, and milk.  
Mix cinnamon, brown sugar and melted butter.  
Dip bread in egg/milk mix for 5 minutes turning once.   
Pour cinnamon mix into baking dish, top with bread and bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
