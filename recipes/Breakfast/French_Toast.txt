French Toast
6 eggs2 t. cinnamon2/3 c. brown sugar1/2 c. butter1 3/4 c. milk
1 loaf french bread
Mix eggs, and milk.  Mix cinnamon, brown sugar and melted butter.  Dip bread in egg/milk 
mix  for 5 minutes turning once.   Pour cinnamon mix into baking dish, top with bread and 
bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
