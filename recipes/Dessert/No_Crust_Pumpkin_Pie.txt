No Crust Pumpkin Pie

15 oz. can pumpkin       
12 oz. can evaporated milk
1/2 c. bisquick
2 eggs
3 t. brown sugar
2/3 c. sugar
1 T. vanilla
2 t. cinnamon
1/2 t. allspice, nutmeg
1/4 t. ginger, cloves
pinch of salt

Spray pan with PAM.
Throw all together and beat thoroughly.
Bake at 400 for 35-40 minutes.
Test with toothpick for doneness.
