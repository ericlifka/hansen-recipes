No Crust Pumpkin Pie
15 oz. can pumpkin       12 oz. can evaporated milk1/2 c. bisquick2 eggs
3 t. brown sugar2/3 c. sugar1 T. vanilla2 t. cinnamon1/2 t. allspice, nutmeg
1/4 t. ginger, clovespinch of salt
Spray pan with PAM.   Throw all together and beat thoroughly.  Bake at 400 for 35-40 
minutes.  Test with toothpick for doneness.
