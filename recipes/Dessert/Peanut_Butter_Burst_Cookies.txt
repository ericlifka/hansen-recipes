Peanut Butter Burst Cookies
2 c. flour1 t. baking powder1/4 t. salt1 c. butter, soft1/2 c. sugar
3/4 c. brown sugar1/2 t. vanilla1 egg12 oz. peanut butter morsels
In bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.  In bowl combine butter, brown 
sugar, sugar and vanilla; beat at medium until creamy.  Add egg; beat well.  Gradually add 
flour.  Stir in peanut butter morsels.  Drop by teaspoons onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 
for 7-9 minutes.
