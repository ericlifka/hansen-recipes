Grasshopper Pie
18 chocolate wafers1/2 c. butter        1/2 c. milk20 marshmallows
1/2 pt. whipping cream1 oz. creme de menthe   1 oz. creme de cocoa
Make crust of wafers and butter.  Melt marshmallows in milk and cool.  Beat cream and add 
liqueurs.  Combine marshmallow mix and cream.  Place in shell and chill.
