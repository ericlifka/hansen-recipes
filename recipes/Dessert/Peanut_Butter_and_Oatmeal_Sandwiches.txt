Peanut Butter and Oatmeal Sandwiches

1 c. peanut butter
1 c. sugar
3/4 c. butter, soft
1/4 c. corn syrup
1 egg
2 T. water
1 t. vanilla
3 c. oatmeal
1 1/4 c. flour
1 t. baking powder
1 c. peanut butter
2/3 c. light corn syrup

Heat oven to 350.
Beat first three ingredients until fluffy.
Add 1/4 c. corn syrup, egg, water and vanilla; mix until smooth.
Stir in combined dry ingredients.
Roll into 1 inch balls.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Press into 2 1/2 inch circles.
Bake 9-11 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool 1 minute; remove to wire rack.
Cool completely.
Mix remaining ingredients until smooth.
Spread rounded teaspoonful onto half of cookie; top with remaining cookies.
Decorate with powdered sugar and melted chocolate, if desired.
