Peanut Butter Pie
1 baked pie shell2/3 c. sugar          2 T. butter1 c. powdered sugar        1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. vanilla1/2 c. peanut butter             2 c. milk, scalded3 eggs, separated
1/4 c. cornstarch
Heat oven to 325.  Combine sugar, peanut butter, blending until mixture has consistency of 
biscuit mix.  Spread half of mixture in pie shell.  Combine cornstarch, 2/3 c. sugar and salt.  
Add milk and mix well.  Beat egg yolks and pour a small amount of milk over yolks.   Mix 
well into milk mixture.  Cook in top of double boiler until mix thickens.  Add butter and 
vanilla.  Pour into shell.  Beat egg whites till stiff.  Top mixture in shell with meringue.  
Sprinkle remaining peanut butter over meringue.  Bake until meringue is brown.
