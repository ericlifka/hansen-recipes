Cream Puffs
1 c. water1/2 c. butter1 c. flour4 eggs
Heat water and butter to a rolling boil.  Stir in flour.  Stir vigorously over low heat about 1 
minute until it forms a ball.  Remove from heat.  Beat in eggs one at a time until smooth.  
Drop dough by tablespoons  3‚Äù apart on greased baking sheets.  Bake at 400 for 35-40 
minutes.  Cool.  Split puffs and pull out any filaments of soft dough.  Fill puffs with fruit, 
whipped cream, vanilla pudding or lemon filling.  Drizzle chocolate sauce over top.
