Rhubarb Strawberry Cake

5 c. chopped rhubarb
1 c. sugar
1 3 oz. package strawberry jello
2 c. mini marshmallows
1 yellow cake box
egg (for cake)
oil (for cake)

Spread rhubarb in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
Sprinkle sugar, jello, and marshmallows over rhubarb
Mix 1 yellow cake mix as directed.
Spread over rhubarb.
Bake at 350 until done.
Serve warm or cold.
