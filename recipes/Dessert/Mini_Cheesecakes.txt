Mini Cheesecakes
1 1/2 c. graham cracker crumbs1/4 c. sugar    1/4 c. butter, melted
3-8 oz. pkg. cream cheese14 oz. sweetened condensed milk3 eggs
2 t. vanilla extract21 oz. can of pie filling
Heat oven to 300.  Combine crumbs, sugar, and butter.  Press crumbs on bottom of 24 
paper lined muffin cups.  In bowl beat cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in milk until 
smooth.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Mix well.  Spoon about 3 T. into prepared cups.  Bake 20 
minutes or until set.  Cool.  Chill.  Top with filling.   Refrigerate.  Can also make the batter 
chocolate by melting 1 c. semisweet chocolate chips and add to batter.
