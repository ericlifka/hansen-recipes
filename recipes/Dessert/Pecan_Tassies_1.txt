Pecan Tassies 1
2-3 oz. pkg. cream cheese2 sticks butter2 c. flour
Filling:2 eggs1 1/2 c. brown sugar2 T. butter2 t. vanilla       1/2 c. pecans
Combine cream cheese, butter and flour, refrigerate for 1 hour.  Roll dough into balls.  Form 
in cup of mini pans up to top making well for filling.  Mix filling ingredients, and chop pecans.  
Fill and bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes.  Allow to cool 5 minutes, remove from pans.
