Summer Surprise Dessert (dear abby)
1 c. seedless grapes, blueberries, strawberries (halved), peaches (bite size), brown sugar
2 c. sour cream.  In a shallow 9 x 12 glass dish, combine all fruit and mix well.  Sprinkle with 
brown sugar over the fruit.  Top with sour cream.  Cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate 3-4 
hours or overnight.  To serve, gently stir the fruit and spoon into glasses.  Fresh peaches, 
grapes and blueberries are necessary.  Frozen and canned fruits are not recommended but 
you can add other fruits such as plums, kiwi, raspberries, pitted cherries, etc.
