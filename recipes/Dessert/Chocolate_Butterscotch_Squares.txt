Chocolate Butterscotch Squares
1 c. sugar1 c. light con syrup1 c. peanut butter 6 c. rice krispies
1 c. each of chocolate and butterscotch chips
Combine sugar and corn syrup and microwave on high until sugar dissolves, about 3 
minutes.  Stir in peanut butter.   Add cereal and mix until cereal is coated with sugar peanut 
butter mix.  Press into a buttered 9 x 13 pan.  Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips in 
microwave on 70 percent power.  Spread over cereal mix.  Chill in refrigerator until firm, 15 
minutes.  Cut into bars.
