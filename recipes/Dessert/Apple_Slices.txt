Apple Slices

2 1/2 c. flour    
1 T. sugar      
1 t. salt
1 c. shortening
1 egg, separated        
milk
5 c. sliced apples
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 t. cinnamon

Sift together flour, sugar and salt.
Cut in shortening.
Put egg yolk into measuring cup and add milk to make 2/3 c.
Add to shortening mix.
Mix just enough to hold together.
Shape into ball.
Roll out half of dough to 15x11 rectangle, transfer to baking sheet.
Cover with apples.
Mix sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle over apples.
Roll out other half of dough for top crust.
Place over apples and pinch edges together.
Beat egg whites till stiff and spread on top of crust.
Bake at 400 for 40 minutes.
While hot drizzle glaze over top.
Glaze:1 c. powdered sugar with 2 T. lemon juice.
