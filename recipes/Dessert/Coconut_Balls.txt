Coconut Balls
Mix 1/2 c. butter, 3/4 c. sugar, 1/2 lb. chopped dates,  1 T. milk, 1 t. vanilla.  Cook over low 
heat until mushy.  Stirring constantly or it will burn.  Remove from heat.  Stir in 2 c. Rice 
Krispies and 1/2 c. nuts.  As soon as cool enough roll into balls and then roll in flaked 
coconut.
