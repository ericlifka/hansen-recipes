Garlic Shrimp Pasta

  1. 8 oz. angel hair pasta or spaghetti
  2. 4 cups spinach leaves
  3. 1 1/2 cups halved cherry tomato
  4. 3 tsp olive oil
  5. 1 med. onion, fine chopped
  6. 1 1/2 lb. shrimp
  7. 3 garlic, chopped
  8. 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  9. 1/2 cups white wine or chicken broth
  10. 1/4 cups chicken broth
  11. 1/4 tsp salt, pepper
  12. 3 Tbsp parsley
  13. 2 Tbsp butter
  • In pan cook pasta as directed on package.
  • Drain return to pan.
  • Stir in spinach, tomatoes and 2 tsp oil.
  • Cover to keep warm.
  • While pasta is cooking in 12‚Äù skillet heat 1 tsp oil over med high heat.
  • Add onion; cook and stir 1 minutes.
  • Add shrimp, garlic and pepper flakes; cook and stir 2 minutes.
  • Stir in wine broth, salt and pepper; cook 2 minutes until shrimp are pink and firm.
  • Remove from heat; stir in parsley and butter until melted.
  • Add shrimp mix to pasta mix; toss to mix.
  • I used 2 cups spinach and 3/4 cups tomato the first time to cut back on the amount of food.
