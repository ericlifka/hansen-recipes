Shrimp Pasta Primavera

  1. 4 oz. angel hair pasta
  2. 8 jumbo shrimp, peeled
  3. 6 asparagus spears, cut in 2” piece
  4. 12 garlic cloves
  5. 1/4 cups olive oil
  6. 1/2 cups fresh mushrooms
  7. 1/2 cups chicken broth
  8. 1 plum tomato, peeled, seeded, diced
  9. 1/4 tsp salt
  10. 1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
  11. 1 Tbsp each basil, oregano, thyme and parsley
  12. 1/4 cups Parmesan
  • cook pasta.
  • Meanwhile, sauté shrimp, asparagus and garlic in oil for 3-4 minutes or until shrimp turn pink.
  • Add mushrooms, broth, tomato, salt and pepper flakes; simmer, uncovered for 2 minutes.
  • Drain pasta.
  • Add pasta and seasonings to skillet; toss to coat; sprinkle with cheese.
