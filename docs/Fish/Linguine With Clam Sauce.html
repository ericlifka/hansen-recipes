Linguine With Clam Sauce

  1. 5 cloves garlic
  2. 1/4 cups olive oil
  3. 3 cans clams (save broth)
  4. 1/2 cups white wine
  5. 1/2-3/4 cups broth
  6. 1/2 cups parsley
  7. 1 tsp basil, oregano, pepper
  8. linguine
  • Heat oil (may use less oil) and add garlic and saut√©.
  • Add clams without broth and cook 3-4 minutes.
  • Add rest of stuff, simmer 15-20 minutes or until desired thickness.
  • The more broth and liquid you add the longer you need to simmer.
  • I also add some half and half if I want it to be more of a white sauce.
