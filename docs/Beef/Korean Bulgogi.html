Korean Bulgogi

  1. 28 oz. flank steak cut in strips (or can use chicken)
  2. 3 Tbsp sugar
  3. 9 Tbsp soy sauce
  4. 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  5. 2 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  6. 3 Tbsp green onion
  7. 1/2 tsp garlic
  • Mix sauce ingredients and pour over meat.
  • Let stand 15 minutes.
  • Cook on foil covered grill or broil in oven.
  • Serve with rice.
