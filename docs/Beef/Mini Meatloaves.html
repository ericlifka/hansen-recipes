Mini Meatloaves

  1. 1/2 lb. hamburger
  2. 1/3 cups uncooked oatmeal
  3. 1 egg
  4. 2 Tbsp ketchup, divided
  5. 2 Tbsp onion
  6. 1 Tbsp parsley or 1 1/2 tsp dried parsley flakes
  7. 1/4 tsp salt, pepper
  8. 1/4 cups cheddar cheese
  • In bowl combine beef, oats, egg, 1 Tbsp catsup, onion, parsley, salt and pepper.
  • Mix well.
  • Shape half of mix to form 4 x 2” rectangles.
  • Sprinkle 2 Tbsp cheese over each.
  • Divide remaining mix in half.
  • Place each half over cheese; press to seal in cheese and form loaf.
  • Place in 8” pan.
  • Bake at 350 for 25 minutes, top with cheese and ketchup.
  • Return to oven until cheese is melted.
