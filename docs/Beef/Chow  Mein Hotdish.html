Chow Mein Hotdish

  1. 1 1/2 lbs.. hamburger
  2. onion
  3. 1 cups celery
  4. 2 cans cr. of mushroom soup
  5. 1 can chicken rice soup
  6. 1 can Veg all vegetables, undrained
  7. 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  8. 2 cups chow mein noodles
  9. almonds, optional
  10. 1 can mushrooms, optional
  • Brown beef, onion, and celery.
  • Add rest and bake at 325 for 1 hour.
  • Add the noodles the last 15 minutes of cooking.
