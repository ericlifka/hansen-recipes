Italian Spaghetti

  1. 1 1/4 lb. hamburger
  2. onion
  3. garlic
  4. 2 envelopes sauce mix
  5. 2 cans tomato sauce
  6. 2 cans tomato paste
  7. 3 cups water (mix water/red wine)
  8. 1 can mushrooms
  9. 1 tsp oregano leaves
  10. 1 tsp basil leaves
  11. spaghetti
  • Cook beef, drain.
  • Add sauce mix and remaining ingredients except spaghetti.
  • Simmer until thickened about 30 minutes.
