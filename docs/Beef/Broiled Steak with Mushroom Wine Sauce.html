Broiled Steak with Mushroom Wine Sauce

  1. 2 boneless rib steaks
  2. 2 Tbsp veggie oil
  3. 1 cups mushrooms, sliced
  4. 2 Tbsp onion
  5. 4 Tbsp red wine
  6. 1/2 cups sour cream
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Heat oil.
  • Add onions and mushrooms and saut√© until golden.
  • Add wine.
  • Cook for minute.
  • Add sour cream and cook until heated.
  • Remove from heat, keep warm.
  • Broil steak.
  • Pour sauce over steak.
