Mexican Layer Casserole

  1. 1 lb. hamburger
  2. 1 onion
  3. 1 can refried beans
  4. 1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
  5. 1 pkg. enchilada sauce mix
  6. 1/2 cups water
  7. 8 oz. tortilla chips
  8. 4 oz. cheese
  9. 1/2 cups black olives
  10. 2 cups shredded lettuce
  11. 1 tomato
  • Cook beef.
  • Add onion.
  • Stir in beans, tomato sauce, sauce mix and water.
  • Cook 5-10 minutes until hot and bubbly.
  • Place crushed chips in 12 x 8 dish.
  • Spoon hot mix over.
  • Sprinkle with cheese and olives.
  • Bake about 20-30 minutes at 350.
  • Top with lettuce and tomato.
