Thai Beef Stir Fry

  1. 2 Tbsp cornstarch
  2. 3/4 cups water
  3. 2 Tbsp plus 1 1/2 tsp chunky peanut butter
  4. 4 Tbsp soy sauce, divided
  5. 1 1/2 lb. top sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  6. 1 1/2 tsp garlic
  7. 1/4 tsp pepper
  8. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  9. 1 each green, red, and yellow pepper, julienned
  10. 8 oz. bamboo shoots, drained
  11. 1/2 cups julienned carrot
  12. 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • In a small bowl, combine cornstarch and water until smooth.
  • Stir in peanut butter and 3 Tbsp soy sauce; set aside.
  • In a large skillet or wok, stir fry the beef, garlic, pepper and remaining soy sauce in oil until meat is no longer pink; remove and keep warm.
  • Add the peppers, bamboo shoots, carrot and pepper flakes; stir fry 2-3 minutes or until tender.
  • Stir cornstarch mix; add to the pan.
  • Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 1 minute or until thickened.
  • Return beef mix to the pan.
  • Serve with rice.
