Salsa

  1. 28 oz. tomato
  2. 3 cups celery
  3. 1 cucumber, peeled, seeded
  4. 19 oz pureed chick peas
  5. 1 cups cilantro
  6. 4 oz. chopped green chilies
  7. 1/2 cups green onion
  8. 2 garlic
  9. 1/2 Tbsp sugar
  10. 1 Tbsp vinegar
  11. 1 tsp cumin
  12. 1 tsp oregano
  13. 1 Tbsp hot sauce
  14. 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • Mix together, cover and refrigerate.
