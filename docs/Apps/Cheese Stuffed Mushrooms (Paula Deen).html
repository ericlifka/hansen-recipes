Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms (Paula Deen)

  1. 24 fresh mushrooms, stems removed
  2. 10 oz. frozen chopped spinach, drained
  3. 4 oz. cream cheese
  4. 2-4 oz. feta
  5. 1/2 cups finely chopped green onion
  6. 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  7. 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  8. 1 cups Parmesan
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • Wipe mushroom caps clean with damp paper towel.
  • Thaw spinach, squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
  • In bowl combine all ingredients except mushrooms and Parmesan.
  • Mix well.
  • Fill mushroom caps with mix and place on cookie sheet.
  • Sprinkle parmesan on top.
  • Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  • When I made this, I used 1 pkg. mushrooms, 1/4-1/2 a box of spinach, 4 oz. feta and cream cheese as Cecelia likes feta.
