Nacho Cheese Dip

  1. 1 lb. each hamburger, hot sausage
  2. 2 lb. Velveeta
  3. 4 cans nacho cheese soup
  4. 2 cans cheddar cheese soup
  5. 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  • Brown hamburger and prepare taco seasoning according to package.
  • Brown sausage and drain.
  • Cut velveeta into chunks.
  • In crock pot layer meat on bottom, velveeta and cheese soups.
  • Cook on high 2-4 hours, low for 4-6.
  • Stir occasionally.
  • Serve with nacho chips.
