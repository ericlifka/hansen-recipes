Olive Garden Artichoke Dip

  1. 1 can artichoke hearts chopped
  2. 1/2 cups chopped spinach
  3. 8 oz. cream cheese
  4. 1/2 cups Parmesan
  5. 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  6. Dash salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • Boil spinach and hearts in a cup of water for 10 min.
  • Drain.
  • Heat cream cheese and add spinach and hearts.
  • Stir.
  • Mix in rest of stuff.
