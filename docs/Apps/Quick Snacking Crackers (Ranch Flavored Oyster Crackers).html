Quick Snacking Crackers (Ranch Flavored Oyster Crackers)

  1. 3/4 cups oil
  2. 1 10z. package milk base ranch dressing
  3. 1/4 cups grated parmesan cheese
  4. 16 oz. oyster crackers
  • Combine first 3 ingredients.
  • Toss with crackers.
  • Let stand several hours, stirring occasionally.
  • Stir in tightly covered container.
