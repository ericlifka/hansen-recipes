Party pretzels

  1. 32 oz mini pretzels
  2. 1 pkg. hidden valley ranch
  3. 1/2 bottle orville redenbacher popcorn oil
  4. 1-3 Tbsp dill weed
  5. 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  6. 1 Tbsp onion powder
  • Mix oil and dry ingredients.
  • Dill, garlic powder, and onion powder are to taste (can do more or less).
  • Mix pretzels with stuff in trash bag, toss, spread on cookie sheet and dry overnight.
