Calorie Commando Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. 1 box frozen spinach, thawed
  2. 1 cups sour cream
  3. 1/2 cups Parmesan
  4. 1 cups mozzarella
  5. 8 oz. reduced fat cream cheese, softened
  6. 4 garlic cloves
  7. 1/2 tsp pepper
  8. 1 tsp hot pepper sauce
  9. 14 oz. can artichoke hearts
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • Squeeze liquid from spinach, place in food processor with sour cream, Parmesan, 1/2 cups mozzarella, garlic, pepper and hot sauce.
  • Process until blended.
  • Place in 1 qt. baking dish, top with remaining mozzarella, and artichokes.
  • Bake 30 min.
