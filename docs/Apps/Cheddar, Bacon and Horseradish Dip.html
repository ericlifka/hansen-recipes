Cheddar, Bacon and Horseradish Dip

  1. 2 8 oz packages of cream cheese, cut in small pieces
  2. 8 oz cheddar cheese,cut in pieces
  3. 1/2 cups half and half
  4. 1/2 cups finely chopped scallions
  5. 1/4 cups prepared horseradish
  6. 3 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  7. 5 slices cooked bacon or
  8. 2 Tbsp bacos (bacon bits)
  9. 2 Tbsp worcestershire
  10. Salt & Pepper
  • Spray crock pot with cooking spray.
  • Add the two cheeses and half and half.
  • Cover and cook undisturbed for 1 hour.
  • Add the remaining ingredients and stir well into the cheese mix.
  • Cover and cook for 1-2 hours more.
  • Stir well and serve.
  • I used a 1 1/2 qt. cooker.
