Baked Pineapple

  1. 20 oz. can crushed pineapple
  2. 1 cups sugar
  3. 2 Tbsp cornstarch
  4. 2 eggs, slightly beat
  5. 1/4 cups water
  6. Butter
  7. Cinnamon
  • Mix cornstarch with water.
  • Add other ingredients except butter and cinnamon.
  • Stir, place in shallow casserole.
  • Bake at 350 for 30 min.
  • Remove; stir.
  • Dot with butter, sprinkle with cinnamon.
  • Bake another 30 minutes.
  • Can be served hot, cold or room temp.
