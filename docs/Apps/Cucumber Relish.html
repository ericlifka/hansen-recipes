Cucumber Relish

  1. 18 large cukes
  2. 2 green peppers
  3. 1 bunch celery (4-5)
  4. 6 large onions
  5. 2 red peppers or 1 big jar of pimento
  • Grind cukes and onions.
  • Cut up celery.
  • Drain stuff.
  • Put 1/3-1/2 cups salt.
  • Soak overnight.
  • Drain well.
  • Drain pimento.
  • Could take several hours.
  • Brine is 1 cups vinegar, 1 cups sugar and 1 tsp turmeric.
  • Boil all together for at least 10 minutes.
  • Cook lids on stove.
  • Fill jars.
  • Microwave jar and then seal immediately.
