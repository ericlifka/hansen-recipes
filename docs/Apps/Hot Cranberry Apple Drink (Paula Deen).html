Hot Cranberry Apple Drink (Paula Deen)

  1. 1/4 cups brown sugar
  2. 3 cinnamon sticks
  3. 1 Tbsp whole cloves (in tea ball)
  4. 8 cups cranberry juice
  5. 6 cups apple juice or cider
  • I used 1 64 oz. thing of cranberry juice, and apple cider.
  • Put all in a big crock pot, or pan on stovetop.
  • Heat on low for a few hours then serve.
