Pork Tenderloin (Paula Deen)

  1. 1 1/2- 2 lb. pork tenderloin
  2. Marinade:
  3. 1/4 cups soy sauce
  4. 2 Tbsp red wine
  5. 1 Tbsp honey
  6. 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  7. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  8. 1 tsp ginger
  9. 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  10. 2 green onion tops
  • Combine marinade and whisk.
  • Pour over tenderloin and marinade overnight.
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • Bake tenderloin 45 minutes or until meat is 145 degrees.
  • Let meat rest 10 minutes before cutting into piece.
  • Drizzle with a small amount of the meat juices from cooking.
