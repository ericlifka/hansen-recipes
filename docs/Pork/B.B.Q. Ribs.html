B.B.Q. Ribs

  1. 1 cups chili sauce
  2. 1 Tbsp worcestershire
  3. 1/2 cups water
  4. 1 Tbsp oil
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 2 Tbsp vinegar
  7. minced onion
  8. 1/4 tsp tabasco
  9. 1/4 cups lemon juice
  10. 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  11. 1/4 tsp paprika
  12. 1/2 cups ketchup
  13. Ribs
  • Mix all but the ribs together.
  • Put sauce on ribs.
  • Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 325.
