Paula Deen’s Potatoes

  1. 3 potatoes
  2. 1/2 cups mayo
  3. 1/2 tsp hot sauce
  4. 1/4 tsp onion salt
  5. 1/2 tsp steak seasoning
  6. 1/8 tsp peppe
  7. 2 cups cornbread dressing or stuffing
  • Cut potato into 6 wedges.
  • Mix mayo with spices.
  • Roll in mayo and then dressing.
  • Place in greased dish and bake at 375 for 45-50 minutes.
