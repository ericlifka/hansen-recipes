Spanish Rice

  1. 1 can stewed tomatoes
  2. 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  3. 2 tsp chili powder
  4. 1 Tbsp butter
  5. 1 1/4 cups white rice, uncooked
  6. 3/4 tsp oregano
  7. 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  • In medium pan combine all ingredients.
  • Bring to boil; reduce heat.
  • Cover and simmer 25 minutes or until rice is done.
