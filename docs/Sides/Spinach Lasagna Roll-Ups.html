Spinach Lasagna Roll-Ups

  1. 10 oz frozen chopped spinach, thawed, squeezed dry
  2. 4 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
  3. 8 oz. 2% cottage cheese
  4. 3/4 cups Parm cheese, divided
  5. 1 egg, lightly beaten
  6. 6 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  7. 30 oz. seasoned tomato sauce or spg. sauce
  • Combine spinach, mozzarella, cottage cheese, 1/2 cups parm and egg.
  • Spread a heaping 1/3 cupful over each noodle.
  • Roll up and secure with toothpick.
  • Place seam side down in a 11 x7 x 2 baking dish, sprayed with PAM.
  • Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  • Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking.
  • Pour tomato sauce over roll-ups.
  • Cover and bake at 350 for 33-38 minutes or until bubbly.
  • Sprinkle with remaining parm cheese.
