Grilled Stuffed Peppers

  1. 4 medium green peppers
  2. 1/4 cups veggie oil
  3. 1 lb. Monterey jack cheese, shredded
  4. 2 Tbsp butter
  5. tabasco
  • Wash and dry peppers.
  • Slice in half lengthwise, remove core and seeds.
  • Coat outside skin with thin coating of oil.
  • Stuff pepper halves with cheese, filling not quite full.
  • Add a dab of butter, and dash of tabasco to taste.
  • Place peppers on lightly oiled aluminum foil.
  • Place on hot coals and grill 20-30 minutes.
  • Can also be done in oven.
