Swiss Vegetable Medley

  1. 16 oz. broccoli, carrot, cauliflower combo, thawed and drained
  2. 1/3 cups sour cream
  3. 1/4 tsp pepper
  4. 4 oz. pimento (opt.)
  5. 1 can cr. mushroom soup
  6. 4 oz. Swiss cheese
  7. 1 can french fried onion
  • Combine veggies, soup 1/2 cups cheese, sour cream, pepper, pimento and 1/2 can onions in 1 qt. casserole.
  • Bake covered at 350 for 30 minutes. Top with remaining cheese and onions.
  • Bake uncovered 5 minutes longer.
