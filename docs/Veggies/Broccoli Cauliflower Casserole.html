Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

  1. 20 oz. frozen broccoli
  2. 10 oz. cauliflower
  3. 1 can cr. of celery
  4. 8 oz. cheese whiz
  5. 1 can fried onion rings
  • Cook broccoli and cauliflower separately.
  • Drain and place in buttered dish.
  • Combine soup and cheese.
  • Pour over veggies and bake uncovered 30 min. at 325 or 350.
  • Add onion rings for last 10 minutes.
