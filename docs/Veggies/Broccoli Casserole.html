Broccoli Casserole

  1. 20 oz. broccoli or 1-2 bunch fresh (steamed)
  2. 8 oz. velveeta
  3. 1 onion
  4. 1 can cr. of mushroom
  5. 1 cups mayo
  6. cracker crumbs or fried onion
  • Combine all but cracker crumbs.
  • Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
  • Top with crumbs or onion and reheat long enough to toast.
