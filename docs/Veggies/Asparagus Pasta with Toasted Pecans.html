Asparagus Pasta with Toasted Pecans

  1. 16 oz. penne pasta
  2. 1 bunch asparagus (1 lb.)
  3. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  4. 1 Tbsp garlic
  5. 1 cups chicken broth
  6. 1 tsp salt and pepper
  7. 3 Tbsp basil
  8. 3/4 cups Parmesan, divided
  9. 2 Tbsp butter
  10. 1 cups pecan halves, toasted and divided
  11. 1 red pepper, seeded, chopped
  • Prepare pasta, rinse and drain.
  • Cut asparagus into 1 1/2” pieces.
  • Sauté asparagus in hot oil over medium heat for 4 minutes.
  • Stir in red pepper and garlic, cook, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes.
  • Stir in chicken broth.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes until asparagus is crisp tender.
  • Stir in salt and pepper.
  • Toss together pasta, asparagus mix, basil, 1/2 cups cheese, butter and 1/2 cups pecans.
  • Sprinkle evenly with remaining cheese and pecans.
