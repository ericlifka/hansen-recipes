Herbed Artichoke Cheese Tortellini

  1. 1 can italian diced tomatoes
  2. 1 can artichoke hearts
  3. 9 oz. refrigerated tortellini
  4. 1 cups onion
  5. 1/4 cups parsley
  6. 2-4 tsp dried basil
  7. 1 tsp garlic
  8. 1/4 tsp oregano
  9. 1/8 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  10. 1/4 cups olive oil
  11. 2oz can sliced olives, drained
  12. 1/4 tsp salt
  13. 1/8 cups grated Parmesan
  • Drain tomatoes, reserving 2/3 cups juice.
  • Drain artichokes, reserving 3/4 cups liquid; chop and set aside.
  • Cook tortellini.
  • In large skillet sate the onions, parsley, basil, garlic, oregano and pepper flakes in oil for 4-5 minutes or until onions are tender.
  • Add the reserved tomatoes, tomato juice and artichoke liquid.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 10-12 minutes or until slightly thickened.
  • Drain tortellini; add to tomato mix.
  • Stir in olives, salt and reserved artichokes; heat through.
  • Sprinkle with parmesan.
