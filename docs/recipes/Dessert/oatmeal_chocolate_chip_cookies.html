Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • 1 tsp soda, salt, and vanilla
  • 1 cups shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cups each brown sugar and white sugar
  • 1 tsp hot water
  • 1 cups nuts
  • 1 pkg. chocolate chips
  1. Mix all together and bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
