Hidden Treasure Cupcakes

  1. Make filling and set aside:
  2. 8 oz. cream cheese
  3. 1 egg plus
  4. 1 egg yolk (save white)
  5. 1/2 cups sugar
  6. pinch of salt
  7. 1 6 oz pkg. chocolate chips
  8. Cupcake batter:
  9. 1 1/2 cups flour
  10. 1 cups sugar
  11. 1/4 cups cocoa
  12. 3/4 cups water
  13. 3/4 tsp soda
  14. 1/2 tsp salt
  15. 1/3 cups veggie oil
  16. 1 egg white
  17. 1 Tbsp vinegar
  18. 1 tsp vanilla
  • Mix batter ingredients together and mix well.
  • Fill about 20 paper muffin cups 1/3 cups full.
  • Top each with heaping teaspoon of filling.
  • Sprinkle generously with white sugar and chopped nuts.
  • Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
