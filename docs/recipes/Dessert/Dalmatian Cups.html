Dalmatian Cups

  • 30 chocolate wafer cookies
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 pkg. (4 serving size) vanilla instant pudding
  • 2 cups cool whip
  1. Break cookies with rolling pin.
  2. Pour milk into bowl.
  3. Add pudding mix.
  4. Beat 1-2 minutes or until well blended.
  5. Let stand 5 minutes or until thickened.
  6. Stir in cool whip and crushed cookies.
  7. Spoon into 8 dishes.
  8. Refrigerate.
  9. To make dalmatians use marshmallows for eyes, mints for nose, black licorice for mouth and cookies for ears.
