Poke and Pour Cake

  • 1 pkg. white cake mix
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cups boiling water
  • 1 pkg. cherry, strawberry or raspberry gelatin
  • 1/2 cups cold water
  1. Prepare cake mix following package directions with egg whites and 1 1/2 cups water.
  2. Pour into greased 9 x 13 pan.
  3. Bake according to package directions.
  4. Cool 15 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare gelatin, following package directions.
  6. With fork poke holes into cake at 1/2 inch intervals.
  7. Gently pour gelatin evenly over cake.
  8. Refrigerate 3-4 hours before serving.
  9. Serve with whip cream, if desired.
