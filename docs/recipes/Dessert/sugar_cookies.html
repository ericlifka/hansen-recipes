Sugar Cookies

  • 1 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 cups white sugar
  • 1 cups butter
  • 1 cups veggie oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 cups +4 Tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp salt, soda and cream of tartar
  1. Cream together butter, oil and sugars until fluffy.
  2. Beat in eggs.
  3. Add rest of ingredients.
  4. Roll into small balls.
  5. Place on greased cookie sheet.
  6. Press down cookies with glass dipped in sugar.
  7. Bake at 375 for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
