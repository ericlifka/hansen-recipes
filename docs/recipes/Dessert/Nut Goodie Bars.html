Nut Goodie Bars

  1. 6 oz. package of chocolate chips
  2. butterscotch chips
  3. 1 cups peanut butter
  4. 1/2 cups butter
  5. 1/4 cups milk
  6. 2 Tbsp vanilla pudding (not instant)
  7. 3 cups powdered sugar
  8. 1/2 Tbsp maple flavoring
  9. 1 cups salted peanuts
  • First Layer:
  • Melt chocolate chips, butterscotch chips, and peanut butter.
  • Spread half of the melted mix in greased 9 x 13 pan.
  • Set in refrigerator.
  • Second layer:
  • melt butter, add milk, and vanilla pudding.
  • Bring to a simmer, remove from stove, add powdered sugar and maple flavoring.
  • Beat until smooth, spread over the first chocolate mixture and let set in refrigerator to harden.
  • Third layer:
  • add 1 cups salted peanuts to chocolate mix that is left over from the 1st layer.
  • Spread over top of the 2nd layer and set in refrigerator.
