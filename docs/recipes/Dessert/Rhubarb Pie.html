Rhubarb Pie

  1. 1 premade Pie crust
  2. 4 cups rhubarb (remove long strands)
  3. 1 2/3 cups sugar
  4. 1/3 cups flour
  5. dash salt
  6. butter
  • Mix that together and let stand 15 minutes.
  • If you use more than 4 cups be sure to add more sugar and flour.
  • Prepare pastry (or buy crust) Line a 9‚Äù pie plate with pastry.
  • Fill with rhubarb mix.
  • Dot with 2 Tbsp butter.
  • Adjust top crust, cutting slits for escape of steam.
  • Seal; flute.
  • Bake at 400 for 50 minutes.
  • (I used Pillsbury crust from the refrigerator section that you roll out.)
