Sweet Potato Pie

  • 1 1/4 cups cooked mashed sweet potato
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cups french vanilla instant pudding
  • 3/4 cups evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 Tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp vanilla
  • 1 unbaked pie shell
  1. In bowl, combine all ingredients except the pie shell.
  2. Beat at medium speed until well blended.
  3. Spread into pie shell.
  4. Bake at 450 for 10 minutes, reduce temperature to 350 and bake 40 minutes longer or until set.
  5. Cool and garnish with whipped cream.
