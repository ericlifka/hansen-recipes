Chocolate Zucchini Cake

  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 1/2 cups veggie oil
  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cups buttermilk (1 Tbsp lemon juice and milk if you donâ€™t have buttermilk)
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 cups cocoa
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 cups nuts
  • 2 cups diced unpeeled zucchini
  1. Cream butter and oil with sugar.
  2. Add eggs, vanilla, and buttermilk.
  3. Add flour, cocoa, soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and cloves.
  4. Add the nuts and zucchini, blending well.
  5. Pour batter in 9 x 13 pan.
  6. Top with 1/2 cups chocolate chips and 1/2 cups nuts.
  7. Bake at 325 for 40-45 minutes or until done.
