Nellie & Joe’s Key Lime Pie (I think this is the one Grandma uses)

  1. graham cracker crust
  2. 14 oz. sweetened condensed milk
  3. 3 eggs
  4. 1/2 cups lime juice.
  • Combine milk, yolks, and juice.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Pour into pie shell and bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
  • Allow to stand 10 minutes before refrigerating.
  • Top with whip cream.
