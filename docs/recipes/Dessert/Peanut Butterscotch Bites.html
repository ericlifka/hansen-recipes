Peanut Butterscotch Bites

  1. 1 cups butterscotch
  2. 1/2 cups peanut butter
  3. 3 cups rice krispies
  4. PAM
  • In 3 qt. saucepan combine butterscotch morsels and peanut butter.
  • Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until smooth.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Stir in rice krispies mixing until well coated.
  • Press mix into 9 x 9 pan coated with PAM.
  • Chill until firm.
