Jumbo Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. 3/4 cups butter
  2. 1/2 cups peanut butter
  3. 1 cups sugar and brown sugar
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1/4 cups milk
  6. 1 tsp vanilla
  7. 2 cups flour
  8. 1 tsp each baking soda, salt and cinnamon
  9. 1 1/2 cups oatmeal
  10. 1 cups chocolate chips
  • Cream butter, peanut butter and sugars until smooth and creamy.
  • Add eggs, milk and vanilla and blend well.
  • Add flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
  • Stir into creamed mix.
  • Blend in oatmeal and chips.
  • Drop by tablespoonfuls (or smaller) onto greased baking sheets about 2‚Äù apart.
  • Bake at 350 for 13 minutes.
