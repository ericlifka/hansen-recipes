Lemon Filling that Melissa likes

  1. 1 egg, beaten
  2. 1/3 cups sugar
  3. 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  4. 2 Tbsp butter, cubed
  5. 1 cups heavy whipping cream
  6. 2 tsp sugar
  • Combine egg, sugar, lemon juice and butter.
  • Bring to a boil over medium heat; cook and stir for 5-7 minutes or until mix is thick enough to coat a metal spoon.
  • I used medium/low heat.
  • Sprinkle top of cream puff with powdered sugar.
