Grasshopper Pie

  • 18 chocolate wafers
  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 1/2 cups milk20 marshmallows
  • 1/2 pt. whipping cream
  • 1 oz. creme de menthe
  • 1 oz. creme de cocoa
  1. Make crust of wafers and butter.
  2. Melt marshmallows in milk and cool.
  3. Beat cream and add liqueurs.
  4. Combine marshmallow mix and cream.
  5. Place in shell and chill.
