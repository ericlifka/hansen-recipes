Peanut Butterscotch Bites

  • 1 cups butterscotch
  • 1/2 cups peanut butter
  • 3 cups rice krispies
  • PAM
  1. In 3 qt. saucepan combine butterscotch morsels and peanut butter.
  2. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until smooth.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in rice krispies mixing until well coated.
  5. Press mix into 9 x 9 pan coated with PAM.
  6. Chill until firm.
