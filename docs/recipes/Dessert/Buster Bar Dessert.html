Buster Bar Dessert

  1. 1 lb. oreos
  2. 1/2 gallon ice cream
  3. 2 cups powdered sugar
  4. 1 1/2 cups evaporated milk
  5. 2/3 cups chocolate chips
  6. 1 cups butter
  7. 1 tsp vanilla
  8. 1 1/2 cups peanuts
  • Crush cookies and mix with 1/2 cups butter like pie crust.
  • Put in a 9 x 13 pan.
  • Refrigerate 1 hour.
  • Soften ice cream and spoon over crust.
  • Pack well and put in freezer.
  • Combine sugar, milk, chips and 1/2 cups butter and boil 8 minutes stirring constantly.
  • Remove from heat and add vanilla.
  • Cool.
  • Sprinkle peanuts over ice cream and put cooled sauce over.
  • Freeze and serve when firm.
