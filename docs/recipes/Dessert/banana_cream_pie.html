Banana Cream Pie

  • 1 pie crust
  • 2-3 bananas
  • instant vanilla or banana cream pudding
  1. Bake pie crust.
  2. Make pudding--directions are on side of box probably need 2 small boxes.
  3. Slice bananas and put on cooled pie crust.
  4. Pour pudding over bananas.
  5. Refrigerate for several hours.
  6. Top with whipped cream.
