Delicious Dessert

  • flour
  • butter
  • pecans
  • walnuts
  • cream cheese
  • powdered sugar
  • cool whip
  • 2 boxes coconut cream pudding or instant chocolate pudding
  • milk
  • toasted coconut
  1. Layer 1:
  2. 1 cups flour
  3. 1/2 cups butter
  4. 1/2 cups pecans, chopped or walnuts.
  5. Mix and pat in 9 x 13 pan.
  6. Bake 15 minutes at 350.
  7. Layer 2:
  8. 8 oz. cream cheese
  9. 1 cups powdered sugar
  10. 1 cups cool whip.
  11. Mix together and spread on cooled crust.
  12. Layer 3:
  13. Cook 2 boxes coconut cream pudding with 3 cups milk.
  14. Make first so it is cool.
  15. Layer 4:
  16. Put remaining cool whip on top of 3rd layer.
  17. Sprinkle with toasted coconut.
  18. Notes:
  19. To make it chocolate do all the same but layer 3 should be 2 boxes of instant chocolate pudding with 3 cups milk.
  20. Omit toasted coconut on top.
