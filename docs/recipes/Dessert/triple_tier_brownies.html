Triple Tier Brownies

  • 1 pkg. fudge brownie mix (13 x 9 pan size)
  • 1 pkg. (11 1/2 oz.) milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cups peanut butter
  • 3 cups rice cereal
  • 16 oz. can cream cheese frosting
  • 1 cups salted peanuts, chopped
  1. Prepare brownie mix according to package directions, used greased 9 x 13 pan.
  2. Cool.
  3. In a large saucepan combine chips and peanut butter.
  4. Cook over low heat 4-5 minutes or until blended, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in cereal; set aside.
  6. Spread frosting on brownies.
  7. Sprinkle with peanuts.
  8. Spread with peanut butter mix.
  9. Chill for 30 minutes or until set before cutting.
  10. Store in refrigerator.
