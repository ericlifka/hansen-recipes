Brownie Trifle

  1. 1 pkg. brownie
  2. vanilla ice cream
  3. 1/2 cups pecans, coconut
  4. 1 jar caramel topping
  • Bake and cut brownies into squares.
  • Bake pecans and coconut at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
  • Layer 2-3 times in bowl brownies, ice cream, caramel, and pecans.
  • May want smaller amts.
