Brownie Trifle

  • 1 pkg. brownie
  • vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cups pecans, coconut
  • 1 jar caramel topping
  1. Bake and cut brownies into squares.
  2. Bake pecans and coconut at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Layer 2-3 times in bowl brownies, ice cream, caramel, and pecans.
  4. May want smaller amts.
