Rhubarb Cake

  1. 1/2 cups shortening
  2. 1 1/2 cups sugar
  3. 1 cups sour milk
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1 tsp vanilla
  6. 3 cups chopped rhubarb
  7. 2 cups flour
  8. 1 tsp soda
  9. 1/2 tsp salt
  • Mix together.
  • Add rhubarb last.
  • Mix some sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over top.
  • Bake in 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 45 min.
