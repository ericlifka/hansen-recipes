Rhubarb Cake

  • 1/2 cups shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cups sour milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 cups chopped rhubarb
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  1. Mix together.
  2. Add rhubarb last.
  3. Mix some sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over top.
  4. Bake in 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 45 min.
