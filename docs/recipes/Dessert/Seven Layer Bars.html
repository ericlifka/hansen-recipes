Seven Layer Bars

  1. butter
  2. graham crackers
  3. shredded coconut
  4. chocolat chips
  5. butterscotch chips
  6. sweetened condensed milk
  7. chopped nuts
  • 1st layer--1/2 cups butter melted in 9 x 13 in pan with 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2nd layer--crushed graham crackers-- 1 1/2 c.
  • 3rd layer--1 cups coconut
  • 4th layer--1 6 oz. package chocolate chips
  • 5th layer--6 oz. package butterscotch chips
  • 6th layer--1 large can sweetened condensed milk
  • 7th layer--1 cups chopped nuts
  • Bake 25 minutes at 350.
