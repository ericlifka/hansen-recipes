Indian Pudding

  1. 6 Tbsp unsalted butter
  2. 3/4 tsp allspice and mace
  3. 1/2 cups + 3 Tbsp dark brown sugar
  4. 2-12 oz. cans evaporated milk
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 1 tsp baking soda
  8. 1 tsp vanilla
  9. 1 cups yellow cornmeal
  • Mix in pan butter, allspice and mace for 2 minutes.
  • Stir in brown sugar until dissolved.
  • Add milk and salt.
  • Cook until milk is ready to boil.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Add soda, vanilla and cornmeal until it is well blended.
  • Add frothy eggs.
  • Pour in casserole dish.
  • Bake at 350 for 45-55 minutes.
