Grasshopper Pie

  1. 18 chocolate wafers
  2. 1/2 cups butter
  3. 1/2 cups milk20 marshmallows
  4. 1/2 pt. whipping cream
  5. 1 oz. creme de menthe
  6. 1 oz. creme de cocoa
  • Make crust of wafers and butter.
  • Melt marshmallows in milk and cool.
  • Beat cream and add liqueurs.
  • Combine marshmallow mix and cream.
  • Place in shell and chill.
