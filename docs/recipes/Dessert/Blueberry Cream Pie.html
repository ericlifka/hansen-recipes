Blueberry Cream Pie

  1. 1 cups sour cream
  2. 5 Tbsp flour, divided
  3. 3/4 cups sugar
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1/ 4 tsp salt
  6. 2 1/2 cups blueberries
  7. baked 9‚Äù pie crust
  8. 1 1/2 tsp butter
  9. 5 Tbsp pecans, chop
  • Combine sour cream, 2 Tbsp flour, sugar, egg and salt; beat 5 minutes or until mixture is smooth.
  • Fold in berries; pour filling into pie shell.
  • Bake at 400 for 25 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, combine 3 Tbsp flour, butter and pecans; sprinkle over filling and bake an additional 10 minutes.
  • Cool, then chill before serving.
