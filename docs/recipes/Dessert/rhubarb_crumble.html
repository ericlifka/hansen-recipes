Rhubarb Crumble

  • 6 cups rhubarb sliced 1/2‚Äù thick
  • 1/4 cups honey
  • 1/3 cups oatmeal and flour
  • PAM
  • 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
  • 3 Tbsp butter cut into small pieces
  1. Combine rhubarb and honey together in bowl, toss to coat.
  2. I added a little more honey to this.
  3. Spray pan with PAM and add rhubarb.
  4. In food processor put oats, flour and brown sugar.
  5. Pulse a couple of times to mix.
  6. Add butter and process until it resembles coarse meal.
  7. Sprinkle mix over rhubarb.
  8. Bake at 375 for 40 minutes or until rhubarb is tender.
  9. I think I doubled the topping since we like alot of topping.
