Rosettes (Old Favorite)

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups milk and flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  1. Beat eggs lightly.
  2. Add sugar, add rest of ingredients and beat until blended.
  3. Heat iron in oil and blot on paper towels, dip into batter being careful not to let batter run over top of the form.
  4. Dip the iron into hot fat with the batter sticking to form.
  5. Cook until nicely browned.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Cool well and dip in powdered sugar.
