Rosettes (Old Favorite)

  1. 3 eggs
  2. 2 tsp sugar
  3. 1/4 tsp salt
  4. 1 1/2 cups milk and flour
  5. 2 tsp vanilla
  • Beat eggs lightly.
  • Add sugar, add rest of ingredients and beat until blended.
  • Heat iron in oil and blot on paper towels, dip into batter being careful not to let batter run over top of the form.
  • Dip the iron into hot fat with the batter sticking to form.
  • Cook until nicely browned.
  • Drain on paper towels.
  • Cool well and dip in powdered sugar.
