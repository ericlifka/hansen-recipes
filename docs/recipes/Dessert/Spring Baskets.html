Spring Baskets

  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 4 cups chow mein noodles
  • jelly beans
  1. Combine marshmallows and butter in 3 qt. saucepan.
  2. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until marshmallows are melted (6-8 minutes).
  3. Stir in noodles until very well coated.
  4. Press mix onto bottom and up sides of buttered muffin pan with buttered fingers.
  5. Refrigerate at least 2 hours or until firm.
  6. Remove from cups; fill with candy.
  7. Store in container for up to 2 days.
