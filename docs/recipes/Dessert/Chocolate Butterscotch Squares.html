Chocolate Butterscotch Squares

  1. 1 cups sugar
  2. 1 cups light corn syrup
  3. 1 cups peanut butter
  4. 6 cups rice krispies
  5. 1 cups each of chocolate and butterscotch chips
  • Combine sugar and corn syrup and microwave on high until sugar dissolves, about 3 minutes.
  • Stir in peanut butter.
  • Add cereal and mix until cereal is coated with sugar peanut butter mix.
  • Press into a buttered 9 x 13 pan.
  • Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips in microwave on 70 percent power.
  • Spread over cereal mix.
  • Chill in refrigerator until firm, 15 minutes.
  • Cut into bars.
