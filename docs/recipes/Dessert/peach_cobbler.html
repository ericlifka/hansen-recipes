Peach Cobbler

  • Filling:
  • 4 cups peaches
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 drops vanilla
  • Topping:
  • 3 oz. sugar
  • 2 oz. butter
  • salt
  • cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 5 oz flour
  1. Mix together filling ingredients and put in pan.
  2. Putting topping ingredients in food processor and blend.
  3. Put over peaches.
  4. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
