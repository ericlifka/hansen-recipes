Butterscotch Squares

  1. 6 Tbsp butter, divided
  2. 10 1/2 oz. mini marshmallows
  3. 6 cups rice cereal
  4. 1/4 cups milk
  5. 12 oz. butterscotch morsels
  6. 1 cups honey roasted peanuts
  • Grease a 9 x 13 pan.
  • Melt 4 Tbsp butter in pot.
  • Add marshmallow and cook over low heat until melted and smooth.
  • Remove from heat and add cereal.
  • Stir to coat.
  • Pat into pan, oiling your fingers with oil to prevent sticking.
  • In another pan melt 2 Tbsp mutter and add butterscotch morsels.
  • Stir until soft.
  • Add the milk stirring until smooth.
  • Mix in nuts and coat well.
  • Pour over cereal and smooth the top.
