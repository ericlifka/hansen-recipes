Chocolate Cake

  1. Cake:
  2. 2 cups sugar
  3. 1/2 cups shortening
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1/2 cups shortening
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 1/4 tsp salt
  8. 4 Tbsp cocoa
  9. 1 cups buttermilk
  10. 2 tsp soda
  11. 1 tsp vanilla
  12. 2 1/2 cups flour
  13. 1 cups water
  14. Frosting:
  15. 2 cups powdered sugar
  16. 2 Tbsp cream
  17. 2 Tbsp butter
  18. 2 Tbsp flour
  19. 2 tsp vanilla
  20. 1/2 cups Crisco
  21. 1 egg white
  22. salt
  • Whip frosting ingredients together and set aside.
  • Mix all but water.
  • Boil water and add last.
  • Bake at 375 for 50 minutes.
  • Let cool until cool to the touch.
  • Coat in frosting.
