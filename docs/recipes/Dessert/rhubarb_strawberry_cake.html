Rhubarb Strawberry Cake

  • 5 cups chopped rhubarb
  • 1 cups sugar
  • 1 3 oz. package strawberry jello
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1 yellow cake box
  • egg (for cake)
  • oil (for cake)
  1. Spread rhubarb in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
  2. Sprinkle sugar, jello, and marshmallows over rhubarb
  3. Mix 1 yellow cake mix as directed.
  4. Spread over rhubarb.
  5. Bake at 350 until done.
  6. Serve warm or cold.
