Chocolate Butterscotch Squares

  • 1 cups sugar
  • 1 cups light corn syrup
  • 1 cups peanut butter
  • 6 cups rice krispies
  • 1 cups each of chocolate and butterscotch chips
  1. Combine sugar and corn syrup and microwave on high until sugar dissolves, about 3 minutes.
  2. Stir in peanut butter.
  3. Add cereal and mix until cereal is coated with sugar peanut butter mix.
  4. Press into a buttered 9 x 13 pan.
  5. Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips in microwave on 70 percent power.
  6. Spread over cereal mix.
  7. Chill in refrigerator until firm, 15 minutes.
  8. Cut into bars.
