Snow Flakes

  1. 1 cups butter
  2. 3 oz. cream cheese
  3. 1 cups sugar
  4. 1 egg yolk
  5. 1 tsp vanilla
  6. 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 2 1/2 cups flour
  9. 1 tsp orange zest
  • Cream shortening and cheese well.
  • Add sugar gradually.
  • Beat in egg yolk, vanilla and orange rind.
  • Add dry ingredients.
  • Put through cookie press.
