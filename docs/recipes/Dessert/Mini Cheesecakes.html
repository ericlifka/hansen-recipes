Mini Cheesecakes

  1. 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  2. 1/4 cups sugar
  3. 1/4 cups butter, melted
  4. 3-8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
  5. 14 oz. sweetened condensed milk
  6. 3 eggs
  7. 2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. 21 oz. can of pie filling
  • Heat oven to 300.
  • Combine crumbs, sugar, and butter.
  • Press crumbs on bottom of 24 paper lined muffin cups.
  • In bowl beat cheese until fluffy.
  • Gradually beat in milk until smooth.
  • Add eggs and vanilla.
  • Mix well.
  • Spoon about 3 Tbsp into prepared cups.
  • Bake 20 minutes or until set.
  • Cool.
  • Chill.
  • Top with filling.
  • Refrigerate.
  • Can also make the batter chocolate by melting 1 cups semisweet chocolate chips and add to batter.
