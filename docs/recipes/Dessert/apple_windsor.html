Apple Windsor

  • apples
  • sugar
  • cinnamon
  • flour
  • baking powder
  • shortening
  • 1 egg
  1. Slice apples in bottom of baking pan.
  2. Add 1 cups sugar and cinnamon over apples.
  3. Then mix 1 cups sugar, 1 cups flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 cups shortening like a pie crust then add 1 egg.
  4. Crumble over apples and bake in 350 oven for 1 hour.
  5. Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
