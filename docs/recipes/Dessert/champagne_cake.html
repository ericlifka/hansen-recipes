Champagne Cake

  • 1 pkg. yellow cake mix
  • 1 small pkg. instant vanilla pudding
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 12 oz. crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 pt. heavy cream
  1. Make yellow cake mix according to directions on package and bake.
  2. Make pudding mix with milk.
  3. Add soft cream cheese and beat until smooth.
  4. Spread over cooled cake.
  5. Drain pineapple well and spread over top of pudding.
  6. Whip cream and frost.
  7. Keep refrigerated.
