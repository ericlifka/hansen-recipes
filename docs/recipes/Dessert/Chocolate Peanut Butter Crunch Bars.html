Chocolate Peanut Butter Crunch Bars

  1. 1 cups peanut butter
  2. 4 Tbsp honey
  3. 3 1/2 cups rice krispies
  4. 1 envelope Knox gelatin
  5. 1/4 cups cold milk
  6. 1/2 cups milk, heated to boiling
  7. 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  8. 1 egg
  9. 1/2 cups ice cubes (3-4)
  • Blend peanut butter with 2 Tbsp honey; stir in cereal.
  • Press half of mix into bottom of 8‚Äù pan, set aside.
  • In a blender, sprinkle gelatin over cold milk; let stand 3-4 minutes.
  • Add hot milk and process at low speed until gelatin is dissolved, about 2 minutes.
  • Add remaining honey, chocolate and egg, process at high speed until blended.
  • Add ice cubes one at a time; process on high until ice is melted.
  • Let stand until mix is slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
  • Turn into pan, press remaining cereal mix onto gelatin.
  • Chill until firm.
