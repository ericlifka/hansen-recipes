Peanut Butter Pie

  • 1 baked pie shell
  • 2/3 cups sugar
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cups peanut butter
  • 2 cups milk, scalded
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cups cornstarch
  1. Heat oven to 325.
  2. Combine sugar, peanut butter, blending until mixture has consistency of biscuit mix.
  3. Spread half of mixture in pie shell.
  4. Combine cornstarch, 2/3 cups sugar and salt.
  5. Add milk and mix well.
  6. Beat egg yolks and pour a small amount of milk over yolks.
  7. Mix well into milk mixture.
  8. Cook in top of double boiler until mix thickens.
  9. Add butter and vanilla.
  10. Pour into shell.
  11. Beat egg whites till stiff.
  12. Top mixture in shell with meringue.
  13. Sprinkle remaining peanut butter over meringue.
  14. Bake until meringue is brown.
