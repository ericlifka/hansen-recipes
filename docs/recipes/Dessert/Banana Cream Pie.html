Banana Cream Pie

  1. 1 pie crust
  2. 2-3 bananas
  3. instant vanilla or banana cream pudding
  • Bake pie crust.
  • Make pudding--directions are on side of box probably need 2 small boxes.
  • Slice bananas and put on cooled pie crust.
  • Pour pudding over bananas.
  • Refrigerate for several hours.
  • Top with whipped cream.
