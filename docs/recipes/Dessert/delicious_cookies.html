Delicious Cookies

  • 1 cups white sugar
  • 1 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cups butter
  • 1 cups veggie oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Mix those ingredients together and add:
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cups flaked coconut
  • 1 cups Rice Krispies
  • 1 cups oatmeal
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 1 cups chocolate chips
  1. Let stand in refrigerator for 15 minutes.
  2. Make small balls or drop by teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet.
  3. Press down with fork dipped in sugar.
  4. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
