Sugar Cookies

  1. 1 cups powdered sugar
  2. 1 cups white sugar
  3. 1 cups butter
  4. 1 cups veggie oil
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 1 tsp vanilla
  7. 4 cups +4 Tbsp flour
  8. 1 tsp salt, soda and cream of tartar
  • Cream together butter, oil and sugars until fluffy.
  • Beat in eggs.
  • Add rest of ingredients.
  • Roll into small balls.
  • Place on greased cookie sheet.
  • Press down cookies with glass dipped in sugar.
  • Bake at 375 for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
