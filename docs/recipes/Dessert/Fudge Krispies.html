Fudge Krispies

  1. 12 oz. package of milk chocolate morsels
  2. 1/2 cups butter
  3. 1/2 cups light corn syrup
  4. 2 tsp vanilla
  5. 1 cups powdered sugar
  6. 4 cups rice krispies

  • Stir over low heat until melted and smooth.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Stir in vanilla and sugar.
  • Add rise krispies and mix lightly until well coated.
  • Spread in a 9 x 13 pan.
  • Chill until firm.
  • Cut into squares.
  • Store in refrigerator.
