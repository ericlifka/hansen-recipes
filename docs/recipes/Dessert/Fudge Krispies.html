Fudge Krispies

  • 12 oz. package of milk chocolate morsels
  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 1/2 cups light corn syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cups powdered sugar
  • 4 cups rice krispies
  1. Combine chocolate chips, margarine and corn syrup in medium size pan.
  2. Stir over low heat until melted and smooth.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in vanilla and sugar.
  5. Add rise krispies and mix lightly until well coated.
  6. Spread in a 9 x 13 pan.
  7. Chill until firm.
  8. Cut into squares.
  9. Store in refrigerator.
