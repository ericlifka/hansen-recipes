Unbelievable Custard Pie

  1. 2 cups milk
  2. 3/4 cups sugar
  3. 1/4 tsp baking powder
  4. 1/2 cups melted butter
  5. 4 eggs
  6. 1/2 cups flour
  7. 1 tsp vanilla
  8. 7 oz. pkg. coconut
  • Measure ingredients into blender container.
  • Process on low until well mixed.
  • Pour into 9 in pie pan.
  • Bake in 350 oven for 40 minutes.
