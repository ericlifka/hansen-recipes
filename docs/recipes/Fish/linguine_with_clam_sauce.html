Linguine With Clam Sauce

  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cups olive oil
  • 3 cans clams (save broth)
  • 1/2 cups white wine
  • 1/2-3/4 cups broth
  • 1/2 cups parsley
  • 1 tsp basil, oregano, pepper
  • linguine
  1. Heat oil (may use less oil) and add garlic and saut√©.
  2. Add clams without broth and cook 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add rest of stuff, simmer 15-20 minutes or until desired thickness.
  4. The more broth and liquid you add the longer you need to simmer.
  5. I also add some half and half if I want it to be more of a white sauce.
