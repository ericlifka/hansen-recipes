Italian Baked Fish

  • tomato
  • seasoned bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper
  • 8 flounder or trout fillets
  • 1/2 cups vermouth or white wine
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Parmesan
  1. Heat oven to 500.
  2. In buttered 9 x 13 pan thinly slice tomato to cover bottom.
  3. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, salt and pepper.
  4. Arrange fish over mixture.
  5. Combine wine, butter and lemon juice in pan.
  6. Heat to boil and cook 3 minutes.
  7. Pour over fish.
  8. Sprinkle with cheese and paprika and lemon juice over all.
  9. Bake for 10-20 minutes.
