Shrimp and Pesto Calzones

  1. 1 10 oz. pkg. refrigerated pizza dough
  2. 3 Tbsp pesto
  3. 6 oz. frozen, thawed small shrimp
  4. 3 thin tomato slices
  5. 3 oz. mozzarella cheese
  6. 1 beaten egg white
  • Divide pizza dough into 3 pieces.
  • On a floured surface roll each piece of dough into a 6” circle. Spread 1 Tbsp of the pesto atop half of each circle to within 1” of edges.
  • Top the pesto with 1/3 of the shrimp, 1 tomato slice, and 1/3 of the cheese leaving the other half of each circle and the edges uncovered.
  • Moisten edges of dough on each circle with egg white.
  • Fold each in half; seal edges with the tines of a fork to prevent filling from oozing out while baking.
  • Prick tops once or twice; brush with remaining egg white.
  • Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes.
