Lobster Tails

  1. lobster tails
  2. butter
  3. old bay seasoning
  • Make sure that if the tails were frozen that they are thawed out--otherwise they are hard to cut and very cold!
  • To cut, I cut them from the top down to the tail.
  • Then, I use a spoon and pull the meat away from the inside of the shell.
  • I place the meat on top of the shell, put on broiler pan.
  • Heat oven to 425.
  • Brush the tail with melted butter, sprinkle with Old Bay seasoning.
  • Bake for 15 minutes, then broil for 6-8 minutes until the meat or shell turns light brown.
  • Remove from oven, serve with melted butter.
