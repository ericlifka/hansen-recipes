Crab-Shrimp Bake

  • 2 cans crab
  • 1 bag shrimp so you have a total of 1 lb. can be both or crab or shrimp
  • 1 can cr. of shrimp soup
  • 2/3 cups milk
  • 1/3 cups sour cream
  • 2 cups egg noodles, uncooked
  • 1/2 cups cheddar
  1. Drain meat and blend soup, milk and sour cream.
  2. Stir in noodles and top with cheese.
  3. Put in a 10 x 6 pan.
  4. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
