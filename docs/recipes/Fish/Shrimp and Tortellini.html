Shrimp and Tortellini

  • 1 lb. shrimp
  • 9 oz pkg. tortellini with cheese filling
  • 1/3 cups butter
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil or 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 cups parmesan
  1. Peel and devein shrimp; set aside.
  2. Cook tortellini according to package directions, drain and set aside.
  3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat; add shrimp, shallot and basil.
  4. Cook about 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Add pasta and cheese, toss gently.
