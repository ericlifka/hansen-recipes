Pasta Provençal

  1. 4 oz. butter or olive oil
  2. 2 Tbsp garlic
  3. 1/2 tsp thyme, salt, pepper
  4. 1 tsp parsley flakes
  5. 1 bunch green onions
  6. 10 mushrooms
  7. 1/4 cups lemon juice
  8. 8 plum tomatoes
  9. 8 oz. shrimp, crab, scallops
  10. pasta (linguine)
  • In skillet heat oil 1 minute.
  • Add seafood, scallions, mushrooms, garlic and seasonings.
  • Cook over high heat 3-5 minutes or until seafood is half cooked.
  • Add lemon juice and tomato and simmer 5-8 minutes.
  • Heat pasta and drain.
  • Serve pasta with seafood over.
