Live Lobsters

  • Lobsters
  • vinegar
  1. Store lobsters in the refrigerator until you are ready to use.
  2. Before cooking I usually put them in cold water to clean them or you can rinse them.
  3. Put some water in a pot with some vinegar.
  4. When the water comes to a boil, put the lobsters in on a steamer basket.
  5. It takes about 5 minutes per lobster, or cook until they are pinkish and no longer moving.
