Shrimp Burritos

  • 1 can cr. celery soup
  • 3 oz. soft cream cheese
  • 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 tsp paprika and pepper
  • 1/8 tsp seafood seasoning
  • 1 lb. shrimp, cooked
  • 4 10‚Äù tortillas
  • 1/2 cups cheddar jack cheese
  1. In skillet combine first 6 ingredients.
  2. Cook and stir over medium heat until cream cheese is melted and blended.
  3. Stir in shrimp; heat through.
  4. Spoon 2/3 filling off center on each tortilla.
  5. Sprinkle with cheese.
  6. Fold sides and ends over filling and roll up.
  7. Serve.
