Soy Sesame Marinade for Salmon

  1. 3/4 cups soy sauce and rice wine vinegar
  2. 3 Tbsp brown sugar
  3. 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  4. 2 green onions
  5. 1 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  6. salmon
  7. Other marinades:
  8. Pineapple soy: 12 oz. pineapple juice concentrate 3 Tbsp soy sauce1 tsp ginger
  9. Orange Pepper: 12 oz. orange juice concentrate1 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Marinade salmon for 3 hours.
  • Grill or broil it depends on the thickness as to how long it takes.
  • It generally takes about 15 minutes.
  • You can also bake it at 400 for 30 minutes.
