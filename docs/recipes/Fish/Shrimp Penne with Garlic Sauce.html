Shrimp Penne with Garlic Sauce

  1. 2 cups uncooked penne
  2. 2 garlic
  3. 1/4 cups butter, cube
  4. 1/4 cups flour
  5. 1 can chicken broth
  6. 5 oz. evap milk
  7. 1 lb shrimp, cooked
  8. 2/3 cups Parm cheese
  9. 2 Tbsp parsley
  10. 1 1/2 tsp seafood seasoning
  • Cook pasta.
  • In separate pan saute garlic in butter.
  • Stir in flour until blended.
  • Add broth and milk gradually.
  • Bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  • Stir in shrimp, parsley and seafood seasoning.
  • Mix with penne.
  • Serve.
