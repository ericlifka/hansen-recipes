No Boil Lasagna With Broccoli and Ham

  • 1 16 oz. bag frozen chopped broccoli
  • 1 14.5-16 oz. jar alfredo style pasta sauce
  • 1 14.5-16 oz. jar reduced fat alfredo style pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cups Madeira or milk
  • PAM
  • 9 no-cook lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cups ham
  • 8 oz. already sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cups Parmesan
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Place broccoli in a colander and run water over it for 2 minutes to begin defrosting.
  3. Drain.
  4. Mix both jars of the pasta sauce, add milk and Madeira and stir until blended.
  5. Coat a 9 x 13 pan with Pam.
  6. Spread 1 1/2 cups sauce evenly in bottom of pan.
  7. Place 3 uncooked noodles over sauce.
  8. Set aside.
  9. Press down on the broccoli in the colander to remove any excess water.
  10. Scatter half of the drained broccoli over the lasagna noodles.
  11. Sprinkle half of the mushrooms and ham of the ham evenly over the dish.
  12. In a small bowl, whisk egg.
  13. Add ricotta, and stir well to blend.
  14. Dollop half of the ricotta mix evenly over the dish.
  15. Place 3 more noodles over the veggie mix.
  16. Pour 1 1/2 cups sauce over noodles.
  17. Sprinkle remaining broccoli over the sauce.
  18. Sprinkle on the remaining mushrooms and ham.
  19. Dollop remaining ricotta mix evenly over dish.
  20. Place 3 more noodles over the ricotta, pressing down if necessary to even out mix.
  21. Pour remaining sauce over top, making sure all of the noodles are coated with sauce.
  22. Cover with aluminum foil.
  23. Bake 1 hour.
  24. Remove dish and uncover.
  25. Spoon some sauce from the edge of the dish over the exposed top noodles.
  26. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan over the top.
  27. Bake 15 minutes more to let cheeses melt.
