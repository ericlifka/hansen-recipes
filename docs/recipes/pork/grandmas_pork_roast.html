Grandma’s Pork Roast

  • 3 lb. rolled roast (boston butt or shoulder)
  • Mix 2 cans cr. mushroom and 2 Tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • onion
  • 1/4 cups dice potato chunks
  • carrots
  1. Grandma makes it in the morning and refrigerates.
  2. She leaves it out at room temp 1 hour
  3. before cooking.
  4. Cover and bake at 350 for 2 hours.
