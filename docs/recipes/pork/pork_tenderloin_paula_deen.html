Pork Tenderloin (Paula Deen)

  • 1 1/2- 2 lb. pork tenderloin
  • Marinade:
  • 1/4 cups soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp red wine
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 green onion tops
  1. Combine marinade and whisk.
  2. Pour over tenderloin and marinade overnight.
  3. Heat oven to 350.
  4. Bake tenderloin 45 minutes or until meat is 145 degrees.
  5. Let meat rest 10 minutes before cutting into piece.
  6. Drizzle with a small amount of the meat juices from cooking.
