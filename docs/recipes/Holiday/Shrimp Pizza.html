Shrimp Pizza

  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • cocktail sauce
  • 1 can tiny shrimp
  • 1 cups gourmet shredded mozarella cheese
  • sliced green olives
  • HiHo crackers
  1. Spread cream cheese on plate.
  2. Pour thin layer of cocktail sauce over cheese about 5-6 oz.
  3. Drain and rinse shrimp, crumble, spread evenly across sauce.
  4. Spread mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Spread olives on top of cheese.
  6. Serve with HiHo crackers.
