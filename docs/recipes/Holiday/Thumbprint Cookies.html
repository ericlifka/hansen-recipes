Thumbprint Cookies

  • 1/4 cups shortening
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1 cups flour
  • 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg, separated
  • 3/4 cups chopped nuts
  • Frosting:
  • 1-2 Tbsp margarine
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1-2 Tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  1. Mix butter, shortening, sugar, egg yolk and vanilla.
  2. Work in flour and salt.
  3. Shape into 1” balls.
  4. Beat egg white slightly.
  5. Dip each ball into egg white and roll in nuts.
  6. Place on baking sheet.
  7. Press thumb into center of each.
  8. Bake 10 min. at 350º.
  9. Remove from cookie sheet.
  10. Top with jelly or frosting.
  11. Mix all frosting ingredients together together and add food coloring.
  12. Drop into cookies.
