Taco Pizza

  1. 8 oz. cream cheese
  2. 1 tomato, chopped
  3. 8 oz. sour cream
  4. lettuce, chopped
  5. 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  6. shredded cheese
  7. black olive slices
  8. onion
  • Mix cream cheese, sour cream and taco seasoning.
  • Spread onto large plate.
  • Then layer lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and olives.
  • Serve with taco chips.
