Coconut Balls

  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1/2 lb. chopped dates
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups Rice Krispies
  • 1/2 cups nuts
  1. Mix butter, sugar, cates, milk, & vanilla together.
  2. Cook over low heat until mushy, stirring constantly or it will burn.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in Rice Krispies and nuts.
  5. As soon as cool enough roll into balls and then roll in flaked coconut.
