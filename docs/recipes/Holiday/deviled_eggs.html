Deviled Eggs

  • eggs
  • option 1:
  • salad dressing
  • salt and pepper
  • relish
  • mustard
  • option 2: (grandma h)
  • 1 Tbsp mustard
  • 1 or more Tbsp sweet relish
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp vinegar
  • 2 whopping Tbsp miracle whip
  • salt and pepper
  1. Put eggs in pan.
  2. Boil 2 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, place cover on pan.
  4. Let stand 17 minutes.
  5. Drain.
  6. slice in half, remove egg yolks.
  7. Mash the egg yolks with the ingeredients from either option 1 or 2.
  8. Spoon mixture back into egg halves.
  9. Refrigerate.
