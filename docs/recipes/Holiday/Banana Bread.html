Banana Bread

  1. 1/2 cups butter
  2. 1 cups sugar
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 2 cups flour
  5. 1/2 cups milk
  6. 1 tsp soda
  7. 3 bananas mashed
  • Cream butter and sugar.
  • Add eggs.
  • Add bananas.
  • Add soda.
  • Add flour and milk alternately.
  • Bake in greased loaf pan 1 hour 15 min. at 350ยบ.
