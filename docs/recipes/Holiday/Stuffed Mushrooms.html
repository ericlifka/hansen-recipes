Stuffed Mushrooms

  • 8 oz. fresh mushrooms
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 box stovetop dressing (stuffing)
  • PAM
  1. Take stem out of mushrooms.
  2. Chop stems up with some onion.
  3. Cook up until softened.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cups stove top dressing.
  5. Mix stove top and onions.
  6. Stuff mushrooms with stove top.
  7. Spray pan with Pam and tops of mushrooms with Pam.
  8. Bake at 350ยบ for 20-25 minutes.
