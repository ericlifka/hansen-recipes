Taco Pizza

  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 8 oz. sour cream
  • lettuce, chopped
  • 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  • shredded cheese
  • black olive slices
  • onion
  1. Mix cream cheese, sour cream and taco seasoning.
  2. Spread onto large plate.
  3. Then layer lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and olives.
  4. Serve with taco chips.
