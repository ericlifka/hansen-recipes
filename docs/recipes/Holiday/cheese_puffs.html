Cheese Puffs

  • 1/2 cups soft butter
  • 1 cups cheddar cheese
  • 1 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • stuffed olives
  1. Cream butter and cheese.
  2. Add flour and salt.
  3. Mold with hands around an olive.
  4. Bake at 400ยบ for 12 - 15 minutes.
  5. (Can be frozen but need to cook 5 minutes longer.)
