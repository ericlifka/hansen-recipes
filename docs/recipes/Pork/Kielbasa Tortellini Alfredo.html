Kielbasa Tortellini Alfredo

  1. 9 oz. refrigerated cheese or spinach tortellini
  2. 1/2 lb. kielbasa
  3. 1 medium sweet red pepper, julienned
  4. 2 tsp v. oil
  5. 16 oz. sun dried tomato
  6. alfredo sauce
  7. 1 cups tomato
  • Cook tortellini according to package directions.
  • In a large skillet, saut√© kielbasa and red pepper in oil for 3 minutes or until pepper is crisp tender.
  • Drain tortellini.
  • Stir tortellini and alfredo sauce into skillet; heat through.
  • Garnish with tomato.
