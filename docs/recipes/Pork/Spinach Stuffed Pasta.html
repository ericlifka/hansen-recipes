Spinach Stuffed Pasta

  1. 1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach, drained
  2. 1-2 cups mozzarella
  3. 16 oz. ricotta cheese
  4. 3/4 cups Parmesan
  5. 1 egg
  6. sausage, optional
  7. jumbo macaroni shells
  • Cook shells (about 20), drain.
  • Mix cheeses and add spinach.
  • Blend and add egg, salt and pepper.
  • Stuff shells.
  • Refrigerate for a few hours, if desired to let flavors marry.
  • Put spaghetti sauce on bottom of pan, top with shells.
  • Spoon more sauce over.
  • Cover and bake for 55 minutes.
  • Sprinkle with parmesan and bake a few minutes longer.
  • Other spinach pasta recipe is in side dishes.
