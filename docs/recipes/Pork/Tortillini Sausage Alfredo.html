Tortillini Sausage Alfredo

  1. 1 pkg. (9 oz.) refrigerated cheese or spinach tortillini (or ravioli)
  2. 1 tsp olive oil
  3. 1/4 tsp salt
  4. 1/4 tsp pepper
  5. 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  6. 1/4 lb. Italian sausage
  7. 1/2 cups onion
  8. 1/4 cups green pepper and mushrooms
  9. 2 Tbsp flour
  10. 1 1/4 cups milk
  11. 1/8 tsp Worcestershire
  12. 1/4 cups Parmesan cheese
  • Bring water to a boil.
  • Add tortellini, oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder.
  • Cook according to package directions until pasta is tender.
  • Meanwhile, cook sausage, onion, green pepper and mushrooms over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
  • Stir in flour until blended.
  • Gradually add milk.
  • Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  • Drain tortillini; add to the sausage mix.
  • Stir in Parmesan cheese and Worcestershire.
