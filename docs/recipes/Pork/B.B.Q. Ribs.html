B.B.Q. Ribs

  • 1 cups chili sauce
  • 1 Tbsp worcestershire
  • 1/2 cups water
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp vinegar
  • minced onion
  • 1/4 tsp tabasco
  • 1/4 cups lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 cups ketchup
  • Ribs
  1. Mix all but the ribs together.
  2. Put sauce on ribs.
  3. Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 325.
