Pork Chop and Potato Bake

  • 6 pork chops
  • veggie oil
  • seasoned salt
  • 1 can cr. of celery soup
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 24 oz. frozen hash browns
  • 4 oz. cheddar cheese
  • 1 can french fried onions
  1. Brown pork chops in skillet.
  2. Sprinkle with salt and set aside.
  3. Combine soup, milk, sour cream, pepper and salt. Stir in potatoes, 1/2 cups cheese and 1/2 can onions.
  4. Spoon mix into 9 x 13 pan.
  5. Arrange chops over potatoes.
  6. Bake covered for 40 minutes at 350.
  7. Top with remaining cheese and onions, bake uncovered 5 minutes longer.
