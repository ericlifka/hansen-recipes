Grandma’s Pork Roast

  1. 3 lb. rolled roast (boston butt or shoulder)
  2. Mix 2 cans cr. mushroom and 2 Tbsp flour
  3. 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  4. onion
  5. 1/4 cups dice potato chunks
  6. carrots
  • Grandma makes it in the morning and refrigerates.
  • She leaves it out at room temp 1 hour
  • before cooking.
  • Cover and bake at 350 for 2 hours.
