Wild Rice With Pork Chops

  1. Pork Chops
  2. 1 cups wild rice
  3. 1 1/2 cups water
  4. 2 cans mushrooms
  5. 3 Tbsp chicken bouillon
  6. 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • In bottom of 9 x 13 pan place 1 cups wild rice.
  • Add 1 1/2 cups water and 2 cans mushrooms with juice.
  • Sprinkle 3 Tbsp chicken bouillon over.
  • Spoon 1 cr. of mushroom over mix.
  • Brown chops and place on rice.
  • Cover pan and bake at 350 for 2 hours.
