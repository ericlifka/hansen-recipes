One Eyed Sailor--Paula Deen

  • Bread:
  • 2 slices thick country bread
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cheese sauce:
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/8 tsp dry mustard
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Dash tabasco & worcestershire
  • 2 cups grated cheddar
  • 1/2 cups Parmesan,
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Using a biscuit cutter, cut a hole in the center of the bread.
  2. Butter both sides of bread and place in a cast iron saut√© pan or griddle over medium heat.
  3. Crack the eggs into the holes and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Once the egg starts to set, flip over and continue cooking the other side until set.
  5. Serve with cheese sauce.
  6. Cheese sauce:
  7. Add butter and flour to make a roux.
  8. Add warm milk slowly until combined.
  9. Stir in dry mustard, cayenne pepper, tabasco, worcestershire, grated cheddar, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  10. Constantly stir until cheese is smooth, thick and melted.
