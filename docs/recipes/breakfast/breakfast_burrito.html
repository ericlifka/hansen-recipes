Breakfast Burrito

  • 4 oz. chorizo
  • 1/2 red onion, chopped fine
  • 3 garlic
  • 4 oz. can chopped gr. chilies
  • 1/2 cups cilantro
  • 1 cups Eggbeaters
  • 4-6” tortillas
  • 1/2c. cheese
  • PAM
  1. Heat nonstick pan and spray with PAM.
  2. Add chorizo and break into small pieces, drain grease.
  3. Add onions, garlic, green chilies and cilantro.
  4. Sauté until onions are softened (1 min.)
  5. Add eggs, stir until scrambled.
  6. Put egg mix in the tortillas, fold like a burrito, top with cheese.
