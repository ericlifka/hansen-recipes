Cheesy Chicken

  • 6 boneless breasts
  • 1 can cr. of chicken soup
  • 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • tortilla chips
  1. Place breasts in 9 x 13 pan.
  2. Mix soup, taco seasoning, milk, and 1 1/2 cups cheese.
  3. Pour over chicken.
  4. Cover and bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover, top with crushed chips and remaining cheese.
  6. Bake an additional 10-15 minutes.
