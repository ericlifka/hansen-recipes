Grilled Chicken Caesars

  1. 3 chicken breasts (1 lb.)
  2. 2 cups cooked, cooled rice
  3. 1/2 cups diced tomato
  4. basil
  5. 1/2 cups diced green pepper
  6. 8 Tbsp Caesar salad dressing
  7. black pepper
  • Tear off 4 sheets of foil, about 12” long.
  • Spray foil with nonstick spray.
  • Cut the breasts into 1” cubes.
  • Pile 1/2 cups rice into center of each piece of foil and top with 1/2 cups chicken.
  • Top each with tomato, bell pepper and basil.
  • Pour 2 Tbsp Caesar dressing on top.
  • Seal tightly foil packets.
  • Cook on grill over low to med. low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  • Allow to cool slightly before opening foil.
  • You can also do in the over for 25 minutes at 350.
  • Times vary by the hotness of your grill so it could take longer.
