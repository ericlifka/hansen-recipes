Light and Zesty Chicken and Rice

  • 4 breast halves
  • 1/3 cups Italian dressing
  • 2/3 cups rice (uncooked)
  • 1 can french fried onion
  • 16 oz. frozen veggie combo
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  1. Place breasts in 12 x 8 dish, top with dressings.
  2. Bake uncovered at 400 for 20 min.
  3. Place rice, veggies and 1/2 cups onion under chicken.
  4. Combine bouillon and seasoning; pour over rice and veggies.
  5. Bake uncovered 25 min.
  6. Top with onions and bake 2-3 minutes.
