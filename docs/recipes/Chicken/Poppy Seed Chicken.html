Poppy Seed Chicken

  1. 6 chicken breast halves
  2. 1 can cr. of chicken soup
  3. 1 cups cr. of mushroom soup
  4. 1/2 cups sour cream
  5. 15 Ritz or club crackers, crushed
  6. 1/4 stick butter, melted
  7. 2 Tbsp poppy seeds
  • Coat a 9 x 13 inch pan with Pam.
  • Place breasts in pan.
  • Combine soups with sour cream and pour over chicken.
  • Combine crumbs, butter, and poppy seeds.
  • Sprinkle over sauce.
  • Bake for 45-60 minutes at 350.
