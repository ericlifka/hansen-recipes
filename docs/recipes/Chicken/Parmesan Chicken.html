Parmesan Chicken

  1. 3 lb. chicken pieces
  2. 1/2 cups Parmesan
  3. 1/4 cups flour
  4. 1 tsp paprika
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. dash pepper
  7. 1 beaten egg
  8. 2 Tbsp milk
  • Rinse chicken; pat dry.
  • In plastic bag combine cheese, flour, paprika, salt and pepper.
  • In shallow dish combine egg and milk.
  • Dip chicken in egg mixture.
  • Place chicken 2-3 pieces at a time in plastic bag.
  • Close bag and shake to coat chicken.
  • Place chicken in 9 x 13 pan.
  • Bake at 375 for 45-55 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  • Serve.
