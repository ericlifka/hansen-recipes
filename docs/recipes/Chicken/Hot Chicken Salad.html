Hot Chicken Salad

  • 2 cans cr. of chicken or mushroom
  • 1 cups mayo or 1/2 cups mayo & 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1 cups each onion and celery
  • 1 can green chilies
  • 2 cans of chicken
  • 3-6 hard boiled eggs
  • 4 cups crushed potato chips
  1. Mix together reserving 1 cups crushed chips.
  2. Put into 9 x 13 sprinkle reserved chips on top.
  3. Bake at 350 for 1 hour, or 400 for 30-45 minutes.
