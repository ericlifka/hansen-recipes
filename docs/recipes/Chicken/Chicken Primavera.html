Chicken Primavera

  1. 1 can cr. of celery or chicken soup
  2. 1/3 cups mayo
  3. 2 tsp paprika
  4. 1 lb. boneless breasts
  5. 2 cups thinly sliced veggies (combo of celery, green or red pepper, green onion)
  6. rice
  • In skillet blend soup mayo and paprika.
  • Stir in chicken and veggies.
  • Over med. heat, heat to boiling.
  • Reduce heat to low.
  • Cover; simmer 20 min. or until chicken is tender, stirring occasionally.
