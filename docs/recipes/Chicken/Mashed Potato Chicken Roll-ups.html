Mashed Potato Chicken Roll-ups

  1. 6 6 oz. chicken breasts
  2. 24 oz. refrigerated cheddar mashed potato, divided
  3. 10 oz. frozen chopped spinach thawed
  4. 1 cups flour
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 2 Tbsp water
  7. 1 1/2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
  8. 1 can cr. of mushroom soup
  9. 1/2 cups milk
  • Flatten chicken to 1/2 inch thickness.
  • Spread with 2 Tbsp potatoes down the center of each; top with spinach.
  • Roll up and secure with toothpicks.
  • Place flour in shallow bowl.
  • In another bowl, whisk eggs and water.
  • Place bread crumbs in third bowl.
  • Coat chicken with flour, dip in egg mix, then roll in crumbs.
  • Place seam side down in a greased pan.
  • BAke uncovered for 40-45 minutes.
  • In small pan combine soup and milk, heat until heated through.
  • Heat rest of the potatoes.
  • Discard toothpicks; serve chicken with sauce and potatoes.
