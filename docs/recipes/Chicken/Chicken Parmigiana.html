Chicken Parmigiana

  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 14 1/2 oz. can Italian stewed tomatoes
  • 1/3 cups Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 boneless breasts
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 slices Mozzarella
  1. In small pan dissolve cornstarch in tomatoes.
  2. Cook, stirring constantly until thickened, keep hot.
  3. Combine bread crumbs with Parmesan.
  4. Pound chicken to 1/4‚Äù thick, dip into crumbs coating well.
  5. Fry in oil 3-4 min. on each side.
  6. Place cheese on each piece of chicken.
  7. Cover and cook until chicken is done and cheese is melted.
  8. Top with sauce.
