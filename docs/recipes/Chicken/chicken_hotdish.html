Chicken Hotdish

  • 2 cups cooked chicken
  • 16 oz. cooked broccoli
  • 1 pkg. cooked noodles
  • 2 cans cr. of chicken soup
  • 1 1/2 cups mayo
  • 1 1/2 tsp curry
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Cut up Spread out chicken in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
  2. Layer boroccoli and noodles over chicken.
  3. Mix together soup, mayo, lemon juice, and curry, spread on top.
  4. Bake 350 for 1 hour.
  5. Spread cheese on top 15 minutes before it's done.
  6. Can be made in advance and can make it smaller.
