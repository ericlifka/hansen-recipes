Fiesta Chicken Burgers

  • 2 1/2 cups finely chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 cups oatmeal
  • 8 oz. plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cups onion
  • 2 egg whites, slightly beaten
  • 5 tsp lime or lemon juice
  • 4 tsp cilantro or parsley
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/8 tsp ground red pepper
  • 6 buns, tortillas, or pita bread
  1. Line rack of broiler pan with foil; oil lightly.
  2. Combine chicken, oats, 1/2 cups yogurt, onion, egg whites, 3 tsp lime juice, 2 tsp cilantro and chili powder; mix well.
  3. Shake into 6 burgers.
  4. Place on rack or over medium hot coals.
  5. Broil or grill about 6 inches from heat 5-6 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  6. For sauce combine remaining 1/2 cups yogurt, 2 tsp lime juice, 2 tsp cilantro and ground red pepper; mix well.
  7. Serve topped with lettuce, chopped red pepper and sauce.
