Cheesy Chicken

  1. 6 boneless breasts
  2. 1 can cr. of chicken soup
  3. 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  4. 1/2 cups milk
  5. 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  6. tortilla chips
  • Place breasts in 9 x 13 pan.
  • Mix soup, taco seasoning, milk, and 1 1/2 cups cheese.
  • Pour over chicken.
  • Cover and bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
  • Uncover, top with crushed chips and remaining cheese.
  • Bake an additional 10-15 minutes.
