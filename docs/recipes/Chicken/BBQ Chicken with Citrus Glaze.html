BBQ Chicken with Citrus Glaze

  1. 1/2 cups each catsup, orange juice
  2. 1/4 cups each brown sugar, lemon juice
  3. 2 tsp cornstarch
  4. 1/2 tsp each grated orange peel, dry mustard
  5. 3 lb. chicken pieces
  • Combine ingredients except chicken in pan.
  • Bring to boil.
  • Cook stirring constantly until thickened and translucent.
  • Pour into jar and cover tightly.
  • Cool and refrigerate.
  • Place chicken on grill.
  • Grill 30 minutes.
  • Brush with glaze and cook until chicken is done, turning and basting every 10 minutes until done.
