Penne pasta or fettuccine

  1. penne
  2. butter
  3. chicken
  4. peppers
  5. onion
  6. garlic
  7. mushrooms
  8. cajun seasonings
  9. salt
  10. pepper
  11. cayenne
  12. tomato
  13. chicken stock
  14. heavy cream
  • Melt butter in pan.
  • Add chicken and cook until no longer pink about 6-10 minutes.
  • Remove from pan.
  • Cook peppers, onion, garlic in pan about 5 minutes.
  • Add mushrooms and chicken.
  • Add seasonings--cajun, salt, pepper, cayenne.
  • Cook a few minutes.
  • Add tomato, stock and cream.
  • Cook all together on low.
  • Add cooked pasta, mix, and heat a few minutes longer.
