Barbecue Chicken Burritos

  1. 1/2 lb. skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  2. 1 1/2 cups green peppers, julienned
  3. 1 cups chopped onion
  4. 4 Tbsp vegie oil, divided
  5. 1/2 cups barbecue sauce
  6. 1 1/2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  7. 4 10 inch tortillas, warmed
  • In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chicken, green peppers, and onion in 2 Tbsp oil for 6-8 minutes or until chicken juices run clear.
  • Stir in barbecue sauce.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat; simmer for 1-2 minutes or until heated through.
  • Sprinkle cheese down the center of each tortilla; top with the chicken mix.
  • Fold sides and ends over the filling and roll up.
  • In a large skillet over medium heat, brown burritos in remaining oil on all sides.
  • Serve with lime wedges, sour cream, lettuce and tomatoes, if desired.
