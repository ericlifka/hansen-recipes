Chicken Masala (Sandra Lee)

  • 4 breasts
  • 1/3 cups flour
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 15 oz. diced tomato, drained
  • 1/2 cups cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cups light coconut milk
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cups peas (optional)
  • rice
  1. Shake chicken in flour.
  2. Heat oil.
  3. Fry breast on both sides.
  4. Remove.
  5. Add onion and garlic--cook until soft.
  6. Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce to simmer, put breasts back in pan, and simmer 15 minutes.
  8. Add peas, and simmer 10 more minutes.
