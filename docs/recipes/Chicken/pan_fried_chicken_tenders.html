Pan Fried Chicken Tenders

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 lb. chicken tenders
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 6 green onions
  • 4 Tbsp Marsala wine
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • salt and pepper
  1. Heat frying pan.
  2. Add the oil, chicken strips, garlic and green onions.
  3. Saut√© over high heat until the chicken is lightly browned and tender.
  4. Remove from pan.
  5. Add the Marsala and lemon juice, and allow liquid to reduce for a moment.
  6. Return chicken to the hot pan and add salt and pepper.
  7. Do not overcook and serve immediately.
