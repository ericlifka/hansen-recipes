Dinner In A Packet

  • 2 large potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 medium onion
  • mushrooms
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oregano
  • basil
  • white wine
  • 14 1/2 oz. can peeled tomato or 2 chopped tomatoes
  1. Tear off 4 (18‚Äù) sheets of heavy duty foil.
  2. Using half the ingredients layer potato, zucchini, onion and mushrooms. Drain canned tomatoes.
  3. Top each packet with chicken, 1 Tbsp butter then remaining potato, zucchini, onion and mushrooms.
  4. Divide tomato among packets.
  5. Sprinkle with spices and 1 Tbsp wine.
  6. Bring sides of foil up and seal.
  7. Place over coals and cook 30 minutes.
