Chicken Deluxe

  • 20 oz. frozen chopped spinach
  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked
  • 1 cups mayo
  • 1 can cr. of chicken soup
  • 1-2 tsp curry
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cups stuffing
  1. Cook spinach until tender and drain.
  2. Spread spinach on bottom of 1 1/2 qt. greased dish.
  3. Layer chicken on top.
  4. Combine mayo, soup, curry and lemon juice.
  5. Pour over chicken and spinach.
  6. Sprinkle stuffing on top.
  7. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
