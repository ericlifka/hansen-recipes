Curry Chicken

  1. Box of S & B Golden Curry Sauce Mix (mild, medium or hot flavor)
  2. 1 1-1/3 lb. chicken (can use beef, lamb or shrimp)
  3. onion
  4. carrots
  5. celery
  6. bell pepper
  7. 2 1/2 cups water
  • Cut meat into cubes and cut up vegetables.
  • Stir fry the meat in oil or butter.
  • Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  • Add water and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat.
  • Cover and simmer until meat is tender about 10-20 minutes.
  • Add the sauce mix and stir until completely melted, about 5 minutes.
  • Serve over noodles or rice.
