Moo Goo Gai Pan

  • 1/2 lb. breasts
  • 1/2 egg white
  • 1 tsp sherry
  • cornstarch
  • dash salt & pepper
  • 24 snow peas
  • 4 Tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt1
  • 2 mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2-3” scallion
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 12 water chestnuts, sliced
  1. Mix Egg white, sherry, cornstarch, and dash of salt & pepper.
  2. Slice chicken meat and mix with the egg mix.
  3. Heat 2 Tbsp oil and add salt, sauté mushrooms, celery, water chestnuts, and snow peas for 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from pan.
  5. Heat 2 Tbsp oil in same pan.
  6. Fry garlic, scallions and chicken meat for 1 minute over light heat.
  7. Add soy sauce and sugar and mix well.
  8. Add cooked veggies and mix well for 1 minute.
