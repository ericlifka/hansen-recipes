Breaded Chicken

  1. Chicken Breasts
  2. Bread Crumbs
  3. White wine
  4. Provolone cheese slices
  • Dip each breast in bread crumbs.
  • Bake 30 minutes at 350.
  • Remove from oven.
  • Add 1/2 - 1 cups white wine.
  • Put a slice of provolone or muenster cheese on top and bake 15 more minutes.
