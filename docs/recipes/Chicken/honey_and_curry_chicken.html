Honey and Curry Chicken

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 cups onion
  • 2 garlic
  • 1/4 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cups honey
  • 1 tsp salt and curry powder
  • 2 Tbsp chutney
  • chicken
  1. Melt butter.
  2. Add onion and garlic.
  3. Add rest and stir.
  4. Set aside to cool.
  5. Coat chicken.
  6. Bake at 400 for 1 hour.
