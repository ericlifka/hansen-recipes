Honey and Curry Chicken

  1. 1 stick butter
  2. 1/2 cups onion
  3. 2 garlic
  4. 1/4 tsp Dijon mustard
  5. 1/2 cups honey
  6. 1 tsp salt and curry powder
  7. 2 Tbsp chutney
  8. chicken
  • Melt butter.
  • Add onion and garlic.
  • Add rest and stir.
  • Set aside to cool.
  • Coat chicken.
  • Bake at 400 for 1 hour.
