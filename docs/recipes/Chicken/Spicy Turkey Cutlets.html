Spicy Turkey Cutlets

  1. 8 oz. tomato sauce
  2. 1 Tbsp veggie oil
  3. 1 Tbsp parsley
  4. 1 1/2 tsp oregano leaves
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. 1 tsp thyme leaves
  7. 1 clove garlic
  8. 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  9. 1 lb. turkey cutlets
  • Combine tomato sauce, oil, oregano, parsley, thyme, salt, garlic and crushed red pepper in a small bowl.
  • Spread 1 Tbsp sauce over each cutlet; roll up.
  • Place seam side down on baking dish.
  • Spoon remaining sauce over cutlets.
  • Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
  • Bake at 350 for 25 minutes uncovered.
