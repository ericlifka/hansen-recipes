Breaded Chicken

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Bread Crumbs
  • White wine
  • Provolone cheese slices
  1. Dip each breast in bread crumbs.
  2. Bake 30 minutes at 350.
  3. Remove from oven.
  4. Add 1/2 - 1 cups white wine.
  5. Put a slice of provolone or muenster cheese on top and bake 15 more minutes.
