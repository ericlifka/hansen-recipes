Tangy Chicken with Tomatoes and Herbs

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp each basil, thyme
  • 16 oz. tomatoes, drained, cut into bite size pieces
  • 3/4 cups ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 to 1/3 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp salt, pepper
  • 2-3 lb. skinned chicken
  • cornstarch-water mix
  • cooked noodles
  1. In skillet sauce onion garlic and herbs in oil until onion is tender crisp.
  2. Stir in tomatoes and ketchup, vinegar, brown sugar, salt and pepper.
  3. Add chicken.
  4. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer covered 45-50 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Thicken sauce with cornstarch water mix.
  6. Serve chicken and sauce with noodles.
