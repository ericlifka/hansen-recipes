Chicken Enchiladas Ole

  1. 2 1/2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  2. 16 oz. sour cream
  3. 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  4. 1/3 cups chopped onion
  5. 12 tortillas
  6. 1 1/2 cups salsa
  • Combine chicken, 1 cups sour cream, 3/4 cups cheese and onions; mix well.
  • Warm tortillas.
  • Top each tortilla with 1/4 cups chicken mix; roll up tightly.
  • Place seam side down in 9 x 13 pan.
  • Top with salsa.
  • Bake at 375 for 25 minutes.
  • Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  • Heat a few minutes more and serve. I food process the chicken so it is more like shredded, but can do cubed.
