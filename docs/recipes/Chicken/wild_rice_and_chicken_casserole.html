Wild Rice and Chicken Casserole

  • 1 6 oz. package long grain and wild rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 medium gr. or red peppers
  • 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cups shredded sharp cheddar
  • 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 cups chopped toasted pecans
  • 2 Tbsp sherry, optional
  1. Cook rice according to package directions.
  2. Saut√© onion, pepper and mushrooms in hot oil 5 minutes or until tender.
  3. Add rice, chicken and next 4 ingredients; add sherry if desired.
  4. Spoon into greased casserole dish.
  5. Bake at 325 for 35 minutes.
  6. Serve.
