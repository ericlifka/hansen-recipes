Strawberry French Toast

  • 5 eggs
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 8 slices of french bread
  • 5 cups frozen strawberries
  • 2/3 cups sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  1. Mix eggs, milk and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Dip french bread into egg mixture for 30 seconds on each side.
  3. Let stand 10 minutes.
  4. Grease 9 x 13.
  5. Spread strawberries in pan.
  6. Combine sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle half over berries.
  7. Put bread on top.
  8. Sprinkle with remaining sugar and cinnamon.
  9. Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.
