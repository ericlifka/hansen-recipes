Bacon Egg Casserole

  1. 3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  2. 1/4 cups butter
  3. 1/4 cups flour
  4. 1 cups milk
  5. 1 cups half and half
  6. 4 cups sharp cheddar cheese
  7. 1 tsp italian seasonings
  8. 1/2 tsp pepper
  9. 12 eggs, scrambled until lightly set
  10. 1 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
  11. Optional: 2 cups soft whole wheat bread crumbs (4 slices)
  12. 3 Tbsp melted butter
  • Sauté potatoes in a little bit of the bacon grease for 5 minutes.
  • Melt 1/4 cups butter in pan over medium heat, add flour and stir until smooth.
  • Cook 1 minutes, stirring constantly.
  • Gradually add milk and half and half; cover over medium heat stirring constantly until thickened and bubbly.
  • Add cheese, italian seasonings, and pepper stirring constantly until cheese melts.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Layer half each of eggs, bacon and cheese sauce in greased 9 x 13” pan (I use a casserole dish).
  • Top with potatoes.
  • Top with remaining eggs, bacon and cheese sauce.
  • Combine bread crumbs and 3 Tbsp butter.
  • Sprinkle over casserole.
  • Cover and chill overnight, if desired.
  • Remove from fridge and let stand at room temp for 30 min.
  • Bake uncovered at 350 for 30 minutes or until heated.
