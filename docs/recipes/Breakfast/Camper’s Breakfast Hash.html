Camper’s Breakfast Hash

  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1 pkg. 20 oz. refrigerated shredded hash brown potatoes
  • 1/4 cups onion
  • 7 oz. brown and serve sausage links, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1/4 cups green pepper
  • 12 eggs lightly beaten
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  1. In a skillet, melt butter.
  2. Add the potatoes, sausage, onion and green pepper.
  3. Cook uncovered over medium heat for 10-15 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned, turning once.
  4. Push potato mix to the sides of pan.
  5. Pour eggs into center of pan.
  6. Cook and stir over medium heat until eggs are completely set.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Reduce heat; stir eggs into potato mix.
  9. Top with cheese; cover and cook 1-2 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  10. I did this but omitted the eggs as we also had French toast.
