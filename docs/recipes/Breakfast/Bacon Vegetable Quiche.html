Bacon Vegetable Quiche

  1. 1 9‚Äù unbaked pastry shell
  2. 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  3. 1 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
  4. 1 cups chopped broccoli
  5. 3/4 cups sweet onion
  6. 2 1/2 tsp olive oil
  7. 3 eggs, beaten
  8. 5 oz. evaporated milk
  9. 1 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
  10. 1/4 tsp salt and pepper
  11. 4 oz. shredded cheddar
  12. 6 crumbled bacon strips
  13. 1/2 cups crumbled tomato and basil
  14. Feta cheese
  • Saute in skillet the spinach, mushrooms, broccoli and onion in oil until tender.
  • In bowl whisk the eggs, milk, rosemary, salt and pepper.
  • Transfer cooled vegetables into egg mix.
  • Stir in cheese and bacon.
  • Pour into crust and top with feta.
  • Cover edges loosely with foil.
  • Bake at 375 for 30-35 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
  • Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
