Blintze

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  1. Mix all together.
  2. Heat cast iron pan, put 1 Tbsp oil in.
  3. Pour a small bit of stuff in pan and spread around.
  4. Flip after a minute or two and cook other side.
  5. This makes about 12, I generally make 2-3 batches for all of us.
