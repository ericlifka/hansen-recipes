Hash Brown Sausage Bake

  1. 20 oz. refrigerated shredded hash brown potatoes
  2. 1/3 cups butter
  3. 1 tsp beef bouillon
  4. 1 lb. pork sausage
  5. 1/3 cups onion
  6. 8 oz. small curd cottage cheese
  7. 3 eggs, beaten
  8. 4 slices American cheese, chopped
  • In a large bowl combine the hash browns, butter and bouillon.
  • Press on bottom and sides of pie plate.
  • Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes until edges are lightly browned.
  • In a skillet cook sausage and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
  • In a large bowl, combine the sausage mix, cottage cheese, eggs and American cheese.
  • Pour into crust.
  • Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
  • Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
