Eggs Scramble

  • 4 cups croutons
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp prepared mustard
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 8 beaten eggs
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups ham or 1 lb. sausage, cooked
  1. Place croutons and cheese in greased 9 x 13.
  2. Combine rest of ingredients except meat.
  3. Pour over croutons and cheese.
  4. Top with meat.
  5. Bake at 325 for 1 hour.
  6. You can halve this recipe for an 8 x 8 pan.
