Brunch Soufflé

  • 8 slices bread, cubed
  • 2/3 lb. velveeta cheese, sliced
  • 8 eggs
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/8 tsp each salt, pepper and dry mustard
  1. Cut crusts from bread and cube.
  2. Put bread in greased 9 x 13” pan and put on sliced cheese.
  3. Beat eggs slightly and add seasonings.
  4. Refrigerate overnight.
  5. Bake at 325 for 1 hour.
