Ana’s Ham Buns

  • 1/2 lb. ham
  • 1/2 lb. Swiss cheese
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 2 Tbsp spicy mustard
  • 3 Tbsp poppy seeds
  • 3 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 24 Pepperidge Farm dinner rolls (small rolls)
  1. Slice buns and divide ham and cheese equally between buns.
  2. Put tops back on buns.
  3. Melt butter and combine other ingredients.
  4. Spoon over buns, cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours or preferably overnight.
  5. Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes.
