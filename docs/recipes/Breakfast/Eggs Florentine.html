Eggs Florentine

  1. 1 can cream of celery soup
  2. 20 oz. chopped spinach, cooked, drained
  3. 1/4 cups milk
  4. 6 eggs
  5. 1 cups shredded cheese
  • In a 10 x 6 x 2 dish combine soup, milk, and spinach.
  • Spread in bottom of dish.
  • Top with eggs, sprinkle with cheese.
  • Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until set.
