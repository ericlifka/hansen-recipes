Eggs Benedict with Cheese

  • 4 toasted, buttered English muffin halves
  • 4 slices ham or Canadian bacon, cooked
  • 4 broiled tomato slices
  • 4 poached eggs
  • Cheese sauce:
  • 1 cups hot milk
  • 1 cups American cheese
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp flour
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1/8 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  1. Top muffin halves with ham, tomato, egg and cover with cheese sauce.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Add flour and seasonings.
  4. Pour milk into mix, slowly stirring constantly.
  5. Add cheese.
  6. Cook until thick and creamy.
  7. You can substitute broccoli for tomato.
