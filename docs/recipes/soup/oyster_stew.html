Oyster Stew

  • 2 pts. oysters, including liquid
  • 2 Tbsp finely chopped onion and garlic
  • 2-4 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cups cream
  • 1 qt. half and half
  • 1/4 tsp Worcestershire salt, pepper
  1. Slowly cook onion in butter.
  2. Do not brown.
  3. Add oysters, including liquid and cook slowly until edges curl.
  4. Stir in milk, cream, Worcestershire.
  5. Heat through but do not boil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with paprika and parsley.
