Veggie Tortellini Soup

  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • 16 oz. Cal. blend veggies
  • 8 oz. dried tortellini
  • 15 oz. Italian diced tomatoes, undrained
  1. Bring broth to a boil, stir in veggies and tortellini, return to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 10-12 minutes or until veggies are tender.
  3. Stir in tomatoes.
  4. Cover and cook for 5-6 minutes until heated through.
