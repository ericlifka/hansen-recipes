Black Bean and Pumpkin Chili

  • 1 med onion
  • 1 med yellow pepper
  • 3 garlic
  • 2 Tbsp o. oil
  • 3 cups chick broth
  • 2 cans black beans, rinse and drain
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken
  • 15 oz pumpkin
  • 14 1/2 oz diced tomato, undrained
  • 2 tsp parsley
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  1. In skillet saute onion, yellow pepper and garlic in oil.
  2. Put in slow cooker with rest of ingredients.
  3. Cook on low 4-5 hours
