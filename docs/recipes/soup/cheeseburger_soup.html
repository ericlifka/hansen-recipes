Cheeseburger Soup

  • 1/2 lb. hamburger
  • 3/4 cups each of onion, carrots, celery
  • 1 tsp basil, parsley
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 3 cups broth
  • 4 cups potato
  • 1/4 cups flour
  • 8 oz. velveeta
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • salt and pepper
  1. Brown beef, set aside.
  2. Saute vegies in 1 Tbsp butter.
  3. Add broth, potato and beef.
  4. Boil, reduce heat and simmer 10-12 minutes.
  5. Melt 3 Tbsp butter, add flour and add milk until thick.
  6. Add to beef/vegie mix and cook about 2 minutes.
  7. Add the velveeta and cook until melted.
