Swiss Broccoli Soup

  • 1 cups instant dry milk
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1 can chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese
  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1/4 stick butter
  • 1/4 cups chopped green onion
  • 10 oz. chopped broccoli, cooked
  1. Combine dry milk, flour and seasonings in 3 qt. pan.
  2. Stir in water and chicken broth until smooth.
  3. Bring to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Boil and stir 1 min.
  5. Saut√© onion in butter until tender.
  6. Stir onion and broccoli into milk.
  7. Remove from heat, add cheese until melted.
  8. Serve.
