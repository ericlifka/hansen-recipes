Turkey Caribe

  • 8 turkey breast slices
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder, onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 8 slices bacon.
  1. Top each of 4 turkey slices with 1 slice cheese folded in half.
  2. Combine spices; sprinkle evenly on cheese.
  3. Top with remaining turkey.
  4. Wrap each with 2 slices bacon, securing with toothpicks.
  5. Grill 6” from coals 15-20 minutes, turning frequently.
  6. Or you can do in broiler, broil 5” from heat 8 minutes, turn, broil 7 minutes more.
