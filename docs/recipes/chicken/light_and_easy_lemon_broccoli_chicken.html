Light and Easy Lemon Broccoli Chicken

  • 1 Tbsp veggie oil
  • 1 lb. boneless breasts
  • 1 can cr. of broccoli soup
  • 1/4 cups milk
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 4 thin lemon slices
  1. In skillet in hot oil cook chicken 10 minutes or until browned on both sides.
  2. Spoon off fat.
  3. Combine soup and milk; stir in lemon juice and pepper.
  4. Pour over chicken; top each chicken piece with lemon slice.
  5. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Cover simmer 5 min. or until chicken is fork tender, stirring occasionally.
