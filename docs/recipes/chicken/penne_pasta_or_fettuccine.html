Penne pasta or fettuccine

  • penne
  • butter
  • chicken
  • peppers
  • onion
  • garlic
  • mushrooms
  • cajun seasonings
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayenne
  • tomato
  • chicken stock
  • heavy cream
  1. Melt butter in pan.
  2. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink about 6-10 minutes.
  3. Remove from pan.
  4. Cook peppers, onion, garlic in pan about 5 minutes.
  5. Add mushrooms and chicken.
  6. Add seasonings--cajun, salt, pepper, cayenne.
  7. Cook a few minutes.
  8. Add tomato, stock and cream.
  9. Cook all together on low.
  10. Add cooked pasta, mix, and heat a few minutes longer.
