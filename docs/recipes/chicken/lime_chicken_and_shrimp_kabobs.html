Lime Chicken and Shrimp Kabobs

  • 2/3 cups veg. oil
  • 3 limes, juiced
  • 2 Tbsp vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 red pepper, cut in 1” pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut in 1” pieces
  • 16 oz. can pineapple rings, drained and cut into 1” pieces
  • 1/2 lb. large shrimp, peeled
  • 6 breasts, cut into 1” pieces
  1. In a small bowl, whisk oil, lime juice, vinegar, sugar and crushed red pepper.
  2. In a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag, combine bell pepper, onion, pineapple, chicken and shrimp.
  3. Pour lime mix over chicken mix.
  4. Seal bag and refrigerate 4-6 hours to marinate.
  5. Heat grill to 350 to 400.
  6. Using tongs remove chicken, vegetable pieces, pineapple and shrimp from marinade; discarding marinade.
  7. On the skewers thread 1 piece of chicken, 1 piece of pepper, 1 piece of onion, and 1 piece of pineapple, alternating to fill skewer.
  8. Do same thing with shrimp alternating shrimp, pepper, onion and pineapple to fill skewer.
  9. Place chicken kabobs on grill, cover with grill lid and cook for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked throughout, turning occasionally.
  10. Place shrimp kabobs on the grill, cover with grill lid and cook for 6-8 minutes or until shrimp are cooked throughout, turning once.
  11. Serve.
