Parmesan Chicken

  • 3 lb. chicken pieces
  • 1/2 cups Parmesan
  • 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • dash pepper
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 2 Tbsp milk
  1. Rinse chicken; pat dry.
  2. In plastic bag combine cheese, flour, paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. In shallow dish combine egg and milk.
  4. Dip chicken in egg mixture.
  5. Place chicken 2-3 pieces at a time in plastic bag.
  6. Close bag and shake to coat chicken.
  7. Place chicken in 9 x 13 pan.
  8. Bake at 375 for 45-55 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  9. Serve.
