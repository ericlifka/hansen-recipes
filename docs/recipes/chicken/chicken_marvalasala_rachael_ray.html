Chicken Marvalasala (Rachael Ray)

  • 4 large thin pieces chicken
  • salt and pepper
  • 5 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic cloves
  • 3 Portabello mushrooms, sliced
  • 24 shitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cups Marsala wine
  • Romano cheese
  • flour
  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper, and a light dusting of flour on both sides.
  2. Heat a non stick skillet with 2 Tbsp olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add chicken to the pan and brown cutlets lightly on both sides.
  4. Remove to a platter and cover with foil to hold in the heat.
  5. To the same skillet add 2 Tbsp olive oil and 1 clove garlic.
  6. Cook for a minute or so, then remove and add the mushrooms.
  7. Let the mushrooms brown evenly, until tender about 8-10 minutes.
  8. To the cooked mushrooms add Marsala and let reduce for 1 minute or so.
  9. Add 2 Tbsp butter to finish the sauce.
  10. Slide chicken back into sauce to warm.
  11. Serve.
  12. Can be served over noodles.
