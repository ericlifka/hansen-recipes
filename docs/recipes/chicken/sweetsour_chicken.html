Sweet&Sour Chicken

  • chicken
  • 8 oz. Russian dressing
  • 8 oz. apricot preserves
  • 1 onion soup mix
  • 1/4 cups water
  • pineapple
  • green pepper
  1. Put chicken in pan.
  2. Mix Russian dressing, apricot preserves, onions soup mix and water together and pour over chicken.
  3. Cover with foil.
  4. Bake covered at 350 for 1 hour.
  5. Uncover and bake another 30 minutes with pineapple and green pepper.
