Bacon Chicken Roll ups

  • 12 bacon strips
  • 6 4 oz. chicken breasts
  • 8 oz. cr. cheese
  • 1 sweet onion sliced
  1. Cook bacon until cooked but not crisp.
  2. Remove to paper towels.
  3. Flatten chicken to 1/8 in thickness.
  4. Spread cr. cheese down the center of each breast.
  5. Top with onion.
  6. Roll up from a long side; tuck ends in.
  7. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  8. Wrap 2 bacon strips around each piece of chicken; secure with toothpicks.
  9. Place in greased pan and bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
  10. When I did this I would undercook the bacon because it was overcooked.
