Southwestern Pan Fried Chicken

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cups Cheddar
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • 3 egg whites
  • 2 chopped tomato
  • 2 tsp cilantro
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 4 gr. onion
  • 1/2 cups cream
  1. Process breasts in whites in processor.
  2. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Shape into patties.
  4. Dip in 2 cups flour, 1 cups milk, and 4 cups bread crumbs.
  5. Brown on both sides.
  6. Cook in oven 7 minutes at 350.
  7. Top with salsa.
