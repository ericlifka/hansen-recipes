Waldorf Salad

  1. 3 large apples, unpeeled and diced, can use 1 Granny Smith, 1 Red Delicious apple, 1
  2. Golden Delicious or Fuji or Brae burn
  3. 1/2 cups chopped celery
  4. 1/2 cups nuts, chopped
  5. For dressing:
  6. 1/4 cups mayo
  7. 1/4 cups cool whip
  8. 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  9. 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • Combine mayo, sugar and lemon juice.
  • Mix in celery and nuts, Stir well.
  • Gently fold in cool whip.
  • Spoon over fruit mix and gently stir to combine.
  • Cover and chill 2 hours.
  • Depending on the size of the apples, you may need to double the amount of dressing.
