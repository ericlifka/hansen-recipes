Cole Slaw

  1. 1 head cabbage
  2. 1 cups sugar
  3. 3/4 cups oil
  4. 1 cups vinegar
  5. 1 tsp each dry mustard and celery seed
  6. 2 tsp sugar
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  • Grate a head of cabbage, add sugar and let sit for 1 hour.
  • Boil together: oil, vinegar, mustard, celery, 2 tsp sugar, and salt.
  • Pour dressing over cabbage and toss together.
  • Refrigerate for 24 hours before serving.
