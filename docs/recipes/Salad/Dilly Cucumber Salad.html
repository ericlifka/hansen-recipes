Dilly Cucumber Salad

  1. 3 cups thinly sliced cukes
  2. 1 small red pepper, julienned
  3. 1/2 cups sweet onion, diced
  4. 1/3 cups sugar
  5. 1/4 cups white wine vinegar
  6. 1 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 1 tsp dill seed
  • In large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
