Dilly Cucumber Salad

  • 3 cups thinly sliced cukes
  • 1 small red pepper, julienned
  • 1/2 cups sweet onion, diced
  • 1/3 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cups white wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dill seed
  1. In large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
