Pistachio Salad

  1. 1 20 oz. can crushed pineapple, undrained
  2. 1 small pkg. instant pistachio pudding
  3. 2 cups mini marshmallows
  4. 8 oz. cool whip
  5. 1/2 cups nuts (optional)
  • Stir dry pudding mix and pineapple and juice together.
  • Add marshmallow and cool whip.
  • Refrigerate.
  • Keeps well and serves 8-10.
