Macaroni Salad

  1. 1 lb small macaroni
  2. 1 cups celery diced
  3. 1 can sliced black olives
  4. 1 jar pimentos
  5. 1/2 cups dill pickles diced
  6. 1 cups cheddar cheese, diced fairly small
  7. 1 small onion diced
  8. Precooked chicken breast, cut into small pieces (optional)
  9. Dressing:
  10. 1 cups mayo
  11. 1 cups sour cream
  12. 1 tsp garlic powder, pepper
  13. 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  14. 1 Tbsp mustard
  15. 1 tsp horseradish (can be omitted)
  16. 1 tsp celery salt.
  • Cook 8 oz. or more small macaroni, drain, cool.
  • Add 1 cups celery, small can sliced black olives, small jar pimento, 1/2 cups cut up dills, 1 c.
  • cut up in small chunks cheddar cheese (or colby), onion to taste (1/2 cups or so).
  • You can add chicken or turkey instead of cheese.
  • Mix dressing ingredients and put over the rest of the ingredients.
  • May top with parsley.
