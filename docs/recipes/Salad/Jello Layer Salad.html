Jello Layer Salad

  • 1 small package each:
  • blackberry jello
  • cherry jello
  • lime jello
  • lemon jello
  • orange jello
  • orange pineapple jello
  • strawberry jello
  • 1 1/2 cups evaporated milk.
  1. Add blackberry jello to 1 1/2 cups boiling water.
  2. Mix and dissolve well, put in dish and let set.
  3. Mix cherry jello with 1 cups boiling water.
  4. Let cool and add 1/2 cups evaporated milk.
  5. Put on first layer.
  6. Mix lime jello and 1 1/2 cups boiling water.
  7. Mix lemon jello and 1 cups boiling water.
  8. Let cool and add 1/2 cups evaporated milk.
  9. Mix orange jello and add 1 1/2 cups boiling water.
  10. Mix orange pineapple jello with 1 cups boiling water.
  11. Let cool and add 1/2 cups evaporated milk.
  12. Mix strawberry jello and 1 1/2 cups boiling water.
  13. Make sure each layer is mixed and dissolved well and set before adding the next layer.
  14. Use a 9 x 13 pan.
