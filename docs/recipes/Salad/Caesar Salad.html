Caesar Salad

  1. 2 cloves garlic
  2. 1 head romaine lettuce
  3. 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  4. 1 Tbsp anchovy paste
  5. 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  6. 1 egg or 2 Tbsp liquid egg stuff
  7. 1/3 cups olive oil
  8. 1/2 cups parmesan cheese
  9. 1 cups caesar croutons (or onion and garlic)
  • In bowl mix garlic, salt, pepper, anchovies, lemon juice, egg.
  • Add olive oil and mix.
  • Add lettuce and toss.
  • Add cheese and croutons.
