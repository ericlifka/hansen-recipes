Outback Steakhouse Caesar Salad Dressing

  1. 1 cups mayo
  2. 1/4 cups egg substitute
  3. 1/4 cups parmesan cheese
  4. 2 Tbsp water
  5. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  6. 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
  7. 1 Tbsp anchovy paste
  8. 2 cloves garlic
  9. 2 tsp sugar
  10. 1/2 tsp pepper
  11. 1/4 tsp salt
  12. 1/4 tsp dried parsley flakes, crushed
  • Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.
  • Beat with mixer for about 1 minutes.
  • Cover bowl and chill for several hours for flavors to develop.
