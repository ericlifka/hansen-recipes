Vegetable Pasta Salad

  1. 4 cups tricolor spiral pasta
  2. 16 oz. salsa
  3. 2 cups thawed frozen corn
  4. 2 cups slice halved cuke
  5. 1 cups halved cherry tomato
  6. 1 med. red onion
  7. 8 oz. no salt tomato sauce
  8. 2 Tbsp fresh parsley
  9. 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Cook pasta and drain.
  • Combine pasta with remaining ingredients and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
