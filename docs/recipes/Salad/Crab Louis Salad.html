Crab Louis Salad

  1. Lettuce
  2. hard boiled eggs
  3. pitted, slice black olives
  4. green pepper rings
  5. Crab meat, lump with cartilage removed or fake crab
  6. celery
  7. Dressing:
  8. 1 cups mayo
  9. 1/4 cups chili sauce
  10. 1/4 cups whip cream or half and half
  11. 1 1/2 Tbsp green pepper
  12. 3 Tbsp onion
  13. 1 egg (boiled, grated) (can skip egg if desired)
  14. 1 tsp Worcestershire
  15. 2 tsp parsley
  16. 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  17. salt and pepper
  • Mix the dressing together and chill.
  • Put lettuce on plate, top with crab, eggs, olives and green pepper and celery.
  • You can use a whole leaf lettuce on plate, and then top with rest of lettuce, crab, etc.
  • Serve with dressing that guests can add to salad.
