Black Bean Salsa (Paula Deen)

  1. 2 15 oz. cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  2. 17 oz. frozen corn, thawed
  3. 2 large tomatoes, seeded and diced
  4. 1 avocado, peeled and diced
  5. 1 onion, diced
  6. 1/8-1/4 cups cilantro leaves, chopped
  7. 2 Tbsp lime juice
  8. 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  9. Salt and pepper
  • Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  • Cover and chill overnight.
  • Taste and add salt, pepper or more lime juice as necessary.
  • Serve with chips as appetizer or with grilled chicken as meal.
