Olive Garden Artichoke Dip

  • 1 can artichoke hearts chopped
  • 1/2 cups chopped spinach
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/2 cups Parmesan
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Dash salt, pepper, garlic powder
  1. Boil spinach and hearts in a cup of water for 10 min.
  2. Drain.
  3. Heat cream cheese and add spinach and hearts.
  4. Stir.
  5. Mix in rest of stuff.
