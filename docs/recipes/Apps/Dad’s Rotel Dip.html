Dad’s Rotel Dip

  • 8 oz. Velveeta, cut into 1” cubes
  • 8 oz. cream cheese cut into pieces
  • 1 10-14 oz. can Rotel diced tomatoes and green chilies
  1. Put both cheeses into a 1 1/2 qt. microwave dish.
  2. Microwave for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir and return to microwave for 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, drain Rotel saving the juice.
  5. Remove the dish from the microwave, stir, add tomatoes and some of the juice.
  6. Heat 3 minutes.
  7. The more juice you add the hotter it will be.
