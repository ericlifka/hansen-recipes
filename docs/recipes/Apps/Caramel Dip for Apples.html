Caramel Dip for Apples

  1. 8 oz. cream cheese
  2. 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  3. 3/4 cups brown sugar
  4. 1 tsp vanilla
  5. 1 cups caramel ice cream topping (1/2 pt. jar)
  6. Up to 2 tsp milk or cream
  • Mix cheese, sugars, vanilla and topping until smooth.
  • If desired thin with milk.
  • Serve at room temperature.
  • Store in refrigerator.
  • Can also add nuts.
  • Everybody likes best!
