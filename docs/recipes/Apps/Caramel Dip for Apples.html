Caramel Dip for Apples

  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cups caramel ice cream topping (1/2 pt. jar)
  • Up to 2 tsp milk or cream
  1. Mix cheese, sugars, vanilla and topping until smooth.
  2. If desired thin with milk.
  3. Serve at room temperature.
  4. Store in refrigerator.
  5. Can also add nuts.
  6. Everybody likes best!
