Fiesta Baked Cheese Dip

  1. 2 8 oz. cr. cheese
  2. 8 oz. mexican shredded cheese blend
  3. 4 oz. can green chilies
  4. 1-1/4 cups sour cream, divided
  5. 1/4-1/2 tsp ground red pepper
  • Beat cream cheese in bowl on medium speed until softened.
  • Reserve 1/4 cups shredded cheese for garnish.
  • Add remaining shredded cheese, chilies, 1/2 cups sour cream and ground red pepper to cream cheese; mix well.
  • Spoon into 10 inch pie plate or quiche dish.
  • Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until lightly browned around edge.
  • Spread remaining 3/4 cups sour cream over baked cheese spread.
  • Sprinkle with 1/4 cups shredded cheese.
  • Garnish with bell pepper.
  • Serve with tortilla chips.
