Hot and Spicy Chex Mix

  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire
  • 2-3 tsp hot pepper sauce
  • 1 1/4 tsp season salt
  • 2 2/3 cups each corn, rice, wheat chex
  • 1 cups nuts, pretzels, and bite size cheese crackers
  1. Melt margarine in pan at 250.
  2. Stir in seasonings.
  3. Gradually add cereals, nuts, pretzels, and cheese crackers.
  4. Stir to coat evenly.
  5. Bake 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
