Swiss Spread Canapés

  1. 1 cups grated Swiss cheese
  2. 1/4 cups crisp crumbled bacon
  3. 1/4 cups minced onions
  4. 1/4 cups mayo
  5. 2 Tbsp chives
  6. 1 tsp worcestershire
  7. Black olives, halved
  8. 20-25 cocktail rye or white sandwich bread with crusts cut off
  • Heat oven to 375.
  • Combine all ingredients except olives.
  • Mix well.
  • Spread about 1 Tbsp cheese mix on each slice.
  • Heat for 10-15 minutes.
  • Place olive on top.
