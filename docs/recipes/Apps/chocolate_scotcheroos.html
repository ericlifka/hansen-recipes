Chocolate Scotcheroos

  • 1 cups light corn syrup
  • 1 cups sugar, peanut butter
  • 6 cups Rice Krispies
  • 1 pkg. (6 oz, 1 c) semisweet chocolate
  • 1 cups butterscotch morsels
  • PAM butter spray
  1. Place corn syrup and sugar into pan.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until sugar dissolves and mix begins to boil.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in peanut butter.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Add rice krispies.
  7. Stir until well coated.
  8. Press mix into 9 x 13 pan coated with PAM butter spray.
  9. Melt chocolate and butterscotch together in small pan over low heat stirring constantly.
  10. Spread over cereal mix.
  11. Let stand until firm.
  12. Cut into bars.
