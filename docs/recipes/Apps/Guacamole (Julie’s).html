Guacamole (Julie’s)

  1. 2 avocados--very ripe peeled and pitted
  2. 1/4 to 1/2 tsp garlic salt per avocado
  3. 1/4 cups picante sauce or salsa (can use more)
  4. 1 tomato, diced small
  • Mash the avocado with a potato masher.
  • Add the rest of the stuff and mix.
  • Serve with tortilla chips.
