Fruit Punch

  1. 3 cups water
  2. 1 cups sugar
  3. 12 oz. frozen orange juice
  4. 23 oz. pineapple juice
  5. 3 mashed bananas
  6. Juice of 1 lemon
  7. 7-up or champagne
  • Cook water and sugar until syrupy, about 3 minutes.
  • Add rest of ingredients and freeze.
  • You can make it a week or 2 ahead.
  • Remove from freezer 2 hours before serving.
  • Let set in container and break apart as it begins to thaw.
  • To serve, put in punch bowl and add 2 qts. 7-up or 1 qt. 7-up and 1 qt. champagne or 2 qt. champagne.
