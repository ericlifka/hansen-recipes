Hot and Spicy Chex Mix

  1. 1/4 cups butter
  2. 1 Tbsp Worcestershire
  3. 2-3 tsp hot pepper sauce
  4. 1 1/4 tsp season salt
  5. 2 2/3 cups each corn, rice, wheat chex
  6. 1 cups nuts, pretzels, and bite size cheese crackers
  • Melt margarine in pan at 250.
  • Stir in seasonings.
  • Gradually add cereals, nuts, pretzels, and cheese crackers.
  • Stir to coat evenly.
  • Bake 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
