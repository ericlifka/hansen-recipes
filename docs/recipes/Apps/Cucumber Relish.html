Cucumber Relish

  • 18 large cukes
  • 2 green peppers
  • 1 bunch celery (4-5)
  • 6 large onions
  • 2 red peppers or 1 big jar of pimento
  1. Grind cukes and onions.
  2. Cut up celery.
  3. Drain stuff.
  4. Put 1/3-1/2 cups salt.
  5. Soak overnight.
  6. Drain well.
  7. Drain pimento.
  8. Could take several hours.
  9. Brine is 1 cups vinegar, 1 cups sugar and 1 tsp turmeric.
  10. Boil all together for at least 10 minutes.
  11. Cook lids on stove.
  12. Fill jars.
  13. Microwave jar and then seal immediately.
