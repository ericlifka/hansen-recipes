Paula Deen’s Artichoke Dip

  • 2 cans artichoke hearts
  • 1 pkg. spinach, thawed and squeezed
  • 1/2 cups mayo
  • 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1 cups Parmesan
  • Pepper jack cheese (about 1/2-1 c.)
  1. Pulse the hearts in food processor.
  2. Add rest of ingredients and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
  3. I generally use 1 can of hearts and half a pkg. of spinach since some of you don’t like spinach much.
  4. I also tend to use 3/4 cups parmesan and 1 cups pepper jack.
