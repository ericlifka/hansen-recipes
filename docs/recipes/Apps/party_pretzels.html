Party pretzels

  • 32 oz mini pretzels
  • 1 pkg. hidden valley ranch
  • 1/2 bottle orville redenbacher popcorn oil
  • 1-3 Tbsp dill weed
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp onion powder
  1. Mix oil and dry ingredients.
  2. Dill, garlic powder, and onion powder are to taste (can do more or less).
  3. Mix pretzels with stuff in trash bag, toss, spread on cookie sheet and dry overnight.
