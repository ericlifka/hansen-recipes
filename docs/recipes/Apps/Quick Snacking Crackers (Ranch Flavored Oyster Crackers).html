Quick Snacking Crackers (Ranch Flavored Oyster Crackers)

  • 3/4 cups oil
  • 1 10z. package milk base ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cups grated parmesan cheese
  • 16 oz. oyster crackers
  1. Combine first 3 ingredients.
  2. Toss with crackers.
  3. Let stand several hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in tightly covered container.
