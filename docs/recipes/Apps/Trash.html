Trash

  • 1 stick butter
  • 12 oz. semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cups peanut butter
  • 1 large box rice chex
  • 1 lb. powdered sugar
  1. Put the butter, chocolate chips and peanut butter in the microwave for 3 minutes.
  2. Remove dish from microwave and with a wooden spoon blend together.
  3. Pour Rice chex into bowl and pour the chocolate mix over the cereal.
  4. Gently fold the mix until all Chex are covered.
  5. Take a medium white kitchen trash bag and pour half the sugar into it.
  6. Pour the chocolate mix on top.
  7. Cover with rest of sugar.
  8. Tie top of bag with a twist tie, and gently roll bag from hand to hand.
  9. Do this about 10 times.
  10. Open and the chex should be covered with sugar and taste like peanut butter cups.
