Houlihan’s Mushrooms (Top Secret Recipe)

  • 6-10 large mushrooms
  • Rondele or Alouette preblended herb cheese
  • 1 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cups milk
  1. Clean the mushrooms and remove the stems.
  2. Stuff the cap with the herb cheese.
  3. Combine flour with salt and cayenne in a small bowl.
  4. Put milk in another small bowl.
  5. Dip each mushroom in the milk, and then in the flour. Do this twice.
  6. Freeze for at least 3 hours.
  7. Heat your deep fryer with oil to 350, fry 8-10 minutes until outside is golden brown.
  8. Drain.
  9. Let sit for a minute before eating as the inside is very hot!
