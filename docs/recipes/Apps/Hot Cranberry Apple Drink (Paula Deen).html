Hot Cranberry Apple Drink (Paula Deen)

  • 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 Tbsp whole cloves (in tea ball)
  • 8 cups cranberry juice
  • 6 cups apple juice or cider
  1. I used 1 64 oz. thing of cranberry juice, and apple cider.
  2. Put all in a big crock pot, or pan on stovetop.
  3. Heat on low for a few hours then serve.
