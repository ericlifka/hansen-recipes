Nacho Cheese Dip

  • 1 lb. each hamburger, hot sausage
  • 2 lb. Velveeta
  • 4 cans nacho cheese soup
  • 2 cans cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  1. Brown hamburger and prepare taco seasoning according to package.
  2. Brown sausage and drain.
  3. Cut velveeta into chunks.
  4. In crock pot layer meat on bottom, velveeta and cheese soups.
  5. Cook on high 2-4 hours, low for 4-6.
  6. Stir occasionally.
  7. Serve with nacho chips.
