Fritos Hot Bean Dip

  1. 15 oz. pinto beans
  2. 4 bottled jalapeno slices
  3. 1 Tbsp juice from bottle
  4. 1/2 tsp salt
  5. 1/2 tsp sugar
  6. 1/4 tsp onion powder
  7. 1/4 tsp paprika
  8. 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  9. 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Mix in processor until smooth.
  • Cover and chill at least an hour.
