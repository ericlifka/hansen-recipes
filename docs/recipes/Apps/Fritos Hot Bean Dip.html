Fritos Hot Bean Dip

  • 15 oz. pinto beans
  • 4 bottled jalapeno slices
  • 1 Tbsp juice from bottle
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  1. Mix in processor until smooth.
  2. Cover and chill at least an hour.
