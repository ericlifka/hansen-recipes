Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms (Paula Deen)

  • 24 fresh mushrooms, stems removed
  • 10 oz. frozen chopped spinach, drained
  • 4 oz. cream cheese
  • 2-4 oz. feta
  • 1/2 cups finely chopped green onion
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cups Parmesan
  1. Heat oven to 350.
  2. Wipe mushroom caps clean with damp paper towel.
  3. Thaw spinach, squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
  4. In bowl combine all ingredients except mushrooms and Parmesan.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Fill mushroom caps with mix and place on cookie sheet.
  7. Sprinkle parmesan on top.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  9. When I made this, I used 1 pkg. mushrooms, 1/4-1/2 a box of spinach, 4 oz. feta and cream cheese as Cecelia likes feta.
