Paula Deen’s Artichoke Dip

  1. 2 cans artichoke hearts
  2. 1 pkg. spinach, thawed and squeezed
  3. 1/2 cups mayo
  4. 1/2 cups sour cream
  5. 1 cups Parmesan
  6. Pepper jack cheese (about 1/2-1 c.)
  • Pulse the hearts in food processor.
  • Add rest of ingredients and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
  • I generally use 1 can of hearts and half a pkg. of spinach since some of you don’t like spinach much.
  • I also tend to use 3/4 cups parmesan and 1 cups pepper jack.
