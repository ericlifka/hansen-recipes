Holiday Fudge

  • 2/3 cups evaporated milk
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 2/3 cups sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cups nuts, chopped
  • 1 tsp peppermint extract
  • 4 cups crushed peppermint candy, optional
  1. In pan over medium heat, bring to a boil the milk, butter, sugar and salt.
  2. Cook, stirring constantly 4-5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in marshmallows, chocolate, extract and nuts.
  5. Stir briskly until marshmallows melt and mix is thoroughly blended.
  6. Pour into buttered 10 x 7 dish.
  7. Sprinkle candy on top.
  8. Let cool, then cut into bite-size squares.
