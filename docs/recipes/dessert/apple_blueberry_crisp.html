Apple Blueberry Crisp

  • 6 cups peeled, cored, sliced apples
  • 1 cups blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice and water
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cups oatmeal
  • 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  1. Spread apple slices in lightly greased 8‚Äù dish.
  2. Top with layer of berries; sprinkle lemon juice and water on.
  3. Combine sugar, flour, oats, nuts and cinnamon.
  4. Cut in butter until mix is crumbly; sprinkle mix over fruit.
  5. Bake at 375 about 45 minutes or until topping is browned.
