Jello Dirt Cups

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 pkg. chocolate instant pudding
  • 8 oz. cool whip
  • 16 oz. oreos
  • 8-10 7 oz. plastic cups
  1. Pour milk into large bowl, add pudding mix and beat until well blended.
  2. Let sit 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in cool whip and half of the cookies.
  4. Place 1 Tbsp cookies into the cups.
  5. Fill cups 3/4 full with pudding mix.
  6. Top with remaining cookies.
  7. Refrigerate 1 hour.
  8. Decorate with gummy worms, frogs, candy flowers or nuts.
