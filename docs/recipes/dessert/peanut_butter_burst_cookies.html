Peanut Butter Burst Cookies

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cups butter, soft
  • 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 12 oz. peanut butter morsels
  1. In bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.
  2. In bowl combine butter, brown sugar, sugar and vanilla; beat at medium until creamy.
  3. Add egg; beat well.
  4. Gradually add flour.
  5. Stir in peanut butter morsels.
  6. Drop by teaspoons onto cookie sheet.
  7. Bake at 375 for 7-9 minutes.
