Tapioca Pudding

  • 1/3 cups sugar
  • 3 Tbsp tapioca
  • 2 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  1. Mix sugar, tapioca, milk and egg in pan.
  2. Let stand 5 minutes.
  3. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly until mix comes to a full boil.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Stir in vanilla.
  6. Put in serving dishes.
  7. Serve warm or cold.
