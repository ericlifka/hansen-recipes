Chocolate Turtle Cheesecake

  • 1- 7 oz. pkg. caramels
  • 1/4 cups evaporated milk
  • 3/4 cups chopped pecans, divided
  • 1 9” chocolate crumb pie crust
  • 2- 3 oz. packages cream cheese, soft
  • 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 (3.9) pkg. chocolate instant pudding
  • 1/2 cups fudge topping
  1. To make a homemade crust use 1 1/2 cups chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs with 3 Tbsp melted butter.
  2. Press into 9” pie plate.
  3. Bake at 350 for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Place caramels and evaporated milk in a pan.
  5. Heat over medium low heat stirring continually, until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in 1/2 cups chopped pecans.
  7. Pour into pie crust.
  8. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and milk in blender.
  9. Process until smooth.
  10. Add pudding mix; process for about 30 seconds longer.
  11. Pour pudding mix over caramel layer, covering evenly.
  12. Chill, loosely covered, until set, about 15 minutes.
  13. Drizzle fudge topping over pudding layer in a decorative pattern.
  14. Sprinkle top with remaining pecans.
  15. Chill loosely covered until serving time.
