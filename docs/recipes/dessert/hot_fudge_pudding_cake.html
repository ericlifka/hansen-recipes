Hot Fudge Pudding Cake

  • 1 1/4 cups sugar, divided
  • 1 cups flour
  • 7 Tbsp cocoa, divided
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/3 cups butter
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups hot water
  1. Heat oven to 350.
  2. In bowl combine 3/4 cups sugar, flour, 3 Tbsp cocoa, baking powder and salt.
  3. Blend in milk, butter and vanilla; beat until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into 9 x 9 pan.
  5. In small bowl combine 1/2 cups sugar, brown sugar, 4 Tbsp cocoa; sprinkle over batter.
  6. Pour hot water over top.
  7. Do not stir.
  8. Bake 40 minutes or until center is set.
  9. Let stand 15 minutes.
  10. Serve.
