Philly Free Mini Cheesecakes

  • 12 vanilla wafers
  • 3-8 oz. pkgs. cream cheese fat free, soft
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cups fruit or reduced calorie pie filling
  1. Place wafers, flat side down on bottom of each of 12 paper-lined muffin cups.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla on medium speed until well blended.
  3. Add eggs; mix just until blended.
  4. Do not over beat after adding eggs.
  5. Pour over wafers, filling each cup.
  6. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes.
  7. Cool.
  8. Refrigerate several hours or overnight.
  9. Top with fruit.
