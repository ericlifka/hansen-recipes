Healthy Coconut Cream Pie

  • Crust:
  • 2 1/2 cups cheerios
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cups butter melted
  • Filling:
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cups canned light coconut milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 4 serving size vanilla instant pudding mix
  • 1/2 cups coconut
  • 2 cups cool whip
  1. toast additional coconut
  2. For crust heat oven to 350.
  3. Crush cereal and mix with sugar.
  4. Stir in butter until mixed.
  5. Press in bottom and up side of 9‚Äù pie plate.
  6. Bake 10 minutes; cool completely--30 minutes.
  7. In bowl mix milk, coconut milk and vanilla.
  8. With whisk , beat in pudding mix until well blended and slightly thickened.
  9. Stir in 1/2 cups coconut.
  10. Gently stir in 1/2 cups cool whip.
  11. Spoon and spread mix into crust.
  12. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or until set.
  13. To serve top with remaining cool whip and sprinkle with toasted coconut.
