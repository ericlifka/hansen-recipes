Zucchini Bread

  • 1 cups oil
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 eggs2
  • c. grated raw, peeled zucchini
  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  1. Mix oil and sugar.
  2. Beat in eggs, one at a time.
  3. Add rest of ingredients.
  4. Bake in 2 greased loaf pans 1 hour at 325.
