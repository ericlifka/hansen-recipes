Hidden Valley Ranch Crescent Rolls

  1. 1 8 oz. tube crescent rolls
  2. 1 pkg. Ranch Milk Recipe Salad Dressing Mix
  3. 1 egg yolk
  4. 1 tsp water
  • Separate dough.
  • Beat yolk with water.
  • Brush on dough.
  • Sprinkle with dressing mix.
  • Roll up in crescent shape.
  • Brush with egg mix.
  • Bake on greased sheet at 375 for 11-13 minutes.
