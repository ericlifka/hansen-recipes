Cheese Garlic Biscuits

  • 4 cups Bisquick
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 4 oz. cheese
  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 1 Tbsp parsley
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  1. Stir bisquick, milk and cheddar together.
  2. Drop by tablespoons on greased cookie sheet.
  3. Bake at 450 for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Brush butter, parsley, and garlic powder over.
