Date Bread

  1. 1 cups chopped dates (hand chop)
  2. 3/4 cups nuts
  3. 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. 1/2 tsp salt
  5. 1/4 cups butter
  6. 3/4 cups boiling water
  7. 2 eggs
  8. 1 tsp vanilla
  9. 1 cups sugar
  10. 1 1/2 cups flour
  • Combine nuts, dates, soda, salt in bowl.
  • Add butter and water.
  • Let sit 15 minutes.
  • Stir.
  • Beat eggs slightly.
  • Add vanilla.
  • Stir in sugar and flour.
  • Add to date mix.
  • Do not overmix.
  • Grease pans bake at 350 for 1 hour.
  • Makes 2 loaves.
