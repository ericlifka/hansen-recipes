Southern Living Sweet Potato Biscuits

  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 Tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 cups butter
  • 3/4-1 cups buttermilk
  • 1 cups sweet potato, mashed
  1. Combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  2. Cut in butter.
  3. Stir together potato and buttermilk.
  4. Turn dough onto floured surface.
  5. Roll to 1/2” thick.
  6. Grease cookie sheet.
  7. Use a 3” biscuit cutter.
  8. Bake at 425 for 10-15 minutes.
