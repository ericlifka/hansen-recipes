Basic Tomato Sauce

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 28 oz. cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • A pinch of crushed red pepper
  1. Heat oil in pan over medium low heat.
  2. Add onion and cook, stirring often, until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and crushed red pepper; cook for 30-60 seconds.
  4. Add tomatoes, tomato paste and oregano; mash with a potato masher.
  5. Bring to a boil.
  6. Simmer, uncovered, over low heat, stirring frequently, until the tomatoes cook down to a thick mass, 45-55 minutes.
  7. Season with pepper.
