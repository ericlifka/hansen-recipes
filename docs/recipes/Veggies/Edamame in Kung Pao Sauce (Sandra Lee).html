Edamame in Kung Pao Sauce (Sandra Lee)

  1. 1 lb. frozen, shelled edamame
  2. 2 Tbsp canola oil
  3. 1/2 cups diced carrots
  4. 1/2 cups chopped onion
  5. 1/2 cups Kung Pao sauce
  6. 2 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add edamame and return to a boil.
  • Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Drain and set aside.
  • In frying pan over medium heat add the oil and cook the carrots and onions until tender.
  • In bowl stir together kung pao sauce, soy sauce and red pepper flakes (if desired).
  • Add in edamame, carrots, and onions.
  • Toss until vegetables are coated.
  • Serve.
