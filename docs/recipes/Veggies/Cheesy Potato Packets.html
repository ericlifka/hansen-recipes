Cheesy Potato Packets

  1. 16 oz. frozen Oâ€™Brien potatoes
  2. 1 Tbsp v. oil
  3. 1/2 tsp seasoning salt
  4. 1 cups cheddar
  • Spray an 18 x 13 inch piece of foil with cooking spray.
  • Place potatoes on foil.
  • Drizzle with oil; sprinkle with salt.
  • Wrap foil around potatoes.
  • Put on grill with medium heat for 30 minutes turning once.
  • Open packet; sprinkle cheese over potatoes.
  • Cover loosely and let stand for 4-5 minutes until cheese is melted.
