Eggplant Parmesan

  1. 2 eggplants (about 2 lbs... total)
  2. 3 egg whites
  3. 3 Tbsp water
  4. 1 cups bread crumbs
  5. 1/2 cups Parmesan, divided
  6. 1/4 cups basil
  7. 2 1/2 cups basic tomato sauce (recipe to follow) or prepared marinara sauce
  8. 3/4 cups mozzarella cheese
  9. 1/2 tsp salt & pepper
  • Preheat oven to 400.
  • Coat 2 baking sheets with cooking spray.
  • Coat an 8 x 11 1/2 inch baking dish with PAM.
  • To prepare eggplant:
  • Cut eggplants crosswise into 1/4‚Äù thick slices.
  • Whisk egg whites with water in a shallow dish until frothy.
  • Combine bread crumbs, 1/4 cups Parmesan, salt and pepper in another shallow dish.
  • Dip the eggplant slices into the egg whites and then coat with the bread crumb mix.
  • Arrange the eggplant slices in a single layer on the baking sheets.
  • Bake for 15 minutes, turn them over and bake until crisp and golden about 15 minutes more.
  • To assemble casserole:
  • Stir basil into tomato sauce.
  • Spread about 1/2 cups of the sauce in the prepared baking dish.
  • Arrange half the eggplant slices over the sauce, overlapping slightly.
  • Spoon 1 cups of the remaining sauce over the eggplant and sprinkle with half the mozzarella.
  • Layer on the remaining eggplant and top with the remaining sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan.
  • Bake the casserole, at 400 uncovered, until the sauce bubbles and the top is golden, 15 to 25 minutes.
