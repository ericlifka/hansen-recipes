Asparagus Pasta with Toasted Pecans

  • 16 oz. penne pasta
  • 1 bunch asparagus (1 lb.)
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • 1 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt and pepper
  • 3 Tbsp basil
  • 3/4 cups Parmesan, divided
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 cups pecan halves, toasted and divided
  • 1 red pepper, seeded, chopped
  1. Prepare pasta, rinse and drain.
  2. Cut asparagus into 1 1/2” pieces.
  3. Sauté asparagus in hot oil over medium heat for 4 minutes.
  4. Stir in red pepper and garlic, cook, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in chicken broth.
  6. Bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes until asparagus is crisp tender.
  8. Stir in salt and pepper.
  9. Toss together pasta, asparagus mix, basil, 1/2 cups cheese, butter and 1/2 cups pecans.
  10. Sprinkle evenly with remaining cheese and pecans.
