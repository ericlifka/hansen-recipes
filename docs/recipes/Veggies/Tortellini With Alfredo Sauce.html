Tortellini With Alfredo Sauce

  1. 16 oz. tortellini (cheese)
  2. 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  3. 1 clove garlic
  4. 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  5. 1/2 cups Parmesan
  6. salt and pepper
  • Cook tortellini according to package directions.
  • Drain.
  • Transfer tortellini to a saut√© pan over medium-high heat and immediately add cream, garlic, Italian seasoning and 1/4 cups Parmesan cheese.
  • Bring to a boil and heat, stirring occasionally, 7-10 minutes until sauce has thickened.
  • Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  • Top with remaining 1/4 cups Parmesan and serve.
