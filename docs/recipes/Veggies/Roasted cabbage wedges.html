Roasted cabbage wedges

  1. 3 T Olive Oil
  2. 1 Cabbage sliced into 1 inch thick rounds
  3. Salt and Pepper
  4. 1 t caraway or fennel seed
  • Preheat oven to 400.
  • Brush a rimmed baking sheet with olive oil.
  • Place cabbage rounds in a single layer on sheet and brush with olive oil.
  • Season with coarse salt and ground pepper, and sprinkle with caraway or fennel seeds.
  • Roast until cabbage is tender and edges are golden, 40-45 minutes.
