Eggplant Casserole 2

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1 onion
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1/4 cups water
  • 1/4 cups bell pepper
  • 1/4 cups grated cheese
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cups bread crumbs, toasted
  1. salt and pepper to taste
  2. beef or sausage, browned
  3. Split eggplant in half, remove inside.
  4. Place butter in pan, add onion and eggplant.
  5. Start cooking, then add water, celery and bell pepper.
  6. Let cook until veggies are tender.
  7. Then add tomatoes and cheese.
  8. Stir in bread crumbs and place in empty eggplant shells.
  9. Garnish with chopped tomatoes and bake until done.
