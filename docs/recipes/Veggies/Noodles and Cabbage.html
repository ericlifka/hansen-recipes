Noodles and Cabbage

  1. 1/4 cups butter
  2. 1 tsp each sugar, dill weed, and wine vinegar
  3. 4 cups shredded cabbage
  4. 1/2 cups onion
  5. 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  6. 6 oz. cooked egg noodles
  • In skillet melt butter.
  • Add onion and cabbage.
  • Cook, stirring for 5 minutes.
  • Stir in noodles and spices.
  • Serve.
