Noodles and Cabbage

  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1 tsp each sugar, dill weed, and wine vinegar
  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cups onion
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • 6 oz. cooked egg noodles
  1. In skillet melt butter.
  2. Add onion and cabbage.
  3. Cook, stirring for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in noodles and spices.
  5. Serve.
