Grilled Zucchini

  • 2 Zucchini
  • 2 Yellow Squash
  • 2 onions
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese
  • Seasoning Salt
  • 1 can stewed tomato
  1. Layer zucchini, yellow squash, onions, parmesan cheese, seasoning salt, and stewed tomato.
  2. Make another layer of stuff.
  3. Bake at 350 for 1 hour uncovered, or grill for 45-60 minutes.
