Grilled Zucchini

  1. 2 Zucchini
  2. 2 Yellow Squash
  3. 2 onions
  4. Shredded Parmesan cheese
  5. Seasoning Salt
  6. 1 can stewed tomato
  • Layer zucchini, yellow squash, onions, parmesan cheese, seasoning salt, and stewed tomato.
  • Make another layer of stuff.
  • Bake at 350 for 1 hour uncovered, or grill for 45-60 minutes.
