Eggplant Casserole 1

  1. 1 med. eggplant, cut in cubes and parboiled
  2. 1/2 cups celery
  3. 1 cups green onions
  4. 1/2 cups bell pepper
  5. 1 can stewed tomatoes, drained
  6. 5-6 slices bread, toasted & cubed
  7. sharp cheese, cut into small pieces
  8. 1 lb. hot sausage, cooked, drained, optional
  • Grease 2 qt. dish.
  • Cover bottom with bread crumbs, then add eggplant, celery, bell pepper, onions, sausage and tomatoes.
  • Cover and bake at 350 for 45 min.
  • Add remaining crumbs and cheese.
  • Return to over 3-4 minutes until cheese melts.
