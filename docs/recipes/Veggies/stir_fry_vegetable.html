Stir Fry Vegetable

  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 1 large onion
  • bean sprouts
  • 2 cups mushrooms
  1. Cut the zucchini into long thin strips and cut into strips 1 to 1 1/2‚Äù long.
  2. For onion, cut like making onion rings and quarter those slices.
  3. Put 2 Tbsp of oil in a pan.
  4. Add onion and zucchini.
  5. Add mushrooms.
  6. Add 2 Tbsp soy sauce, 1 Tbsp butter, dash salt and pepper.
  7. Add bean sprouts.
  8. Cook a minute or two and serve.
