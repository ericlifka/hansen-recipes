Spinach Pie

  • 4 pkg. spinach
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 bunch dill
  • 8 oz. cr. cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups feta
  • 1 lb. phyllo
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/4 cups olive oil
  1. Mix spinach, cheese and cook in oil.
  2. For phyllo, melt butter and spread on layers of phyllo.
  3. Layer half the phyllo and then put spinach on and layer other half of phyllo.
  4. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
  5. (Make sure to read the phyllo label as it dries quickly.)
