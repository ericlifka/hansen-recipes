Green Bean Casserole

  • 1 can cr. of mushroom
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 4 cups green beans
  • 1 1/3 cups french fried onions
  1. Mix soup, milk, soy sauce, green beans and 2/3 cups french fried onions.
  2. Put in 1 1/2 qt. casserole.
  3. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
  4. Stir and sprinkle with rest of onions.
  5. Cook another 5 minutes.
