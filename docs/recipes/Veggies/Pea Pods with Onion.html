Pea Pods with Onion

  • 1/2 lb. fresh pea pods
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 cups onion
  • 3 Tbsp butter, divided
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. Place pea pods and water in a microwave bowl; cover and cook for 3-5 minutes until crisp tender.
  2. Meanwhile in small skillet, cook onion in 2 Tbsp butter over medium heat until crisp tender.
  3. Drain peas, add to onion.
  4. Add salt, pepper and remaining butter.
  5. Cook till heated.
