Beer Brats and Kraut

  1. 3 bottles beer
  2. 6 brats
  3. 1 large jar sauerkraut
  4. 6 buns
  5. mustard
  • Place beer in pot and heat over medium heat.
  • Place brats and kraut in beer and simmer for approximately 10 minutes.
  • With tongs, remove brats from beer and place 1 in each bun.
  • Using a slotted spoon, cover brat in sauerkraut.
  • Drizzle mustard on top of kraut.
