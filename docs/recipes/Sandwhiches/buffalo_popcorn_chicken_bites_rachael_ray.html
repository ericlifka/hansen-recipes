Buffalo Popcorn Chicken Bites (Rachael Ray)

  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 2 1/2 cups complete pancake mix, divided
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 8 tsp hot sauce (Tabasco)
  • 1 1/4 lb. chicken tenders cut into small bite size pieces
  • 1 cups good quality blue cheese dressing (Marieâ€™s brand)
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 tsp pepper
  1. In a deep sided skillet heat 1 1/2 inches of vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. If you wish to test the oil add a 1 inch cube of bread.
  3. If it turns deep golden brown in color in a count of 40 the oil is ready.
  4. While the oil is heating make the batter.
  5. In a wide mixing bowl combine 2 cups pancake mix 1 1/4 cups water and about 6 tsp hot sauce.
  6. Place the remaining 1/2 cups pancake mix in another wide mixing bowl.
  7. Arrange the batter and the bowl of plain pancake mix near the cook top and the heating oil.
  8. Line a plate with a few sheets of paper towels and keep within reach.
  9. Once the oil is heated and ready, toss the chicken pieces in 2 tsp of hot sauce and then toss in plain pancake mix, coat evenly and shake off excess.
  10. The plain dry pancake mix will help the batter stick to the chicken.
  11. Add some of the chicken to the batter.
  12. You are going to want to work in 3-4 batches coating and frying.
  13. Using a fork, toss the bites in the batter.
  14. Remove the first batch from the batter, shaking off excess as you carefully add them to the hot oil.
  15. Fry 2 minutes on one side or until deep golden brown, flip and continue to fry for another 2 minutes or until deep golden brown all over.
  16. Remove from the oil and drain on the paper towel lined plate, season with salt.
  17. Repeat until all the chicken is fried.
  18. Serve immediately with blue cheese dressing with green onions and black pepper stirred into it for dipping.
  19. Garnish platter with celery sticks.
