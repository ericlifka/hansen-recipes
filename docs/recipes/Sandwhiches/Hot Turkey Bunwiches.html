Hot Turkey Bunwiches

  1. 1 1/2 cups chopped turkey
  2. 2 hard cooked eggs, chopped
  3. 1/3 cups onion
  4. 1/2 cups celery
  5. 1/2 cups mayo
  6. 1/8 tsp salt and pepper
  7. 4 tsp butter
  8. 4 buns
  • In a bowl combine the first eight ingredients.
  • Spread butter over sides of each bun.
  • Spread about 2/3 cups turkey over each bun bottom.
  • Replace tops.
  • Wrap individually in foil Place on baking sheet.
  • Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted.
