Sloppy Joes

  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 Tbsp catsup
  • 2 Tbsp mustard
  • 1 can chicken gumbo soup
  • 6 buns
  1. In medium skillet, cook and stir hamburger and onion until meat is brown and onion is tender.
  2. Stir in catsup, mustard and soup.
  3. Simmer 15 minutes or more until mixture thickens slightly, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve with buns.
