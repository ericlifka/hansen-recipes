Pizza Sandwiches

  • 8 slices Italian bread
  • 8 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 8 slices tomato
  • 4 tsp Parmesan
  • 1/4 tsp garlic salt
  • 24 slices pepperoni
  • 1/4 cups softened butter
  1. On four slices of bread, layer one slice of mozzarella, two slices of tomato, 1 tsp Parmesan cheese, dash of garlic salt and six slices pepperoni.
  2. Top with remaining mozzarella and bread.
  3. Spread outside of sandwiches with butter.
  4. On a hot griddle, toast sandwiches for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  5. Serve with pizza sauce.
  6. I spread some pizza sauce on one side of the bread before putting the cheese on.
