Pizza Sandwiches

  1. 8 slices Italian bread
  2. 8 slices mozzarella cheese
  3. 8 slices tomato
  4. 4 tsp Parmesan
  5. 1/4 tsp garlic salt
  6. 24 slices pepperoni
  7. 1/4 cups softened butter
  • On four slices of bread, layer one slice of mozzarella, two slices of tomato, 1 tsp Parmesan cheese, dash of garlic salt and six slices pepperoni.
  • Top with remaining mozzarella and bread.
  • Spread outside of sandwiches with butter.
  • On a hot griddle, toast sandwiches for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  • Serve with pizza sauce.
  • I spread some pizza sauce on one side of the bread before putting the cheese on.
