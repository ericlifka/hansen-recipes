Grilled Tomato Cheese Sandwiches

  • 3/4 cups grape tomatoes, chopped or roma
  • 1 green onion sliced
  • 8 slices Italian bread
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices swiss cheese
  • 4 slices provolone
  • 4 slices monterey jack cheese
  • 8 tsp butter, softened
  1. Sprinkle tomato and onion over four bread slices.
  2. Layer with cheese slices; top with bread.
  3. Butter outsides of sandwiches.
  4. Toast on heated griddle for 2-3 minutes on each side until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted.
