Grilled Tomato Cheese Sandwiches

  1. 3/4 cups grape tomatoes, chopped or roma
  2. 1 green onion sliced
  3. 8 slices Italian bread
  4. 4 slices cheddar cheese
  5. 4 slices swiss cheese
  6. 4 slices provolone
  7. 4 slices monterey jack cheese
  8. 8 tsp butter, softened
  • Sprinkle tomato and onion over four bread slices.
  • Layer with cheese slices; top with bread.
  • Butter outsides of sandwiches.
  • Toast on heated griddle for 2-3 minutes on each side until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted.
