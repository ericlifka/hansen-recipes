Baked Quesadillas (Rachael Ray)

  1. 6 8‚Äù tortillas
  2. 1 cups pepper jack, shredded
  3. 1 cups cheddar, shredded
  4. 3 gr. onions
  • Paint one side of 2 tortillas with oil and place them oiled side down on a cookie sheet.
  • Mix the cheddar with green onions and divide between 2 tortillas.
  • Top with another tortilla and top each with pepper jack cheese.
  • Set the last tortilla on top and brush with oil.
  • Bake at 300 for 10 minutes.
  • Cool for 5 minutes.
  • Cut into 6 wedges.
