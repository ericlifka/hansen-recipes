Shrimp Salad Sandwich (Paula Deen)

  • 1 lb. cooked shrimp, peeled
  • 2-3 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, mince
  • 1/2 cups mayo
  • dash onion powder
  • salt and pepper
  • old bay seasoning
  • celery salt
  • 8 slices bread
  • lettuce and tomato slices
  1. Place shrimp in food processor and pulse until chopped.
  2. Transfer to bowl and add eggs, celery, and mayo.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Add seasonings to taste and stir to combine.
  5. Spread mayo on both sides of bread.
  6. Heap salad onto bread, top with lettuce and tomato.
  7. Cut sandwiches in half.
  8. Serve.
