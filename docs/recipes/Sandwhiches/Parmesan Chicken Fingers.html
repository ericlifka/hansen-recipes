Parmesan Chicken Fingers

  1. 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  2. 3/4 tsp hot pepper sauce
  3. 1 lb. chicken tenderloins
  4. 1/4 cups mayo
  5. 5 Tbsp Parmesan cheese, divided
  6. 2 Tbsp sour cream
  7. 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  8. 1/2 tsp onion powder
  9. 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  10. 3/4 cups seasoned bread crumbs
  • In resealable plastic bag, combine 1/2 cups buttermilk and pepper sauce; add chicken.
  • Seal bag, turn to coat; refrigerate 30 minutes.
  • In another bag, combine bread crumbs and 3 Tbsp cheese.
  • Drain and discard marinade.
  • Add chicken to crumb mix; seal bag and shake to coat.
  • Bake chicken in greased 15x10 pan at 450 for 15 minutes.
  • For dipping: combine 2 Tbsp cheese, mayo, sour cream, juice, powders and 3/4 cups buttermilk.
  • Cover and refrigerate.
