Grilled Chicken Fingers

  1. 2 lbs.. chicken tenders
  2. 3 Tbsp honey
  3. 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  4. 1 1/2 tsp ketchup
  5. 1/4 tsp cumin
  6. 1 tsp salt
  • In dish combine honey, lemon juice, ketchup, salt, and cumin.
  • I generally double the amount of marinade.
  • Put chicken in zip lock bag and pour marinade over.
  • Toss to coat.
  • Marinade in refrigerator at least one hour, up to 8 hours (I also do it overnight sometimes).
  • Drain chicken and discard marinade.
  • I usually cook it in the oven at 350 for 30 minutes, or you can grill them.
