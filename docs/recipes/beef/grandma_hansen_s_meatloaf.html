Grandma Hansen’ s Meatloaf

  • 1 lb. hot jimmy dean sausage
  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1/2 lb. ground pork
  • 1 onion
  • 5 slices of dry white bread broken into small pieces.
  • 1 cups milk
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sage
  1. Mix milk, eggs, salt, pepper, & sage together then pour over bread.
  2. I squish meats together with my hands and then add bread mixture and 1 whole large cut up onion.
  3. Put into greased casserole pan.
  4. Cover with lots of ketchup.
  5. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
