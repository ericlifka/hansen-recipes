Marinade for Beef Kebobs

  • 1 cups red wine vinegar and veggie oil
  • 1/4 cups vinegar and ketchup
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp rosemary or basil
  1. Combine all ingredients and marinade at least 6 hours or overnight in refrigerator.
  2. Use 3 lbs.. sirloin or top round.
  3. Grill 20-25 minutes.
