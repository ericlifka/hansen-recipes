Cole Slaw

  • 1 head cabbage
  • 1 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cups oil
  • 1 cups vinegar
  • 1 tsp each dry mustard and celery seed
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  1. Grate a head of cabbage, add sugar and let sit for 1 hour.
  2. Boil together: oil, vinegar, mustard, celery, 2 tsp sugar, and salt.
  3. Pour dressing over cabbage and toss together.
  4. Refrigerate for 24 hours before serving.
