Poppy Seed Fruit Salad

  • Fruit mix:
  • 1 cups strawberries
  • 1 cups pineapple
  • 1 cups blueberries
  • 1 cups watermelon
  • Dressing:
  • 1/4 cups honey
  • 1/4 cups limeade
  • 2 tsp poppy seeds
  1. Drizzle dressing over fruit, toss gently.
  2. Serve.
  3. Can use almost any fresh fruit.
