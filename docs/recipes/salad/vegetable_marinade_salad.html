Vegetable Marinade Salad

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1-2 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery
  • 1 small head cauliflower in pieces (night before)
  • 20 mushrooms, sliced
  • Dressing:
  • 1 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp dry mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups salad oil
  1. Mix dressing ingredients well.
  2. Put all the vegetables into a large bowl except for the mushrooms which are added the next day.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and refrigerate overnight.
  4. In the morning it will seem more watery and thatâ€™s how it should be.
  5. Add the mushrooms and remix.
