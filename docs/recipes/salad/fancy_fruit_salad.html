Fancy Fruit Salad

  • 3 Tbsp Tang
  • 1 small box instant vanilla or french vanilla pudding
  • 2 cups chunk pineapple, save juice
  • 1 cups mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 cups chopped pecans
  • 3 large bananas, sliced
  1. Mix pineapple juice with Tang.
  2. Sprinkle pudding over fruit.
  3. Pour juice over fruit and mix well.
