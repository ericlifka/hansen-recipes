Seven Layer Salad

  • 1 head lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cups celery
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 gr. onions
  • 1/2 pkg. peas, uncooked
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 cups salad dressing
  • Options:
  • cheese
  • bacon bits
  • water chestnuts
  • shrimp
  1. Arrange vegetables in layers in a pretty bowl, topping with salad dressing mixed with sugar.
  2. Cover and chill several hours or overnight.
  3. Just before serving top with cheese and bits.
