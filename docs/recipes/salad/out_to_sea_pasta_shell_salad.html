Out to Sea Pasta Shell Salad

  • 3 cups medium pasta shells
  • 2/3 cups shredded carrots
  • 2/3 cups thawed peas
  • 4 bacon strips, cooked and diced
  • 4 oz. cr. cheese
  • 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 envelope ranch dressing
  1. Cook shells and drain.
  2. In bowl combine carrots, peas and bacon.
  3. In small bowl beat cream cheese and sour cream.
  4. Add milk and dressing mix; beat until combined.
  5. Add pasta to veggies and add dressing mix, toss to coat.
  6. Chill until serving.
