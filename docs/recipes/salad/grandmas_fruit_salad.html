Grandma’s Fruit Salad

  • Fresh fruit:
  • apples
  • strawberries
  • bananas
  • grapes slice in half
  • Canned fruit:
  • Queen Ann cherries
  • pears
  • peaches
  • pineapple
  • Dressing:
  • 6 oz. frozen concentrate orange juice
  • 9 oz. Clover Honey (I use slightly less)
  1. Mix fruit with dressing.
  2. Can add new fruit and still eat.
