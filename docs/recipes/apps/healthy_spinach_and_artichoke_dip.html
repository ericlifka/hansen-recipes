Healthy Spinach and Artichoke Dip

  • 4 cups fresh baby spinach leaves or frozen
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/4 cups mayo
  • 4 Tbsp parm cheese
  • 1/4 cups milk
  • 2 garlic
  • 1 tsp basil leaves
  • 14 oz. artichoke hearts, chop
  1. Heat oven to 350.
  2. Spray 1 qt. dish with PAM.
  3. If using fresh spinach put in big casserole dish with 1/4 cups water; cover and microwave 2 minutes.
  4. Drain, remove excess liquid and chop.
  5. In bowl, beat cream cheese with spoon.
  6. Beat in mayo, 3 Tbsp parm cheese, milk, garlic and basil until well blended.
  7. Stir in spinach and artichokes.
  8. Spread in baking dish.
  9. Sprinkle with remaining tablespoon cheese.
  10. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until heated through.
