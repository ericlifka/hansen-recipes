Holiday Chex Mix

  • 3 cups each Corn Chex, Wheat Chex, Rice Chex
  • 6 Tbsp butter
  • 2T. Worcestershire
  • 1 1/2 tsp seasoned salt
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 cups pretzels
  • 1 cups bagel chips
  1. Heat oven to 250.
  2. Melt butter in large pan in oven.
  3. Stir in seasonings.
  4. Gradually add rest of stuff until evenly coated.
  5. Bake 1 hour, stirring every 15 min.
