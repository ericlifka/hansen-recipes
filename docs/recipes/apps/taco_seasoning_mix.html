Taco Seasoning Mix

  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp ground cumin,
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar
  1. Combine and use like the envelope ones.
  2. Add 3/4 cups water and simmer 5 minutes with 1 lb. hamburger.
