Crispix Mix Ranch Recipe

  • 8 cups Crispix
  • 2 1/2 cups pretzel sticks
  • 2 1/2 cups bite size cheddar crackers
  • 3 Tbsp veggie oil
  • 1 pkg. (1 oz) Hidden Valley Ranch Salad Dressing Mix.
  1. In 2 gallon bag, combine cereal, pretzels and crackers.
  2. Pour oil over.
  3. Close bag and gently toss cereal mix until well coated.
  4. Add Ranch dressing mix and close bag.
  5. Gently toss cereal mix until well coated.
