Buffalo Potato Wedges

  1. 1/3 cups melted butter
  2. 1 tsp cider or white vinegar
  3. 3-4 tsp red pepper sauce
  4. 1/2 tsp salt
  5. 4 unpeeled, medium potatoes
  6. 1 cups blue cheese dressing
  • Heat coals or gas grill.
  • Mix butter, vinegar, pepper sauce and salt in shallow dish.
  • Cut each potato lengthwise into 4 wedges ; pat dry with paper towels.
  • Dip potato wedges into butter mix.
  • Cover and grill potatoes 4-6 inches from med. heat 25-35 minutes brushing with butter mix and turning once or twice until tender.
  • Serve with dressing.
