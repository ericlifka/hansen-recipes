Wild Rice Casserole

  1. 1 cups wild rice
  2. 2 cups water
  3. 1 cups celery diced
  4. olive oil
  5. 1 envelope onion soup
  6. 1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
  7. 1 bullion cube (I use Knox)
  8. 1 cups water
  9. 2 Tbsp Worchester sauce
  • Add wild rice and water to pan and cook until tender, about an hour.
  • Saute celery in 2-3 tablespoons of Olive Oil until tender.
  • Add Onion soup, cream of mushroom soup, bullion cube, 1 cups water, and worchester sauce.
  • Add rice to mixture.
  • Right before baking Saute 1 package of fresh mushrooms, in a little oil (or butter) and add to casserole.
  • Bake at 350 for an hour.
  • This gets better when it sits.
