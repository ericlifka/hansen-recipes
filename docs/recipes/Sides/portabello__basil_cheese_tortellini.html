Portabello & basil cheese tortellini

  • 19 oz cheese tortellini (big size)
  • 1 lb. sliced baby portabello
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/3 cups butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cups heavy whip cream
  • 1/2 tsp s & p
  • 1/3 cups Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil or 2 tsp dried
  1. Cook tortellini.
  2. In skillet saut√© mushrooms and onion in butter until tender.
  3. Add garlic, cook 1 minutes.
  4. Stir in broth.
  5. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, simmer uncovered for 12-15 minutes or until liquid is reduced by half.
  7. Add the cream, salt and pepper and cook 4-5 minutes or until slightly thickened.
  8. Drain tortellini, add to skillet.
  9. Stir in cheese and basil.
