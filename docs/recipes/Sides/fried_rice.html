Fried Rice

  • 1-3 c cooked rice
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1-2 c frozen veggie blend, thawed
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 T butter or oil
  1. Prepare 1 cups or more rice.
  2. Scramble a few eggs in a small pan.
  3. I thaw out some frozen veggies (about 1-2 c.) or use a can of veg-all.
  4. I also chop up a small onion.
  5. Mix all together.
  6. I heat up about 2 Tbsp butter over med./high heat.
  7. I dump the rice mix in and add about 2 Tbsp soy sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook about 5-10 minutes.
  9. Serve.
  10. You can also mix this in advance and refrigerate (do not add butter, soy and seasonings).
  11. When ready to eat cook in the butter and soy sauce.
  12. You can also cook this in the oven for about 30 minutes.
