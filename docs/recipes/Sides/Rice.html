Rice

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  1. For perfect rice, use a pan of 1 qt. capacity for each cup of rice: 1 cups rice to 1 qt. pot, 2 cups rice to 2 qt. pot, etc.
  2. Add 2 cups water and 1/4 tsp salt for each cup of rice.
  3. Bring to a boil with the lid off and keep it off for 3 minutes.
  4. Put the lid on the pot and turn heat to low.
  5. Let it cook for 15 minutes and then turn off heat.
  6. Do not open lid.
  7. Let the pot stand on the burner another 5-6 minutes.
  8. Serve.
