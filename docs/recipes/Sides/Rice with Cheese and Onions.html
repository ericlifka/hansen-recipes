Rice with Cheese and Onions

  • 1 1/2 cups converted rice
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil1 onion, peeled and sliced
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cups Swiss cheese, grated
  • 1/8 cups Parmesan cheese
  1. Put olive oil in pan and add the rice.
  2. Cook for a few minutes over medium heat until rice begins to lightly brown.
  3. Remove from the pan to a 2 qt. covered pan.
  4. Put butter in frying pan and saut√© the onions until they being to lightly brown.
  5. Place the onions, broth, salt in the pan with the rice and bring to a boil.
  6. After the pot boils for 3 minutes, cover with the lid and turn heat to low.
  7. After 15 minutes, turn off heat.
  8. Do not remove the lid for 5 more minutes.
  9. Stir in cheeses and pepper.
