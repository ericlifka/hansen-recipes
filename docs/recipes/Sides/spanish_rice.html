Spanish Rice

  • 1 can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 cups white rice, uncooked
  • 3/4 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  1. In medium pan combine all ingredients.
  2. Bring to boil; reduce heat.
  3. Cover and simmer 25 minutes or until rice is done.
