Spinach Lasagna Roll-Ups

  • 10 oz frozen chopped spinach, thawed, squeezed dry
  • 4 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 oz. 2% cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cups Parm cheese, divided
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 6 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 30 oz. seasoned tomato sauce or spg. sauce
  1. Combine spinach, mozzarella, cottage cheese, 1/2 cups parm and egg.
  2. Spread a heaping 1/3 cupful over each noodle.
  3. Roll up and secure with toothpick.
  4. Place seam side down in a 11 x7 x 2 baking dish, sprayed with PAM.
  5. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  6. Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking.
  7. Pour tomato sauce over roll-ups.
  8. Cover and bake at 350 for 33-38 minutes or until bubbly.
  9. Sprinkle with remaining parm cheese.
