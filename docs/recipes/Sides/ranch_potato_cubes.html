Ranch Potato Cubes

  • 7 med. potatoes, cut in 1/2 in cubes
  • 1/4 cups butter, cubed
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 1 env. ranch salad dressing mix
  • 4 oz shredded cheddar
  1. Place potatoes in 11 x 7 dish.
  2. Dot with butter.
  3. Cover and bake at 325 for 60 minutes or til tender.
  4. Combine sour cream and ranch; spoon over potatoes.
  5. Sprinkle with cheese.
  6. Bake uncovered for 5-10 miinutes or until cheese melts.
