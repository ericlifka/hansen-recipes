Onion Roasted Potato

  • 1 envelope Lipton onion soup mix
  • 4 med. potato
  • 1/3 cups oil
  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. Cut potato into large chunks.
  3. In 9 x 13 pan, combine all ingredients until evenly coated.
  4. Bake uncovered, stirring occasionally 35 minutes or until potatoes are tender and golden brown.
