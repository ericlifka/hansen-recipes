Fettuccine Alfredo

  • 12 oz. fettuccine
  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 1 cups whipped cream
  • 3/4 cups Parmesan
  • 2 Tbsp parsleysalt and pepper
  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Drain and place in warm serving dish large enough for tossing.
  3. Add butter, whipping cream, cheese and parsley; toss until pasta is well coated.
  4. Salt and pepper to taste.
