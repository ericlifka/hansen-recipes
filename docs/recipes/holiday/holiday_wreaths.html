Holiday Wreaths

  • 1/2 cups margarine
  • 30 large marshmallows
  • 4 cups corn flakes
  1. Spray waxed paper and 2 spoons with Pam.
  2. Over low heat melt margarine and marshmallows.
  3. Stir constantly until smooth.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Stir in corn flakes and food coloring.
  6. Drop onto waxed paper and mold into wreaths.
  7. Decorate with red hot candies.
