Cajun Jalapenos

  • 8 large jalapeno
  • 3 oz. soft cream cheese
  • 1/2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 tsp cajun seasoning
  • bacon or bacos, opt.
  1. Mix cream cheese, cheddar and cajun.
  2. Cut jalapenos in half and stuff with 1 1/2 tsp cream cheese.
  3. Top with bacon.
  4. Bake 350 for 25-30 minutes.
