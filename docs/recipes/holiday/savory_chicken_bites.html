Savory Chicken Bites

  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 2- 8 oz. cans refrigerator crescent rolls
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 - 2 oz. jar chopped pimento, drained
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves, crushed
  • 1/3 cups finely chopped celery
  • 1/4 tsp onion salt
  • 1 cups finely chopped cooked chicken
  • 1/8 tsp oregano leaves
  • 1/8 tsp thyme leaves, crushed
  1. Combine cream cheese, juice and seasonings, mixing until well blended.
  2. Stir in chicken, celery and pimento.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Separate dough into eight rectangles; firmly press perforations to seal.
  5. Spread each rectangle with cream cheese mixture, leaving 1/2” of dough on one long edge.
  6. Roll up, starting with long edge spread with cream cheese mix, seal edges.
  7. Cut each roll into 4 pieces.
  8. Place seam side down on lightly greased cookie sheet.
  9. Bake at 375º 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
