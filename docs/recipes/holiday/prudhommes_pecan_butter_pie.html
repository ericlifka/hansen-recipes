Prudhomme’s Pecan Butter Pie

  • Pie crust
  • 1/2 cups pean pieces or haves, dry roasted until dark
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cups sugar
  • 1 cups dark corn syrup
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 cups pecan halves
  1. Roast pecans at 410 for 5 minutes.
  2. Process the roasted pecans in food processor until they become a relatively smooth butter, 2-3 minutes, scrapping sides as needed.
  3. Place eggs in mixing bowl and beat on high until frothy.
  4. Add the sugar, corn syrup, butter, vanilla, salt and the pecan butter.
  5. Beat a few seconds until well mixed, pushing down sides as needed.
  6. Stir in unroasted pecans.
  7. Pour mix into pie shell.
  8. Place on cookie sheet.
  9. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes .
  10. Reduce heat to 325 and cook until filling is browned on top and crust edges are lightly brown, about 40-45 minutes.
  11. Remove from heat.
  12. Let cool at least 30 minutes before serving.
  13. Serve with whipped cream or cool whip.
