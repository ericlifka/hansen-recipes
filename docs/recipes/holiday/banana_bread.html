Banana Bread

  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 1 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 3 bananas mashed
  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs.
  3. Add bananas.
  4. Add soda.
  5. Add flour and milk alternately.
  6. Bake in greased loaf pan 1 hour 15 min. at 350ยบ.
