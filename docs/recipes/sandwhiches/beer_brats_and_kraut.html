Beer Brats and Kraut

  • 3 bottles beer
  • 6 brats
  • 1 large jar sauerkraut
  • 6 buns
  • mustard
  1. Place beer in pot and heat over medium heat.
  2. Place brats and kraut in beer and simmer for approximately 10 minutes.
  3. With tongs, remove brats from beer and place 1 in each bun.
  4. Using a slotted spoon, cover brat in sauerkraut.
  5. Drizzle mustard on top of kraut.
