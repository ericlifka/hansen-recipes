Maureen’s Quesadillas

  • Chicken breast, cut in cubes
  • taco seasoning mix
  • peppers, red, green, orange
  • tortillas (burrito size)
  • cheese
  1. Cook chicken until done.
  2. Add taco seasoning mix and water and cook until done.
  3. Cook chopped peppers until tender.
  4. Spray PAM in bottom of skillet.
  5. Put tortilla on top.
  6. Top with chicken, peppers and cheese.
  7. Fold other side of tortilla over.
  8. After a few minutes, flip.
  9. I cook both sides until cheese is melted.
