Dive Brick Oven Mushroom and Turkey Cheese Sub (Top Secret Recipe)

  • 4 mushrooms, sliced 1/2-3/4 c.
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 6 oz. deli roasted turkey breast
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/4 cups white cheddar dipping sauce
  • 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1-2 oz. cheddar
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 7” baguette, sliced open
  • 2 oz. Swiss cheese
  1. For the sauce combine cream, cheese, and butter in small pan over medium heat and bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. The sauce should thicken.
  3. If you don’t want to go through the effort, just buy a can of Campbell’s cheddar cheese soup and heat up.
  4. Heat oven to 450.
  5. Sauté the mushroom slices in the butter and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Heat the breast in the microwave until warm.
  7. Cut the baguette so that with the top off it looks like a “v” but do not cut all the way through.
  8. Inside the v Spread some of the cheese sauce, turkey breast more cheese sauce, mushrooms.
  9. Bake the sandwich for 4 minutes.
  10. Put Swiss or provolone on top and bake 2 minutes more until the cheese melts.
  11. Put top on and slice in half for eating.
  12. I generally just use sub buns.
