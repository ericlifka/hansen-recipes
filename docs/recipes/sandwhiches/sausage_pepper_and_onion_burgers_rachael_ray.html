Sausage, Pepper and Onion Burgers (Rachael Ray)

  • 3/4 lb. hamburger
  • 3/4 lb. ground pork
  • 1/2 Tbsp fennel seeds
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cups flat leaf parsley leaves, chopped
  • 1 to 1 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 handfuls grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 1 Tbsp grill seasoning (Montreal Steak Seasoning)
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large onions, thickly sliced
  • 1 red and 1 green pepper, seeded and thickly sliced
  • 2 large or 4 small Cubanelle peppers, seeded and thinly sliced
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 Kaiser rolls
  • provolone cheese
  1. In a bowl, combine the ground beef and pork with the fennel seeds, garlic, parsley, crushed red pepper flakes, Parmigiano, grill seasoning and Tbsp of olive oil.
  2. Mix all together well and make your burgers about 1” thick.
  3. Drizzle them with olive oil, cook on the grill about 6-8 minutes per side.
  4. Rachael made four burgers, but I used 1 lb. of both meats and made 9 burgers.
  5. Place a large sauté pan over medium high heat, add 2 Tbsp olive oil.
  6. Add the onions, and bell and Cubanelle peppers and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Cook stirring occasionally until the onions and peppers are tender, about 8-10 minutes.
  8. Split the rolls and toast briefly on the grill, until just marked.
  9. Place a patty on a roll, top with 2 slices of cheese and the onion pepper mix.
  10. I used 1 red, green, cubanelle, and onion.
  11. I also put the pepper mix on the bottom, topped with a burger, and then top with cheese.
  12. Dad and Steven liked these better than plain hamburgers.
