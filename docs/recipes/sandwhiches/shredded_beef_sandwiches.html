Shredded Beef Sandwiches

  • 3-4 lb. arm cut roast or chuck roast, boneless
  • onion soup mix
  • salt, pepper
  • bouillon cube (I use about 4)
  • buns
  1. Put that stuff on top of the roast and pour water over the meat.
  2. Cook in slow oven 275 for 5 or 6 hours.
  3. When meat is tender, pull apart with forks.
  4. You can also do it overnight or in a crock pot.
