Beef Enchiladas

  • 1 1/2 lb. hamburger
  • onion
  • 2-3 cans enchilada sauce
  • 8 oz. tomato sauce
  • tortillas
  • cheese
  • green onions
  1. Brown hamburger, drain.
  2. Add onion.
  3. Add 1 can enchilada sauce and cook until onion is done and meat mix is hot.
  4. Take off heat.
  5. Microwave tortillas (10 second/tortilla or 2 minutes/bag).
  6. Put some meat mix on center of each tortilla, top with cheese and roll up.
  7. I mix 1 can enchilada sauce with tomato sauce so it is not as spicy.
  8. Put some on bottom of 9 x 13 so that tortillas donâ€™t stick.
  9. Put tortillas in.
  10. Top with rest of sauce.
  11. Bake for 25-30 minutes at 350.
  12. Top with cheese and green onions and cook until cheese is melted.
