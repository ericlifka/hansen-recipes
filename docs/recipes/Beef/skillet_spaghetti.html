Skillet Spaghetti

  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 8 oz. spaghetti
  • dash pepper
  • onion
  • 8 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 cups tomato juice
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  1. Combine all ingredients but spaghetti.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.
  4. Add spaghetti and simmer covered another 30 minutes.
