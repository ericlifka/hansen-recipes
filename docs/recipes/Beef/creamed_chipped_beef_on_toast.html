Creamed Chipped Beef on Toast

  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp onion
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 2 cups hot milk
  • 1/2 lb. dried chipped beef
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • peas
  • pepper
  • tabasco (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp dry sherry (optional)
  1. In pan melt butter and saut√© onion until clear.
  2. Stir in flour with a whisk and then in hot milk, stirring constantly until mix thickens.
  3. I heat the milk in the microwave first.
  4. When mix is thick, add rest of stuff and heat.
  5. Serve over toast.
