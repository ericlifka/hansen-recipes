Beef Stroganoff

  1. 1 lb. hamburger
  2. 1 onion
  3. 1 garlic
  4. 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  5. 8 oz. sour cream
  6. salt and pepper
  7. can mushrooms
  • Brown hamburger.
  • I drain off the fat.
  • Add onion.
  • Stir in soup and seasonings.
  • Simmerabout 20 minutes.
  • I add the mushrooms and sour cream and cook on low for another 15-20 minutes.
  • Serve over noodles.
  • Hamburger can be ground chuck.
  • I spoon off fat.
