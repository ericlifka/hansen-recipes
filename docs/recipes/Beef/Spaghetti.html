Spaghetti

  1. 2 lb. hamburger
  2. 4 32 oz. cans tomato sauce
  3. 1 large can tomato paste
  4. 1 onion
  5. 1 cups dried parsley
  6. 1 tsp oregano
  7. 2 tsp garlic, basil
  8. salt and pepper
  • Brown beef, drain.
  • Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.
  • Simmer for 1 1/2 hour, stirring frequently.
