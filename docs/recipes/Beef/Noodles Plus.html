Noodles Plus

  1. 1 lb. ground chuck
  2. 15.5 oz can tomato sauce
  3. 10-12 oz broad egg noodles
  4. 8 oz. cream cheese
  5. 1 cups cream style cottage cheese
  6. 1/4 cups sour cream
  7. chopped green onions
  8. dash Tabasco
  • Cook beef until it loses itâ€™s red color.
  • Take off heat and stir in tomato sauce and tabasco.
  • Cook noodles according to package directions.
  • Stir together the cream cheese, cottage cheese and sour cream until well mixed; stir in green onions.
  • In buttered 2 qt. dish spread half the noodles; top with cheese mixture; cover with remaining noodles; top with beef mmix.
  • Refrigerate up to 2 days.
  • Bake at 350 till hot in center and bubbling around edges, 30-40 minutes.
  • Let stand 10 minutes before eating.
  • If refrigerated it takes longer to cook.
