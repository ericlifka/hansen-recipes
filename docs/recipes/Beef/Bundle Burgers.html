Bundle Burgers

  1. 1 1/2 cups dressing
  2. 1/2 cups evaporated milk
  3. 1 can cr. of mushroom soup
  4. 1 1/2 lb. hamburger
  5. 1 Tbsp ketchup
  6. 2 tsp Worcestershire
  • Prepare dressing (Stove top).
  • Add milk to meat and mix.
  • Roll out meat between waxed paper in 6 circles.
  • Put some dressing on top of circles.
  • Shape in balls around dressing.
  • Mix soup with ketchup and Worcestershire.
  • Put meat into casserole.
  • Top with soup mix.
  • Bake uncovered at 350 about 45-50 minutes.
  • I usually double the soup mix for that amount of meat for gravy to top potatoes.
