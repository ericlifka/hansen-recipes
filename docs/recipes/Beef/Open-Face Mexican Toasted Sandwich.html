Open-Face Mexican Toasted Sandwich

  1. 1 lb. hamburger
  2. 1 onion
  3. 1 garlic
  4. 1/4 cups gr. pepper
  5. 2 Tbsp olive oil, divided
  6. 1/2 tsp salt, pepper
  7. 1/4 tsp chili powder
  8. 4 oz. can drained chopped green chilies
  9. 1 cups drained tomatoes
  10. 1 lb. Velveeta
  11. 1/4 cups half and half
  12. toast
  • Heat 1T. oil.
  • Cook beef, drain. Heat remaining oil and saut√© onion, garlic and green pepper until limp.
  • Add chilies tomatoes, half and half, salt, pepper and chili pepper.
  • Simmer 10 minutes.
  • Add cheese and beef, stir till melts, serve on toast.
  • Slow cooker can be used.
