Spaghetti with Meatballs in Tex Mex Sauce

  1. Meatballs:
  2. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  3. 3/4 cups chopped onions
  4. 2 tsp garlic
  5. 1 1/2 lb. hamburger
  6. 1/2 cups bread crumbs
  7. 1/4 cups coriander
  8. 1 egg
  9. 1 tsp cumin
  10. 1 tsp chili powder
  11. salt and pepper
  12. Sauce:
  13. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  14. 1 cups onion
  15. 1 Tbsp garlic
  16. 1 cups red and green pepper, cut into 1/2” pieces
  17. 28 oz. crushed tomato
  18. 1/2 tsp red pepper
  19. 1 cups water
  20. salt and pepper
  21. spaghetti
  • To prepare meatballs heat 1 Tbsp oil in pan.
  • Add onion and garlic, stirring until wilted.
  • Let cool.
  • Combine rest of ingredients except 1 Tbsp oil and blend.
  • Shape into 1 1/2 “ meatballs.
  • Heat oil in skillet and add meatballs.
  • Cook over medium heat turning so they brown evenly, about 10 minutes.
  • Keep them warm.
  • For sauce: Add ingredients, blend and bring to a boil.
  • Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Add meatballs and simmer 10 minutes more.
  • Cook spaghetti.
  • Serve.
