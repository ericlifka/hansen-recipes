Chinese Soy Steak

  1. 1-2 lb. round steak
  2. 2 Tbsp oil
  3. 2 Tbsp flour
  4. 1 onion
  5. 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  6. 1/2 cups catsup
  7. 2 cups water
  8. 4 oz. mushrooms
  9. green pepper, cut into strips
  • Cut steak into strips or small bite size pieces.
  • Brown steak in oil, push to side of pan.
  • Brown flour stir with meat; add onion, catsup, soy sauce and water.
  • Stir, simmer for 45 minutes.
  • Add green pepper and mushrooms.
  • Cook a few more minutes until green peppers are tender crisp.
  • Serve on rice or noodles.
  • You can also cook the stuff together on stove top for a few minutes and then bake in the oven at 350 for 1 hour.
  • Add green pepper for the last 15 minutes.
