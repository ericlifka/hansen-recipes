Salisbury Steak with Gemelli

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cups soft bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp pepper and minced garlic
  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 1 cups chicken broth
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • hot gemelli or spiral pasta
  1. In a large bowl combine the egg, bread crumbs, Italian seasoning, pepper and garlic.
  2. Crumble beef over mix and mix well.
  3. Shape into 4 patties.
  4. In a large skillet, cook patties in oil over medium high heat for 5-7 minutes on each side or until meat is no longer pink.
  5. Remove and keep warm.
  6. Drain, reserving 2 Tbsp drippings.
  7. Saute mushrooms in drippings until tender.
  8. Stir in flour until blended.
  9. Gradually stir in broth, ketchup, and Worcestershire sauce.
  10. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  11. Return patties to the skillet.
  12. Bring to a boil.
  13. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 3-4 minutes or until heated through.
  14. Serve with gemelli.
