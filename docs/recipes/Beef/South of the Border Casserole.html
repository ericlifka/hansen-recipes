South of the Border Casserole

  1. 7 oz. elbow macaroni
  2. 1 lb. turkey
  3. 1 onion
  4. 1 cups celery
  5. 1 garlic
  6. 16 oz. can whole tomatoes, cut up and undrained
  7. 1/2 cups ripe olive
  8. 4 oz. green chilies
  9. 2 tsp beef flavored instant bouillon
  10. 1/2 tsp seasoned salt
  11. 1/4 tsp cumin
  12. dash cayenne pepper
  13. 6 oz. muenster cheese
  14. 6 oz. cheddar cheese
  • Prepare macaroni and drain.
  • In skillet combine turkey, onion, celery and garlic.
  • Cook until turkey is browned.
  • Stir in rest of ingredients except the cheeses.
  • Simmer 10 minutes.
  • Combine macaroni, meat mix and cheeses.
  • Put in casserole dish.
  • Cover.
  • Bake at 350 about 20 minutes.
