Steak Strips Jardin

  • 1 lb. round steak
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 cups onion
  • 2 cups each celery and carrots
  • 1 can cheddar cheese soup
  • 3 oz. cr. cheese, room temp
  • 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • rice
  1. Cut meat into strips.
  2. Brown meat on all sides in oil in skillet.
  3. Add onions, celery, carrots.
  4. Cook until veggies are tender crisp.
  5. Stir in soup, cream cheese, broth, garlic salt and pepper.
  6. Cover and simmer about 30 minutes or until meat is tender.
  7. Serve over rice.
