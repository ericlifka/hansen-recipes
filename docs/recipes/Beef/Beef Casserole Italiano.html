Beef Casserole Italiano

  1. 1 lb. hamburger
  2. 1 onion
  3. 16 oz. tomato sauce
  4. 1 tsp parsley flakes, basil
  5. 1/2 tsp oregano
  6. 1/2 tsp salt, divided
  7. 1/4 tsp pepper
  8. 20 oz. spinach, thawed,drained
  9. 2 cups ricotta cheese
  10. 8 oz. mozzarella cheese slices
  • Brown ground beef and onion, drain.
  • Stir in tomato sauce, parsley, oregano. basil, 1/4 tsp salt and pepper.
  • Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Meanwhile, combine spinach, ricotta and remaining salt.
  • Spoon spinach mix around edges of 9 x 13 pan; pour beef mix in center.
  • Cut mozzarella slices into 3 strips and arrange in a lattice pattern over meat.
  • Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
