Grandma Hansen’ s Meatloaf

  1. 1 lb. hot jimmy dean sausage
  2. 1 lb. hamburger
  3. 1/2 lb. ground pork
  4. 1 onion
  5. 5 slices of dry white bread broken into small pieces.
  6. 1 cups milk
  7. 2 beaten eggs
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. 1/2 tsp pepper
  10. 1/2 tsp sage
  • Mix milk, eggs, salt, pepper, & sage together then pour over bread.
  • I squish meats together with my hands and then add bread mixture and 1 whole large cut up onion.
  • Put into greased casserole pan.
  • Cover with lots of ketchup.
  • Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
