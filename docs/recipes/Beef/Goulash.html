Goulash

  • 1 1/4 lb. hamburger
  • onion
  • celery
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • S&P
  • can corn
  • tomato juice
  • stewed tomato
  • 2 1/2 cups egg noodles, cooked
  1. Brown hamburger and drain.
  2. Add onion and celery.
  3. Turn off heat.
  4. Add noodles, corn, add soup, stewed tomatoes with liquid and add juice or tomato sauce to moisten.
  5. Heat in oven for 45-60 minutes at 350.
