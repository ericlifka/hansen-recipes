Chili

  1. 2 Tbsp oil
  2. 2 Tbsp chuck steak (cubed)
  3. 1 Tbsp flour 1 onion
  4. 3/4 cups red wine
  5. 1 cups tomato sauce
  6. 1 garlic
  7. 1 carrot
  8. 1 green pepper
  9. 2 bay leaves
  10. 40 oz. canned beans (different kinds)
  11. 1/2 tsp cumin
  12. 1 tsp oregano
  13. 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  14. 10 1/2 oz. can minestrone and lentil soup.
  • Heat oil.
  • Brown steak in oil with flour.
  • Add rest of ingredients.
  • Simmer 1 1/2 hour.
  • Uncover last 30 minutes if needed to thicken.
