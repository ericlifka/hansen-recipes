Italian Spaghetti

  • 1 1/4 lb. hamburger
  • onion
  • garlic
  • 2 envelopes sauce mix
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 3 cups water (mix water/red wine)
  • 1 can mushrooms
  • 1 tsp oregano leaves
  • 1 tsp basil leaves
  • spaghetti
  1. Cook beef, drain.
  2. Add sauce mix and remaining ingredients except spaghetti.
  3. Simmer until thickened about 30 minutes.
