Porcupine Meatballs

  1. 1/2 cups uncooked rice
  2. 1 lb. hamburger
  3. 1 Tbsp onion
  4. 1/2 tsp salt, pepper
  5. 1/4 tsp chili powder
  6. 1 can tomato soup and juice
  • Mix rice and meat with seasonings.
  • Shape into small balls.
  • Place in baking dish and cover with a mix of tomato soup and juice (I use 1 can soup, 1 can juice).
  • Bake at 350 for 60-80 minutes.
