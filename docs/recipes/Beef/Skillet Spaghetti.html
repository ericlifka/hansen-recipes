Skillet Spaghetti

  1. 1 lb. hamburger
  2. 1 tsp garlic
  3. 1 can tomato paste
  4. 1 tsp chili powder
  5. 1 tsp oregano
  6. 8 oz. spaghetti
  7. dash pepper
  8. onion
  9. 8 oz. tomato sauce
  10. 1 1/2 cups water
  11. 2 cups tomato juice
  12. 2 tsp salt
  13. 1 tsp sugar
  • Combine all ingredients but spaghetti.
  • Cover and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.
  • Add spaghetti and simmer covered another 30 minutes.
