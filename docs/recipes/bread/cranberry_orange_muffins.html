Cranberry Orange Muffins

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 5 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 3/4 cups orange juice
  • 1/3 cups veggie oil
  • 1 cups cranberries, chopped
  1. In bowl stir together flour, 2 Tbsp sugar, baking powder, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  2. Make a well in center.
  3. In a small bowl combine egg, orange juice, and oil; add all at once to flour mixture.
  4. Stir until moistened.
  5. Toss cranberries with 2 Tbsp sugar, fold into batter.
  6. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full.
  7. Sprinkle muffins with remaining sugar.
  8. Bake at 400 for 18-20 minutes.
