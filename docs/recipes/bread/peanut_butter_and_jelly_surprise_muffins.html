Peanut Butter and Jelly Surprise Muffins

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/3 cups sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/3 cups melted butter
  • 1/2 cups jam or jelly
  1. Heat oven to 375, line muffin tin with paper cups.
  2. In a bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  3. In another bowl mix the peanut butter and egg; add the milk a little at a time, then add the butter.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Pour the wet batter into the bowl with the dry ingredients and stir gently to combine, the batter will be stiff.
  6. Put a heaping tablespoon of batter into bottom of each cup.
  7. Use your finger to make an indentation in the center and put a teaspoon of jelly in the hole.
  8. Cover this with another heaping tablespoon of batter to fill each cup 3/4 full.
  9. Spread the top batter gently so no jelly shows.
  10. Bake 20 minutes and put on baking rack to cool.
