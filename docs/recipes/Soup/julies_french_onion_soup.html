Julie’s French Onion Soup

  • 1 qt. beef stock
  • 3 cups onion
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 1/2 cups white wine
  • 1 cups Parmesan cheese (optional)
  1. Put stock in crock pot.
  2. Cook onions in butter for about 15 minutes covered.
  3. Uncover, add salt, sugar, flour and wine.
  4. Stir well.
  5. Add to stock.
  6. Cover and cook on low 6-8 hours.
