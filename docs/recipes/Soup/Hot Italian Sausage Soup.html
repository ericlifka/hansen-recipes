Hot Italian Sausage Soup

  1. 1 lb. hot Italian sausage
  2. 1 14 1/2 oz. can Italian stewed tomatoes (I used 2 cans)
  3. 8 oz tomato sauce
  4. 1 cups frozen Italian veggies
  5. 1/4 cups chopped onion
  6. 3/4 cups julienned red, green and/or yellow pepper
  7. 1/4 cups white wine or chicken broth
  8. 1 tsp brown sugar
  9. 1 tsp minced fresh parsley
  10. 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  11. 1/8 tsp salt and pepper
  • In large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink.
  • In another pan combine remaining ingredients.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes until veggies are tender.
  • Drain sausage; add to soup and heat through.
