Black Bean and Pumpkin Chili

  1. 1 med onion
  2. 1 med yellow pepper
  3. 3 garlic
  4. 2 Tbsp o. oil
  5. 3 cups chick broth
  6. 2 cans black beans, rinse and drain
  7. 2 1/2 cups chicken
  8. 15 oz pumpkin
  9. 14 1/2 oz diced tomato, undrained
  10. 2 tsp parsley
  11. 2 tsp chili powder
  12. 1 1/2 tsp oregano
  13. 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • In skillet saute onion, yellow pepper and garlic in oil.
  • Put in slow cooker with rest of ingredients.
  • Cook on low 4-5 hours
