Broccoli Chowder

  1. 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  2. 2 med. potatoes, diced
  3. 1 1/2 cups water
  4. 2 carrots, sliced
  5. 1 onion, chopped
  6. 1 celery, chopped
  7. 4 cups milk, divided
  8. 2 tsp chicken bouillon granules
  9. 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  10. 3/4 tsp salt
  11. 1/2 tsp pepper
  12. 1/3 cups flour
  13. 1 cups velveeta
  • In pan combine first 6 ingredients (broc.-celery).
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat; cover and simmer for -10 minutes or until veggie are tender.
  • Add 3 cups milk and the bouillon, worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.
  • In a small bowl combine flour and remaining milk and mix until smooth; gradually add to soup.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  • Remove from heat, stir in cheese until melted.
  • Serve.
