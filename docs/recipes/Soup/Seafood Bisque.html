Seafood Bisque

  • 8 Tbsp flour
  • 8 Tbsp butter
  • 1 qt. whole milk
  • 1 tsp garlic powder, salt, pepper
  • 1/2 cups sherry
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 lb. each scallops, shrimp, crab
  • 2 tsp Accent
  1. Make a roux of flour and butter.
  2. Place milk in double boiler and add garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. When milk is hot add roux and blend well.
  4. Cook over medium heat until it thickens.
  5. Melt 2 Tbsp butter with sherry, add seafood and saut√© 2 minutes.
  6. Fold all ingredients together.
  7. Turn off heat and let stand 30 minutes so flavors marry.
  8. Reheat and serve.
