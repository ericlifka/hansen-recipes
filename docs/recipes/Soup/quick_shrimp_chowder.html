Quick Shrimp Chowder

  • 2 Tbsp butter1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cans cream of potato soup
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 lb. medium size shrimp peeled or can use same amount frozen, thawed
  • 4 oz. monterey jack cheese
  1. Melt butter in oven over medium heat; add onion, and saut√© 8 minutes or until tender.
  2. Stir in cream of potato soup, milk, and pepper; bring to a boil.
  3. Add shrimp; reduce heat and simmer, stirring often, 5 minutes or just until shrimp turn pink.
  4. Stir in cheese until melted.
  5. Garnish if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and can also add oyster crackers.
