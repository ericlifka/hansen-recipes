Broccoli Cheese Soup

  1. 6 cups chicken stock
  2. 1 cups onion
  3. 1 tsp salt, pepper, garlic powder
  4. 1 stick butter
  5. 2 1/2 cups broccoli or 1 16 oz. bag frozen broccoli
  6. 1 cups flour
  7. 2 cups milk cheese to taste
  • Bring stock, onion and seasonings to a boil.
  • Add broccoli, reduce heat to simmer and cook until broccoli is soft about 8 minutes.
  • In separate pan melt butter, and add flour.
  • Whisk for 5 minutes until smooth over med. heat.
  • Slowly add milk stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  • Add broccoli mix slowly until soup begins to thicken.
  • Garnish with cheese.
