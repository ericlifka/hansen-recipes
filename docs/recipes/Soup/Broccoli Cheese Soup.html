Broccoli Cheese Soup

  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cups onion
  • 1 tsp salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 1/2 cups broccoli or 1 16 oz. bag frozen broccoli
  • 1 cups flour
  • 2 cups milk cheese to taste
  1. Bring stock, onion and seasonings to a boil.
  2. Add broccoli, reduce heat to simmer and cook until broccoli is soft about 8 minutes.
  3. In separate pan melt butter, and add flour.
  4. Whisk for 5 minutes until smooth over med. heat.
  5. Slowly add milk stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  6. Add broccoli mix slowly until soup begins to thicken.
  7. Garnish with cheese.
