Chili

  1. 2 Tbsp oil
  2. 2 lb. chuck steak (cubed)
  3. 1 Tbsp flour.
  4. 1 onion
  5. 3/4 cups red wine
  6. 1 cups tomato sauce
  7. 1 clove garlic
  8. 1 carrot
  9. 1 green pepper
  10. 40 oz. canned beans (different kinds)
  11. 2 bay leaves
  12. 1/2 tsp cumin
  13. 1 tsp oregano
  14. 10 1/2 oz. can minestrone and lentil soup
  15. 1/4 tsp red pepper
  • Cube steak into 1/2 to 1 inch pieces.
  • Toss steak in flour, brown in oil.
  • Add all ingredients.
  • Simmer 1 1/2 hr.
  • Uncover last 30 minutes if needed to thicken.
