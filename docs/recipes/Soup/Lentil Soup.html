Lentil Soup

  • 1 ham hock
  • 1 lb. lentils
  • 3 cups mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • salt, pepper
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 8 cups water
  • 3 Tbsp parsley
  1. Combine all ingredients, boil over moderately high heat.
  2. Reduce heat, simmer partially covered 1 hour.
  3. Remove bone.
  4. Add meat from bone and simmer 20 more minutes.
