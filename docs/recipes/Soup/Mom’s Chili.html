Mom’s Chili

  1. 1 lb. hamburger
  2. 2-3 cans kidney beans
  3. 1 onion
  4. 1 cups macaroni
  5. 1 tsp salt, pepper
  6. chili powder (however much you like)
  7. tomato juice
  • Cook hamburger.
  • Put rest of ingredients in pot--the amount of juice depends on how thick you want it and the amount of beans also is up to you.
  • Cook about 45-60 minutes.
  • Serve.
