Clam Chowder 2

  • 2 large potato
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 strips bacon
  • 1/4 cups onion
  • 3 (8 oz.) clams
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 qt. half and half or milk
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cups white wine
  • 1 cups half and half
  1. Cook bacon until crisp, and chop into small pieces.
  2. Cook unpeeled potato in boiling water and salt.
  3. Drain, and cube if not already cubed.
  4. Cook onion in bacon fat until clear.
  5. Add clams and broth and simmer 5 minutes.
  6. In 4 qt. pot melt butter.
  7. Add flour and stir in 1 qt. milk and wine.
  8. Cook over medium heat; stirring constantly until mix comes to a boil and thickens.
  9. Reduce heat, add potato, clam, bacon and pepper.
  10. Add remaining half and half and heat until hot.
  11. I have made both of these versions and sometimes, I combine them into a mix of both.
