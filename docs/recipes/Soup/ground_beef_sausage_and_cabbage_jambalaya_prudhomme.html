Ground Beef, Sausage and Cabbage Jambalaya (Prudhomme)

  • 1 Tbsp oi
  • 1 1/2 lb. hamb.
  • 3/4 lb. andouille or kielbasa, cut in slices
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 3/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups chopped gr. pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups onions
  • 1 cups celery and green onions
  • 1/2 cups chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • 1 1/4 cups cajun tomatoes (1 can)
  • 7 cups chopped cabbage
  • 4 3/4 cups beef stock
  • 2 cups uncooked converted rice
  1. Place oil in 6 qt. pan. Add beef and cook over high heat, breaking meat p into small chunks.
  2. Add the sausage and cook until the hamburger is browned about 4 minutes; stirring occasionally and continuing to break up meat chunks.
  3. Stir in salt, red and black peppers, cook about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the bell peppers, onions, celery, green onions, parsley and garlic; cook about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the tomatoes and cook and stir about 2 minutes more.
  6. Now add the cabbage and do not stir.
  7. Cover pan and cook about 25 minutes; stirring occasionally after mix on the bottom has browned and scraping pan bottom well.
  8. Stir in 1 1/2 cups stock, scraping bottom until sediment is dissolved.
  9. Cook uncovered 15 minutes, stirring.
  10. Add 1 1/2 cups more stock, cook 10 more minutes and then add 1 3/4 cups stock.
  11. Add the rice, stirring well.
  12. Cover pan, reduce heat to very low and cook about 25 minutes.
  13. Check after 20 minutes to make sure mix is not scorching.
  14. Remove pan from heat and let sit covered until rice is tender, but still crunchy about 25 minutes more.
  15. Stir well and serve immediately.
