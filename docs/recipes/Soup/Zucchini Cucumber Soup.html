Zucchini Cucumber Soup

  1. 4 Tbsp butter
  2. 2 cups onion
  3. 2 lb. zucchini
  4. 2 cucumbers, seeded but with skins on
  5. 3 cups chicken broth
  6. 1/4 cups fresh dillsalt and pepper
  7. Garnish:
  8. 1/2 cups cuke, tomato
  • Melt butter and add onions.
  • Cook 10 min.
  • Add zucchini and cukes.
  • Cook over medium for 10 min.
  • Add broth and cook 15 minutes more.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Cool.
  • Process in blender or food processor.
  • Serve hot or cold.
