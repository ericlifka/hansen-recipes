French Onion Soup (Good)

  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 5 medium red onions (3 lb.)
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 3/4 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cups red wine
  • 2 sprigs parsley
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  1. Melt butter.
  2. Add onions.
  3. Cook 3 minutes until onions are reduced.
  4. Stir in rest of stuff.
  5. Simmer about 20 minutes.
  6. Stir in vinegar and seasonings.
