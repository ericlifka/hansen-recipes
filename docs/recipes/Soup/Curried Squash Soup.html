Curried Squash Soup

  • 3/4 cups chopped onion
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 4 cups peeled, diced squash
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 3 Tbsp sherry
  • 1/2 cups whipped cream
  1. Melt butter, add onion.
  2. Saut√© until limp, add squash, broth, sugar, curry powder, salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer till veggies are tender.
  4. Run soup through food processor.
  5. Stir in sherry and cream.
