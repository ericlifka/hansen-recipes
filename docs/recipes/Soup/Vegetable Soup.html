Vegetable Soup

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 Soup Bone
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes
  • 2 cups chopped carrots, onion, & celery
  • 1 cups diced potato
  • 2 bay leaf
  • salt and pepper
  1. Boil soup bone for about an hour.
  2. Remove from water and cut off meat.
  3. Add stewed tomato, carrots, onion, celery, potato, bay leaf and salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer for about an hour.
