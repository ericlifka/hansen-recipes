Black Bean Soup

  • 1 lb. black turtle beans
  • 2 qt. water
  • 1/2 lb. thick bacon
  • 1 1/2 cups onion, celery, carrot
  • 1 Tbsp garlic1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 3 Tbsp cumin
  • 1/2 Tbsp oregano
  • salt and pepper
  • 10 cups chicken broth
  • 2 ham hocks
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne
  • 4 Tbsp lime juice
  1. Soak beans overnight in water.
  2. Drain.
  3. Saut√© bacon until brown.
  4. Add onion, celery, and carrots about 5 min.
  5. Add garlic, bay leaf, thyme, 1 Tbsp cumin, oregano.
  6. Cook 5 min.
  7. Add broth and ham hocks.
  8. Boil and cook over med. heat 2 to 2 1/2 hours.
  9. Remove hocks and add meat, cayenne, lime juice and cumin.
  10. Serve with dollop of sour cream.
