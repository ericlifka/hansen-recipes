Julie’s French Onion Soup

  1. 1 qt. beef stock
  2. 3 cups onion
  3. 3 Tbsp butter
  4. 1 tsp salt
  5. 1 Tbsp sugar
  6. 2 Tbsp flour
  7. 1/2 cups white wine
  8. 1 cups Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Put stock in crock pot.
  • Cook onions in butter for about 15 minutes covered.
  • Uncover, add salt, sugar, flour and wine.
  • Stir well.
  • Add to stock.
  • Cover and cook on low 6-8 hours.
