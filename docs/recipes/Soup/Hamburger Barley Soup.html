Hamburger Barley Soup

  1. 2 lb. ground beef or turkey
  2. 16 oz. tomatoes, chopped
  3. 6 oz. tomato juice
  4. 7 cups water
  5. 1/2 lb. string beans
  6. 1 green pepper
  7. 1 cups celery
  8. 1 tsp salt, pepper
  9. 2 tsp garlic powder
  10. 2 Tbsp light soy sauce
  11. 1 bay leaf
  12. 1/2 tsp paprika, thyme
  13. 1/2 cups pearl barley
  14. 2 carrots
  15. 1 large potato
  • Brown hamburger.
  • Add everything but carrot and potato.
  • Bring to a boil, reduce heat simmer 1 hour.
  • Add carrots and cook 10 minutes.
  • Add potato and cook 20 minutes more.
  • Can use almost any veggie.
