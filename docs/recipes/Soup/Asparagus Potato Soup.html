Asparagus Potato Soup

  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/3 cups onion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 pkg. frozen asparagus (10 oz.)
  • 1 1/2 cups cream
  • 4 oz. cream cheese with pimento
  1. Dice potatoes and cook in broth, onion, salt and nutmeg.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer covered 5-8 min. until potatoes are tender.
  4. Add asparagus and boil.
  5. Reduce heat, simmer 5 min. until tender.
  6. Add cream cheese.
  7. When melted, serve.
