Tomato Tortillini Soup

  1. 9 oz cheese tortillini
  2. 2 cans tomato soup
  3. 2 cups broth
  4. 2 cups 2% millk, & half and half
  5. 1 tsp onion powder, garlic powder, dried basil
  6. 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 1/2 cups parmesan
  • Cook and drain the tortillini.
  • Add all of the other stuff together and cook 15-30 min.
  • Add the tortillini to the soup and cook a few minutes more.
