Clam Chowder (Mary Brauti)

  1. 3-4 slices bacon, chopped and fried
  2. 1/4 cups onion, chopped
  3. 1-2 celery, chopped
  4. 1-2 plus small potatoes
  5. 1 12 oz can evaporated milk
  6. 1 or 2 cans chopped clams undrained
  7. Salt and papper
  • Saute onion and celery in bacon grease, drain.
  • Add potatoes and enough water to cover and boil until potatoes are soft.
  • Add clams with juice.
  • Add evaporated milk and heat just until hot and ready to serve.
  • She usually uses 1 can clams, but if you use more then drain one can.
