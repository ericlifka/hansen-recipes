Senate Bean Soup

  • 1 lb. white navy beans
  • 1 meaty ham bone or 1 1/2 lb. ham hocks
  • 1 cups onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cups celery
  • 2/3 cups instant mashed potato flakes
  • 1/4 cups parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp each nutmeg, oregano, basil1 bay leaf
  1. In pot, over beans with hot water.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Boil 2 minutes.
  4. Turn off and let stand 1 hr.
  5. Drain.
  6. Add 2 qts.. cold water and ham bone or hocks.
  7. Bring to a boil.
  8. Simmer 1 1/2 hours.
  9. Stir in rest of ingredients.
  10. Simmer 30 min. or until beans are tender.
  11. Remove bones from meat and discard bone.
  12. Put meat in soup in small pieces.
  13. This can be frozen.
