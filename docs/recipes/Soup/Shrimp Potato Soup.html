Shrimp Potato Soup

  1. 1/2 stick butter
  2. 1 onion
  3. 2 carrots
  4. 2 Tbsp flour
  5. 8 potatoes
  6. 4 cups milk
  7. 1/2 cups milk
  8. 2 chicken cubes
  9. 1 cups half and half
  10. 1 tsp salt, pepper
  11. 2 cups water
  12. 1 lb shrimp (or corn).

  • Whisk in flour, add potatoes, milk, chicken cubes.
  • Cook 15 minutes.
  • Add half and half, salt and pepper.
  • Cook shrimp in water and add to soup.
