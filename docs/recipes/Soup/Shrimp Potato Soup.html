Shrimp Potato Soup

  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 8 potatoes
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 chicken cubes
  • 1 cups half and half
  • 1 tsp salt, pepper
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 lb shrimp (or corn).
  1. Saut√© butter, onion, and carrots for 5 minutes.
  2. Whisk in flour, add potatoes, milk, chicken cubes.
  3. Cook 15 minutes.
  4. Add half and half, salt and pepper.
  5. Cook shrimp in water and add to soup.
