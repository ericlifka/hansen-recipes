Clam Chowder 1

  • 1/2 c butter
  • 1 c diced onion
  • 1 c diced celery
  • 1/2 c flour
  • 4 c clam broth
  • 2 1/2 oz salt pork
  • 12 oz minced clams
  • 2 cups cubed potatoes
  • 1 pint half and half
  1. Saute onion and celery in butter for five minutes.
  2. Whisk in flour to make a thick roux.
  3. Add clam broth in small parts mixing until entirely incorporated before adding more.
  4. Simmer 20 minutes.
  5. Add salt pork, clams, and potatoes
  6. Simmer 20 more minutes, until potatoes are tender.
  7. Mix in half and half.
  8. Let stand 20 minutes before serving
