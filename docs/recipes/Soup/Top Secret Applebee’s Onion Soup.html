Top Secret Applebee’s Onion Soup

  1. 3 Tbsp oil
  2. 6 medium white onions, sliced
  3. 8 cups beef broth
  4. 1 cups water
  5. 2 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  7. 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Add oil to pan over medium high heat.
  • Add onions and sauté 20 minutes until the onions begin to get soft and become translucent.
  • Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat to a simmer and simmer for 45 minutes.
