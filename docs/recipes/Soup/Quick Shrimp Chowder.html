Quick Shrimp Chowder

  1. 2 Tbsp butter1 onion, chopped
  2. 2 cans cream of potato soup
  3. 3 1/2 cups milk
  4. 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  5. 1 1/2 lb. medium size shrimp peeled or can use same amount frozen, thawed
  6. 4 oz. monterey jack cheese
  • Melt butter in oven over medium heat; add onion, and saut√© 8 minutes or until tender.
  • Stir in cream of potato soup, milk, and pepper; bring to a boil.
  • Add shrimp; reduce heat and simmer, stirring often, 5 minutes or just until shrimp turn pink.
  • Stir in cheese until melted.
  • Garnish if desired.
  • Serve immediately and can also add oyster crackers.
