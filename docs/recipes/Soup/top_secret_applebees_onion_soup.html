Top Secret Applebee’s Onion Soup

  • 3 Tbsp oil
  • 6 medium white onions, sliced
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 cups water
  • 2 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  1. Add oil to pan over medium high heat.
  2. Add onions and sauté 20 minutes until the onions begin to get soft and become translucent.
  3. Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and simmer for 45 minutes.
