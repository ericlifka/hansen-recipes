Jackson’s Tortilla Soup

  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 4- 6 in. tortillas, cut in strips
  • 1/2 cups onion
  • 1/4 cups celery
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp seeded jalapeno peppers
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 6 oz. tomato paste
  • 14.5 oz. diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans chicken broth
  • 1 cups frozen corn
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder, salt, pepper
  1. Heat oil over medium high.
  2. Add tortilla strips; cook until browned and crisp, stirring occasionally.
  3. Put these in oven at 250.
  4. Add onion, celery, jalapeno and garlic to pan.
  5. Sauté 3 min.
  6. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces.
  7. Grill the breast in separate pan until done.
  8. Add tomato paste and tomatoes to onion.
  9. Bring to a simmer, add broth corn cumin, cilantro, seasonings and breast pieces.
  10. Simmer 30-45 min.
  11. Serve top with tortilla strips and cheese.
