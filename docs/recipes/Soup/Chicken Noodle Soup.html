Chicken Noodle Soup

  1. Chicken or Turkey bones
  2. 1 c chopped carrots
  3. 1 c chopped celery
  4. 1 onion, diced
  • Boil chicken or turkey bones in water with carrots, celery, and onion for about 1-2 hours.
  • The vegetables can be in big pieces.
  • Remove and discard bones and veggies.
  • Remove fat. You can refrigerate at this point, and later the fat will rise to the top which makes it easy to remove.
  • Put stock in pan with salt, pepper, 2 bay leaves, onion, celery, and carrots.
  • Simmer about 10 minutes.
  • Add wide egg noodles and cook another 20 minutes or so, until noodles are tender.
  • Add diced chicken and simmer again.
  • You can also do this with a whole chicken, but will take longer for the chicken to be done.
