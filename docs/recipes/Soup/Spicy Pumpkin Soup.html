Spicy Pumpkin Soup

  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 cups onion
  • 1 1/2 cups pumpkin
  • 1 cups whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 3/4 tsp red pepper--use less!
  • 1/4 cups sour cream
  • 1/4 cups cocoa milk
  • 1/2 tsp lime juice.
  1. Cook butter and onion 10 minutes and then add rest of stuff through red pepper.
