Broccoli Cauliflower Soup

  • 1 8 oz. pkg. frozen cauliflower
  • 1 can(10 3/4) chicken broth
  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 10 oz. chopped broccoli
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seed, salt, pepper, dried dill, mace
  • 1/3 cups chopped onion
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cups Swiss cheese.
  1. Cook cauliflower in half the broth 5-8 minutes.
  2. Combine broth, broccoli, seasonings cook 5-8 min.
  3. Cover and keep warm.
  4. Saut√© onion in butter till tender.
  5. Stir in flour, salt and pepper.
  6. Add milk slowly.
  7. Cook and stir until thick and bubbly.
  8. Stir sauce, cauliflower, broccoli and cheese together.
  9. Sir until cheese is melted.
