Down Home Split Pea Soup

  1. 1 lb. dry split green peas
  2. 1 1/2 qt. chicken broth
  3. 2 large smoked ham hocks
  4. 2 cups milk
  5. 1 large onion, chopped
  6. 1 large potato, peeled and diced
  7. 2 bay leaves
  8. 1-2 Tbsp fresh dill
  9. 3 dashes of hot pepper sauce
  10. pepper
  • Rinse the split peas and combine them with all the remaining ingredients in a large pot.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.
  • Reduce the heat and bring the liquid just to a simmer.
  • Cover and cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  • Remove the cover and cook for an additional 1/2 hour.
  • Remove the ham hocks.
  • Slice the meat off the bones and cut into bite size pieces.
  • Discard the fat, bones, and bay leaves.
  • Return the meat to the pot and cook 1/2 hour longer.
  • I usually make this with a ham bone and add leftover ham.
