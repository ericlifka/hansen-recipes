Chicken Chili (RR)

  1. 32 oz. chicken stock
  2. 3 cans undrained white beans
  3. 5 cups chicken rotisserie or boiled
  4. 16 oz. salsa
  5. 8 oz. block grated pepper jack
  6. 2 tsp cumin
  7. 2 garlic cloves
  8. pepper
  9. 1/2 cups fine crush corn chips
  10. sour cream for garnish
  • Place all in crock pot except chips.
  • Cook on high until cheese is melted.
  • Or you can do on stove top until cheese is melted.
  • When chili is ready add chips and simmer 10 min.
