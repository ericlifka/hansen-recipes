Chicken Chili (RR)

  • 32 oz. chicken stock
  • 3 cans undrained white beans
  • 5 cups chicken rotisserie or boiled
  • 16 oz. salsa
  • 8 oz. block grated pepper jack
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • pepper
  • 1/2 cups fine crush corn chips
  • sour cream for garnish
  1. Place all in crock pot except chips.
  2. Cook on high until cheese is melted.
  3. Or you can do on stove top until cheese is melted.
  4. When chili is ready add chips and simmer 10 min.
