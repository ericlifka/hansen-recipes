Paula Deen’s Potatoes

  • 3 potatoes
  • 1/2 cups mayo
  • 1/2 tsp hot sauce
  • 1/4 tsp onion salt
  • 1/2 tsp steak seasoning
  • 1/8 tsp peppe
  • 2 cups cornbread dressing or stuffing
  1. Cut potato into 6 wedges.
  2. Mix mayo with spices.
  3. Roll in mayo and then dressing.
  4. Place in greased dish and bake at 375 for 45-50 minutes.
