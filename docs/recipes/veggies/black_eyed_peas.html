Black Eyed Peas

  • 1 Tbsp veggie oil
  • 1 cups onion
  • 1/2 cups celery and carrot
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 lb. dried black eyed peas
  • 1 ham hock
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  1. Heat oil in large pot.
  2. Add onion, celery, carrot.
  3. Saut√© 3-4 minutes until veggies are tender.
  4. Set aside.
  5. Put peas, ham hock, bay leaves, salt, pepper and water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally 1- 1/2 hours or until peas are tender.
  7. Add veggies for the last half hour.
  8. You can add all of the stuff together at once and do it, but the veggies tend to fall apart.
  9. You can also not cook the onion, celery and carrot and just add.
