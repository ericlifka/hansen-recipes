Summertime Zucchini Sauté

  • 1/4 cups butter
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp basil leaves
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 cups (4 med.) shredded zucchini
  • 1 cups cubed tomato
  • 2 tsp sliced olives
  • 1 cups cheese
  1. In skillet melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Stir in onion, salt, basil and garlic powder.
  3. Cook uncovered over medium heat.
  4. Stirring occasionally until onion is tender 4-5 min.
  5. Stir in zucchini.
  6. Continue cooking uncovered, stirring constantly until zucchini is heated through 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add tomatoes, olives and cheese.
  8. Cover and continue cooking 2 minutes.
