Tortellini With Alfredo Sauce

  • 16 oz. tortellini (cheese)
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cups Parmesan
  • salt and pepper
  1. Cook tortellini according to package directions.
  2. Drain.
  3. Transfer tortellini to a saut√© pan over medium-high heat and immediately add cream, garlic, Italian seasoning and 1/4 cups Parmesan cheese.
  4. Bring to a boil and heat, stirring occasionally, 7-10 minutes until sauce has thickened.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  6. Top with remaining 1/4 cups Parmesan and serve.
