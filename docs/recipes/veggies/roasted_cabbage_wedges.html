Roasted cabbage wedges

  • 3 T Olive Oil
  • 1 Cabbage sliced into 1 inch thick rounds
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 t caraway or fennel seed
  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Brush a rimmed baking sheet with olive oil.
  3. Place cabbage rounds in a single layer on sheet and brush with olive oil.
  4. Season with coarse salt and ground pepper, and sprinkle with caraway or fennel seeds.
  5. Roast until cabbage is tender and edges are golden, 40-45 minutes.
