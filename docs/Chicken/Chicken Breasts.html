Chicken Breasts

  1. 6 breasts, skinned, deboned if desired
  2. 1 can mushroom soup
  3. 1/2 cups sherry or wine
  4. 1 cups sour cream
  5. 1 small can mushrooms with juice
  • Lay chicken in baking dish.
  • Mix other ingredients together and pour over chicken.
  • Sprinkle with paprika.
  • Bake uncovered 325-350 about 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
