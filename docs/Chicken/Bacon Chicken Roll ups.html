Bacon Chicken Roll ups

  1. 12 bacon strips
  2. 6 4 oz. chicken breasts
  3. 8 oz. cr. cheese
  4. 1 sweet onion sliced
  • Cook bacon until cooked but not crisp.
  • Remove to paper towels.
  • Flatten chicken to 1/8 in thickness.
  • Spread cr. cheese down the center of each breast.
  • Top with onion.
  • Roll up from a long side; tuck ends in.
  • Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  • Wrap 2 bacon strips around each piece of chicken; secure with toothpicks.
  • Place in greased pan and bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
  • When I did this I would undercook the bacon because it was overcooked.
