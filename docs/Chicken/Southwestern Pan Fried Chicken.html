Southwestern Pan Fried Chicken

  1. 4 chicken breasts
  2. 1 cups Cheddar
  3. 1 Tbsp garlic
  4. 3 egg whites
  5. 2 chopped tomato
  6. 2 tsp cilantro
  7. 1 chili pepper
  8. 4 gr. onion
  9. 1/2 cups cream
  • Process breasts in whites in processor.
  • Add remaining ingredients.
  • Shape into patties.
  • Dip in 2 cups flour, 1 cups milk, and 4 cups bread crumbs.
  • Brown on both sides.
  • Cook in oven 7 minutes at 350.
  • Top with salsa.
