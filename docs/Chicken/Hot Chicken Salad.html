Hot Chicken Salad

  1. 2 cans cr. of chicken or mushroom
  2. 1 cups mayo or 1/2 cups mayo & 1/2 cups sour cream
  3. 1 cups each onion and celery
  4. 1 can green chilies
  5. 2 cans of chicken
  6. 3-6 hard boiled eggs
  7. 4 cups crushed potato chips
  • Mix together reserving 1 cups crushed chips.
  • Put into 9 x 13 sprinkle reserved chips on top.
  • Bake at 350 for 1 hour, or 400 for 30-45 minutes.
