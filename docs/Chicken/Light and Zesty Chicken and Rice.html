Light and Zesty Chicken and Rice

  1. 4 breast halves
  2. 1/3 cups Italian dressing
  3. 2/3 cups rice (uncooked)
  4. 1 can french fried onion
  5. 16 oz. frozen veggie combo
  6. 1 3/4 cups chicken bouillon
  7. 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Place breasts in 12 x 8 dish, top with dressings.
  • Bake uncovered at 400 for 20 min.
  • Place rice, veggies and 1/2 cups onion under chicken.
  • Combine bouillon and seasoning; pour over rice and veggies.
  • Bake uncovered 25 min.
  • Top with onions and bake 2-3 minutes.
