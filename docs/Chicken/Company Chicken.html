Company Chicken

  1. 5 boneless breasts
  2. 5 slices Swiss, muenster, or provolone
  3. 1 can cr. chicken soup
  4. 1/4 cups white wine, sherry or water
  5. 1 cups herb dressing
  6. 1/4 to 1/2 cups butter
  • Place chicken in baking dish.
  • Top each with a slice of cheese.
  • Combine soup and wine.
  • Pour over chicken.
  • Top with dressing mix and dot with butter.
  • Bake at 250 for 2 hours or 350 for 1 hour.
  • Serve over rice.
