Chicken Zucchini Hotdish

  1. 4 oz. uncooked spag. noodles
  2. 1 can cr. of mushroom
  3. 1 zucchini
  4. 1/2 cups onion
  5. 1/4 cups parmesan
  6. 1 cups mozzarella
  7. 1 clove garlic
  8. 1/2 cups milk
  9. 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • Break noodles in half.
  • Cook, drain.
  • Cook onion and garlic.
  • Blend in soup.
  • Add zucchini and milk.
  • Cook until zucchini is tender.
  • Stir in mozzarella.
  • Add noodles and chicken.
  • Put in casserole; add parmesan on top.
  • Bake 40 min. at 350.
