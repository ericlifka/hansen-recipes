Rachel Rays Turkey Chimichangas

  1. 1 lb. smoked turkey, chopped (I use deli turkey mesquite is good have them sliced thick)
  2. 1 Tbsp chili powder
  3. 2 cups cabbage slaw
  4. 1-2 chipotles in adobo sauce (I use 1)
  5. 1 cups tomato sauce
  6. 3 green onions
  7. 4 12‚Äù tortillas
  8. 1 1/2 cups cheese
  9. 2 Tbsp oil
  10. 1 cups sour cream
  11. 2 Tbsp parsley
  12. salt and pepper
  • Put turkey in bowl with chili powder.
  • Mix cabbage, chipotles, tomato sauce and green onions.
  • Mix with turkey.
  • Put cheese near 1 edge of tortilla.
  • Pile 1/4 of filling.
  • Tuck sides up and roll tightly.
  • Brush pan with oil.
  • Brush wraps with oil and bake 15-20 minutes at 400.
