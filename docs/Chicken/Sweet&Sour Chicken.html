Sweet&Sour Chicken

  1. chicken
  2. 8 oz. Russian dressing
  3. 8 oz. apricot preserves
  4. 1 onion soup mix
  5. 1/4 cups water
  6. pineapple
  7. green pepper
  • Put chicken in pan.
  • Mix Russian dressing, apricot preserves, onions soup mix and water together and pour over chicken.
  • Cover with foil.
  • Bake covered at 350 for 1 hour.
  • Uncover and bake another 30 minutes with pineapple and green pepper.
