Greek Chicken

  1. 1 quarter fryer chicken
  2. 2 green peppers, sliced
  3. 1 clove garlic
  4. 4 Tbsp olive oil
  5. 1 cups chicken broth
  6. 1 large onion
  7. 1 tsp salt
  8. 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Heat oil in skillet.
  • Saut√© garlic, onion and peppers until limp.
  • Set aside. In same oil, brown chicken.
  • Place chicken in casserole.
  • Pour green peppers over chicken with broth.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Cover and bake at 350 for 1 hour.
