Stir Fry Vegetables With Chicken Breast

  1. 1 chicken breast, diced
  2. 1/2 lb. green beans
  3. 2 carrots, cut thinly
  4. 1 stalk celery, sliced
  5. 1 cups mushrooms
  6. 1 cups cauliflower florets
  7. 2 cups cabbage, cut in 2x2” piecessalt and pepper
  8. 2 Tbsp cornstarch with 2 Tbsp water
  9. 2 Tbsp fish sauce
  10. 6 Tbsp oil
  11. 1/2 onion, sliced
  12. marinade:
  13. 1 tsp sherry
  14. 1 tsp cornstarch
  15. salt & pepper
  • Mix marinade ingredients and marinade chicken.
  • Sauté chicken breast in 3 Tbsp oil till tender.
  • Set aside.
  • Heat 3 Tbsp oil.
  • Sauté garlic and onions.
  • Add fish sauce.
  • Stir in vegetables according to their tenderness.
  • Heat and stir for about 4-5 minutes.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Put in cornstarch mix last and stir another 1-2 minutes.
