Chicken Rice Casserole

  1. 2 Tbsp oil
  2. 1 onion
  3. 3 cups cooked chicken
  4. 2 15 oz. cans green beans, drain and rinse
  5. 8 oz. water chestnuts, chopped
  6. 4 oz. pimento
  7. 1 cr. of celery soup
  8. 1/2 cups mayo
  9. 1/2 cups sour cream
  10. 6 oz. box cooked wild rice
  11. 1 cups cheddar cheese
  • Saute onion in oil
  • Mix in all other ingredients
  • Put in greased pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
