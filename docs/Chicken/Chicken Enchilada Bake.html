Chicken Enchilada Bake

  1. 2 Tbsp butter
  2. 1/2 cups chopped onion
  3. 1 clove garlic
  4. 4 oz. green chilies, drained
  5. 1/2 cups sour cream
  6. 1/2 cups sliced ripe olives
  7. 1 can cr. of chicken soup
  8. 2 cups chicken
  9. 4 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
  10. 8 tortillas
  11. 1/4 cups milk
  • Melt butter.
  • Saut√© onion and garlic till tender.
  • Stir in 1/4 c.
  • olives, green chilies, sour cream and soup.
  • Mix well.
  • Reserve 3/4 cups soup mix and set aside.
  • Fold in chicken and 1/2 cups cheese to remaining sauce.
  • Warm tortillas.
  • Fill with chicken mix.
  • Can put some cheese on top of the chicken.
  • Roll up.
  • Place seam side down in 9 x 13.
  • In small bowl combine reserved 3/4 cups sauce and milk.
  • Spoon over tortillas.
  • Bake at 350 about 35 min.
  • Sprinkle with remaining olives and cheese and bake a few more minutes to melt cheese.
