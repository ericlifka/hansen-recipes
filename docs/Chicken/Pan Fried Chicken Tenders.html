Pan Fried Chicken Tenders

  1. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  2. 2 lb. chicken tenders
  3. 2 cloves garlic
  4. 6 green onions
  5. 4 Tbsp Marsala wine
  6. 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  7. salt and pepper
  • Heat frying pan.
  • Add the oil, chicken strips, garlic and green onions.
  • Saut√© over high heat until the chicken is lightly browned and tender.
  • Remove from pan.
  • Add the Marsala and lemon juice, and allow liquid to reduce for a moment.
  • Return chicken to the hot pan and add salt and pepper.
  • Do not overcook and serve immediately.
