Chicken Hotdish

  1. 2 cups cooked chicken
  2. 16 oz. cooked broccoli
  3. 1 pkg. cooked noodles
  4. 2 cans cr. of chicken soup
  5. 1 1/2 cups mayo
  6. 1 1/2 tsp curry
  7. 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  8. 1 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Cut up Spread out chicken in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
  • Layer boroccoli and noodles over chicken.
  • Mix together soup, mayo, lemon juice, and curry, spread on top.
  • Bake 350 for 1 hour.
  • Spread cheese on top 15 minutes before it's done.
  • Can be made in advance and can make it smaller.
