Chicken Pasta

  1. 2-4 Tbsp butter
  2. 2 breast halves, cut up
  3. 1 of each green, red pepper
  4. 1 garlic
  5. 1 onion, diced
  6. 1 tomato, chopped
  7. 1 cups chicken stock
  8. 3/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  9. 2 tsp cajun seasoning
  10. salt, pepper
  11. 1 can mushrooms
  12. 8 oz. heavy cream
  • Cook pasta, drain, set aside
  • Cook breasts and vegetables in butter
  • Deglaze with chicken stock
  • mix in seasonings and cream
  • reduce heat and bring to a simmer
  • serve over pasta
