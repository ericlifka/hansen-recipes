Chicken or Turkey Tetrazzini

  1. 1/4 cups butter
  2. 1/4 cups flour
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 1/4 tsp pepper
  5. 1 cups chicken broth
  6. 1 cups whipping cream
  7. 2 Tbsp sherry
  8. 8 oz. spaghetti, cooked, broken in small pieces, drained
  9. 2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey
  10. 1 can sliced mushrooms, drained
  11. 1/2 cups Parmesan
  • Melt butter over low heat.
  • Add flour and seasonings.
  • Stir till smooth.
  • Remove from heat and add broth and milk.
  • Return to heat and stir until boiling.
  • Boil 1 min.
  • Add sherry, stir, then add noodles, chicken and mushrooms.
  • Pour into 8 x 8 x 2 pan.
  • Sprinkle with cheese.
  • Bake 30 minutes or until bubbly.
