Enchiladas Fantastic

  1. 1 lb. ground turkey
  2. 2 cups picante sauce
  3. 10 oz. frozen spinach, thawed, squeezed dry
  4. 2 tsp ground cumin, divided
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 8 oz. cr. cheese
  7. 12 tortillas
  8. 1 can (14 1/2 oz) diced tomatoes in juice
  9. 1 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Optional toppings:
  11. lettuce
  12. olives
  13. avocado
  14. sour cream
  • Cook turkey until o longer pink, breaking it into small pieces with a spoon.
  • Add 1 cups Picante, spinach 1 1/2 tsp cumin, and salt.
  • Cook and stir 5 min. until most of the liquid has evaporated.
  • Add cream cheese, stirring just until melted.
  • Spoon 1/3 cups filling down center of each warm tortilla.
  • Roll up and place seam side down in greased 9 x 13.
  • Combine tomatoes and remaining picante and 1/2 tsp cumin.
  • Spoon over enchiladas.
  • Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes or until hot.
  • Sprinkle with cheese, cook a few more minutes.
  • If you make any of these in advance and refrigerate, the cooking time will be about 10 or 15 minutes longer.
