Chicken Elegante

  1. 2 cups Stove top
  2. 1 cups boiling water
  3. 1/4 cups butter
  4. 1 jar (12 oz) chicken gravy
  5. 1 jar mushrooms, drained
  6. 1-2 Tbsp wine or apple juice
  7. 4 boneless breasts
  8. 1 1/3 cups broccoli cuts, thawed
  • Combine stuffing mix, water, and 2 Tbsp butter.
  • Let stand 5 min.
  • Mix gravy, mushrooms and wine.
  • Set aside.
  • Lightly brown chicken in 2 Tbsp butter.
  • Place in baking pan; top each with stuffing, broccoli and gravy mix.
  • Cover and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
