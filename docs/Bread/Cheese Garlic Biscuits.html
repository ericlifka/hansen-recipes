Cheese Garlic Biscuits

  1. 4 cups Bisquick
  2. 1 1/3 cups milk
  3. 4 oz. cheese
  4. 1/2 cups butter
  5. 1 Tbsp parsley
  6. 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Stir bisquick, milk and cheddar together.
  • Drop by tablespoons on greased cookie sheet.
  • Bake at 450 for 6-8 minutes.
  • Brush butter, parsley, and garlic powder over.
