Southern Living Sweet Potato Biscuits

  1. 4 cups flour
  2. 2 Tbsp baking powder
  3. 2 tsp salt
  4. 1 cups butter
  5. 3/4-1 cups buttermilk
  6. 1 cups sweet potato, mashed
  • Combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  • Cut in butter.
  • Stir together potato and buttermilk.
  • Turn dough onto floured surface.
  • Roll to 1/2” thick.
  • Grease cookie sheet.
  • Use a 3” biscuit cutter.
  • Bake at 425 for 10-15 minutes.
