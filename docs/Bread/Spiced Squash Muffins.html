Spiced Squash Muffins

  1. 2 cups flour
  2. 1/3 cups brown sugar
  3. 2 tsp baking powder
  4. 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1/4 tsp ginger
  7. 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  8. 2 eggs
  9. 3/4 cups squash
  10. 3/4 cups light corn syrup
  11. 1/4 cups butter, melted
  12. 1/4 cups veggie oil
  13. 1 tsp vanilla
  14. Topping:
  15. 4 Tbsp butter
  16. 1/2 cups brown sugar
  17. 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Combine all dry ingredients and then wet muffin mix stuff.
  • Fill paper lined muffin cups 3/4 full.
  • Combine topping mix until crumbly.
  • Sprinkle on muffins.
  • Bake 400 for 15-20 minutes.
  • Cool 5 minutes before putting on wire rack.
  • Serve warm.
