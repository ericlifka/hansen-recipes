Zucchini Bread

  1. 1 cups oil
  2. 2 cups sugar
  3. 3 eggs2
  4. c. grated raw, peeled zucchini
  5. 3 cups flour
  6. 3 tsp cinnamon
  7. 1 tsp soda
  8. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Mix oil and sugar.
  • Beat in eggs, one at a time.
  • Add rest of ingredients.
  • Bake in 2 greased loaf pans 1 hour at 325.
