Poppy Seed Orange Cupcakes

  1. Cupcake Portion:
  2. 1 1/2 cups flour
  3. 1 cups sugar
  4. 1/2 cups sour cream
  5. 1/3 cups butter, softened
  6. 1 egg
  7. 1 Tbsp poppy seeds
  8. 2 Tbsp orange peel zest, and orange juice
  9. 1/2 tsp baking soda and salt
  10. Icing Portion:
  11. 1/2 cups butter
  12. 3 oz. cream cheese
  13. 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  14. 1 Tbsp orange zest
  • Cupcake Portion:
  • In large bowl combine all ingredients; beat on low speed until ingredients are moistened.
  • Spoon into muffin pans.
  • Bake at 400 for 18-22 minutes or until lightly browned.
  • Icing Portion:
  • Beat together all icing ingredients until creamy, then add on top of the cupcakes.
