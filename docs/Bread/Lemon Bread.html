Lemon Bread

  1. 1 pkg. lemon cake mix
  2. 1/2 cups oil
  3. 1 small pkg. instant lemon pudding
  4. 1 cups cold water
  5. 1/2 cups poppy seeds
  6. 4 eggs
  • Mix all ingredients together and beat 4 min.
  • Pour into 2 bread pans.
  • Spray pans with Pam so bread comes out easier.
  • Bake 350ยบ for 40 min.
  • Cool and wrap in foil.
