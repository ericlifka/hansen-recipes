Sweet Potato Bread

  1. 3 cups flour
  2. 1 tsp baking soda
  3. 1 tsp salt
  4. 3 tsp cinnamon
  5. 2 cups sugar
  6. 4 eggs
  7. 2 cups cooked sweet potato
  8. 1/2 cups nuts
  9. 1 cups oil or melted shortening
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • Coat 2 loaf pans with vegetable spray.
  • In bowl combine flour, soda, salt, cinnamon, sugar, sweet potatoes, and nuts and mix together.
  • With a spoon make a deep well in center and add eggs and oil.
  • Stir carefully to dampen all the dry ingredients.
  • Pour into pans and bake 1 hour or until done.
