Cranberry Orange Muffins

  1. 1 3/4 cups flour
  2. 5 Tbsp sugar
  3. 2 tsp baking powder
  4. 1 beaten egg
  5. 3/4 cups orange juice
  6. 1/3 cups veggie oil
  7. 1 cups cranberries, chopped
  • In bowl stir together flour, 2 Tbsp sugar, baking powder, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  • Make a well in center.
  • In a small bowl combine egg, orange juice, and oil; add all at once to flour mixture.
  • Stir until moistened.
  • Toss cranberries with 2 Tbsp sugar, fold into batter.
  • Fill muffin cups 3/4 full.
  • Sprinkle muffins with remaining sugar.
  • Bake at 400 for 18-20 minutes.
