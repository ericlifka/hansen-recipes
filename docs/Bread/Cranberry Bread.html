Cranberry Bread

  1. 2 cups flour
  2. 1/2 tsp salt
  3. 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. 3/4 cups sugar
  6. 1 egg
  7. 2 Tbsp shortening
  8. 1/2 cups orange juice
  9. 2T. hot water
  10. 1/2 cups nuts
  11. 1 cups cranberry
  • Cut cranberries and nuts.
  • Sift the dry ingredients together.
  • Add liquid and combine until dry ingredients are moistened.
  • Fold in nuts, cranberries.
  • Bake 1 hour and 10 minutes at 350.
