Pumpkin Bread

  1. 4 eggs
  2. 2/3 cups butter
  3. 3 cups sugar
  4. 15 oz pumpkin
  5. 3 cups flour
  6. 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. 2 tsp baking soda
  9. 1 tsp cinnamon
  10. 1/2 tsp cloves
  11. 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  12. 1 cups chopped nuts (optional)
  • Combine eggs, butter and sugar, blending well.
  • Add the rest of ingredients.
  • Pour into 2 greased 8 1/2 x 5 1/2 loaf pans and bake at 350 for 1 hour.
  • Turn onto racks and cool.
