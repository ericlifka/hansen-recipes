Date Muffins

  1. 1 cups chopped dates
  2. 1 cups boiling water
  3. 1 Tbsp shortening
  4. 1 egg, beaten
  5. 1 tsp vanilla
  6. 1 1/2 cups flour
  7. 1 cups sugar
  8. 1 tsp baking powder
  9. 1/2 tsp salt
  10. 1 cups pecans
  • Combine dates, water, and shortening.
  • Let stand 1 hour.
  • Stir in egg and vanilla.
  • Combine flour and rest of ingredients.
  • Add date mix and stir till moist.
  • Spoon into muffin pans.
  • Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
