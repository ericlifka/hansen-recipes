Tuna Salad

  1. Macaroni rings, or sea shells
  2. tuna, drained
  3. celery
  4. onion
  5. Dressing:
  6. Mayo
  7. milk
  8. salt
  9. pepper
  10. garlic powder
  • Mix however much of the ingredients you want.
  • For the dressing start by mixing 1 cups mayo with some milk (add a little at a time until it is the right consistency) add seasonings to taste.
  • Mix all together.
