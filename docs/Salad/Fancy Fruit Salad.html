Fancy Fruit Salad

  1. 3 Tbsp Tang
  2. 1 small box instant vanilla or french vanilla pudding
  3. 2 cups chunk pineapple, save juice
  4. 1 cups mandarin oranges, drained
  5. 1 cups chopped pecans
  6. 3 large bananas, sliced
  • Mix pineapple juice with Tang.
  • Sprinkle pudding over fruit.
  • Pour juice over fruit and mix well.
