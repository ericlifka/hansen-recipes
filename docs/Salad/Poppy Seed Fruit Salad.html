Poppy Seed Fruit Salad

  1. Fruit mix:
  2. 1 cups strawberries
  3. 1 cups pineapple
  4. 1 cups blueberries
  5. 1 cups watermelon
  6. Dressing:
  7. 1/4 cups honey
  8. 1/4 cups limeade
  9. 2 tsp poppy seeds
  • Drizzle dressing over fruit, toss gently.
  • Serve.
  • Can use almost any fresh fruit.
