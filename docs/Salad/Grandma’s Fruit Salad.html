Grandma’s Fruit Salad

  1. Fresh fruit:
  2. apples
  3. strawberries
  4. bananas
  5. grapes slice in half
  6. Canned fruit:
  7. Queen Ann cherries
  8. pears
  9. peaches
  10. pineapple
  11. Dressing:
  12. 6 oz. frozen concentrate orange juice
  13. 9 oz. Clover Honey (I use slightly less)
  • Mix fruit with dressing.
  • Can add new fruit and still eat.
