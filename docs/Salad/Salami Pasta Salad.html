Salami Pasta Salad

  1. 2 cups small pasta shells
  2. 3/4 cups green pepper and fresh tomato
  3. 1/2 cups salami, cube
  4. 1/2 cups pepperoni, chopped
  5. 1/2 cups quartered whole ripe olives
  6. 1/3 cups onion
  7. 2 oz provolone cubed
  8. Dressing:
  9. 1/3 cups v. oil
  10. 1/4 cups red wine vinegar
  11. 2 Tbsp sugar
  12. 1 1/2 tsp salt and dried oregano
  13. 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Cook pasta and drain.
  • Add rest of ingredients except dressing to pasta.
  • In a jar combine dressing ingredients and shake well.
  • Pour over pasta and mix.
  • Cover and refrigerate. before serving.
