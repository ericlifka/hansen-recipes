Spinach Strawberry Salad

  1. 2 bunches spinach
  2. 1 basket strawberries
  3. 10 thin slices purple onion
  4. Dressing:
  5. 1/2 cups oil
  6. 1/2 cups sugar
  7. 1/4 cups apple cider vinegar
  8. 1/4 tsp Worcestershire
  9. 1/2 tsp minced onion
  10. 1 Tbsp poppy seeds
  11. 2 Tbsp sesame seeds
  12. 1/8 tsp paprika
  • Rinse and dry spinach.
  • Remove stems.
  • Slice strawberries.
  • Layer spinach, sliced strawberries and onions 3 times (9 layers).
  • When ready to serve put on dressing.
