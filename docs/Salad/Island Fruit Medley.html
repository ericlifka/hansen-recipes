Island Fruit Medley

  1. 1/2 cups Catalina
  2. 2 Tbsp light brown sugar
  3. 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  4. 1 mango or peach
  5. 1/2 cups pineapple chunks
  6. 2 kiwi
  7. 1 cups cantaloupe
  8. 8 pineapple slices
  9. 1/2 cups raspberry or strawberry halves
  10. 8 cantaloupe slices
  • Mix catalina, brown sugar and cinnamon.
  • Toss pineapple chunks, mango, kiwi, cantaloupe and raspberry with dressing.
  • Arrange slices of pineapple and cantaloupe on plate, top with marinated fruit mix.
