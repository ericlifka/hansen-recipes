Frozen Summer Fruit Salad

  1. 1 can toasted coconut
  2. 3 Tbsp melted butter
  3. 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  4. 1 cups mini marshmallows
  5. 1 cups pecans
  6. 3/4 cups chopped peaches or nectarines
  7. 8 oz black cherry yogurt
  8. 1 cups pitted and halved cherries
  9. 3/4 cups grape halves
  10. 1/2 cups lemon juice
  11. 4 oz. cool whip
  • Line a loaf pan with foil extending above side of pan.
  • Combine coconut and butter.
  • Press on bottom of pan.
  • In bowl combine remaining ingredients except Cool Whip.
  • Mix well.
  • Fold in cool whip.
  • Pour into pan.
  • Cover, freeze 6 hours.
  • To serve remove from pan; peel off foil and slice.
