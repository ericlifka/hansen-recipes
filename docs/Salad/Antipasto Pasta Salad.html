Antipasto Pasta Salad

  1. 16 oz. tricolor spiral pasta
  2. 16 oz. giardiniera, drained and cut up
  3. ripe olives - 1 3.8 oz and one 2 1/4 oz.
  4. 5 3/4 oz. pimento stuffed olives, sliced
  5. 7 oz. roasted red pepper, drained and chopped
  6. 8 oz. summer sausage, cubed
  7. 8 oz pepper jack, cubed
  8. 1 cups Italian salad dressing
  • Cook pasta and drain, combine with veggies and meat and cheese, add dressing, toss.
  • Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.
