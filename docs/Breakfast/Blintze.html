Blintze

  1. 4 eggs
  2. 2 cups milk
  3. 1 1/2 cups flour
  • Mix all together.
  • Heat cast iron pan, put 1 Tbsp oil in.
  • Pour a small bit of stuff in pan and spread around.
  • Flip after a minute or two and cook other side.
  • This makes about 12, I generally make 2-3 batches for all of us.
