Eggs Scramble

  1. 4 cups croutons
  2. 1 tsp salt
  3. 1 tsp prepared mustard
  4. 2 cups shredded cheese
  5. 8 beaten eggs
  6. 4 cups milk
  7. 1/8 tsp pepper
  8. 2 cups ham or 1 lb. sausage, cooked
  • Place croutons and cheese in greased 9 x 13.
  • Combine rest of ingredients except meat.
  • Pour over croutons and cheese.
  • Top with meat.
  • Bake at 325 for 1 hour.
  • You can halve this recipe for an 8 x 8 pan.
