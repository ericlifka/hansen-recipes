Strawberry French Toast

  1. 5 eggs
  2. 3/4 cups milk
  3. 1 tsp vanilla
  4. 8 slices of french bread
  5. 5 cups frozen strawberries
  6. 2/3 cups sugar
  7. 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Mix eggs, milk and vanilla in a bowl.
  • Dip french bread into egg mixture for 30 seconds on each side.
  • Let stand 10 minutes.
  • Grease 9 x 13.
  • Spread strawberries in pan.
  • Combine sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle half over berries.
  • Put bread on top.
  • Sprinkle with remaining sugar and cinnamon.
  • Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.
