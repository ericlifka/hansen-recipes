Breakfast Casserole

  1. 3 cups frozen hash browns
  2. 1 cups diced ham
  3. 4 eggs
  4. 1/4 tsp pepper, salt
  5. 3/4 cups Monterey jack cheese
  6. 1/4 cups green onion
  7. 12 oz evaporated milk
  • Grease 2 qt. dish.
  • Put potatoes on bottom.
  • Sprinkle with cheese, ham, onion.
  • Combine rest of ingredients and pour over.
  • Bake uncovered 350 for 45 min. or until set.
