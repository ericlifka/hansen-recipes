Pannekoeken

  1. 2 Tbsp butter
  2. 6 eggs
  3. 1 cups flour
  4. 2 Tbsp sugar
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1 cups milk
  • Preheat oven to 400.
  • Place butter in pan.
  • Heat in oven for about 2 minutes, or until butter melts.
  • Spread melted butter evenly over bottom of pan.
  • Set aside.
  • In bowl, beat eggs slightly.
  • Stir in flour, sugar, and salt.
  • Gradually add milk, beating until smooth.
  • Pour into pan.
  • Bake at 400 for 15 minutes.
  • Reduce temperature to 325.
  • Bake 40-45 minutes longer, or until deep golden brown.
  • Immediately loosen pannekoeken from pan and slide onto serving plate.
  • Top with fresh fruit and powdered sugar.
  • Cut into wedges.
