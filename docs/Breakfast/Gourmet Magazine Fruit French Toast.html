Gourmet Magazine Fruit French Toast

  1. 3 eggs
  2. 1 1/2 cups milk
  3. 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  4. 1 tsp vanilla
  5. 1/4 cups brown sugar
  6. 1 cups fruit
  • Mix all but fruit.
  • Pour over french bread.
  • Can refrigerate overnight.
  • Top bread with fruit.
  • Heat 2 Tbsp butter with 1/4 cups brown sugar.
  • Pour over bread.
  • Bake 20 minutes.
