Omelets for a Crowd (Paula Deen)

  1. 6 large eggs
  2. Salt and pepper
  3. 2-3 resealable plastic freezer bags
  4. Add ins (all are optional):
  5. Cheddar cheese
  6. Feta
  7. Mozzarella
  8. Cooked bacon or sausage crumbled
  9. Diced ham
  10. Chopped bell peppers
  11. Chopped spinach
  12. Diced red onion
  13. Diced tomato
  14. Saut√©ed mushrooms
  15. Black olives
  • In a medium bowl whisk together eggs, salt, pepper and any of the desired add ins.
  • Divide and pour the egg mix into resealable plastic bags.
  • Boil a large pot of water and drop in the sealed bags.
  • Simmer the bags 5-8 minutes (can take longer).
  • Remove bags from the water with tongs and drain them on a paper towel lined plate.
  • Allow to cool slightly before handling.
  • Open the bags and pour the cooked omelet on a plate.
  • Also be careful so that your bags do not touch the side of the pan or you could get a hole in your bag.
