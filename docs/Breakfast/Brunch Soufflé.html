Brunch Soufflé

  1. 8 slices bread, cubed
  2. 2/3 lb. velveeta cheese, sliced
  3. 8 eggs
  4. 4 cups milk
  5. 1/8 tsp each salt, pepper and dry mustard
  • Cut crusts from bread and cube.
  • Put bread in greased 9 x 13” pan and put on sliced cheese.
  • Beat eggs slightly and add seasonings.
  • Refrigerate overnight.
  • Bake at 325 for 1 hour.
