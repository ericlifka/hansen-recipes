Apple French Toast

  1. 1 cups brown sugar
  2. 1/2 cups butter
  3. 2 Tbsp light corn syrup
  4. 2 apples, peeled, sliced
  5. 3 eggs
  6. 1 cups milk
  7. 1 tsp vanilla
  8. 9 slices french bread or baguettes
  9. Syrup:
  10. 1 cups apple sauce
  11. 10 oz. jar apple jelly
  12. 1/2 tsp cinnamon and ground cloves
  • In small sauce pan, heat brown sugar, butter and corn syrup until thick.
  • Pour into an ungreased 9 x 13‚Äù pan.
  • Arrange apple slices on top.
  • Beat together eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  • Dip bread into egg mix and place bread over apples.
  • Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  • Remove 30 minutes before baking.
  • Bake uncovered for 35-40 minutes at 350.
  • Combine syrup ingredients in pan.
  • Cook and stir until hot.
  • Serve over toast.
