Cajun Jalapenos

  1. 8 large jalapeno
  2. 3 oz. soft cream cheese
  3. 1/2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
  4. 1 tsp cajun seasoning
  5. bacon or bacos, opt.
  • Mix cream cheese, cheddar and cajun.
  • Cut jalapenos in half and stuff with 1 1/2 tsp cream cheese.
  • Top with bacon.
  • Bake 350 for 25-30 minutes.
