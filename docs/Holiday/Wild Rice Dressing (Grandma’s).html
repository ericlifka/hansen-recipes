Wild Rice Dressing (Grandma’s)

  1. 1 cups wild rice
  2. Rice toppings:
  3. 1 huge onion diced
  4. 4 cloves garlic chopped
  5. 1 lb. mushrooms cut up.
  6. Gizzards:
  7. 1 lb. gizzards
  8. 1 onion chopped up (not diced)
  9. 4 cloves chopped garlic
  10. 1 tsp. thyme
  11. 1 tsp. sage
  12. 1 tablespoon oregano leaves
  13. 1/2 tsp. salt
  14. 1/2 tsp. pepper
  15. 1 tsp. garlic salt
  • Soak wild rice in water the night before.
  • Cook gizzards and neck, etc. from the fowl for 2 hours covered in water with all gizzard seasoning ingredients.
  • Drain off liquid and save.
  • Cut up gizzards into tiny pieces and refrigerate until putting dressing together.
  • Put 3 cups of saved liquid into pot with drained rice.
  • Cook 45 minutes.
  • While rice is cooking, sauté rice toppings.
  • Cook each separately in butter.
  • Do the garlic first, then add onions.
  • Put into dish and use same pan to do mushrooms.
  • Don’t be stingy with butter.
  • Throw rice, onions, garlic, mushrooms, gizzards together.
  • It can be served right away or reheated later.
  • You can also add pork sausage.
  • You can half this recipe.
