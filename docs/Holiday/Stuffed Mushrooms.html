Stuffed Mushrooms

  1. 8 oz. fresh mushrooms
  2. 1 small onion
  3. 1 box stovetop dressing (stuffing)
  4. PAM
  • Take stem out of mushrooms.
  • Chop stems up with some onion.
  • Cook up until softened.
  • Prepare 1/2 cups stove top dressing.
  • Mix stove top and onions.
  • Stuff mushrooms with stove top.
  • Spray pan with Pam and tops of mushrooms with Pam.
  • Bake at 350ยบ for 20-25 minutes.
