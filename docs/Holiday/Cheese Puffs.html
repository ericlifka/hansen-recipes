Cheese Puffs

  1. 1/2 cups soft butter
  2. 1 cups cheddar cheese
  3. 1 cups flour
  4. 1/2 tsp salt
  5. stuffed olives
  • Cream butter and cheese.
  • Add flour and salt.
  • Mold with hands around an olive.
  • Bake at 400ยบ for 12 - 15 minutes.
  • (Can be frozen but need to cook 5 minutes longer.)
