Viva Mushrooms

  1. 1 lb. mushrooms
  2. 1 tsp salt
  3. 1/3 cups softened butter
  4. 1/8 tsp pepper
  5. 1 Tbsp parsley flakes
  6. 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  7. 2 Tbsp dry onions
  8. 1 1/2 Tbsp flour
  9. 1 Tbsp prepared mustard
  10. 1 cups heavy cream
  • Place cleaned mushrooms in buttered casserole dish.
  • Combine butter, parsley, onion, mustard, seasonings and flour.
  • It will be like a paste.
  • Dot on top of mushrooms.
  • Pour cream over.
  • Bake at 350ยบ for 55 minutes.
