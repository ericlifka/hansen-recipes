Cranberry Whip

  1. 1 bag cranberries
  2. 1 cups sugar, divided
  3. 12-14 oz. crushed pineapple, drained
  4. 1 1/2 cups mini marshmallows
  5. chopped nuts
  6. 1/2 pt. whipping cream
  • Drain and grind a bag of cranberries.
  • Stir in 1/2 cups sugar, crushed pineapple, mini marshmallows, chopped nuts.
  • Let sit in the fridge over night.
  • Next day: Whip cream with 1/2 cups sugar.
  • Fold into the cranberry mix.
