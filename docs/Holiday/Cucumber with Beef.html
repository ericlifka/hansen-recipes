Cucumber with Beef

  1. 1 cucumber
  2. 3-4 oz. cr. cheese (or more)
  3. Buddig corned beef
  • Have cream cheese at room temperature.
  • I take the beef and dot each corner with cream cheese and top with another beef.
  • You will need 1 package per cucumber.
  • I get the beef all ready first.
  • Spread cream cheese over the cucumber.
  • Put the beef on top of the cucumber.
  • I wrap in saran wrap and then put in ziploc bag.
  • Refrigerate.
  • Cut into slices.
  • You can also do this with a pickle.
  • Grandma H. uses deli corned beef.
