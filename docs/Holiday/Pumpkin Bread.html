Pumpkin Bread

  1. 4 eggs
  2. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. 2/3 cups soft butter
  4. 2 tsp baking soda
  5. 3 cups sugar
  6. 1 tsp cinnamon
  7. 15 oz. pumpkin
  8. 1/2 tsp cloves
  9. 3 cups flour
  10. 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  11. 1/2 tsp salt
  12. 1 cups chopped nuts
  • Combine eggs, butter and sugar, blending well.
  • Add the rest of the ingredients.
  • Pour into 2 greased loaf pans.
  • Bake at 350ยบ for 1 hour.
  • Turn onto racks and cool.
