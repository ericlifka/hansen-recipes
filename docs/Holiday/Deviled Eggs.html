Deviled Eggs

  1. eggs
  2. option 1:
  3. salad dressing
  4. salt and pepper
  5. relish
  6. mustard
  7. option 2: (grandma h)
  8. 1 Tbsp mustard
  9. 1 or more Tbsp sweet relish
  10. 1 Tbsp sugar
  11. 1 Tbsp vinegar
  12. 2 whopping Tbsp miracle whip
  13. salt and pepper
  • Put eggs in pan.
  • Boil 2 minutes.
  • Remove from heat, place cover on pan.
  • Let stand 17 minutes.
  • Drain.
  • slice in half, remove egg yolks.
  • Mash the egg yolks with the ingeredients from either option 1 or 2.
  • Spoon mixture back into egg halves.
  • Refrigerate.
