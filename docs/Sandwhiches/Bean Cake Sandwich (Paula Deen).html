Bean Cake Sandwich (Paula Deen)

  1. 3 cups leftover cooked white beans (can use canned hopefully with a ham hock flavor)
  2. flour, enough to bind patties
  3. 1 Tbsp butter
  4. 1 Tbsp veggie oil
  5. mayo
  6. 8 slices white bread
  7. 1 onion sliced in rings
  • In a bowl, coarsely mash beans with a fork and add flour until well combined.
  • Shape bean mix into 4 patties.
  • Melt butter and oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add patties and brown on both sides, till crispy.
  • Spread mayo on bread, top with patty and onions.
