Planet Hollywood Chicken Crunch

  1. Vegetable oil for frying
  2. 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (or tenders)
  3. 2 cups Capt. Crunch cereal
  4. 1/2 cups corn flake crumbs
  5. 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 1/4 tsp pepper
  9. 1 beaten egg
  10. 1 cups milk
  11. Creole Mustard Sauce:
  12. 2 Tbsp Grey Poupon Dijon mustar
  13. 3 Tbsp mayo
  14. 1 tsp mustard
  15. 1 tsp cream style horseradish
  16. 1 tsp honey
  • Preheat oil to 350.
  • I do it in the deep fryer at 350 for 4 min. 30 sec.
  • I use tenders, but if you use breast halves, cut them into strips.
  • Smash cracker crumbs.
  • Combine cereals, and spices.
  • Mix egg with milk.
  • Dredge chicken in milk, then coat with the cereal mix.
  • Do this to all your tenders.
  • Fry.
  • Drain on paper towels.
  • Serve with Creole mustard Sauce.
