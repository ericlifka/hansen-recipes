Ranch Chicken Salad Sandwiches

  1. 1/4 cups mayo
  2. 3 Tbsp ranch dressing
  3. 3 Tbsp sour cream
  4. 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  5. 1/8 tsp pepper
  6. 2 cups cooked chicken breast
  7. 1/2 cups diced celery
  8. 2 Tbsp diced sweet red pepper
  9. 1 Tbsp green onion
  10. 6 hamburger buns
  11. lettuce
  12. tomato slices
  • In a small bowl, combine mayo, ranch dressing, sour cream, lemon juice, and pepper.
  • Stir in chicken, celery, red pepper and onion until combined.
  • Spoon 1/3 cups onto each bun bottom; top with lettuce and tomato.
  • Replace bun tops.
