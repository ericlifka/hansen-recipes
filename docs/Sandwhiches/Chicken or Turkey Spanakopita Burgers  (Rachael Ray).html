Chicken or Turkey Spanakopita Burgers (Rachael Ray)

  1. 1 Tbsp olive oil
  2. 1 Tbsp butter
  3. 3 cloves garlic
  4. 1 red onion, half chopped, half sliced
  5. 10 oz. defrosted spinach
  6. 2 tsp oregano
  7. 1/4 lb. feta
  8. 1 1/3 lb. ground chicken or turkey
  9. 1 Tbsp montreal steak seasoning
  10. 1/3 seedless cucumber, thinly sliced lengthwise
  11. 2 plum tomato
  12. salt and pepper
  13. 1 cups arugula, chopped
  14. 4 crusty rolls
  15. Topping:
  16. 2 roasted red peppers
  17. 1/4 cups flat leaf parsley
  18. 1/4 cups kalamata olives
  19. hot pepper rings, optional
  • For topping:
  • Put in food processor, season with salt and pepper and process until a paste forms.
  • For burgers:
  • Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  • Add a little olive oil and a Tbsp of butter.
  • When butter melts, add chopped garlic and red onion.
  • Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Transfer onions and garlic to a bowl to cool.
  • Return pan to heat.
  • Wring the defrosted spinach dry.
  • Separate the spinach as you add it to the bowl with cool onions, garlic and season with 1 tsp of oregano.
  • Add in feta then chicken, steak seasoning and a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Mix and form into 4 patties.
  • Raise heat on pan to medium high.
  • Add patties and cook 6 minutes on each side. Place cooked burgers on roll bottoms.
  • Top with sliced cucumber, tomato. sliced onion, shredded argula and hot peppers if desired.
  • Spread topping on top of rolls.
