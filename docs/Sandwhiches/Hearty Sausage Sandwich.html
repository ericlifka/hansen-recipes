Hearty Sausage Sandwich

  1. 1 lb. Italian sausage1 onion
  2. 1/2 cups chopped green pepper
  3. 1 garlic, mince
  4. 8 oz cubed cream cheese
  5. 1/4 cups fresh mushrooms
  6. 1/4 cups Parmesan cheese
  7. 1/4 cups water
  8. 1/4 tsp oregano
  9. 1 loaf unsliced Italian bread
  10. 1 1/2 cups (6 oz) mozzarella shred
  • In large skillet cook sausage, onion and green pepper and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
  • Add the cream cheese, mushrooms, parm cheese, water and oregano.
  • Cook and still until cheese is melted.
  • Cut bread in half horizontally; hollow out top and bottom, leaving a 3/4 inch shell.
  • Place bread bottom on baking sheet.
  • Sprinkle with half the mozzarella cheese; top with sausage mix and remaining cheese.
  • Replace bread top.
  • Bake at 375 for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
