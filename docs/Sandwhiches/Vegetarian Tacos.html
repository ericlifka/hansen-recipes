Vegetarian Tacos

  1. 8 taco shells
  2. 3 cups shredded cabbage
  3. 1 cups onion, sliced
  4. 1 cups julienned red pepper
  5. 2 Tbsp veg. oil
  6. 2 tsp sugar
  7. 15 oz. black beans, rinsed and drained
  8. 1 cups salsa
  9. 1 can green chilies
  10. 1 tsp chili powder
  11. 1 tsp garlic
  12. 1/4 tsp cumin
  13. 1/2 cups cheddar
  14. 1 medium ripe avocado, peeled and sliced
  • Heat taco shells according to package directions.
  • Meanwhile in a large skillet saut√© cabbage, onion and red pepper in oil for minutes or until crisp tender.
  • Sprinkle with sugar.
  • Stir in beans, salsa, chilies, chili powder, garlic and cumin.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until heated through.
  • Spoon into taco shells.
  • Garnish with cheese and avocado.
