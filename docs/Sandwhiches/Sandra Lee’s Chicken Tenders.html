Sandra Lee’s Chicken Tenders

  1. 1/2 cups flour
  2. 1/2 packet southwest steak marinade mix
  3. pepper
  4. 3 cups corn flakes
  5. Mix egg and 1/2 cups buttermilk
  6. 1 lb. tenders
  7. Paula Deen’s honey mustard:
  8. 3/4 cups mayo
  9. 3 Tbsp honey
  10. 2 Tbsp mustard
  11. 1 Tbsp lemon juice or juice from 1/2 lemon
  12. horseradish to taste
  13. 2 Tbsp orange juice (adjust amount for desired dressing consistency)
  • Combine flour, marinade and pepper.
  • Soak chicken in buttermilk, toss into flour, shake off and dip in milk again, then crumbs.
  • Fry until done (my deep fryer is 4 min. 30 sec.).
