Wendy’s Spicy Chicken

  1. 1/3 cups Frank’s red hot sauce mixed with 2/3 cups water
  2. 1 cups flour
  3. 2 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 3 tsp cayenne (or more)
  5. 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  6. 1/2 tsp paprika
  7. 1 tsp pepper and onion powder
  8. Buns
  9. breast fillets
  10. mayo
  11. lettuce
  12. tomato
  • I take a breast and beat to about 1/4-1/2” thickness.
  • I cut each breast to a size that will fit a bun.
  • Coat breast in flour + seasonings blend, then pepper sauce, then in flour again.
  • Fry in pan or deep fryer at 350 for 8-12 minutes, until brown and crispy, I usually do it 8 minutes.
