Janetta’s Sandwiches

  1. 2 boxes Steak-ums
  2. 1 can sliced mushrooms
  3. 3-4 green peppers
  4. 1 onion
  5. 4 beef bouillon cubes
  6. 1 pkg. onion soup mix
  7. salt
  8. pepper
  9. basil
  10. 2 garlic
  • Brown meat.
  • Sauté mushrooms, green peppers and onion.
  • Add to meat, then add other ingredients.
  • Simmer 1 hour.
  • Serve on kaiser rolls or sandwich buns.
