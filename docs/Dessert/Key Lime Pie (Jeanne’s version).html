Key Lime Pie (Jeanne’s version)

  1. 14 oz. sweetened condensed milk
  2. 4 egg yolks
  3. 1/2 cups key lime juice
  4. 1 graham cracker crust
  5. whipped cream
  • Combine milk and egg yolks at low speed.
  • Slowly add lime juice.
  • Mix until well blended.
  • Pour into crust and refrigerate overnight.
  • Top with whipped cream.
