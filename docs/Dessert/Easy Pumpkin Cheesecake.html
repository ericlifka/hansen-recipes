Easy Pumpkin Cheesecake

  1. 1 pkg. no bake cheesecake mix
  2. 3/4 cups pumpkin
  3. 1 tsp cinnamon
  4. 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  5. 1/4 tsp cloves
  6. 1/2 tsp vanilla
  7. 3/4 cups milk
  • Prepare crust in cheesecake mix according to directions, in a 9‚Äù pie plate.
  • Chill in freezer while preparing pie filling.
  • In small bowl combine cheesecake mix, 1 tsp cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.
  • Add milk, pumpkin, and vanilla.
  • Beat at low speed of mixer until blended; beat 3 minutes at medium speed.
  • Pour mix into crust.
  • Chill at least 1 hour.
  • Garnish with whipped cream.
