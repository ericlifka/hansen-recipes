Oatmeal Cake

  1. 1 cups oatmeal
  2. 1 1/2 cups water
  3. 1 cups each white and brown sugar
  4. 1/2 cups butter
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 1 Tbsp cinnamon
  7. 1 tsp salt, soda, and vanilla
  8. 1 1/3 cups flour
  • Beat everything but flour until mixed well.
  • Add flour and beat to mix.
  • This will be runnier than average cake mix.
  • Put in 9 x 13 pan and bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
  • Can be moist but not wet.
  • For topping while cake is in oven do this: put in small pan 2/3 cups butter and melt.
  • Add 1 1/3 cups brown sugar.
  • Do this over low heat.
  • Add 1/2 cups milk and 1 cups oatmeal.
  • As soon as this is mixed well take off stove.
  • When cake is done put topping on cake and put in oven on broil (not too near broiler) check every few minutes until nice and bubbly and golden brown.

  • It hardens as it cools.
  • Top with whip cream.
  • Can warm in microwave to serve warm.
