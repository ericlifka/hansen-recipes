Pumpkin Cheesecake

  1. 12 gingersnap cookies, crumbled
  2. 2 Tbsp butter, melted
  3. 6 oz. fat free cream cheese
  4. 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
  5. 16 oz. can pumpkin
  6. 3 Tbsp orange juice
  7. 3 eggs or 3/4 egg substitute
  8. 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  9. 1/4 tsp salt
  • Heat oven to 325.
  • Crush cookies and mix with butter.
  • Press firmly on bottom and halfway up side of 9‚Äù springform pan.
  • Set aside.
  • In bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
  • Gradually beat in condensed milk, pumpkin, eggs, orange juice, spice and salt.
  • Pour into crust.
  • Bake 60-75 minutes or until cake springs back when lightly touched.
  • Cool to room temperature.
  • Chill 25 minutes.
