Swirl Brownies

  1. 20 oz. pkg. brownie mix
  2. 8 oz. soft cream cheese
  3. 1/3 cups sugar
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • Prepare brownie mix as directed on pkg.
  • Combine cream cheese and sugar, mixing until well blended.
  • Blend in egg and vanilla.
  • Reserve 1/2 cups batter; spread remaining batter onto greased 9 x 13 pan.
  • Cover with cream cheese mix; spoon on reserved batter.
  • Cut through batter several times for marble effect.
  • Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes or until cream cheese mix is lightly browned.
