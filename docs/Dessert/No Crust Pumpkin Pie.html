No Crust Pumpkin Pie

  1. 15 oz. can pumpkin
  2. 12 oz. can evaporated milk
  3. 1/2 cups bisquick
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 3 tsp brown sugar
  6. 2/3 cups sugar
  7. 1 Tbsp vanilla
  8. 2 tsp cinnamon
  9. 1/2 tsp allspice, nutmeg
  10. 1/4 tsp ginger, cloves
  11. pinch of salt
  • Spray pan with PAM.
  • Throw all together and beat thoroughly.
  • Bake at 400 for 35-40 minutes.
  • Test with toothpick for doneness.
