Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. 1 1/2 cups flour
  2. 2 cups oatmeal
  3. 1 tsp soda, salt, and vanilla
  4. 1 cups shortening
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 3/4 cups each brown sugar and white sugar
  7. 1 tsp hot water
  8. 1 cups nuts
  9. 1 pkg. chocolate chips
  • Mix all together and bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
