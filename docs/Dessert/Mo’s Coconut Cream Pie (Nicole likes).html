Mo’s Coconut Cream Pie (Nicole likes)

  1. 1-9” deep dish pie crust
  2. 2 cups frozen coconut, thawed (2- 6 oz. pkg.)
  3. 4 eggs
  4. 2/3 cups sugar
  5. 1/4 tsp salt
  6. 1 tsp vanilla
  7. 3/4 cups heavy cream
  8. 1 cups half & half
  9. fresh nutmeg
  10. 1 Tbsp raw sugar
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • Place coconut in crust.
  • Combine eggs and sugar until light and creamy.
  • Stir in salt and vanilla.
  • Add cream and half and half, stirring to combine well.
  • Pour over coconut.
  • Grate nutmeg over and sprinkle with 1 Tbsp sugar.
  • Bake 45-50 minutes until pie is firm when jiggled.
  • Serve at room temperature with cream or caramel sauce.
