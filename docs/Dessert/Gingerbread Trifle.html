Gingerbread Trifle

  1. 14 oz Boxed Gingerbread
  2. 5.1 oz box Instant Vanilla Pudding
  3. 2 15 oz cans pumpkin puree
  4. 1 cups brown sugar
  5. 2 tsp cinnamon
  6. 12 oz tub cool whip
  • 1. Bake box of gingerbread and cool. 14 oz size
  • 2. Make large box vanilla instant pudding. 5.1 oz
  • 3. Mix 2 15 oz cans pumpkin with 1 cups brown sugar and 2 tsp cinnamon.
  • 4. Thaw 8-12 oz. cool whip.
  • 5. Layer crumbled gingerbread, vanilla pudding, pumpkin, whip cream until all is used up.
  • Garnish with a few gingerbread crumbs and sprinkles.
