Chocolate Chip Cookie Delight 2

  1. 1 tube 16 1/2 oz. refrigerated choc. chip cookie dough
  2. 8 oz. cream cheese softened
  3. 1 cups powdered sugar
  4. 12 oz. cool whip, thawed , divided
  5. 3 cups milk
  6. 1 3.9 package of instant choc. pudding
  7. 1 3.4 package instant vanilla pudding
  8. Chopped nuts and chocolate curls, optional
  • Let cookie dough stand at room temperature for 5-10 minutes to soften.
  • Press in ungreased 13 x 9 x 2 baking pan.
  • Bake at 350 for 14-16 minutes or until golden brown.
  • Cool.
  • In a large mixing bowl beat cr. cheese and powdered sugar until smooth.
  • Fold in 1 3/4 cups cool whip.
  • Spread over crust.
  • In a large bowl whisk milk and pudding mixes for 2 minutes.
  • Let stand 2 minutes until soft set.
  • Spread over cream cheese layer.
  • Top with remaining whipped topping.
  • Sprinkle with nuts.
  • Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
