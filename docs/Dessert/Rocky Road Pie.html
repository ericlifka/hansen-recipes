Rocky Road Pie

  1. 1 1/2 cups half and half
  2. 1 pkg. (4 serving) chocolate instant pudding
  3. 8 oz. cool whip
  4. 1/3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  5. 1/3 cups mini marshmallows and nuts
  6. graham crust
  • Pour half and half in bowl.
  • Add pudding.
  • Beat 1 minute or until well blended.
  • Let stand 5 minutes.
  • Fold in cool whip, chips, marshmallows, and nuts.
  • Spoon into crust.
  • Freeze 6 ours.
  • Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
