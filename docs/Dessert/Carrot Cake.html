Carrot Cake

  1. 4 eggs
  2. 2 cups sugar
  3. 1 1/2 cups veggie oil
  4. 1 tsp salt
  5. 2 tsp cinnamon
  6. 2 tsp soda
  7. 2 cups flour
  8. 1 cups nuts
  9. 1 cups coconut
  10. 3 cups grated carrots
  • Mix all and bake in a 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 40-45 minutes.
  • Frosting is made with 3-4 oz. cream cheese, 1/2 cups butter, 2 cups powdered sugar (may need more) 1 Tbsp milk, vanilla.
