Cream Puffs

  1. 1 cups water
  2. 1/2 cups butter
  3. 1 cups flour
  4. 4 eggs
  • Heat water and butter to a rolling boil.
  • Stir in flour.
  • Stir vigorously over low heat about 1 minute until it forms a ball.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Beat in eggs one at a time until smooth.
  • Drop dough by tablespoons 3‚Äù apart on greased baking sheets.
  • Bake at 400 for 35-40 minutes.
  • Cool.
  • Split puffs and pull out any filaments of soft dough.
  • Fill puffs with fruit, whipped cream, vanilla pudding or lemon filling.
  • Drizzle chocolate sauce over top.
