No Bake Pumpkin Dessert

  1. Crust:
  2. 1 cups flour
  3. 1/2 cups butter
  4. 2 Tbsp powdered sugar
  5. Mix together and bake in a 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Filling:
  7. 1 Tbsp Knox gelatin dissolved in 1/4 cups cold water.
  8. Mix together:
  9. 3/4 cups brown sugar
  10. 1/2 tsp salt
  11. 1/2 tsp ginger
  12. 2 tsp cinnamon (or more)
  13. 1/2 tsp allspice
  14. or in place of the spices 2 tsp pumpkin spice
  15. 3 egg yolks, beaten
  16. 1/2 cups milk
  17. 1 1/3 cups pumpkin.
  • Mix together and bring to boil over low heat.
  • Boil 1 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and add gelatin.
  • Cool.
  • Beat egg whites with 1/2 cups cream of tartar and 6 Tbsp sugar.
  • Fold into mix and pour on crust.
  • Refrigerate 24 hours.
