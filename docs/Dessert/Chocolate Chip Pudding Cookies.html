Chocolate Chip Pudding Cookies

  1. 2 1/2 cups flour
  2. 1 tsp baking soda
  3. 1 cups butter or 1/2 cups oil and melted butter
  4. 3/4 cups light brown sugar
  5. 1/4 cups sugar
  6. 1 pkg. (4 serving size) instant vanilla pudding
  7. 1 tsp vanilla
  8. 2 eggs
  9. 12 oz. Hershey's milk chocolate chips
  10. 1 cups nuts
  • Combine butter, sugars, pudding mix and vanilla in large bowl; beat until creamy.
  • Beat in eggs.
  • Gradually add flour and baking soda, then stir in chips and nuts.
  • Batter will be sort of stiff.
  • Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes.
