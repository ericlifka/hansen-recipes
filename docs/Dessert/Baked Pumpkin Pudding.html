Baked Pumpkin Pudding

  1. 1/2 cups butter
  2. 2 cups sugar
  3. 5 eggs
  4. 1/2 cups flour
  5. 1/2 cups evaporated milk
  6. 2 cups pumpkin
  7. 1 tsp cinnamon
  8. 1/2 tsp each nutmeg, ginger, mace, cloves
  9. 2 tsp vanilla
  • Combine butter and sugar.
  • Mix well.
  • Add eggs, mix well.
  • Stir in flour, pumpkin, spices and vanilla.
  • Mix until smooth.
  • Pour into a well greased 2 qt. casserole.
  • Bake at 350 for 55 minutes or until firm.
  • Top with pecan halves.
