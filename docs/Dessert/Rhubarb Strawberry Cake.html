Rhubarb Strawberry Cake

  1. 5 cups chopped rhubarb
  2. 1 cups sugar
  3. 1 3 oz. package strawberry jello
  4. 2 cups mini marshmallows
  5. 1 yellow cake box
  6. egg (for cake)
  7. oil (for cake)
  • Spread rhubarb in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
  • Sprinkle sugar, jello, and marshmallows over rhubarb
  • Mix 1 yellow cake mix as directed.
  • Spread over rhubarb.
  • Bake at 350 until done.
  • Serve warm or cold.
