Hot Fudge Pudding Cake

  1. 1 1/4 cups sugar, divided
  2. 1 cups flour
  3. 7 Tbsp cocoa, divided
  4. 2 tsp baking powder
  5. 1/4 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 cups milk
  7. 1/3 cups butter
  8. 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  9. 1/2 cups brown sugar
  10. 1 1/4 cups hot water
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • In bowl combine 3/4 cups sugar, flour, 3 Tbsp cocoa, baking powder and salt.
  • Blend in milk, butter and vanilla; beat until smooth.
  • Pour batter into 9 x 9 pan.
  • In small bowl combine 1/2 cups sugar, brown sugar, 4 Tbsp cocoa; sprinkle over batter.
  • Pour hot water over top.
  • Do not stir.
  • Bake 40 minutes or until center is set.
  • Let stand 15 minutes.
  • Serve.
