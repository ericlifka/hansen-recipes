Cherry Winks

  1. 2 1/4 cups flour
  2. 2 tsp baking powder
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 3/4 cups butter
  5. 1 cups sugar
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 2 Tbsp milk
  8. 1 tsp vanilla
  9. 1 cups nuts
  10. 1 cups finely chopped dates
  11. 1/3 cups finely chopped cherries
  12. 2 2/3 cups crushed corn flakes
  13. 15 cherries cut in quarters
  • Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
  • Set aside.
  • In bowl mix butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Add eggs, beat well.
  • Stir in milk and vanilla.
  • Add flour.
  • Mix well.
  • Stir in nuts, dates and cherries.
  • Shape tablespoon of dough into balls.
  • Roll in corn flakes.
  • Place on cookie sheets.
  • Top with cherry quarter.
  • Bake at 350 for 10-14 minutes.
