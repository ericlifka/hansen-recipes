Peanut Blossoms

  1. 1 3/4 cups flour
  2. 1/2 cups of both shortening and peanut butter
  3. 1 tsp baking soda1 egg
  4. 1/2 tsp salt
  5. 2 Tbsp milk
  6. 1/2 cups sugar
  7. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. 1/2 cups brown sugar (packed)
  9. Chocolate candy kisses
  • Combine all ingredients except candy.
  • Shape dough into balls.
  • Roll in sugar and place on cookie sheet.
  • Bake at 375ยบ for 10-12 minutes.
  • Top each cookie with candy, pressing down firmly.
