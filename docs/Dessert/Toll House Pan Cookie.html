Toll House Pan Cookie

  1. 2 1/4 cups flour
  2. 3/4 cups brown sugar
  3. 1 tsp baking soda, salt, and vanilla
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1 cups butter
  6. 12 oz. semisweet chocolate morsels
  7. 3/4 cups sugar
  8. 1 cups nuts
  • Heat oven to 375.
  • In bowl mix butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  • Beat until creamy.
  • Beat in eggs.
  • Add flour, salt, baking soda, mix.
  • Stir in morsels and nuts.
  • Spread the dough in greased 15 x 10 pan.
  • Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
  • Can also be cookies.
