Philly 3 Step Mini Cheesecakes

  1. 2 (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
  2. 1/2 cups sugar
  3. 1/2 tsp vanilla
  4. 2 eggs
  5. cool whip
  6. 2 pkg. (4 oz.) ready to use single serve graham cracker crumb crusts (12)
  • Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with mixer on medium speed until well blended.
  • Add eggs; mix until blended.
  • Pour into crusts on cookie sheet.
  • Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until centers are almost set.
  • Cool.
  • Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight.
  • Top with cool whip and sprinkle with candies, gummy bear, candy corn, or whatever.
