Champagne Cake

  1. 1 pkg. yellow cake mix
  2. 1 small pkg. instant vanilla pudding
  3. 1 1/3 cups milk
  4. 8 oz. cream cheese
  5. 12 oz. crushed pineapple, drained
  6. 1 pt. heavy cream
  • Make yellow cake mix according to directions on package and bake.
  • Make pudding mix with milk.
  • Add soft cream cheese and beat until smooth.
  • Spread over cooled cake.
  • Drain pineapple well and spread over top of pudding.
  • Whip cream and frost.
  • Keep refrigerated.
