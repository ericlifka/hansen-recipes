Hershey’s Chocolate Cake

  1. 2 cups sugar
  2. 1 3/4 cups flour
  3. 3/4 cups hershey’s cocoa
  4. 1 1/2 tsp baking powder & soda
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 1 cups milk
  8. 1/2 cups veggie oil
  9. 2 tsp vanilla
  10. 1 cups boiling water
  • Heat oven to 350.
  • Combine dry ingredients in bowl.
  • Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla.
  • Beat 2 minutes on medium speed.
  • Stir in boiling water.
  • Batter will be thin.
  • Pour into 2 9” pans.
  • Bake 30-35 minutes.
  • Cool and frost
