Sweet Potato Pie

  1. 1 1/4 cups cooked mashed sweet potato
  2. 3/4 cups sugar
  3. 1/2 cups brown sugar
  4. 1/2 cups french vanilla instant pudding
  5. 3/4 cups evaporated milk
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 6 Tbsp butter, softened
  8. 1 1/2 Tbsp vanilla
  9. 1 unbaked pie shell
  • In bowl, combine all ingredients except the pie shell.
  • Beat at medium speed until well blended.
  • Spread into pie shell.
  • Bake at 450 for 10 minutes, reduce temperature to 350 and bake 40 minutes longer or until set.
  • Cool and garnish with whipped cream.
