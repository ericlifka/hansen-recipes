California Medley Cheese Soup

  1. 1 1/2 qt. water
  2. 6 chicken bouillon cubes
  3. 1 cups celery
  4. 1/2-2 cups onion
  5. 2 1/2 cups potato cubes
  6. 1 cups diced carrots
  7. 16 oz. pkg. frozen california medley vet.
  8. 2 cans cr. of chicken soup
  9. 1/2 to 1 lb. cubed velveeta cheese
  • Combine water, bouillon, celery and onion.
  • Cooked covered 20 min.
  • Add potatoes, carrots and frozen veggies.
  • Cook until tender, about 30 min.
  • Add chicken soup and cheese.
  • Heat until cheese melts.
