Paula Deen’s Taco Chili with Mix

  1. Taco Chili mix:
  2. 1 cups dried kidney beans
  3. 1/2 cups dried pinto beans
  4. 1 pkg. taco mix
  5. 1 1 oz. pkg. buttermilk salad dressing mix
  6. 3 Tbsp dried minced onion
  7. 1 tsp chili powder
  8. 1/4 tsp cumin
  9. 1 1/2 cups corn or tortilla chips
  10. Taco Chili:
  11. 1 packet taco chili mix
  12. 4 cups water
  13. 11 3/4 ox. can diced tomatoes and green chilies
  14. 16 oz. tomato sauce
  15. 1 lb. hamburger, cooked
  • Mix up taco chili mix.
  • For chili Place beans in bowl cover with water and soak overnight.
  • Drain. Add to pan with taco seasoning mix, water, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and hamburger.
  • Boil, reduce to low and simmer for 2 hours until beans are tender.
  • Serve with chips.
  • I generally just skip the dried beans and use a can of kidney and pinto beans.
