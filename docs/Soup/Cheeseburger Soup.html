Cheeseburger Soup

  1. 1/2 lb. hamburger
  2. 3/4 cups each of onion, carrots, celery
  3. 1 tsp basil, parsley
  4. 4 Tbsp butter
  5. 3 cups broth
  6. 4 cups potato
  7. 1/4 cups flour
  8. 8 oz. velveeta
  9. 1 1/2 cups milk
  10. salt and pepper
  • Brown beef, set aside.
  • Saute vegies in 1 Tbsp butter.
  • Add broth, potato and beef.
  • Boil, reduce heat and simmer 10-12 minutes.
  • Melt 3 Tbsp butter, add flour and add milk until thick.
  • Add to beef/vegie mix and cook about 2 minutes.
  • Add the velveeta and cook until melted.
