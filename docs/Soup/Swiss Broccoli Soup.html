Swiss Broccoli Soup

  1. 1 cups instant dry milk
  2. 3 Tbsp flour
  3. 1/2 tsp salt, pepper, garlic powder
  4. 1 can chicken broth
  5. 1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese
  6. 2 1/4 cups water
  7. 1/4 stick butter
  8. 1/4 cups chopped green onion
  9. 10 oz. chopped broccoli, cooked
  • Combine dry milk, flour and seasonings in 3 qt. pan.
  • Stir in water and chicken broth until smooth.
  • Bring to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  • Boil and stir 1 min.
  • Saut√© onion in butter until tender.
  • Stir onion and broccoli into milk.
  • Remove from heat, add cheese until melted.
  • Serve.
