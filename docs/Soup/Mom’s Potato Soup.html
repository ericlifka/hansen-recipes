Mom’s Potato Soup

  1. 3-4 diced potato
  2. 1 onion
  3. 1 1/2 cups macaroni
  4. 1/2 to 1 qt. milk or half and half
  5. salt, pepper
  6. 1 Tbsp butter
  • Put potato, onion and macaroni in pan.
  • Add water just to cover.
  • Cook about 5-8 minutes or until potato is tender.
  • Do not drain.
  • Add butter, salt and pepper.
  • Add milk to desired thickness.
  • Serve.
