TGI Friday’s Black Bean Soup

  1. 2 Tbsp veggie oil
  2. 3/4 cups onion
  3. 3/4 cups celery
  4. 1/2 cups carrot
  5. 1/4 cups green pepper
  6. 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  7. 4 15 oz. cans black beans
  8. 4 cups chicken stock
  9. 2 tsp chili powder
  10. 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  11. 1/2 tsp each cayenne pepper, cumin, salt
  12. 1/4 tsp concentrated liquid smoke (hickory)
  • Heat oil in large pan over med/ low heat.
  • Add onion, celery, carrot, bell pepper and garlic to oil and simmer slowly for 15 minutes or until onions are practically clear.
  • Keep heat low so veggies don’t brown.
  • Pour beans into a strainer and rinse with cold water.
  • Measure 3 cups of the beans into a food processor with 1 cups chicken stock.
  • Puree until smooth.
  • When veggies are ready, pour the pureed beans, the whole beans, rest of chicken stock and rest of ingredients to the pot.
  • Bring mix to a boil, reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 50-60 minutes or until soup has thickened and all ingredients are tender.
  • Serve.
