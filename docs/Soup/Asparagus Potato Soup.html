Asparagus Potato Soup

  1. 3 medium potatoes
  2. 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  3. 1/3 cups onion
  4. 1 tsp salt
  5. 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  6. 1 pkg. frozen asparagus (10 oz.)
  7. 1 1/2 cups cream
  8. 4 oz. cream cheese with pimento
  • Dice potatoes and cook in broth, onion, salt and nutmeg.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer covered 5-8 min. until potatoes are tender.
  • Add asparagus and boil.
  • Reduce heat, simmer 5 min. until tender.
  • Add cream cheese.
  • When melted, serve.
