Cream of Cilantro Soup

  1. 1 bunch cilantro
  2. 32 oz. chicken broth
  3. 2 Tbsp butter
  4. 2 Tbsp flour
  5. 8 oz. cream cheese
  6. 8 oz. sour cream
  7. 1 clove garlic
  8. 1/4 tsp salt, red pepper and cumin
  • Remove stems and chop cilantro.
  • Process cilantro and 1 cups broth in blender.
  • Melt butter, whisk in flour.
  • Slowly add 3 cups broth till smooth.
  • Boil 1 minute.
  • Stir in cilantro and rest of ingredients.
  • Simmer 15 minutes.
