Spicy Pumpkin Soup

  1. 3 Tbsp butter
  2. 3 cups onion
  3. 1 1/2 cups pumpkin
  4. 1 cups whole milk
  5. 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  6. 3/4 tsp red pepper--use less!
  7. 1/4 cups sour cream
  8. 1/4 cups cocoa milk
  9. 1/2 tsp lime juice.
  • Cook butter and onion 10 minutes and then add rest of stuff through red pepper.
