French Onion Soup (Good)

  1. 2 Tbsp butter
  2. 5 medium red onions (3 lb.)
  3. 6 cups chicken broth
  4. 1 3/4 cups beef broth
  5. 1/4 cups red wine
  6. 2 sprigs parsley
  7. 1 tsp thyme
  8. 1 bay leaf
  9. 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Melt butter.
  • Add onions.
  • Cook 3 minutes until onions are reduced.
  • Stir in rest of stuff.
  • Simmer about 20 minutes.
  • Stir in vinegar and seasonings.
