Black Bean Soup

  1. 1 lb. black turtle beans
  2. 2 qt. water
  3. 1/2 lb. thick bacon
  4. 1 1/2 cups onion, celery, carrot
  5. 1 Tbsp garlic1 bay leaf
  6. 1 tsp thyme
  7. 3 Tbsp cumin
  8. 1/2 Tbsp oregano
  9. salt and pepper
  10. 10 cups chicken broth
  11. 2 ham hocks
  12. 1/4 tsp cayenne
  13. 4 Tbsp lime juice
  • Soak beans overnight in water.
  • Drain.
  • Saut√© bacon until brown.
  • Add onion, celery, and carrots about 5 min.
  • Add garlic, bay leaf, thyme, 1 Tbsp cumin, oregano.
  • Cook 5 min.
  • Add broth and ham hocks.
  • Boil and cook over med. heat 2 to 2 1/2 hours.
  • Remove hocks and add meat, cayenne, lime juice and cumin.
  • Serve with dollop of sour cream.
